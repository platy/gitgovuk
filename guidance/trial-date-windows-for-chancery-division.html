About trial date windows

Chancery cases are usually given a five-day window during which they are listed to start at the High Court.

Most trials begin on the first or second day of the window.

Length of trial Trial held within these dates Final day for appointment to fix trial date
1 to 2 days July 2022 to December 2022 3 May 2022
Between 2 and 5 days March 2023 to June 2023 3 May 2022
Between 5 and 10 days April 2023 to July 2023 3 May 2022
Over 10 days June 2023 onward 3 May 2022
