Changes to commodity codes

Add the following commodity codes:

  • 4421 2000 10 — of fibreboard

  • 4421 2000 90 — other

  • 8485 8000 10 — machines for additive manufacturing by sand, concrete or other mineral products deposit
  • 8485 8000 90 — other

CHIEF has been updated and Tariff online will be updated shortly.

More information

To stop getting the Tariff stop press notices, or to add recipients to the distribution list, email: tariff.management@hmrc.gov.uk.

See the UK Trade Tariff for the full duty rate, unit of quantity, preferences, quotas, footnotes and VAT rate.
