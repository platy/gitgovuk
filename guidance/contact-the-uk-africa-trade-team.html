How we can help

Our team can help you to:

  • find opportunities in the UK for your exports
  • understand the technicalities of exporting, such as taxes and regulations
  • know what support the UK government and other organisations can provide to help you trade

Read guidance on how to export goods to the UK.

Contact us

Email GrowthGateway@fcdo.gov.uk and tell us:

  • the country(ies) you are based in
  • which market(s) you are interested in
  • the sector your business operates in
  • the product(s) or service(s) your business produces/sells
  • if you have contacted a UK government trade team before
  • which trade and/or investment issues you want help with

Eligibility

Some trade activities are excluded from our support. For example, trade in weapons and other prohibited or restricted goods.

Confidentiality

We will keep your answers confidential and secure in accordance with UK data protection regulations. Read the Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office privacy notice.
