Overview

Colleges, providers, schools and academies receive annual funding allocations from Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) for the provision of 16 to 19 education.

We have published further information on how 16 to 19 funding works, including detailed information on the 16 to 19 funding formula. You can read more about how we allocate funding on the 16 to 19 funding allocations page.

On this page, you will find the guidance documents for each academic year that all education institutions receiving 16 to 19 formula funding must comply with.

Funding guidance for institutions

2022 to 2023

Funding regulations

The funding regulations guide for 2022 to 2023 provides the funding principles, rules on eligibility, and other regulations used in the 16 to 19 funding model.

Rates and formula

The rates and formula guide for 2022 to 2023 provides information on the funding rates and formula used for 16 to 19 funding calculations.

Individualised learner record (ILR) funding returns

We aim to publish the ILR funding returns guide for 2022 to 2023 in June. It provides technical advice on making final funding claims. It sets out the reconciliation process for contract institutions.
