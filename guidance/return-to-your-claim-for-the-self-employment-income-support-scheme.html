Return to your claim

Use this service to check:

  • the status of your payment
  • how much you were paid for SEISS grants
  • why you are not eligible

Start now

If you think the grant amount is too low

We have used the information you or your tax agent or adviser sent us on your Self Assessment tax returns to work out your grant amount.

If you think the grant amount is too low, you can check how we calculated the grant or contact your tax agent or adviser for help.

If you still think the grant amount is too low, you or your agent can contact HMRC. You must contact us by 28 February 2022.

You or your agent will need:

  • your grant claim reference
  • your National Insurance number
  • the Unique Taxpayer Reference you used on your claim
  • details about why you think the grant amount is too low
  • the Government Gateway user ID you used to make a claim

You may also need:

  • the figures we gave you in the grant calculation when you made your claim
  • to answer further questions

If you still need to make a claim

You must contact us by 28 February 2022 if you’ve not been able to make a claim due to:

  • an HMRC error
  • other exceptional circumstances

You’ll need to explain why you’ve not been able to claim. You’ll also need your:

  • National Insurance number
  • Unique Taxpayer Reference

You may also have to answer further questions.

Check guidance for the fifth grant

Claims for the fifth grant have now closed. You can check:

  1. Eligibility criteria for the fifth grant.
  2. How to work out your turnover.
  3. Records you need to keep after making a claim.

You can also read guidance for previous SEISS grants on The National Archives website.

How to pay back a grant

You can tell HMRC and pay back some or all of the grant if you received:

  • a grant and were not eligible
  • more than you were entitled to
