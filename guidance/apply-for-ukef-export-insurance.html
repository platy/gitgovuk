Apply for UKEF export insurance

Make a full application for UK Export Finance (UKEF) export insurance by downloading and filling in the proposal form.

From:
UK Export Finance
Published
7 September 2022

Use this form for either a single or multiple contract policy.

You’ll need your:

  • company details and financial information
  • buyer’s details
  • company’s trading history with the buyer, if you’ve worked together before
  • your code of conduct, if you have one

Depending on your export contract, you may also need:

  • your buyer’s annual report and accounts
  • details about the environmental impact of your export contract

Make a separate application for each buyer you want to export to.

Download form

You can find out more about UKEF export insurance, if you want to know who’s eligible and what’s covered.
