Making Tax Digital for VAT

HMRC will send an email to customers who have signed up for Making Tax Digital for VAT using the sign up for Making Tax Digital page.

HMRC will confirm you can submit VAT Returns within 72 hours using the email address you gave. The email will not ask you for any financial information.

Online sales tax research

From April 2022 up to and including 31 March 2023, HMRC is contacting online businesses to take part in research on a new potential online sales tax.

  • used for research purposes only

Notices issued by email will contain a link to this webpage, so you can see that HMRC is using email for this purpose. We will not ask you for any financial information.

VAT debts reminders
