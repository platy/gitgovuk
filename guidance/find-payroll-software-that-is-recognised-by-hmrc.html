Choosing software

Before choosing payroll software you should find out how to get your business ready to employ staff.

This payroll software is recognised by HMRC. You can use it to report PAYE to us online.

You should consider which features you need and check the software you choose has those features.

HMRC cannot recommend one product or service over another. We’re also not responsible for any problems you have with software you’ve bought.
