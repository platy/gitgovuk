About this notice

This privacy notice explains how we collect, use and protect your personal data; and the rights you have regarding that data.

This notice applies to processing your personal data through The Local Government Capacity Centre’s Learning Programme. Please refer to Homes England’s main privacy notice for further information on how we process personal data for other purposes.

Who we are

Homes England is the trading name adopted by the Homes and Communities Agency, our legal entity.

We are the data controller of the personal data processed. This means that we are responsible for deciding how we collect, use and store your personal data.

Information we collect

Homes England collects personal data about you when you sign up via MS Webinar and through our feedback survey.

We collect, store and use the following categories of personal data relating to your:

  • first name
  • surname
  • email address
  • profession
  • region
  • company or organisation
  • images

The learning sessions are recorded for the purposes of publishing to Homes England’s Youtube and other channels. You may be identifiable through the personal data you provide. Before publication, a supplier will be appointed to remove all participants’ personal data (including audio, chat and images).

How we process your information

We use the personal data stated above for sending you:

  • joining instructions to the learning programme

  • presentation slides

  • links to feedback surveys

  • links to the presentation recordings

Lawful basis to process personal data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. This is called the lawful basis. In regards to the above purposes, we rely on the following lawful basis.

Legitimate interest

Processing is necessary for the purposes of the legitimate interests pursued by Homes England or by a third party, except where such interests are overridden by your interests or fundamental rights and freedoms which require the protection of personal data.

Who your information is shared with

We may share your data with third parties, including third-party service providers.

We require third parties to respect the security of your data and treat it according to the law. We may transfer your personal information outside of the EU. If we do, you can expect a similar degree of protection in respect of your personal information.

Homes England may share your data with:

  • A supplier to be appointed for the purposes of removing any participant auditory, chat or video content from the live recordings of the learning sessions to enable them to be published on the Homes England YouTube channel.

  • The learning sessions will be run using Microsoft Teams, and participants will be invited to interact using Microsoft Forms. Both these products sit within Homes England’s Microsoft tenancy

Where your personal data is stored and processed

All personal data collected directly by Homes England will be stored securely on Homes England IT systems, with only personnel who need access for the purposes of the processing set out above able to access the data.

Where data is shared with third parties, it will be undertaken through secure file transfer or upload. Please refer to the third-party privacy notices to determine the processing and data storage at the point when personal data is shared with those third parties.

How long we keep your personal data for

Homes England will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it is collected.

Your rights

Under data protection legislation, in certain circumstances, you have the following rights over the processing of your personal data as described in this notice:

  • right of access to personal data

  • right to correction or rectification

  • right to request deletion

  • right to data portability

Homes England must respond to a request to exercise these rights within one month. Please contact the data protection officer if you want to submit a request.
