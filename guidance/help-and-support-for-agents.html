Live webinars

HMRC is delivering a programme of webinars about the support available to help customers and agents during the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic.

Find out more about help and support if your business is affected by COVID-19.

You can also use HMRC’s digital assistant to find more information about COVID-19 support schemes.

Penalties for enablers of defeated tax avoidance

Watch a recorded webinar about penalties for enablers of defeated tax avoidance.

This webinar gives an overview of the penalties for enablers of defeated tax avoidance legislation. It is specifically designed to help tax agents understand how this legislation impacts them and under what circumstances they could be liable for a penalty.

Loan charge compliance and further information

Watch a recorded webinar about loan charge compliance and further information.

This webinar covers:

  • HMRC’s compliance activity in relation to individuals who should have paid the loan charge but failed to do so
  • disguised remuneration
  • updates on the loan charge since the last webinar

Profit diversion compliance facility

Watch a recorded webinar about profit diversion compliance facility.

This webinar gives an update on HMRC’s profit diversion facility including:

  • the approach to tackling transfer pricing and diverted profits tax risks
  • key aspects of the profit diversion compliance facility process
  • benefits of the facility
  • an overview of how the facility has been operating so far

Notification of uncertain tax treatment by large business

Watch a recorded webinar about the notification of uncertain tax treatment by large business.

The webinar gives an overview of uncertain tax treatment. It explains:

  • the main requirements
  • early identification by large businesses
  • how to comply

Business taxes

Basis periods

Watch a recorded webinar about basis periods.

This webinar gives information about:

  • the different rules that apply to commencement years
  • the effect on a basis period when there’s a change of accounting date
  • overlapping periods

We also look at some pointers when dealing with a change from sole trader to partnership, and from partnership to sole trader.

Capital allowances and vehicles

  • annual Self Assessment programme covering the rules for cars
  • qualifying expenditure
  • pools and rates
  • vehicle hire purchase

Trade losses

This webinar is part of our annual Self Assessment programme. It gives examples of:

  • how trade losses can be relieved
  • the different loss relief provisions
  • time limits applicable to each claim
  • further points to consider

Employing people

Risks for agencies and other businesses working with umbrella companies operating avoidance schemes

Watch a recorded webinar about risks for agencies and other businesses.

This webinar gives an overview of the risks for agencies and other businesses who use the services of umbrella companies in their supply chains. It is targeted at employers who use temporary labour.

Check Employment Status for Tax tool

Watch a recorded webinar about the Check Employment Status for Tax (CEST) Tool to learn how it can help you make robust and accurate status determinations. During this session, we explain a status determination using a mock engagement.

Off-payroll working rules from April 2021 — contracted out services and statements of work

Watch a recorded webinar about off-payroll working rules from April 2021 — contracted out services and statements of work.

This webinar explains:

  • when HMRC will consider a service as being fully contracted out
  • the effect of statements of work in the context of the off-payroll working rules

Off-payroll working rules from April 2021 — contractors

Watch a recorded webinar about off-payroll working rules from April 2021 — contractors.

This webinar gives an overview of the changes to the off-payroll working rules which came into effect on 6 April 2021. It is specifically designed to help contractors understand what these changes mean for them.

Off-payroll working rules 2021 — international matters

Watch a recorded webinar about off-payroll working rules from April 2021 — international matters.

Off-payroll working rules from April 2021 — engagement

This webinar explains:

Off-payroll working rules from April 2021 — status determinations and disagreements

Watch a recorded webinar about off-payroll working rules from April 2021 — status determinations and disagreements.

This webinar explains what status determination statements (SDS) are, within the off-payroll working rules, and how to deal with disagreements following the status determination.

Off-payroll working rules from April 202 — fee-payer responsibilities

Watch a recorded webinar about off-payroll working rules from April 2021 — fee-payer responsibilities.

This webinar looks at fee-payers responsibilities under the off-payroll working rules including:

  • who feepayers are and their responsibilities
  • qualifying persons
  • deemed employers and
  • how to work out the deemed direct payment

Online services

Making Tax Digital for VAT

Watch a recorded webinar about Making Tax Digital for VAT.

Register for the next live webinar about Making Tax Digital for VAT.

This webinar explains the basics of Making Tax Digital for VAT, including:

  • what’s changing
  • using software and keeping digital records
  • an introduction to penalty reform

From April 2022, these requirements apply to all VAT-registered businesses, including those that have a turnover below the VAT threshold.
