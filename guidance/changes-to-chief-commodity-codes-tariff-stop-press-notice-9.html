Changes to commodity codes

Add the following commodity codes:

  • 2202 9919 95 — de-alcoholised wine with an alcoholic strength by volume not exceeding 0.5% volume
  • 2922 1900 10 — 4,4-bis(dimethylamino)-4’’-(methylamino)trityl alcohol (with ≥ 0.1% of Michler’s ketone (EC No 202-027-5) or Michler’s base CAS No: 561-41-1
  • 2930 9098 54 ― 2-ethylhexyl 10-ethyl-4, 4-dioctyl-7-oxo-8-oxa-3, 5-dithia- 4-stannatetradecanoate (DOTE)
  • 2931 1000 10 — Tetramethyl lead
  • 2931 1000 20 — Tetraethyl lead
  • 3812 3990 50 — reaction mass of 2-ethylhexyl 10-ethyl-4, 4-dioctyl-7-oxo- 8-oxa-3,5-dithia-4-stannatetradecanoate and 2-ethylhexyl 10-ethyl-4-[[2-[(2-ethylhexyl)oxy]-2-oxoethyl]thio]-4-octyl- 7-oxo-8-oxa-3,5-dithia-4-stannatetradecanoate (reaction mass of DOTE and MOTE)
  • 3824 9992 21 ― reaction products of 1,3,4-thiadiazolidine-2,5-dithione, formaldehyde and 4-heptylphenol, branched and linear (RP-HP) (with ≥ 0.1% w/w 4-heptylphenol, branched and linear)
  • 7310 1000 10 — kegs, vessels, drums, tanks, casks and similar containers, excluding cans and boxes, refillable, of stainless steel, with bodies approximately cylindrical in shape, with a wall thickness of 0.5 millimetres or more, of a kind used for materials other than crude oil and petroleum
  • 7310 1000 90 — other
  • 7310 2990 10 — kegs, vessels, drums, tanks, casks and similar containers, excluding cans and boxes, refillable, of stainless steel, with bodies approximately cylindrical in shape, with a wall thickness of 0.5 mm or more, of a kind used for materials other than crude oil and petroleum
