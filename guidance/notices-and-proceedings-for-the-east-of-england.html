Areas covered

The Counties of Bedfordshire, Buckinghamshire, Cambridgeshire, Hertfordshire, Essex, Leicestershire, Lincolnshire (except the Districts of North Lincolnshire and North East Lincolnshire), Norfolk, Northamptonshire and Suffolk.

The Districts of Leicester, Luton, Milton Keynes, Peterborough, Rutland, Southend-on-Sea and Thurrock.

Check applications or find operators

You can:

  • check vehicle operator licensing Notices and Proceedings by name, date and area
  • find a bus operator by name, address or licence number
  • find registered local bus services by operator name, licence number, or start and finish point
