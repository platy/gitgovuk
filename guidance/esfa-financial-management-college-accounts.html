ESFA financial management: college accounts

The latest release of data is taken from Education and Skills Funding Agency’s database of college finance records for the year ended 31 July 2021. The data file contains 227 of 231 expected returns. National colleges are not included in this dataset.

Finance records should replicate colleges’ audited financial statements insofar as they are able. They are not subject to independent external audit.

Only fully complete 2020 to 2021 finance records submitted to ESFA by 10 May 2022 have been included.

We do not validate or cleanse finance record data used in this data release and we are not responsible for any decisions or judgements made using the information.

Additional information is set out in our college accounts direction.

This page contains 5 years of financial information.

Please raise any queries using our enquiry form.
