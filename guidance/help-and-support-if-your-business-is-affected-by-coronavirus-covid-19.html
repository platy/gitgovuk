Guidance

Help and support if your business is affected by coronavirus (COVID-19)

Watch videos, sign up for email alerts and register for free webinars to learn more about the support available if your business is affected by coronavirus.

HM Revenue & Customs

You can sign up for email alerts about support available if your business is affected by coronavirus (COVID-19).

If you register for a webinar, you:

  • must register and sign in 5 minutes before it starts
  • can ask questions using the on-screen text box

  • the extension of the scheme
  • how employers will be affected
  • flexible furloughing
  • key dates
  • support available

Self-Employment Income Support Scheme (SEISS) – fourth grant

Watch a video about the Coronavirus (COVID-19) Self-Employment Income Support Scheme (SEISS) – fourth grant.

Watch a video about the Coronavirus (COVID-19) Self-Employment Income Support Scheme (SEISS) – fourth grant.

Watch a recorded webinar about the Self-Employment Income Support Scheme (SEISS) – fourth grant. You will learn about:

  • the aim of the scheme
  • who can apply
  • how much you may be entitled to
  • how to claim the fourth grant
  • what happens after you have claimed

Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) Rebate Scheme

Watch a video about the Coronavirus Statutory Sick Pay Rebate Scheme.

Watch a video about the Coronavirus Statutory Sick Pay Rebate Scheme.

  • who can claim
  • when to start paying SSP
  • employees you can claim for
  • making a claim
  • keeping records and more
