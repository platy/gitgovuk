If you would like your details published on GOV.UK, email SDSTeam@hmrc.gov.uk and include your:

  • business name
  • email address
  • telephone number

The following list has contact details of software developers who can provide help with entry summary declarations (safety and security declarations for imports).

Software developers who have not agreed to have their details published on GOV.UK are not included.

HMRC is not responsible for any losses or expenses you may incur using software from these providers.
