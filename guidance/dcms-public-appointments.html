Welcome to DCMS public appointments

DCMS public appointments are vacancies to join the boards of our public bodies — from well-known museums and galleries, to sports organisations and heritage trusts. Board members offer their own perspective on how our organisations should be run so that our public bodies deliver for everyone.

Diversity in DCMS public appointments

We are committed to ensuring DCMS’s public bodies are fully representative of the communities they serve. If you’ve never thought about applying for a public appointment in the past, we would love to hear from you. These board roles provide the opportunity to make a real difference, and our public bodies are at their very best when they are led by people with a diverse range of views and ideas. We encourage applications from talented individuals from all backgrounds and across the whole of the United Kingdom..
