You can sign up for email alerts about the Construction Industry Scheme.

If you register for a webinar, you:

  • must register and sign in 5 minutes before it starts
  • can ask questions using the on-screen text box

Contractors

Watch a recorded webinar about the Construction Industry Scheme for contractors.

It will give you an overview of the scheme and you will learn about:

  • how to engage and pay subcontractors
  • what information you need to report to HMRC
  • how long you need to keep records for

Subcontractors

Watch a recorded webinar about the Construction Industry Scheme for subcontractors. It will give you an overview of the scheme and you will learn about:

  • how to start the scheme
  • payment and deduction statements
  • records you need to keep

VAT reverse charge for construction services

Watch a recorded webinar on the VAT reverse charge for construction service.

If you are in the Construction Industry Scheme, you will find out:

  • if the changes affect you
  • how they may impact your cashflow
  • how to prepare for the change

Register for the next live webinar about the VAT reverse charge for construction service.
