Making Tax Digital for VAT

Register for the next live webinar about Making Tax Digital for VAT.

This webinar will provide some of the basics of Making Tax Digital for VAT, including:

  • what’s changing
  • using software
  • keeping digital records
  • an introduction to penalty reform

From April 2022, these requirements will apply to all VAT-registered businesses, to include those that have a turnover below the VAT threshold.
