Haulier advice site locations

Find sites where HGV and coach drivers can get free COVID-19 tests and check their documents to transport goods to the EU.

At the Department for Transport’s haulier advice sites, drivers and crew of HGVs, LGVs, coaches and vans can:

  • take a free coronavirus (COVID-19) test
  • find out about the rules and documents needed to move goods between the UK and EU by getting a border readiness check

COVID-19 testing

Hauliers who enter England from abroad need to take tests if they are in England for 2 days or longer.

Hauliers travelling from England should check foreign travel advice for any COVID-19 entry requirements for the countries they are travelling to.

Find your nearest haulier advice site

Haulier advice sites are at motorway service stations and truck stops. Most haulier advice sites are open between 6am and 9.30pm, 7 days a week.

Haulier advice sites aboard ferries

Hauliers can also get advice about the rules and documents needed to move goods between the UK and EU on the following ferry routes.

Ferry Route Ferry Operator Days when advice site is active
Harwich to Hook of Holland Stena Line Monday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday
Portsmouth to Caen Brittany Ferries Monday, Thursday and Friday
Dover to Calais DFDS Monday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday
Dover to Dunkirk DFDS Monday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday
Newhaven to Dieppe DFDS Monday, Wednesday, Thursday and Friday

Hook of Holland advice site

There is also a haulier advice site located in the Hook of Holland port.

Opening hours

Monday to Friday from 6am to 10pm

Saturday and Sunday from 8am to 8pm.
