Find sites where HGV and coach drivers can get free COVID-19 tests and check their documents to transport goods to the EU.

From:
Department for Transport
Published
4 November 2020
Last updated
23 May 2021 — See all updates
Applies to:
Wales, England, and Scotland

At the Department for Transport’s haulier advice sites, drivers and crew of HGVs, LGVs, coaches and vans can:

  • take a free coronavirus (COVID-19) test
  • find out about the rules and documents needed to move goods between the UK and EU by getting a border readiness check

COVID-19 testing

Hauliers need proof of a negative COVID-19 test to enter some countries.

Hauliers who enter England from abroad need to take tests if they are in England for longer than 2 days.

Find your nearest haulier advice site

Haulier advice sites are at motorway service stations and truck stops. Most haulier advice sites are open between 6am and 9.30pm, 7 days a week.
