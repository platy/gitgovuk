At the Department for Transport’s haulier advice sites, drivers and crew of HGVs, LGVs, coaches and vans can:

  • take a free coronavirus (COVID-19) test
  • find out about the rules and documents needed to move goods between the UK and EU by getting a border readiness check

COVID-19 testing

Hauliers who enter England from abroad and are fully vaccinated will not need to take any COVID-19 travel tests before or after arrival in England.

Hauliers who do not qualify as fully vaccinated must take an arrival lateral flow test by day 2.

Hauliers travelling from England should check foreign travel advice for any COVID-19 entry requirements for the countries they are travelling to.

Find your nearest haulier advice site

Haulier advice sites are at motorway service stations and truck stops. Most haulier advice sites are open between 6am and 9.30pm, 7 days a week.
