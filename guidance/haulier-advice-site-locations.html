Applies to England, Scotland and Wales

All haulier advice sites will shut down 8pm, Friday 13 May.

At haulier advice sites, drivers and crew of HGVs, LGVs and vans can get a border readiness check to find out about the rules and documents needed to move goods between the UK and EU.

Find your nearest haulier advice site

Haulier advice sites are at motorway service stations and truck stops. The sites are open between 8am and 8pm.

Site Information and advice operational hours
Charnock Richard Services northbound Monday to Saturday, 8am to 8pm (closed Sundays). Site shuts down 8pm, Friday 13 May.
Lockerbie Truck Stop Monday to Friday, 8am to 8pm (closed at weekends). Site shuts down 8pm, Friday 13 May.
Membury eastbound Monday to Saturday, 8am to 8pm (closed Sundays). Site shuts down 8pm, Friday 13 May.
Rugby Stobart Truck Stop Monday to Saturday, 8am to 8pm (closed Sundays). Site shuts down 8pm, Friday 13 May.
Thurrock Services Monday to Saturday, 8am to 8pm (closed Sundays). Site shuts down 8pm, Friday 13 May.

COVID-19 testing

Hauliers who enter England from abroad do not need to take any COVID-19 travel tests before or after arrival in England.

This applies whether you are vaccinated or not.

Hauliers travelling from England should check foreign travel advice for any COVID-19 entry requirements for the countries they are travelling to.

COVID-19 testing for hauliers at haulier advice sites ended 31 March.
