Report your gender pay gap data

Use this service to report the pay gap between men and women in your organisation.

From:
Government Equalities Office
Published
10 January 2022
Last updated
30 August 2022 — See all updates

You must report your gender pay gap data if your organisation has 250 or more employees on a specific date each year.

Find out who needs to report their gender pay gap data.

The deadlines each year are:

  • 30 March – for most public authority employers
  • 4 April – for private, voluntary and all other public authority employers

Before you start

Read the guidance for employers on:

  • what you have to report

  • how to gather your data

  • how to make your calculations

You can input, edit and save your calculations if you are not ready to submit them.
