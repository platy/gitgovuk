Guidance

Software developers providing customs declaration software

Check a list of software developers who can provide Customs Declaration Service and Customs Handling of Import and Export Freight (CHIEF) software.

From:
HM Revenue & Customs
Published
20 October 2020
Last updated
25 February 2022 — See all updates

HMRC cannot recommend or endorse any one product or service over another.

If you would like your details published on GOV.UK, you will need to give your:

  • business name
  • email address
  • telephone number

For developers providing customs declaration software, send an email to the Software Developer Support Team at: SDSTeam@hmrc.gov.uk.

For developers providing Customs Handling of Import and Export Freight (CHIEF) software send an email to: cfsp_cope@hmrc.gov.uk.

The following list has contact details of developers who provide software for the Customs Declaration Service or CHIEF.

Software developers who have not agreed to have their details published on GOV.UK are not included.

HMRC is not responsible for any losses or expenses you may incur using software from these providers.
