Search UK trade mark classes

Use this service to find goods and services that would be acceptable as part of a UK trade mark application.

From:
Intellectual Property Office
Published
1 January 2021
Last updated
17 May 2022 — See all updates

The search UK trade mark classes service will be unavailable between 6:30am and 10pm Saturday 21 May 2022.

When applying to register a trade mark, you must use the classification system to specify the goods and/or services you’ll be using on it.

Our search UK trade mark classes service can help you search for, and classify, the goods and/or service you need to apply for to register your trade mark.

If you need further help and support you can:

  • email the Intellectual Property Office Classification Team
  • speak to a Trade Mark Attorney. The Chartered Institute of Trade Mark Attorneys can help you locate one in your area
