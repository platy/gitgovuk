Who we are

Our team brings together international commercial expertise from across UK government and the private sector. We direct businesses and other organisations to the most relevant UK government information and resources on trade, finance, and investment. We also provide specialist guidance and support.

How we can help

Our business support service promotes 2-way trade and investment between UK and African businesses, creating connections for a positive development and commercial impact. We support African businesses wanting to export to, or invest in, the UK, and UK businesses wanting to export to, or invest in, Africa by:

  • helping you access UK government trade and investment services and programmes
  • providing guidance and information relevant to your business and business goals

Read our guidance on:

  • exporting to the UK from Africa
  • investing in the UK

Contact us

Email GrowthGateway@fcdo.gov.uk and tell us:

  • the country(ies) you are based in
  • which market(s) you are interested in
  • the sector your business operates in
  • the product(s) or service(s) your business produces/sells
  • if you have contacted a UK government trade team before
  • which trade and/or investment issues you want help with
