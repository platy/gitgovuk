Applications are listed by product type. Products that are eligible will be published for consultation.

Designation categories are:

  • protected designation of origin (PDO)
  • protected geographical indication (PGI)
  • traditional speciality guaranteed (TSG)

Fresh meat products

This category includes:

  • fresh meat
  • fresh offal
  • cooked, salted, smoked and other preparations of meat product
