Guidance

Create a traineeship vacancy

How training providers can create traineeship vacancies and advertise them on Find a traineeship.

From:
Education and Skills Funding Agency
Published
30 June 2022

Applies to England

Contents

  1. What you’ll need
  2. What happens next

You can create a traineeship vacancy in the ‘Recruit a trainee’ section of your apprenticeship service account.

To create a vacancy, you’ll need to be on the Register of apprenticeship training providers.

You will need the employer to give you permission within their apprenticeship service account to create traineeship vacancies on their behalf.

We usually approve or reject traineeship vacancies within 24 hours.

If you deliver traineeships but don’t deliver apprenticeships, you can work with a provider on the Register of apprenticeship training providers who can deliver an apprenticeship following your traineeship. They can advertise traineeship vacancies for you on Find a traineeship.

Before you create a vacancy, learn about preventing discrimination in recruitment.

What you’ll need

Before you start, you’ll need to know the:

  • name of the traineeship vacancy
  • traineeship sector
  • employer that will provide the work experience placement and whether they are disability confident
  • employer’s address and location
  • number of positions available
  • start date and application closing date
  • duration of the traineeship and details of a typical working week

You can save each vacancy as a draft and edit it before you submit it to us, as well as preview how it’ll look.

What happens next

You can check the status of each vacancy in your traineeship dashboard. The statuses are:

  • rejected
  • draft
  • live
  • closed
  • pending review

We’ll check the vacancy and let you know if you need to make any edits within 24 hours. You can view what edits you need to make on your dashboard, but you can also choose to get email notifications.

Once we’ve approved the vacancy, you can view it on Find a traineeship.

You can also extend the closing date after the vacancy has gone live.
