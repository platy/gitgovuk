Temporary jobcentres

This page lists the locations and opening dates of temporary Jobcentres.

From:
Department for Work and Pensions
Published
23 March 2021
Last updated
22 April 2022 — See all updates

Applies to England, Scotland and Wales

DWP is setting up temporary jobcentres to support the government’s Plan for Jobs which includes youth hubs and the Way to Work campaign.

DWP has acquired 194 temporary jobcentre sites. Over 150 of these have already opened or extended to help more people across the UK access DWP’s services. These new temporary jobcentres will increase capacity to provide tailored face-to-face support.

Read the history mode guidance for more information as to what this means.
