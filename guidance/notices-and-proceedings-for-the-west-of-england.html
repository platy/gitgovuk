Areas covered

The Counties of Cornwall, Devon, Dorset, Gloucestershire, Hampshire, Oxfordshire, Somerset and Wiltshire.

The Districts of Bath and North East Somerset, Bournemouth, Bracknell Forest, Bristol, Isle of Wight, North Somerset, Plymouth, Poole, Portsmouth, Reading, Slough, Southampton, South Gloucestershire, Swindon, Torbay, West Berkshire, Windsor and Maidenhead and Wokingham.

Check applications or find operators

You can:

  • check vehicle operator licensing Notices and Proceedings by name, date and area
  • find a bus operator by name, address or licence number
  • find registered local bus services by operator name, licence number, or start and finish point
