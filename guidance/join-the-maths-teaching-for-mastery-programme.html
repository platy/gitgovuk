What you can get

The programme includes:

  • leadership and teacher training in the mastery approach to teaching maths
  • subject knowledge training
  • classroom resources
  • professional development sessions

Who can join the programme

Teaching for Mastery is open to all state-funded schools in England.

When it’s available

Activities take place throughout the year at 40 maths hubs across England.

What it costs

Funding is available to support participation. Contact your maths hub for details.

How to apply

Find your nearest maths hub and contact them directly for information about their programme, events and how to apply.

Find out more

See the National Centre for Excellence in the Teaching of Mathematics (NCETM) for more information.
