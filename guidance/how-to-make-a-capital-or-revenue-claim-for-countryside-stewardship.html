Countryside Stewardship annual revenue claims are closed

The final deadline to submit your annual revenue claim was midnight on 10 June 2022.

Claims received after 10 June 2022 will be rejected (apart from cases accepted as force majeure and exceptional circumstances).

Capital claims

You can submit a capital claim at any time of year. However, this must be:

  • within 3 months of the end of your agreement
  • after the capital work is finished
  • after you have paid for it and the money has left your back account

All the information you need to know about Countryside Stewardship capital claims is in the relevant scheme manual.

You can claim online using the Rural Payments service.

Use this guide to find out How to submit a capital claim online.

If you’re unable to claim online, call us to request a Countryside Stewardship capital claim form.

Use this guide to find out How to complete a capital claim form.

How to return your completed form

You can post or scan and email your completed claim, and any supporting documents and evidence, using our contact details.

Use this guide to help you when emailing documents to the Rural Payments Agency.
