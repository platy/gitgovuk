Before you can get a young person into a job

You must have:

  • successfully applied online or applied through a Kickstart gateway for a Kickstart Scheme grant before midday on 17 December 2021
  • signed and returned your Kickstart Scheme grant agreement to DWP or your Kickstart gateway by 11:59pm on 7 January 2022

You now need to:

  • submit your Kickstart Scheme vacancies by 11:59pm on 31 January 2022

How to get the young people into the jobs

  1. Work coaches at Jobcentre Plus will use the vacancies you or your gateway submitted to match candidates to the jobs. They’ll do this until 1 March 2022.

  2. You or your Kickstart gateway will receive a ‘referral’ email each time a young person is referred to the vacancy.

  3. Referred candidates may choose to apply for the job placement.

  4. You’ll then be able to interview the candidates who have applied.

  5. The young person must start the job on or before 31 March 2022.

  6. You or your Kickstart gateway should tell DWP by 11:59pm on 30 April 2022 that the young person has started so we can process the funding. You’ll be told how to do this in the ‘referral’ email.

You can advertise vacancies yourself, but all jobs must receive an introduction through a DWP work coach to receive full funding. DWP also uploads the approved vacancies to Find a job on your behalf – so you do not have to do this. Young people can search this site for Kickstart vacancies themselves. We’ll advertise Kickstart jobs on Find a job until 28 February 2022.

If a young person leaves the job early

You must email DWP as soon as possible if a young person either:

  • leaves their job before the end of the 6-month period
  • needs to temporarily leave their job (for example for special leave or COVID-19-related restrictions)

The email address is the same one you use to tell DWP about the young person’s start date. You’ll be told how to do this in the ‘referral’ email you get when a work coach refers a young person to your job vacancy.

If a young person leaves their job early, the next scheduled wage payment from DWP will be the last you receive for them.

DWP may be able to extend the funding period if the young person has to temporarily stop working.

You can replace the person who left early with another young person referred to the vacancy by a work coach. They must start on or before 31 March 2022.

Get help and support with managing your Kickstart Scheme jobs

Contact your local or national Kickstart Scheme contact if you need help managing your Kickstart Scheme jobs.
