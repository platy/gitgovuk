Check if an alcohol wholesaler in the Isle of Man is approved

Check the list of alcohol wholesalers in the Isle of Man that have approval under the Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS).

From:
HM Revenue & Customs
Published
26 June 2019
Last updated
9 August 2022 — See all updates

If you buy alcohol to sell on, you must check that the wholesalers you buy from have approval under the Alcohol Wholesaler Registration Scheme (AWRS). This is a list of all approved wholesalers from the Isle of Man.

You can read more information about AWRS.

You can also check alcohol wholesalers from the rest of the UK using the online service.
