HM Land Registry: digital applications

We’re changing the way you create and submit AP1 applications using the portal.

From:
HM Land Registry
Published
8 November 2021
Last updated

Applies to England and Wales

We are moving away from electronic applications, which are scanned copies currently uploaded and submitted using the HM Land Registry portal electronic Document Registration Service.

Instead, customers should use the new portal Digital Registration Service or lodge a digital application using software connected to our Business Gateway platform. Digital applications are quicker to create and process, and see fewer requisitions due to automatic error checking.

To create and submit a digital AP1 application, you can use:

  • the Digital Registration Service on the portal
  • a case management system connected to our Business Gateway platform

The Digital Registration Service offers a guided process. No training is required. For more information, see Submit a digital AP1 form to HM Land Registry.

Or watch the video.

From November 2022 we will no longer accept scanned or PDF copies of AP1s for changes to registered titles using the portal.

Digital Registration Service

If you use the HM Land Registry portal to upload scanned or PDF AP1 forms, you can submit digital applications using the Digital Registration Service within the HM Land Registry portal.

If you’re not already a portal user, you can apply to our Business e-services.

Business Gateway

If you use a case management system or forms provider, you can submit digital applications using our Business Gateway platform.

If you’re not already a Business Gateway user, email ChannelPartners@landregistry.gov.uk or see HM Land Registry: Business Gateway.

Contact

If you would like to discuss the move to digital applications with us, please email: DRS@landregistry.gov.uk.

Opt out of email

If you do not want to receive emails that are specific to the Digital Registration Service, you can enter your email address to opt out.
