Background

It can be complicated to submit import and export customs declarations, so you may want to find a company which offers training in this area.

The attached list aims to provide:

  • customs training provider name, website, email address and telephone number
  • services they offer as part of their training course
  • if the training is online, face to face or both
  • locations of any face to face training
  • if qualifications are provided
  • if the level of training is beginner, intermediate or advanced

Training providers on this list and the courses they offer are not vetted, approved or recommended by HMRC. The information is provided to HMRC by the training providers and we do not verify or guarantee how current or accurate it is.

How to add to or amend this list

If you offer customs training you can add or amend your details on the list. Email customsagentstrainingproviders@hmrc.gov.uk and tell us:

  • your training provider name, website, email address and telephone number
  • if you provide training online, face to face or both
  • locations of any face to face training
  • if you provide qualifications
  • if the level of your training is beginner, intermediate or advanced

Also let us know if you offer training in:

  • import and export processes
  • transit
  • valuing goods
  • classifying goods
  • delaying and reducing duties
  • safety and security declarations (import or export)
  • dealing with EU customs responsibilities, including EU import and export processes
  • customs software and systems
  • temporary admission
