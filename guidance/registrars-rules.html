About the registrar’s rules

The Registrar of Companies is given authority to make rules governing the filing of documents at Companies House. These rules are made under section 1117 of the Companies Act 2006 and must be complied with whenever a document is delivered to Companies House.

Latest versions of each volume of the rules:

  • Requirements Applicable to Documents Delivered to the Registrar in Electronic Form 2022
  • The Registrar’s Coronavirus Easements (Upload Service) Rules 2020
  • The Registrar’s (Requirements For Paper Documents) Rules 2009 - Volume 2
  • The Registrar’s (Requirements For CD or DVD - Rom) Rules 2009 - Volume 3
  • The Registrar’s (Requirements For Informal Correction) Rules 2009 - Volume 4
  • The Registrar’s (Authentication By The Registrar Of Electronic Certificate) Rules 2009 - Volume 5
  • The Registrar’s (Nomination Of An Address To Be The Registered Office Address) Rules 2016 - Volume 6
