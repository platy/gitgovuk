Peer Reviewer Panel opportunity

We are offering individuals the opportunity from 4 July 2022 to apply to become Peer Reviewers for the Legal Aid Agency.

This opportunity is open to both existing Peer Reviewers and new applicants. The deadline for applications is 11.59pm on 31 July 2022.

What is a Peer Reviewer?

Peer Reviewers independently assess random samples of provider case files using guidance developed in consultation with the Law Society, law centres and not-for-profit advice agencies. This work is important for our auditing and assurance processes. You can find out more on the LAA audits page.
