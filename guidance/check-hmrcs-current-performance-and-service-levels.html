The information in this dashboard is updated weekly.

Use HMRC’s service dashboard to check:

  • current performance and service levels
  • processing dates
  • the date we aim to return to normal service levels where there’s a delay

You can find out about our service levels for:

  • agent services
  • Corporation Tax
  • Income Tax
  • Self Assessment
  • tax credits
  • VAT

You will not need to sign in or create a Government Gateway user ID and password.
