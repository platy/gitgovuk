Plant health export service webinars

Defra is hosting a series of webinars to demonstrate how to register for and use the plant health export service. The service will be available from September 2021.

The plant health export service is for traders who export:

  • used agricultural farm machinery
  • Plant Product (HH91) - products or ingredients from plants, seeds and pulses that have been processed such as being dried, milled or cooked
  • Plant Produce (PHE36) - plants, trees and cuttings for planting, cut flowers, fresh fruit and vegetables for food

Continue to use eDomero if you export potatoes, seed, grain or bulbs or you use the plant health exports audited trader (PHEAT) scheme.
