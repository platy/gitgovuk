Ips typographus Authorised Processers

The list below describes businesses that are authorised to receive and process spruce material that originates in the Ips typographus demarcated area.

There are differing risk categories of this material and you must ensure that the business is authorised to receive the appropriate category of material from your site. For more information or if you have difficulty reaching any of these businesses please contact Plant Health Forestry Ips typographus authorisation team.

The list below describes businesses that are authorised to receive relevant material from within the Phytophthora pluvialis demarcated areas if it has been inspected and is not known to be infected with Phytophthora pluvialis.

Any infected material may only be moved to a business explicitly authorised to received and process infected material. For more information or if you have difficulty reaching any of these businesses please contact Plant Health Forestry Phytophthora pluvialis authorisation team.
