You must complete a passenger locator form (PLF) online before you arrive in the UK.

Watch this video to understand how to:

  • complete and submit your passenger locator form online
  • set up an account to save your details if you travel frequently
  • upload or scan a QR code to include proof of your coronavirus (Covid-19) vaccination status

video

Guidance on how to fill out your passenger locator form is available in Welsh and 21 other languages.

If you need more help completing the form you can call the passenger locator form helpline.

Calling from the UK
Passenger locator form helpline
Telephone: 0800 678 1767
Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm
Calls are free of charge.

Calling from outside the UK
Passenger locator form helpline
Telephone: 0044 207 113 0371
Monday to Friday, 9am to 5pm
Call charges depend on the network provider.
