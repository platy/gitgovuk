Protected wine and aromatised wine names: applications

See the list of applications to protect the geographical name of wine and aromatised wine in the UK.

From:
Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs
Published
22 April 2021
Last updated
15 June 2022 — See all updates

Applications are listed by product type. Products that are eligible are published for consultation.

Designation categories are:

  • protected designation of origin (PDO)
  • protected geographical indication (PGI)

Wine products

This category includes:

  • red wine
  • white wine
  • rosé wine

There are currently no applications in this category.

Aromatised wine

This category includes:

  • aromatised wine
  • aromatised wine-based drinks
  • aromatised wine-product cocktails

There are currently no applications in this category.
