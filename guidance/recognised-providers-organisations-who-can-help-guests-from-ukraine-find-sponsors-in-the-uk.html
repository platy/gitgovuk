Recognised Providers: Organisations who can help guests from Ukraine find sponsors in the UK

A list of voluntary and community sector organisations running schemes which provide support for and help match people coming from Ukraine with sponsors in the UK.

From:
Department for Levelling Up, Housing and Communities
Published
6 May 2022

This is a list of voluntary and community sector organisations running schemes which provide support for and help match people coming from Ukraine with sponsors in the UK, as part of the Homes for Ukraine scheme.

These organisations can help you find a sponsor and provide more information about what it’s like to be sponsored in the UK. They are not immigration advisors and cannot guarantee to find you a sponsor in the location of your choice.

At the moment, organisations may have limited capacity to take on new guests from Ukraine, but this will change over the coming weeks. We expect this list to grow over time and encourage you to check it regularly.

We also recommend that you visit charities’ websites for helpful guidance and other types of information and support.

If you are living in the UK and are interested in sponsoring a guest from Ukraine, you may wish to check the Recognised Providers: Organisations who can help UK citizens become sponsors page regularly to see if any Recognised Providers are open to applications from sponsors.

If you are an organisation interested in developing a matching service, you may wish to read the government guidance for voluntary and community sector organisations.
