Self Assessment or PAYE for individuals

Phone

If your client has formally authorised you to act on their behalf, you can call the Agent Dedicated Line.

Post

Use the list of common headings when sending letters to HMRC about your clients’ affairs.

Debts

If you’re an authorised agent you can call the Debt Management and Banking Agent Dedicated Line about your client’s debts.

Tax credits

Call the Agent Priority Line if you’re handling tax credits issues on your clients’ behalf.

Other taxes, duties or specialist teams

If you need contact details for a tax and duty that does not have an agent dedicated telephone number or address, you can search the list of HMRC contacts.
