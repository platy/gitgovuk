The information in this tool is updated weekly.

You can use this tool for queries or requests you’ve made about:

  • Income Tax
  • Self Assessment
  • tax credits
  • VAT
  • Corporation Tax

If you’re an agent, you can also check how long it will take HMRC to:

  • register you as an agent to use HMRC online services
  • process an application for authority to act on behalf of a client
  • amend your agent details

Start now

If your query is about anything else, contact HMRC.
