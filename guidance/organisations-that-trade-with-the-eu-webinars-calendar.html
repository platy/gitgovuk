Border and Protocol Delivery Group (BPDG) Industry Day Programme 2021

These events will provide practical details about moving goods between the EU and GB. They will cover procedures and systems at the borders and detail about the new import requirements in Great Britain (GB). Following presentations from Government officials there will be question and answer sessions.

The events are open to EU and UK audiences and will cover details of interest to all in the supply chain, traders, hauliers, intermediaries and trade associations.

02 September

Belgium Ports and BPDG Industry Day

Watch a recording of the Belgium Ports and BPDG Industry day. The webinar will provide practical details about moving goods between the EU and GB, highlighting technical issues and best practise at the Borders.

This recording outlines the processes for moving agri-food products between the EU and GB, including the new GB import requirements. Please note that the recording was accurate as of the time of recording - 2nd September 2021. On 14th September 2021, the UK Government outlined a pragmatic new timetable for introducing border controls. Please see more information here
