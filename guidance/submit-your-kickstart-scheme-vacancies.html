Applications to the Kickstart Scheme are closing soon.

After 17 December 2021 you will not be able to:

  • apply for a new Kickstart Scheme grant
  • add more jobs to an existing grant agreement

We will process applications submitted before this time or that are already in progress.

Before you sign in to manage your vacancies

You need to:

  • successfully apply online before 17 December 2021 for a Kickstart Scheme grant
  • sign and return the grant agreement to DWP or your Kickstart gateway

You or your Kickstart gateway will receive the grant agreement by email after you’ve received the decision on your application.

Use the application service if you’d like to add more jobs or employers to your grant agreement (grant variation).

How to submit your vacancies

If you applied through a Kickstart gateway

Give your vacancy details to your gateway. They will submit them to DWP on your behalf using the job placement template.

If you applied online

We’ll either:

  • email you the job placement template to complete and send back for approval
  • invite you to create an online account where you can submit your vacancies for approval online

If you’ve received an invitation to create an online account

Some employers who apply online will be invited by email to create an account.

If you’re invited, you’ll be able to:

  • submit details of your vacancies online for approval
  • check the approval status of your vacancies

The email will explain how to create your account.

Sign in to submit your vacancies

After you’ve added your vacancies

  1. Your vacancies will go live once we’ve approved them. It can take up to 30 days to approve them.

  2. Work coaches at Jobcentre Plus will refer young people to your vacancies (‘referrals’). A referral is made when we recommend your vacancy to a candidate. This does not guarantee the candidate will put in an application.

  3. You or your Kickstart gateway (depending on your agreement with them) will interview suitable candidates who have applied.

  4. You or your Kickstart gateway will tell us when the young person has started so we can process the funding.

  5. You can manage your funding and add more jobs to your existing grant agreement until 17 December 2021.

You can advertise vacancies yourself, but all jobs must receive an introduction through a DWP work coach to receive full funding.

Get help with the Kickstart Scheme

Contact your local or national Kickstart Scheme contact if you need help with the Kickstart Scheme process.
