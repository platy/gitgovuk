If you register for a webinar, you:

  • must register and sign in 5 minutes before it starts
  • can ask questions using the on-screen text box

Available webinars are listed by subject and can last up to an hour.

Accounting schemes

Watch a recorded webinar about VAT accounting schemes. Find out about the 3 most popular schemes:

  • Cash Accounting Scheme
  • Flat Rate Scheme
  • Annual Accounting Scheme

VAT Flat Rate Scheme

Watch a recorded webinar about the VAT Flat Rate Scheme. You will learn about:

  • what the Flat Rate Scheme is
  • about limited cost business and how to check if this applies to you
  • how to:
    • keep VAT records
    • fill in your VAT Return

How to do your VAT Return

Watch a recorded webinar about how to do your VAT Return.

This webinar is suitable for businesses using VAT standard accounting and looks at:

  • how and when to send your VAT Return
  • useful tips on how to complete a VAT Return

How to apply the VAT reverse charge for construction services

Watch a recorded webinar about the VAT reverse charge.

If you are in the Construction Industry Scheme you’ll find out:

  • if the changes affect you
  • how they may impact your cashflow
  • how to prepare for the change

Register for the next live webinar about the VAT reverse charge for construction services.
