Applies to England

The Rural Payments Agency (RPA) is responsible for administering SFI.

Questions about SFI

If you have any questions about SFI, fill in the query form or email us at ruralpayments@defra.gov.uk.

Use ‘Sustainable Farming Incentive’ in the subject header and include your Single Business Identifier (SBI).

You can also call or write.

Telephone: 03000 200 301

Monday to Friday 8.30am to 5pm, except bank holidays

By post:

PO Box 352

Worksop

S80 9FG
