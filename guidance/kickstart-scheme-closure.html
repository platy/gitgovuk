Guidance

Kickstart Scheme closure

Information about how the Kickstart Scheme closure will affect employers and Kickstart gateways.

From:
Department for Work and Pensions
Published
5 October 2021

Applies to England, Scotland and Wales

Contents

  1. Employers who applied for a Kickstart Scheme grant before 17 December 2021
  2. Employers or Kickstart gateways who applied for more jobs to be added to their grant agreement before 17 December 2021
  3. Employers who want to apply for a Kickstart Scheme grant after 17 December 2021
  4. Get in touch with the Kickstart Scheme

Employers who applied for a Kickstart Scheme grant before 17 December 2021

Your application will be processed. We’ll aim to email you with a decision on the application within one month.

If your application is successful

If your application is successful, you can spread the job start dates up until 31 March 2022. You’ll get funding until 30 September 2022 if a young person starts their job on 31 March 2022.

Find out how to get the young people into the jobs.

If your application is not successful

You can apply again until 17 December 2021. You cannot apply again after this date.

Check if you’re eligible for funding from other employment schemes.

Employers or Kickstart gateways who applied for more jobs to be added to their grant agreement before 17 December 2021

If your application is successful

Your application will be processed. If your application is successful, you can spread the job start dates up until 31 March 2022. You’ll get funding until 30 September 2022 if a young person starts their job on 31 March 2022.

Find out how to get the young people into the jobs.

If your application is unsuccessful

You can apply again until 17 December 2021. You cannot apply again after this date.

Check if you’re eligible for funding from other employment schemes.

Employers who want to apply for a Kickstart Scheme grant after 17 December 2021

After 17 December 2021, you cannot:

  • apply for a new Kickstart Scheme grant
  • add more jobs to an existing grant agreement

Check if you’re eligible for funding from other employment schemes.

Get in touch with the Kickstart Scheme

Contact your local or national Kickstart Scheme contact if you have questions about the Kickstart Scheme.

Find out why Kickstart Scheme applications are closing
