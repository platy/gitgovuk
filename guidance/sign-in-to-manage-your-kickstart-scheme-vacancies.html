Sign in to manage your Kickstart Scheme vacancies

Manage your Kickstart Scheme vacancies online after you've received a successful outcome to your online Kickstart Scheme grant application.

Employer guidance

  1. Find out how the Kickstart Scheme works and check if your organisation is eligible to apply

  2. Apply online for a Kickstart Scheme grant or apply through a Kickstart gateway

  3. Sign in to manage your vacancies (only for employers who have applied online)

  4. Get young people into the Kickstart Scheme jobs

  5. Manage your funding

If you’re a Kickstart gateway, check the guidance for gateways.

Before you sign in to manage your vacancies

You need to:

  • successfully apply online for a Kickstart Scheme grant
  • sign and return the grant agreement to DWP or your Kickstart gateway

You or your Kickstart gateway will receive the grant agreement by email after you’ve received the decision on your application.

If you applied through a Kickstart gateway, you do not need to sign in to manage your vacancies. You will give your vacancy details to your gateway, who will share them with DWP on your behalf.

What you need to manage your vacancies

You need to activate your online account (you’ll receive an email explaining how to do this).

Sign in to manage your vacancies

After you’ve added your vacancies

  1. Your vacancies will go live once we’ve approved them. This can take up to 30 days.

  2. Work coaches at Jobcentre Plus will refer young people to your vacancies (‘referrals’). A referral is made when we recommend your vacancy to a candidate. This does not guarantee the candidate will put in an application.

  3. You or your Kickstart gateway (depending on your agreement with them) will interview suitable candidates who have applied.

  4. You or your Kickstart gateway will tell us when the young person has started so we can process the funding.

  5. You can manage your funding and add more jobs to your existing grant agreement.

You can advertise vacancies yourself, but all jobs must receive an introduction through a DWP work coach to receive full funding.

Get help with the Kickstart Scheme

Contact your local or national Kickstart Scheme contact if you need help with the Kickstart Scheme process.
