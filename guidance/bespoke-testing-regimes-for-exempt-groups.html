Bespoke testing for jobs with travel exemptions

Everyone coming to England from abroad must take one or more tests for coronavirus (COVID-19). Some will also have to quarantine.

You may be exempt from some or all COVID-19 travel and entry requirements because of your job.

Check if your job has travel exemptions.

If you have a job with travel exemptions you must follow a bespoke testing programme. This is a legal requirement (mandatory).

There are different rules if you’re:

  • actively working as a domestic sea fisherman or woman
  • a diplomat

You should check with your employer.

The bespoke testing programme you follow depends on the sector you work in.

Testing will:

  • identify cases of COVID-19, including variants
  • help stop it spreading
  • help you take suitable precautions to keep you and others safe

Follow the guidance on how to stay safe.

What you need to do

Bespoke testing is designed to be flexible. It can’t be booked in advance.

Before you travel you must complete a passenger locator form.

You do not have to do this if you’re exempt from completing a passenger locator form.

You will not have to provide a testing booking reference on the form.

At the border you’ll need to show evidence of the work you’ll be carrying out in England.

You can be fined up to £2,000 if you do not take a test when you are legally required to.

Where to get a bespoke test

You can get a bespoke test at:

  • your workplace – available to employers for employees who can’t work at home
  • home by ordering test kits online or by calling 119
  • a haulier advice site if you’re an HGV driver or operator

Test on arrival

Everyone coming to England from abroad must take a COVID-19 test on or before day 2.

The day you arrive in England is day 0.

What you else you must do depends on:

  • whether you’re fully vaccinated under an approved vaccination programme (or qualify for fully vaccinated rules for other reasons)
  • whether you’ve been in a country on the red list in the 10 days before you arrive in England
  • what sector you work in

Check whether you qualify for fully vaccinated rules.

Check what bespoke testing programme you need to follow for your type of work.

If you’re travelling every day, or for no more than 2 days at a time, you must also take a lateral flow test at least once every 3 days.

Text reminders

You’ll be sent text reminders to follow your bespoke testing programme from GOV.UK.

If you’re in the UK the text will be sent from:
+ 44 (0)7984 404 870.

This may display as coming from a different number if you’ve left the UK or have a non-UK phone number.

If your test is positive

If any of your tests are positive you must self-isolate for 10 days from the day after you took the test.

You’ll be sent more advice with your test results.

You will have to take a follow-up PCR test to check for new variants. You’ll have to buy this test from a private testing provider.

Check general travel rules

Read the standard rules for entering England from abroad if you do not have a job with travel exemptions.
