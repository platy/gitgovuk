GDS Advisory is part of the organisation responsible for creating the Government Design Principles, Service Standard and Technology Code of Practice.

We help public sector organisations inside and outside the UK to deliver simple, accessible digital services and modern technology platforms, including:

  • central governments
  • agencies and arms length bodies
  • devolved administrations
  • regional governments
  • local authorities

How we work

Our team of digital, data, technology, design and user research professionals can work with your organisation in a number of different ways - from short-term help scoping a piece of work, through to long term support delivering a project.

We charge for our services on a cost recovery basis.

Our work with the UK public sector

Our UK team will find a way to work with you that meets your organisation’s needs. For example:

  • a one-off technical or design review, workshop or training session
  • help scoping a piece of work or defining a problem
  • coaching in agile delivery, product management or user centred design
  • help finding individual specialists or delivery teams for urgent or priority project
  • planning or delivery of a new website, digital service or technology platform
  • a long-term partnership to help deliver technology reform or a digital transformation programme

Our work with international partners

Our international team uses a peer-to-peer, partnership approach to understand your needs, define goals and help you provide better services. We can help with:

  • one-off advice - for example, an expert or technical review, workshop or training
  • a ‘deep dive’ focused on a particular area of reform or objective - for example, developing and implementing a new digital strategy, reviewing a website or single domain or designing an approach to digital governance and assurance
  • long-term digital transformation reform (typically a multi-year, multi-workstrand programme using agile methodologies to create sustainable change)

Contact us to find out how we can help

If you’re a public sector organisation in the UK, contact gds-departmental-services@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk.

If you’re a public sector organisation based outside the UK, contact international@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk.

Get insights from digital governments around the world

We research and gather insights into how other governments solve common problems and can share international best practice to help you on your transformation journey.

If you’re a public sector organisation in the UK and want to find out more about our research and insights work on digital government, contact international@digital.cabinet-office.gov.uk.
