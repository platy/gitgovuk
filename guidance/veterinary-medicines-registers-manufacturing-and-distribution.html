Register of Veterinary-Only Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) Sites

This Register lists those sites that only manufacture, import, store or Quality Control (QC) test veterinary medicine products (VMPs) as part of their authorisation requirements, or veterinary medicines manufactured under the Exemption for Small Pet Animals.

Register of Veterinary-Only GMP Sites

Register of Approved SQP Retailers and Feed Businesses

This register includes premises currently approved by the VMD for the purpose of supply of veterinary medicines or medicinal feed additives by approved Suitability Qualified Persons (SQP). Any previous amendments made will only show in the Archive register.

Register of Veterinary-Only manufacturers of extemporaneous products for use under the cascade (ManSA)

This Register lists those sites authorised to manufacture extemporaneous products under the cascade (also known as ‘specials’), for veterinary use only.

Register of Veterinary-Only manufacturers of extemporaneous products for use under the cascade (ManSA)

The Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency (MHRA) authorises sites that manufacture both human ‘specials’ (MS) and veterinary ‘specials’ (ManSA) and the register of those manufacturers can be found on the MHRA pages of GOV.UK.

Register of Specific Manufacturing Authorisation Holders (NFABBA, ESCCA, AVA)

The following types of authorisation are known collectively as Specific Manufacturing Authorisations: Non-Food Animal Blood Bank Authorisation, Equine Stem Cell Centre Authorisation, and Autogenous Vaccine Authorisation.
