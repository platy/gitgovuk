Helplines

Helplines are available to:

  • get energy advice and support
  • get help and support for your business
  • get advice on your rights at work
  • get consumer protection advice
  • get help with exporting

Online

GOV.UK is the best place to find up to date information on our services.

We often get enquiries about the following topics. Check the guidance if you need to:

  • find out how to get the Energy Bills Support Scheme discount
  • find out how to get the Warm Home Discount
  • find ways to save energy in your home
  • complain about your energy supplier
  • check if you’re getting paid the minimum wage
  • calculate your holiday entitlement
  • complain about pay and work rights
  • find business finance and grants
  • set up a business
  • get a Driver CPC card

Write to a minister

Write to:

[Name of Minister]
Ministerial Correspondence Team
1 Victoria Street
London
SW1H 0ET
United Kingdom

General enquiries about schemes or programmes should be sent to the relevant team.

We aim to reply to correspondence within 15 working days.

We do not reply to correspondence that:

  • contains abusive or offensive language
  • sells or promotes products or services
  • is for information only and does not ask a question
  • is part of a campaign or petition
  • is unclear due to language or handwriting
  • repeats an enquiry you’ve already sent
