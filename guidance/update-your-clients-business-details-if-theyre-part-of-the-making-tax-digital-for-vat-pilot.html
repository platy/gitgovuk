Guidance

Manage your client's details for Making Tax Digital for VAT

  • English
  • Cymraeg

If you’re a tax agent, use this service to manage your client's details if they have signed up to Making Tax Digital for VAT.

From:
HM Revenue & Customs
Published
16 October 2018
Last updated
1 April 2022 — See all updates

Contents

  1. Before you start
  2. When you cannot use this service
  3. Get help

Use this service for clients who’ve signed up to Making Tax Digital to:

  • change VAT details
  • print VAT certificates
  • cancel VAT registration
  • print VAT Returns

Online services may be slow during busy times. Check if there are any problems with this service.

Start now

Before you start

You’ll need the Government Gateway user ID and password for your agent services account and information about the business.

If your client’s business details change, you must tell HMRC within 30 days.

When you cannot use this service

There’s a different way to:

  • manage your own business’s VAT account
  • tell HMRC about other changes to your client’s details

Get help

If you need help changing your client’s details, contact the VAT online services helpdesk.
