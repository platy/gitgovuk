Areas covered

The metropolitan boroughs within South Yorkshire, Tyne and Wear and West Yorkshire.

The counties of Durham, Northumberland, Nottinghamshire and North Yorkshire.

The districts of Darlington, East Riding of Yorkshire, Hartlepool, Kingston upon Hull, Middlesbrough, North Lincolnshire, North East Lincolnshire, Nottingham, Redcar and Cleveland, Stockton-on-Tees and York.

Check applications or find operators

You can:

  • check vehicle operator licensing Notices and Proceedings by name, date and area
  • find a bus operator by name, address or licence number
  • find registered local bus services by operator name, licence number, or start and finish point
