Choosing the right software

You may already keep your business records in a digital format using software. The difference with Making Tax Digital for Income Tax compatible software is that it will be capable of:

  • maintaining business records as set out in the regulations
  • preparing and sending quarterly updates and end of period statements using the information maintained in those records
  • finalising your business income and submitting your declaration after the end of the tax year
  • communicating with HMRC digitally through our application programming interface (API) platform

Find out more about using Making Tax Digital for Income Tax.

You’ll need compatible software before you sign up to Making Tax Digital for Income Tax to start sending updates.

If you’re already using software to keep records, ask your provider if they plan on making the software compatible with Making Tax Digital for Income Tax.
