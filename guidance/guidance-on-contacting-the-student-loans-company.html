Student Finance England

For general information about student finance, you can check www.gov.uk/student-finance.

Repayment customers can log in to the online repayment service at www.gov.uk/sign-in-to-manage-your-student-loan-balance

There’s lots of things you can do online without needing to call us.

You can sign in to your Student Finance account to:

  • check what stage your application is at
  • check how much and when you’ll be paid
  • change your personal or course details
  • reset your password

Usually, it takes 6 to 8 weeks to process an application or evidence. If it’s been less than 8 weeks, please allow more time for it to be processed, you do not need to call us for an update.

Visit our dedicated page for our latest guidance for students, parents and partners.
