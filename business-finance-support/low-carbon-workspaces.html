How much you can get

Grants of between £1,000 to £6,750.

Who it’s for

Businesses in Buckinghamshire, Bedfordshire, Berkshire, Hertfordshire, Northamptonshire, Luton and Milton Keynes.

Maximum employees

249

What can you get

As well as match-funded grants you can get advice from an energy adviser.
