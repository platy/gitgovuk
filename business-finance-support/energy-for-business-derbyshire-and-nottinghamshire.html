How much you can get

Capital grants of between £1000 and £8000. Funding is available for 40% of the project cost up to a maximum of £20,000 project value.

Who it’s for

SMEs in the Derby, Derbyshire, Nottingham, Nottinghamshire, Leicester, and Leicestershire regions.

What you can get

  • specialist academic consultancy
  • technical support for energy projects
  • events and workshops
  • student placements and Knowledge Transfer Partnerships
  • access to facilities

The programme is funded by the European Regional Development Fund.

Maximum employees

249

Organiser

The Energy Innovation and Collaboration team at the University of Nottingham
