Merchant Cash Advance - UK

Finance for SMEs in the UK with no fixed payments, fixed terms or security required. The advance is repaid as a percentage of future card sales.

How much you can get

£10,000 to £300,000

Who it’s for

UK businesses that have been trading for at least six months, and take at least £10,000 per month in debit and/or credit card sales.

What you can get

An advance that can be used for any purpose, such as:

  • Renovation and refurbishment
  • Purchasing stock
  • Working capital
