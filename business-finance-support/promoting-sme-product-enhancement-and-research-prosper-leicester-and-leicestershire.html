How much you can get

  • Up to 30% for eligible capital costs, up to a spend of £80,000 (i.e. average grant is 30% of £80,000)
  • Up to 31% for eligible revenue costs, up to a spend of £20,000 (i.e. average grant is 31% of £20,000)

What you can get

PROSPER offers both financial and non-financial support:

Grants (part-funding) towards external expenditure for capital and revenue items and support from the University for projects that aim to develop or significantly change a new product or service. This includes process innovation if the process contributes to the development of a product or service.

Support can include up to 2 days’ support from the University including:

  • one-to-one mentoring
  • help with an undergraduate/graduate placement
  • information sourcing
  • access to university academics (for up to 5 days of collaboration)

This project is part financed through ERDF (European Regional Development Fund).
