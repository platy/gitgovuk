Vocational technical qualifications (VTQs)

A VTQ gives you the opportunity to gain skills and experience in a specific job or a broad employment area, such as childcare or engineering.

Higher technical qualifications (HTQs)

"I joined the South-Central Institute of Technology in 2020 to advance my education in cyber security. It has been the best choice for me to experience working with employers and get the chance to explore different sectors of technology."
Deborah, 19, HND cyber security and networking

A levels

A levels are subject-based qualifications for those aged 16 and over. They can lead to university, further study or starting work.

Higher education

There are many different types of higher education qualifications, such as diplomas, degrees and foundation years.

Supported internships

“My supported internship has been the best opportunity of my life. My job coach from Lighthouse Futures Trust supported me every step of the way and helped me unlock my hidden potential. I'm now working for Johnson & Johnson and achieving things I never thought I would achieve.”

School leaver schemes

Some companies offer school leaver schemes to train you in a certain job after you've finished your A levels.
