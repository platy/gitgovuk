Overview

NIHR’s Research and Innovation for Global Health Transformation (RIGHT) programme’s fifth funding call is seeking research proposals to investigate how health services can adapt to disruption caused by extreme weather events in low and middle income countries (LMICs). Funding awards of between £1 million and £3 million over 3 to 5 years are available to researchers in the UK and LMICs, with researchers based in LMICs able to lead applications. The call opens on 2 March 2022 and closes on 29 June 2022.

What the fund will achieve

RIGHT 5 is focused on adaptation and resilience of health services and provision in the context of disruption caused by climate change and extreme weather events in LMICs. The RIGHT programme aims to improve health and health systems in LMICs and worldwide, as well as strengthening capacity for research and boosting knowledge exchange through funding equitable partnerships between researchers in the UK and those in LMICs.
