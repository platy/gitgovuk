Written statement to Parliament

Announcement of Chair - the Armed Forces' Pay Review Body

Mr Julian Miller has been appointed as the next Chair of the Armed Forces’ Pay Review Body.

From:
Ministry of Defence and Leo Docherty MP
Published
7 June 2022
Delivered on:
18 May 2022

I am pleased to announce that the Prime Minister has appointed Mr Julian Miller as the next Chair of the Armed Forces’ Pay Review Body. His appointment will commence on 17 June 2022 and run until 16 June 2025.

The appointment has been conducted in accordance with the 2016 Governance Code on Public Appointments.
