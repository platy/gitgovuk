Details

Langport range involves live firing with rifles.

Dry military training at Beech Wood involves blank ammunition, pyrotechnics, vehicles and helicopters.

Do not enter the danger area when the range is in use, this is a danger to life. Learn more about accessing MOD training areas safely.

Read more about ash dieback works around Langport Training Area taking place between 20 September and 15 October 2021.
