HMRC do not carry out any form of security testing of developer products or services. Ask your supplier for information about security.

You can send online information for these forms:

  1. Add Scheme Administrator to Scheme.
  2. Amend certain pension scheme details (includes details of pension schemes in existence on or before 6 April 2006).
  3. Amend Event Report.
  4. Amend or update Practitioner details.
  5. Amend or update Scheme Administrator details.
  6. Amend Pension Scheme Return.
  7. Amend Relief at Source.
  8. Associate Scheme Administrator to Scheme.
  9. Authorise or de-authorise HMRC to deal with a Practitioner on behalf of a Scheme Administrator.
  10. Notify you are no longer acting on behalf of a Scheme Administrator.
  11. Register to operate Relief at Source.
  12. Registered Pension Scheme Event Report.
  13. Registered Pension Scheme Return.
  14. Reporting cessation as a Scheme Administrator.

Contracting out is not available as an online form.

HMRC will accept valid returns for the products listed.
