Details

This document complements the existing Key Information and the UK Standards for Microbiology Investigations Serology Guidance.

It provides additional information for medical professionals and researchers. It is not intended to direct clinical care but should provide guidance to professionals.

The guidance covers the latest information on:

  • indications for antibody testing

  • natural history of antibody responses to SARS-CoV-2 infection and vaccination

  • antibodies and protection against infection

  • antibody responses in special groups

  • assay types
