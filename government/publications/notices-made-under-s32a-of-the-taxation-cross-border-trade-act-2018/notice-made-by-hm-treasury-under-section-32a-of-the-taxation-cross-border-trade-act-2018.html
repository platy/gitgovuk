The following text has the force of law by virtue of Section 32A(2) to the Taxation (Cross-border Trade) Act 2018

Replacement of the document “Authorised Use: Eligible Goods and Authorised Uses, version 2.3, dated 28th December 2021”

1) The document entitled “Authorised Use: Eligible Goods and Authorised Uses, version 2.3, dated 28th December 2021” referred to in regulation 32(2) of the Customs (Special Procedures and Outward Processing) (EU Exit) Regulations 2018, is replaced by the document entitled “Authorised Use: Eligible Goods and Authorised Uses, version 2.4, dated 27th April 2022”.

Replacement of the document “The Tariff of the United Kingdom, version 1.7, dated 28th December 2021”

2) The document entitled “The Tariff of the United Kingdom, version 1.7, dated 28th December 2021”, referred to in regulation 1(2) of the Customs Tariff (Establishment) (EU Exit) Regulations 2020, is replaced by the document entitled “The Tariff of the United Kingdom, version 1.8, dated 27th April 2022”.

Replacement of the document ““UK Reliefs document, version 1.3, dated 28th December 2021”

3) The document entitled “UK Reliefs document, version 1.3, dated 28th December 2021” referred to in regulation 2(1) of the Customs (Reliefs from a Liability to Import Duty and Miscellaneous Amendments) (EU Exit) Regulations 2020, is replaced by the document entitled “UK Reliefs document, version 1.4, dated 27th April 2022”.

Replacement of the document “Authorised Use: Eligible Goods and Rates, version 1.3, dated 28th December 2021”

4) The document entitled “Authorised Use: Eligible Goods and Rates, version 1.3, dated 28th December 2021” referred to in regulation 20(4) of the Customs (Reliefs from a Liability to Import Duty and Miscellaneous Amendments) (EU Exit) Regulations 2020, is replaced by the document entitled “Authorised Use: Eligible Goods and Rates, version 1.4, dated 27th April 2022”.

Replacement of the document “Tariff Quotas, version 3.0, dated 22 February 2022”

5) The document entitled “Tariff Quotas, version 3.0, dated 22 February 2022” referred to in regulation 2(1) of the Customs (Tariff Quotas) (EU Exit) Regulations 2020, is replaced by the document entitled “Tariff Quotas, version 3.1, dated 27th April 2022”.

Replacement of the document “Suspensions of Import Duty Rates Document, version 1.5, dated 28th December 2021”

6) The document entitled “Suspensions of Import Duty Rates Document, version 1.5, dated 28th December 2021” referred to in regulation 2 of the Customs Tariff (Suspension of Import Duty Rates) (EU Exit) Regulations 2020 is replaced by the document entitled “Suspensions of Import Duty Rates Document, version 1.6, dated 27th April 2022”.
