Details

This guide gives advice about the amendment of the register following an order of apportionment or a certificate of redemption given by a Secretary of State or the Welsh Parliament, or other government department where it affects a registered estate. It is aimed at conveyancers and members of the public and you should interpret references to ‘you’ accordingly. HM Land Registry staff will also refer to it.
