Details

Following the appointment of the Rt Hon Dame Elish Angiolini QC as Chair, terms of reference have been agreed for part 1 of the independent inquiry focusing on establishing a comprehensive account of the police career of the killer of Sarah Everard, to identify any missed opportunities and to make recommendations based on the findings.

If you have any queries about the Angiolini Inquiry or wish to get in touch with the inquiry, please email the Inquiry Team info@angiolini.independent-inquiry.uk.
