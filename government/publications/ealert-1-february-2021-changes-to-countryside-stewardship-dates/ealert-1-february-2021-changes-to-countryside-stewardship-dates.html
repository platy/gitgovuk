2. Important dates for Countryside Stewardship 2021

Updated Woodland Creation and Maintenance Grant.

An updated Countryside Stewardship Woodland Creation and Maintenance (WCM) grant will be open from 9 February 2021. The rates of the grants are not affected but the application process has been refreshed. If you are seeking to create new woodland through Countryside Stewardship in 2021/22 onwards please apply for the Woodland Creation Maintenance grant, full details including new manuals and application forms are available from 9 February on the GOV.UK Countryside Stewardship: how to apply page under Woodland support.

For applicants thinking of applying for the Higher Tier Woodland Improvement (WD2 and associated capital items) grant, for either Mixed or Woodland Only applications, please note:

  • the deadline for requesting a Higher Tier application pack is 31 March 2021
  • the initial deadline for application submission is 30 April 2021
  • the deadline for finalising applications is 31 August 2021
