Details

This guidance outlines personal protective equipment (PPE) advice for unpaid carers who do not live with the person they care for. Other unpaid carers may choose to use this guide. It should be used alongside guidance for those who provide unpaid care to friends or family and local policies where they exist such as in care homes or day centres.

Copies of this leaflet are available to download or order in the following languages:

English, Albanian, Arabic, Bengali, Brazilian Portuguese, Bulgarian, Chinese, Estonian, Farsi, Greek, Gujarati, Hindi, Latvian, Lithuanian, Panjabi, Pashto, Polish, Romanian, Romany, Russian, Somali, Spanish, Tagalog, Turkish, Twi, Ukrainian, Urdu and Yiddish.

The English version of the Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) guide for unpaid carers can be ordered from the Health Publications website using product code: COV2021PPE1
