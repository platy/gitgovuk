Details

This guidance outlines Personal Protection Equipment (PPE) advice for unpaid carers who do not live with the person they care for. Other unpaid carers may choose to use this guide. It should be used alongside guidance for those who provide unpaid care to friends or family and local policies where they exist such as in care homes or day centres.
