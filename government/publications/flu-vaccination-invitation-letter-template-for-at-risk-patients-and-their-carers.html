Details

This letter is for GP practices to invite patients who are eligible for flu vaccination because of:

  • a learning disability
  • their age
  • a medical condition
  • pregnancy
  • being the main carer of someone who is elderly or disabled
  • being part of a household where someone is shielding

The template can be adapted locally.
