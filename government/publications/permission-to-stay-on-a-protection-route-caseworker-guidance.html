Details

This instruction provides guidance to decision-makers on the duration of and conditions attached to permission to stay, previously referred to as leave to remain, to be granted to those who have been granted refugee status or humanitarian protection under Part 11 of the Immigration Rules. This includes those granted at initial decision stage or following an allowed appeal.

This guidance only applies to asylum applications made on or after 28 June 2022.
