Details

This notice should be read in conjunction with MGN 636 (M) amendment 1 and MGN 587 (F) amendment 1 ILO work in fishing convention: health and safety responsibilities which give guidance on the merchant shipping and fishing vessels (health and safety at work) regulations 1997 (SI 1997/2962 and replaces MGN 428 (M+F) amendment 1.

Notice to

  • ship, yacht and fishing vessel owners
  • operators and managers and other employers of seafarers
  • masters
  • officers and seafarers on merchant ships
  • hovercraft and yachts
  • skippers and crew of fishing vessels
  • small commercially operated vessels and yachts with paid crew, whether operating at sea or on inland waters.
