Details

In the context of the Agreement, the UK and New Zealand have signed a number of side arrangements on a range of issues. These have been negotiated in conjunction with the Free Trade Agreement. These copies are unsigned and have been signed alongside the treaty.

The agreement is not yet in force. Both the UK and New Zealand are required to complete their respective domestic procedures for the agreement to come into effect. Once approved by both parliaments, businesses will be able to trade under its terms.

Accessible versions of these documents are being created and will be available soon.
