(1) Tariff allocations in Wales are all paid at fiscal events rather than quarterly. These amounts will be paid from DLUHC to the Welsh Government, who will distribute funding to councils. Tariff payments are subject to a reconciliation process which may result in some of these amounts being reduced, and tariff reassigned to other local authorities. Accordingly, these amounts should be treated as indicative at this stage.

(2) This funding is provided quarterly in arrears using the financial assistance power (s.50/51) of the UK Internal Market Act.

(3) The Welsh Government will receive payments with regard to the Devolved Government Sponsorship route, and will apportion this to councils as appropriate once guests move out of welcome centre accommodation. Tariff payments are subject to a reconciliation process which may result in some of these amounts being reduced, and tariff reassigned to other local authorities. Accordingly these amounts should be treated as indicative at this stage.

The number of thank you payments may be less than the number of sponsoring households due to delays in authorities making thank you payments, or sponsors not accepting thank you payments.

The data in this publication will not match the weekly data published on the Ukraine Sponsorship Scheme.

This is because the data are collected over a different period of time, and this data is returned directly by local authorities whereas the Ukraine Sponsorship Scheme data is management information from operational systems.
