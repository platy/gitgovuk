The number of thank you payments may be less than the number of sponsoring households due to delays in authorities making thank you payments, or sponsors not accepting thank you payments.

The data in this publication will not match the weekly data published on the Ukraine Sponsorship Scheme. This is because the data are collected over a different period of time, and this data is returned directly by local authorities whereas the Ukraine Sponsorship Scheme data is management information from operational systems.

  1. Payments to the Northern Ireland Executive are made at fiscal events. These allocations are therefore published for comparison purposes and will be paid later in the year. Tariff payments are subject to a reconciliation process which may result in some of these amounts being reduced, and tariff reassigned to other local authorities. Accordingly these amounts should be treated as indicative at this stage. ↩
