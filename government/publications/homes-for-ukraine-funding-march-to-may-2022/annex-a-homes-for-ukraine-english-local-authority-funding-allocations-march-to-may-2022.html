A number of councils have reported 0 ‘thank you’ payments during the Q1 reporting period, but have confirmed to the Department they have since started making ‘thank you’ payments (e.g. in June).

The number of thank you payments may be less than the number of sponsoring households due to delays in authorities making thank you payments, or sponsors not accepting thank you payments.

The data in this publication will not match the weekly data published on the Ukraine Sponsorship Scheme.

This is because the data are collected over a different period of time, and this data is returned directly by local authorities whereas the Ukraine Sponsorship Scheme data is management information from operational systems.
