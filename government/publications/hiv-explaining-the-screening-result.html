HIV: what your positive screening test result means

Details

Healthcare professionals and women can refer to this information to learn about HIV and to discuss their screening result and follow-on care.

This information is also available in 12 other languages: Arabic, Bengali, Chinese, French, Lithuanian, Latvian, Polish, Portuguese, Punjabi, Romanian, Somali and Urdu.

Contact the screening helpdesk with any queries about this publication, making sure you include its full title.
