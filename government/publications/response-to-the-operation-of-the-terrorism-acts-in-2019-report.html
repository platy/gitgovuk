Details

In March 2021, the government published the annual report on the operation of the terrorism acts in 2019 by Jonathan Hall QC, the Independent Reviewer of Terrorism Legislation.

The government response addresses the following topics that were covered in the report:

  • threat picture
  • terrorist groups
  • terrorist investigations
  • arrest and detention
  • ports and borders
  • terrorism trials and sentencing
  • civil powers
  • Scotland and Northern Ireland
  • statistics
