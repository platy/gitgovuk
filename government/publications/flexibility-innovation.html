Details

The 2021 Smart Systems and Flexibility Plan and Energy Digitalisation Strategy identified key BEIS policy priorities to enable the transition to a smart, flexible and decarbonised energy system.

To support the transition, Flexibility Innovation Programme seeks to enable large-scale widespread electricity system flexibility through smart, flexible, secure, and accessible technologies and markets. Up to £65 million will be made available. This overarching programme will fund innovation across a range of key smart energy applications. New activities under the Flexibility Innovation Programme will be launched over the coming year.

The Flexibility Innovation Programme forms a substantial part of a commitment set out in the Prime Minister’s 10 point plan for a green industrial revolution, to invest at least £100 million on energy storage and flexibility innovation challenges.

The Flexibility Innovation Programme is in development phase but it is anticipated areas of focus could include integrating systems for flexibility, data and digitalisation solutions, and markets for flexibility.

The programme is part of the Net Zero Innovation Portfolio.
