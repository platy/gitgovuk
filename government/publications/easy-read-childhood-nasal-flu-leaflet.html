Details

All about flu and how to stop getting it: adult version

This leaflet is aimed at adults with low literacy who are eligible for the annual flu vaccination. It provides simple advice on:

  • the flu virus and why you need the vaccine every year
  • signs and symptoms of flu
  • how to get your flu vaccination

Printed copies of this leaflet will be available free to order from mid-October 2022.

All about flu and how to stop getting it: child version

This leaflet is aimed at children with low literacy who are offered the nasal flu vaccination. It provides simple advice on:

  • the flu virus and why you need the vaccine
  • signs and symptoms of flu
  • how the nasal flu spray is given

Further information is available for the current annual flu programme.

Printed copies of this leaflet can be ordered free of charge from the Health Publications orderline using product code FLU2021STC.

See the easy-read resources leaflet, poster and flyer for adults with a learning disability at easy-read flu vaccination resources
