Guidance

Sending a copy of the appeal form to the council

You must send a copy of your appeal and all the supporting documents to the local planning authority.

From:
Planning Inspectorate
Published
1 January 2013
Last updated
3 May 2022 — See all updates

Applies to England

Documents

Sending a copy to the council

HTML

Details

Please ensure you send a copy of the appeal form and supporting documents to the local planning authority, this can be done by post or e-mail. Some councils will accept an e-mail copy of an appeal made online.
