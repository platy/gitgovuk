Details

The Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office (FCDO) annual report and accounts provides detail on the activities of the FCDO during financial year 2021 to 2022.

The report consists of:

  • a performance report
  • an accountability report
  • financial statements

The financial statements and certain sections of the accountability report are audited by the National Audit Office before they are presented to Parliament. The report was laid in Parliament on 19 July 2022.
