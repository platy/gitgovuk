Charges under this scheme increase annually on 1 April in line with any increase in the Consumer Prices Index published by the Office for National Statistics (ONS).

We take a measure of inflation as at 30 September in the immediately preceding year. All charges will increase by 3.1% from 1 April 2022, in line with the ONS measure of inflation published in their October report.

With the exception of aviation subsistence charges, all customers invoiced from 1 April 2022 must pay the 1 April 2022 to 31 March 2023 charges. Aviation subsistence is paid in arrears, so invoices from 1 April 2022 will be based on the 1 April 2021 to 31 March 2022 charges.
