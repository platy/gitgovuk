Details

This release contains data about the Environmental Monitoring for Health Protection (EMHP) wastewater programme.

The data covers the period 15 July 2020 to 30 March 2022.

This includes data on the concentration of SARS-CoV-2 RNA in wastewater sampled from sewage treatment works and detection of SARS-CoV-2 variants and mutations as part of the EMHP National Monitoring programme.
