EU VAT e-commerce package

HTML

Details

The EU’s e-commerce package will introduce changes from 1 July 2021 in respect of the movement of goods from Northern Ireland to the EU and imports of low value goods into the EU or Northern Ireland. The package also introduces new rules for supplies made through online marketplaces, similar to those already applying in Great Britain and partly in Northern Ireland.
