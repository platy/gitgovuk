Details

These maps show the Freeport tax sites within Teesside Freeport which are designated and recognised in law as geographical areas where businesses can benefit from tax reliefs to bring investment, trade and jobs to regenerate regions across the country that need it most.

The tax sites for the Teesside Freeport have been designated with effect from 19 November 2021.
