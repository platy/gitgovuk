  1. 1 January 2022

    Notices made under the Customs (Import Duty) (EU Exit) Regulations 2018 and List of roll on roll off (RoRo) locations under the Customs (Import Duty) (EU Exit) Regulations 2018 have been updated to reflect changes taking effect from 1 January 2022. Notices for other listed locations have been removed because no locations are listed from 1 January 2022.

  2. 1 December 2021

    The roll on roll off (RoRo) listed locations under Regulation 130(1) of The Customs (Import Duty) (EU Exit) Regulations 2018 have been added. The other listed airports and airfields, rail terminals and maritime ports and wharves locations under Regulation 131E(1) of the Customs (Import Duty) (EU Exit) Regulations 2018 have also been added.

  3. 5 November 2021

    Notices made under the Customs (Import Duty) (EU Exit) Regulations 2018 has been updated.

  4. 21 October 2021

    A new entry has been added in the 'Rail Depots - Rolling Stock' section of the notices.

  5. 10 June 2021

    Updated Section 12 – the period in which a simplified customs declaration can be made, followed by a supplementary declaration 175 days later, for imports made using a fixed transport installation has been extended to 31 December 2021.

  6. 6 May 2021

    Harry Needle Railroad Company (HNRC), Holbeck, Holgate and Singlewell Infrastructure Maintenance Depot have been added to 'RAIL DEPOTS – ROLLING STOCK'.

  7. 28 April 2021

    The Notices made under the Customs (Import Duty) (EU Exit) Regulations 2018 has been updated.
