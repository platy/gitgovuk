Details

Before you apply for the UK Seafood Fund: Infrastructure Scheme, you will need to state you have read the following documents in your Qualification Questions form and in the UK Fisheries Support System.

Form of application (Annex A)

The Form of application sets out what you are agreeing to if you make an application to us.

Terms and conditions (Annex B)

This document outlines terms and conditions such as:

  • monitoring and reporting
  • auditing and assurance
  • conflicts of interest
  • statutory duties

Privacy notice

The notice sets out how the Marine Management Organisation will collect your data in the UK Fisheries Support System when you apply for the Infrastructure Scheme.
