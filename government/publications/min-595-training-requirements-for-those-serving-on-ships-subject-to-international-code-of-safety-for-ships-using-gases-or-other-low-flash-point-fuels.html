Details

This MIN details the training and certification requirements for personnel working on ships subject to the IGF code, those working on ships operating in polar waters and the new requirements for those working on passenger ships. It also details the new entry requirements for HELM Management in line with MSN 1856.

Notice to all ship owners, Masters, Officers, Ratings and those concerned with maritime training.

This notice should be read with MSN 1866 and MGN 637

This MIN has been extended and expires 31st October 2022.
