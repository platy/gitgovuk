Coronavirus (COVID-19): antibody testing

Information about the government’s coronavirus antibody testing programme. Antibody tests are to check whether a person has had the virus.

Published 22 May 2020
Last updated 24 February 2021 — see all updates
From:
Department of Health and Social Care
Applies to:
Wales, England, and Northern Ireland

This guidance tells you about the government’s antibody testing programme for coronavirus. It sets out:

  • what antibody testing is
  • what an antibody test will tell you
  • who is eligible for testing
