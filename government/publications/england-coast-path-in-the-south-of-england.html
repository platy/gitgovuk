Details

Find out about the work being carried out to improve public access to each stretch of the coast in the south of England.

Stretch name Progress
Highcliffe to Calshot Stage 4: Determine
Calshot to Gosport Stage 5: Open (not yet available for public use - work to establish the route is currently taking place)
Isle of Wight - East Cowes to Wootton Bridge Stage 2 and 3: Develop and Propose
Isle of Wight - Wootton Bridge to the Medina Stage 4: Determine
Gosport to Portsmouth Stage 5: Open (not yet available for public use - work to establish the route is currently taking place)
Portsmouth to South Hayling Stage 4: Determine
South Hayling to East Head Stage 4 and 5: Partially approved (not yet available for public use - work to establish the route is currently taking place on approved lengths)
East Head to Shoreham Stage 5: Open (not yet available for public use - work to establish the route is currently taking place)
