Industry Security Notice

Number 2019-02

This Industry Security Notice (ISN) should be read in conjunction with ISN 2017/06.

Introduction

  1. This ISN informs suppliers to the MOD of the planned updates to the Risk Assessment and Supplier Assurance Questionnaire. This is part of the Cyber Security Model set out in DEFCON 658.

Detail

  1. The Defence Cyber Protection Partnership (DCPP) will be updating the Cyber Protection Tool with the new Risk Assessment (RA) and Supplier Assurance Questionnaire (SAQ) to reflect the changing threat landscape.

  2. This release is planned for 12 November 2019. The new RA and SAQ workflow documents are available online and can be downloaded from the DCPP website.

  3. The more simplified RA question set will be based on classification of data and sharing of personal information. The SAQ will address the current threat landscape through applying controls appropriate to the risk profiles. This may require additional controls when existing risk assessments go through their annual review process.

  4. The DEFCON 658 and Defence Standard 05-138 are currently under review and will be published shortly.

  5. The current RA and SAQ were released in October 2017. Any RAs that are created by MOD before the update on 12 November 2019, will continue to flow-down using the same RA and the same SAQ.

Validity / Expiry Date

  1. This notice is valid with immediate effect and remains so until further notice.

Contact Details
