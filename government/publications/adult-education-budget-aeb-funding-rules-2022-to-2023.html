Details

We have published version 1 of the ESFA adult education budget (AEB) funding rules for the 2022 to 2023 funding year (1 August 2022 to 31 July 2023) in HTML format.

The rules apply to all providers of education and training who receive AEB funding from the Secretary of State for Education acting through ESFA.

If you have any feedback about the funding rules document, please contact us using our online enquiry form or speak to your territorial lead contact.
