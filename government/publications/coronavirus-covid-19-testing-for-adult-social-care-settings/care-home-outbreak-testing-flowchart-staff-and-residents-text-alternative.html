Step 1: Have there been 2 or more positive COVID-19 cases in a 14-day period?

Yes:

  • contact the HPT (or if applicable, your community infection prevention and control team, LA or CCG)

  • outbreak testing:

    • day 0/1: all staff and residents take PCR (if an individual has tested positive for COVID-19 in the last 90 days, their testing maybe different. Read the IPC-COVID-19 supplement for further information) and LFD tests
    • day 4 to 7: staff and residents take one PCR (if an individual has tested positive for COVID-19 in the last 90 days, their testing maybe different. Read the IPC-COVID-19 supplement for further information) and one LFD on one of these days
    • day 7 on: staff continue regular twice weekly symptomatic LFDs

No:

No positive tests:

Continue regular asymptomatic staff testing. If any staff member or resident becomes symptomatic, they should take an LFD test on day 0 and then on day 2. If either of these are positive, follow the guidance in the IPC-COVID-19 supplement.

1 positive case:

If there is one positive case in the home, staff should conduct rapid response testing of daily LFDs for five days.

Step 2: Has it been 10 days since the last member of staff or resident tested positive for COVID-19 or experienced symptoms during the outbreak?

Yes:

  • outbreak recovery testing: all staff and residents conduct a PCR test (if an individual has tested positive for COVID-19 in the last 90 days, their testing maybe different. Read the IPC-COVID-19 supplement for further information)

    • were any new positives found?
      • yes: HPT (or if applicable, your community infection prevention and control team, LA or CCG) will advise that the outbreak restrictions should continue until no further positives are found
      • no: HPT (or if applicable, your community infection prevention and control team, LA or CCG) will advise you can lift outbreak restrictions and follow normal testing

No:

  • outbreak continues: continue regular asymptomatic staff testing and continue LFD testing aany newly symptomatic individuals

Step 3: Have there been any positive cases in the home after outbreak recovery testing?

Yes:

This should be classed as a new potential outbreak and the care home should follow the process from step 1.

No:

Regular staff asymptomatic testing continues

If any staff or resident becomes newly symptomatic, they should take an LFD test when they become symptomatic. If this test is negative, take another test 48 hours later. If either of these are positive, follow the guidance in the IPC-COVID-19 supplement.
