Details

Technical guidance note EPR 8.01 provides additional information for operators of landspreading permits and U10 and U11 exemptions. It gives guidance on appropriate measures and technical standard for deployment.

The appendices provide additional information on permitted wastes, soils properties.

Changes to How to comply with your landspreading permit

We have reformatted this guidance. See the reformatted guidance (and the guidance it links to) Landspreading to improve soil health. You can feedback on the reformatted guidance by email to groundwater.enquiries@environment-agency.gov.uk . We will withdraw How to comply with your landspreading permit (TGN 8.01) in 12 weeks time.
