Details

Pre-print paper prepared by the National Institute for Communicable Diseases, South Africa on the B.1.1.529 Omicron variant of concern. This paper was available for participants to read at SAGE 101 on 23 December 2021, but not considered or discussed in the meeting. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
