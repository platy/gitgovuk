Details

Local authorities wishing to apply for a new special free school in their area, should:

  • read the guidance on how to apply to set up a special free school (for local authorities)
  • complete the application and specification forms which were sent via email upon completion of the mandatory pre-registration (which closed on 11 July)
  • submit the completed application and specification forms via email to fs.applications@education.gov.uk by midday on Friday 21 October 2022

We are also seeking applications to run the new pre-approved special free school in Bury. Proposer groups must:

  • read the guidance on how to apply to set up a special free school in Bury, in response to the local authority’s specification
  • complete the application forms which were sent via email upon completion of the mandatory pre-registration (which closed on 11 July)
  • submit the completed application forms via email to fs.applications@education.gov.uk by midday on Friday 21 October 2022

If you did not complete the pre-registration, which closed on 11 July 2022, you will be unable to submit an application for a new special free school in this wave.
