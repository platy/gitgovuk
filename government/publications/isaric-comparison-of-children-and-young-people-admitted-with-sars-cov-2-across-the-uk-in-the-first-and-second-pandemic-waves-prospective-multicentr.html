Details

Paper prepared by the International Severe Acute Respiratory and Emerging Infection Consortium (ISARIC) on COVID-19 cases involving children and young people. This paper uses data provided by the COVID-19 Clinical Information Network (CO-CIN). It was considered at SAGE 95 on 9 September 2021. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper should be read alongside the supporting paper ISARIC: Supplementary Information - Comparison of children and young people admitted with SARS-CoV-2 across the UK in the first and second pandemic waves: prospective multicentre observational cohort study, 9 September 2021, from SAGE 95.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
