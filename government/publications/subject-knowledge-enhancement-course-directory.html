Details

Use this course directory to search for existing providers of SKE courses.

This guidance is for School Direct lead schools and initial teacher training (ITT) providers who are referring individuals for SKE courses.

The Department funds SKE courses in:

  • biology
  • chemistry
  • computing
  • design and technology
  • English
  • languages
  • mathematics
  • primary mathematics
  • physics
  • religious education

School Direct is a school-led route into ITT. It is run by a partnership between a lead school, other schools and an accredited teacher training provider.
