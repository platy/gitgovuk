Details

To reduce the risk of carbon leakage, operators covered by the UK Emissions Trading Scheme (UK ETS) with a valid application are provided with a free allocation of allowance.

The Allocation Table contains a list of each installation’s free allocation for the 2021 to 2025 allocation period.

Your installation will not be included in the table if either of the following apply:

  • your application has been marked as invalid
  • your installation is part of the Hospital and Small Emitter or Ultra-Small Emitter opt-out schemes

Read more about how free allocation works under the UK ETS.
