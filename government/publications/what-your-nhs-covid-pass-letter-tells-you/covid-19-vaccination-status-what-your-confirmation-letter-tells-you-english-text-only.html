On the front of the letter: bottom

Find out about COVID-19 symptoms, testing, vaccination and self-isolation on the NHS website.

Data protection

The Department of Health and Social Care (DHSC) is the data controller and is responsible for processing your personal data for the purposes of the NHS COVID Pass programme. To find out more you can access the DHSC privacy notice. The NHS COVID Pass privacy notice is available within the guidance.

On the back of the letter

Questions you may have

When and where could I be asked to show my NHS COVID Pass?

You may be asked to show your NHS COVID Pass if you travel abroad.

Is there a digital alternative?

You can use the NHS App to access your NHS COVID Pass and show your vaccination record.

What should I do if I lose this document?

Your NHS COVID Pass is important. Keep it safe. It confirms that you have been vaccinated against COVID-19. If you lose it you can get information on what to do on the NHS website or please call 119 free of charge, 7 days a week.

Is this information available in other languages, large type or braille?

You can get this information in alternative languages and formats. Please call 119 free of charge, 7 days a week.

You should keep this original English language version as you may be asked to show it if you travel abroad.
