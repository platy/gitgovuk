Decree of the President of Ukraine on the boundaries and a list of districts, towns, settlements and villages and parts of their territories which are temporarily occupied in the Donetsk and Luhansk regions.

Pursuant to section 2 of article one of the Ukrainian Act “On the features of state policy on the maintenance of the state sovereignty of Ukraine in the temporarily occupied territories in the Donetsk and Luhansk regions, I decree as follows:

  1. The boundaries and a list of districts, towns, settlements and villages and parts of their territories in the Donetsk and Luhansk regions (appended) be ratified.

  2. That this Decree enter into effect on the day of its promulgation.

President of Ukraine: P. POROSHENKO Kyiv on 7 February 2019 No.32/2019
