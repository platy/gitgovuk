Planned downtime

Customs Declaration Service

Saturday 29 January 2022 9:30pm to 5am Sunday 30 January 2022

Due to scheduled maintenance to the Customs Declaration Service, you will be unable to enter or validate Customs Declaration Service declarations to get a goods movement reference (GMR) from the Goods Vehicle Movement Service from 9:30pm on Saturday 29 January 2022 to 5am on Sunday 30 January 2022.

The core services of the Goods Vehicle Movement Service will be available. You will still be able to enter and validate Customs Handling of Import and Export Freight (CHIEF) and New Computerised Transit System (NCTS) declarations to obtain a goods movement reference during this downtime.

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.
