Details

On the date of publication, this document also outlines the current position on areas in which we are working jointly with the Ministry of Housing, Communities and Local Government and assurances we have received from relevant public authorities, including the London Fire Brigade, National Fire Chiefs Council and relevant emergency services, on their current progress to address and implement the Phase 1 recommendations directed to them.

Please note that this document will be updated quarterly to reflect progress and does not reflect finalised government policy.
