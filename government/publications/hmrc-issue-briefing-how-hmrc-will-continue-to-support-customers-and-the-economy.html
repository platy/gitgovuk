Details

This document sets out how we will work with our customers and stakeholders over the coming months, while coronavirus (COVID-19) continues to impact the country. There are many strands to our work which have changed and will continue to change over the coming months, and we are committed to being as open and as transparent as possible when we make any changes.

The document contains information about the support schemes and policy changes that HMRC has implemented and our principles for the next steps around tax collection, benefits payments, compliance checks and debt activity.
