Successful applications for CIF 2022 to 2023

This document published by The Department for Education (DfE) outlines the Condition Improvement Fund 2022 to 2023 outcome. CIF 2022 to 2023 will provide funding of Â£498 million for 1,405 projects at 1,129 academies, sixth-form colleges and voluntary aided schools.

We have contacted all applicants to inform them of the outcome of their application. Further information, setting out next steps for applicants has been provided.

CIF 2022 to 2023 appeals

The appeals round for CIF 2022 to 2023 is now open. The deadline for applications is 4pm on 7 June 2022. Appeals will be for those applicants that can demonstrate that a material error has been made in the assessment of their application.

Feedback, scores and guidance are now available through the CIF portal.
