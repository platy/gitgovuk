Part 1 claim: A45 Chowns Mill Scheme

Notice of period for filing claims between 11 December 2022 and 10 December 2028 for land depreciation as a result of the A45 Chowns Mill Scheme.

From:
National Highways
Published
25 February 2022

Applies to England

A45 Chowns Mill Scheme

Details

Under Part I of the Land Compensation Act 1973, compensation can be claimed by anyone having a qualifying interest in land if the value of that inter­est has been depreciated by more than £50 by physical factors caused by the use of the altered highway.

This notice contains a summary of the law governing such claims.
