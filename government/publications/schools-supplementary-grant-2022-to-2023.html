Details

The guidance is for:

  • local authorities
  • school leaders
  • school teachers
  • governing bodies and their representatives

It relates to local authority-maintained schools and academies in England.

Conditions of grant

This guidance sets out the terms and conditions applied to the schools supplementary grant allocations and payments to mainstream schools for the financial year beginning 1 April 2022.

Methodology

This guidance will help local authorities and schools understand how the grant is calculated for 2022 to 2023 and how this will be paid to schools and academies.

Allocations

You can view the allocations for the schools supplementary grant. A first payment of SSG will be made to local authorities in May 2022, and June 2022 for academies. A second payment will be made to local authorities in October 2022, and November 2022 for academies.

Further details

You can find out more about the dedicated schools grant (DSG) settlement for 2022 to 2023 following the autumn 2021 spending review.
