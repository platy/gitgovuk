Details

This guidance is for exam centres delivering GCSEs, A levels and other general and vocational qualifications.

Exam centres include:

  • schools
  • colleges
  • private exam centres

It provides information about the private candidate support grant. This is funding available for exam centres to cover the additional costs associated with assessing private candidates in summer 2021.
