Details

Tools to assists travel concession authorities and bus operators in calculating the reimbursement due from concessionary fares schemes for the elderly and disabled.

Current payment calculations are made through the latest financial years bus travel reimbursement calculator, the previous year’s calculator is retained for comparison purposes only.

The software, for making claims, should be used with the:

  • guidance on reimbursing bus operators for concessionary travel
  • research into the reimbursement of concessionary fares
  • concessionary bus travel application guide
  • concessionary bus travel applications proforma
