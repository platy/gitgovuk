Details

Read the exceptional costs guidance to find out how to claim exceptional costs related to coronavirus (COVID-19) mass testing carried out between 4 and 14 January 2022.

The deadline to submit a claim is Sunday, 20 February 2022.

Allocations for the first half of the spring term will be made available in due course. For an estimated funding value please refer to the Workforce planning tool which provides estimated allocations from when students return following the Christmas holiday to the February half term.

Read the conditions of grant to find out more about eligibility, payments, assurance and recovery of funding. Please note that a revised conditions of grant document will be issued shortly; however, you should continue to refer to the August 2021 edition until that is made available.
