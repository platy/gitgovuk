Details

The exceptional costs guidance outlining out how to claim exceptional costs related to coronavirus (COVID-19) mass testing carried out between 4 and 14 January 2022 is for information only.

The deadline to submit a claim was Sunday, 20 February 2022 and no further claims can be submitted.

Allocations for the first half of the spring term will be made available in due course. For an estimated funding value please refer to the Workforce planning tool which provides estimated allocations from when students return following the Christmas holiday to the February half term.

Read the conditions of grant to find out more about eligibility, payments, assurance and recovery of funding.
