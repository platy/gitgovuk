Details

The range danger area consists of the Dee Salt Marshes and has no tracks or road network.

Do not enter the danger area when the range is in use, this is a danger to life. If ‘red flags’ or lights are hoisted, regardless of the timings, do not enter the training area. Learn more about accessing MOD training areas safely.

The full perimeter of the range danger area is signed to warn the public of the danger. Red flags will be flown when the range is in use.

Updated information on the firing taking place on Sealand Range Complex can be obtained by ringing:

  • Ops Room (24 hours): 01874635599
  • Sealand Range: 01244280106 / 07766991807
  • Main Office: 01743741607
