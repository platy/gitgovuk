Details

Paper by SPI-B on horizon scanning potential security and policing challenges. This is advice to Government produced by SPI-B, this is not endorsed by the MOD or Home Office nor does it constitute the views of MOD or Home Office.

This paper was available to SAGE participants, but was not formally tabled or discussed in a SAGE meeting. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
