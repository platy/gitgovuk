Details

Guidance for employers in the creative sector on how to sponsor a creative worker on the Creative Worker route.

This route allows creative workers who can make a unique contribution to the UK’s rich cultural life, and their entourage, to come to the UK for up to 12 months initially, with the option to extend their permission up to a maximum of 24 months (if continuing to work for the same sponsor).
