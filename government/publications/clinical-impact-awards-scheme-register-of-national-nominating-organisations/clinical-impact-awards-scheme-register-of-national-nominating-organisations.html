The Academy of Medical Royal Colleges (AoMRC) is an accredited nominating organisation. However, as the process for providing support is under discussion, AoMRC will not be making nominations in the 2022 award round.

Universities UK continues to be an accredited national nominating organisation supporting National Clinical Impact Awards for clinical academics, working with its constituent universities, the Medical Schools Council and Medical Research Council. However, as the process for providing support is still under discussion it will not be making nominations for the 2022 round.

How to request support

If you wish to request support from one of the organisations above you must contact them directly.

You will also need to confirm the organisation from which you are seeking support on your application.
