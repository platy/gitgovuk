Details

Paper by CO-CIN for SAGE regarding the VOC2020-01. This paper was available for participants to read at SAGE 80 on 11 February 2021, but not considered or discussed in the meeting. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

An earlier version of this paper was published on 12 February 2021 which went to SAGE 77 on 21 January 2021.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
