Details

This guide explains about the conditions you must meet to qualify for the disability element of Working Tax Credit and Child Tax Credit.

The 3 conditions you must meet are, you:

  • usually work for 16 hours or more a week
  • have a disability that puts you at a disadvantage in getting a job
  • currently get, or have been getting, a qualifying sickness or disability benefit

Helpsheets are for guidance only and show HMRC’s position at the time of writing.
