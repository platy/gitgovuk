Details

These documents present the national screening standards for the NHS Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm (AAA) Screening Programme.

The most recent standards (2022) apply to data collected from 1 April 2022 onwards.

The previous standards (2020) apply to data collected from 1 April 2020 to 31 March 2022 inclusive.

See Population screening: our approach to screening standards for the consistent approach taken to screening standards across all 11 NHS screening programmes.
