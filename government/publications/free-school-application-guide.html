Details

Submit your expression of interest form by 5pm on Friday 16 September 2022 to register your interest in opening a new free school.

We are looking to open 15 new mainstream free schools in areas:

  • that currently have the lowest educational standards
  • where there is also a need for additional school places

Once you have submitted an expression of interest form, we will send you an application form in July. You will then have until 31 October 2022 to submit your application.

We have published 2 maps showing the latest estimates of the need for new primary and secondary school places to help you identify potential locations for your school.
