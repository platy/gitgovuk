Details

Submit your application by 5pm on Wednesday 30 November 2022.

We are looking to open 15 new mainstream free schools in areas:

  • that currently have the lowest educational standards
  • where there is also a need for additional school places

The expression of interest process closed on 16 September 2022. If you completed your expression of interest form by this deadline then you should have received an application form.

We are no longer accepting expressions of interest for this wave. If you have not registered your interest then you cannot submit an application in this wave.

We have published 2 maps showing the latest estimates of the need for new primary and secondary school places to help you identify potential locations for your school.
