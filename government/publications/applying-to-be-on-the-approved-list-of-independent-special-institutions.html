Guide for independent special institutions applying for inclusion on the Secretary of State approved list

HTML

Details

Section 41 of the Children and Families Act 2014 allows the Secretary of State to publish a list of approved:

  • independent educational institutions in England
  • independent special schools in Wales
  • special post-16 institutions

The guide explains the process for applying to be added to the list of approved independent special institutions. It also sets out the timescales and information required to complete the application form.

Institutions must be able to provide evidence and demonstrate they meet the criteria listed within the guidance.

The 2022 application window is now open. Complete the online application form to apply to be on the approved list of independent special institutions.
