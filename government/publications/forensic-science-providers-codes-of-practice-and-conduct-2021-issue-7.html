Details

These are the revised codes of practice and conduct for forensic science providers and practitioners in the criminal justice system.

This is the seventh issue of the codes. It is issued to reinstate section 23.4 omitted in error from issue 6. These are effective from 22 March 2021 and replace all previously issued versions.
