Details

The Freeport tax sites are marked on these maps for convenience only and to provide an overview. They do not show the legal boundaries of the Freeport tax sites.

The map of Humber Freeport includes a proposed tax site at Goole which has not been designated.

The map of Solent Freeport dated 23 June 2022 replaces the previous map of Solent Freeport dated 21 February 2022.

You can also check previous maps of UK freeports on the National Archives website.

Check the full list of Freeport maps and the Freeport tax site locations within them.
