3. Agency

You can use this word in your proposed name provided it does not imply a connection with a government department or body, a devolved administration or a local or specified public authority.

4. Assembly

You can use this word in your proposed name provided it does not imply a connection with a government department or body, a devolved administration or a local or specified public authority.

5. Authority

This word is often associated with public authorities. Please ensure the use of this word in your proposed name does not imply a connection with a public authority, a government department or body or a devolved administration or a local authority.

6. Border Agency / Border Force

The use of these expressions in a proposed name could imply a connection with the UK Border Force or UK Visas and Immigration.

If you proceed with your application, please include a copy of a letter or email of non-objection from the Home Office.

public.enquiries@homeoffice.gov.uk

Home Office
Direct Communications Unit
