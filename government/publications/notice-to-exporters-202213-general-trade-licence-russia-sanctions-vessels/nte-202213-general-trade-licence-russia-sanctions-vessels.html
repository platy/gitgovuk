The General Licence has been revised to include aircraft and aeroengines and the provision of insurance services relating to aviation within the scope of the licence.

Read the general trade licence.

The licence comes into force on 05 April 2022. Any person seeking to rely on the licence must register online via SPIRE within 30 days of their first use of the licence.

Contacts

For any questions about the impact of these sanctions on UK businesses operating in any sector, or business operations in Ukraine or Russia, contact the Export Support Service who are providing a front line Russia sanctions support service to all businesses.
