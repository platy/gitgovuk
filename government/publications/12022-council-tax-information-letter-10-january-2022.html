Details

This letter introduces an updated council tax collection fund deficit spreading tool and guidance notes for the second year of the collection fund deficit phasing scheme. These will assist billing authorities in making the estimates and apportionment of liability required in January 2022 by the Local Authorities (Funds)(England) Regulations 1992.
