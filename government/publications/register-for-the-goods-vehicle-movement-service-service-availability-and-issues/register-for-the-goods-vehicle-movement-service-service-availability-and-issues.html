Service availability

Sign in to the Goods Vehicle Movement Service.

Planned downtime

Saturday 19 February 2022 10pm to 11:30pm

Due to scheduled maintenance the following Goods Vehicle Movement Service (GVMS) actions that link to the New Computerised Transit System (NCTS) may be affected between 10pm to 11:30pm on Saturday 19 February 2022:

  • Create/Update GMR: goods movement references (GMRs) will go to ‘finalisable’ state if a NCTS declaration is being attached during this maintenance
  • Check-in GMR: no issue
  • Embark GMR (inbound only): ‘Completed’ status on GMR will be processed once maintenance has completed

The core services of the GVMS will be available. You will still be able to enter and validate Customs Declaration Service and Customs Handling of Import and Export Freight (CHIEF) declarations to obtain a goods movement reference during the scheduled maintenance.

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

Saturday 19 February 2022 11:30pm to 1am Sunday 20 February 2022

Due to scheduled maintenance the GVMS will not be available between 11:30pm on Saturday 19 February to 1am on Sunday 20 February 2022:

  • Creating/Update GMRs: will not be available
  • Check-in/Embark: GMRs will not be validated

Messages will be queued and processed when the maintenance ends.

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

Sunday 20 February 2022 7:30pm to 8:30pm

Due to scheduled maintenance between 7:30pm and 8:30pm on Sunday 20 February 2022 the following GVMS actions may be affected:

  • Creating/Update goods movement references (GMRs): an error will occur if CHIEF declarations are being associated to the GMR
  • Check-in GMRs (Outbound) during this maintenance: GMRs with CHIEF declarations will go to a controlled state, queued messages will be processed and risking response may change from ‘controlled’ to ‘release’ when the maintenance ends
  • Embark GMR (Outbound): departure messages will be queued and will be processed and updated when the maintenance ends
  • Embark GMR (Inbound): GMRs with CHIEF declarations will go to a controlled state, queued messages will be processed and risking response may change from ‘controlled’ to ‘release’ when the maintenance ends

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

Saturday 26 February 2022 11pm to 12:30am Sunday 27 February 2022

Due to scheduled maintenance on the Customs Declaration Service from 11pm on Saturday 26 February 2022 to 12:30am on Sunday 27 February 2022, the following Goods Vehicle Movement Service (GVMS) actions that link to the Customs Declaration Service may be affected:

  • Create/Update GMR: goods movement references (GMRs) will go to ‘non-finalisable’ state if a Customs Declaration Service declaration is being attached during this maintenance
  • Check-in GMRs (Outbound) during this maintenance: GMRs with Customs Declaration Service declarations will go to a controlled state, queued messages will be processed and risking response may change from ‘controlled’ to ‘release’ when the maintenance ends
  • Embark GMR (inbound): GMRs with Customs Declaration Service declarations will go to a controlled state, queued messages will be processed and risking response may change from ‘controlled’ to ‘release’ when the maintenance ends

The core services of the GVMS will be available. You will still be able to enter and validate Customs Declaration Service and Customs Handling of Import and Export Freight (CHIEF) declarations to obtain a goods movement reference during the scheduled maintenance.

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.
