Service availability

Full service available.

Planned downtime

Saturday 13 August 2022 10pm to 10:45pm

Sunday 14 August 2022 10pm to 10:45pm

Due to scheduled maintenance on the New Computerised Transit System, creating, updating, or embarking a goods movement reference during this time will be affected. Declarations submitted in this time will be processed at the end of the downtime. 

If you need to complete a goods movement reference during the downtime, make sure any Movement Reference Numbers you add have been created and released before the downtime. 

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.

Saturday 27 August 2022 10pm to 1:30am Sunday 28 August 2022

There is planned maintenance for the Goods Vehicle Movement Service from 10pm on Saturday 27 August to 1:30pm on Sunday 28 August. As a result, creating, updating, or embarking a goods movement reference during this time will be affected. Declarations submitted in this time will be processed at the end of the downtime. 

If you need to complete a goods movement reference during the downtime, make sure any Movement Reference Numbers you add have been created and released before the downtime. 

The Customs Declaration service will also be unavailable from Saturday 27 August 2022 from 9:30pm to 4am Sunday 28 August 2022.

We apologise for any inconvenience this may cause.
