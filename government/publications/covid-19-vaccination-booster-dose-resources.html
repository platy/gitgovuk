Details

People aged 18 years and over, and those aged over 16 years who are at risk (including health and social care workers) will be offered a booster dose of coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccine. You can book online, or call 119 to make an appointment to have your booster. Further information on the booster vaccination is available on NHS.UK.

The COVID-19 vaccination: a guide to booster vaccination for individuals aged 18 years and over is currently available for download only. Paper copies will be available shortly to order.

The COVID-19 vaccination: a guide to booster vaccination for individuals aged 40 years and over leaflet in English is available to order. Translated versions will be available shortly to order.

Paper copies of the booster leaflet for individuals aged 50 years and over are available to order for free or download in the following languages and formats:

English, English large print, Albanian, Arabic, Bengali, Brazilian Portuguese, Bulgarian, Chinese, Estonian, Farsi, Greek, Gujarati, Hindi, Latvian, Lithuanian, Panjabi, Pashto, Polish, Romanian, Romany, Russian, Somali, Spanish, Tagalog, Turkish, Twi, Ukrainian, Urdu and Yiddish.

A Braille version is available to order.

A British Sign Language (BSL) video of this leaflet is available to view or download.

An audio version of this leaflet is available to download.
