Details

This coronavirus (COVID-19) rapid nose-only test comes in a blue, white and grey pack with the MP Biomedicals brand on it.

This guide tells you how to:

  • prepare the test
  • take a swab from yourself or a child
  • process the sample
  • read and report the result

If you need a test kit, order rapid lateral flow tests.

There’s separate information on how to do a PCR home test. This is the type of test you usually do if you have COVID-19 symptoms.

Find help to do the test and report your result

To watch a video demonstration and for help with reporting results, see how to do a COVID-19 rapid test at home.
