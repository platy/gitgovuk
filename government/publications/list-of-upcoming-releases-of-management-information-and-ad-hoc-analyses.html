Details

This document provides details of upcoming releases of Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) management information and ad hoc analyses.

These are published in the interests of transparency and are not part of our regular Official and National Statistics publications.

Published 11 October 2018
Last updated 23 March 2022 + show all updates
  1. 23 March 2022

    Advance notice that a release of DWP management information about a State Pension Legal Entitlements and Administrative Practices (LEAP) exercise will be published on Thursday 24 March 2022.

  2. 17 February 2022

    Advance notice that a release of COVID Local Support Grant management information for 17 April to 30 September 2021 will be published at 9:30am on 24 February 2022.

  3. 7 December 2021

    Advance notice that a new release of information relating to a Personal Independence Payment (PIP) administrative exercise (with data at 1 November 2021) will be published at 9:30am on 14 December 2021.

  4. 20 October 2021

    Corrected the date of publication of the release of DWP management information about a State Pension Legal Entitlements and Administrative Practices (LEAP). This will be published at 9:30am on 22 October 2021.

  5. 19 October 2021

    Advance notice that a release of DWP management information about a State Pension Legal Entitlements and Administrative Practices (LEAP) exercise will be published at 9:30am on 28 October 2021.

  6. 26 July 2021

    Advance notice that a release of information about the number of outstanding Employment and Support Allowance (ESA) claims at 31 May 2021 will be published at 9:30am on 27 July 2021.

  7. 18 February 2021

    Advance notice that a new release of information relating to a Personal Independence Payment (PIP) administrative exercise (with data at 17 January 2021) will be published at 9:30am on 25 February 2020.

  8. 30 April 2020

    Announced for publication on 5 May 2020 a release of management information providing daily counts of Universal Credit declarations (claims) and advances from 1 March to 28 April 2020.

  9. 5 September 2019

    Announced for publication in the week beginning 9 September 2019 the ad hoc release 'Informal carers and employment: summary report of a systematic review'.

  10. 26 June 2019

    Advance notice that a new release of information relating to the Personal Independence Payment (PIP) administrative exercise: progress on cases cleared (data at 14 June 2019) will be published at 9:30am on 4 July 2019.

  11. 3 June 2019

    Advance notice that new analysis relating to State Pension age changes from the 1995 and 2011 Pensions Acts will be published at 9:30am on 7 June 2019.

  12. 28 February 2019

    Announced for publication on 6 March 2019 the management information 'Number of parents on DWP benefits and with ongoing child maintenance liabilities and/or arrears'.

  13. 27 February 2019

    Announced for publication on 28 February 2019 the ad hoc release 'Mixed age couples: benefit impacts of ending access to Pension Credit and pension age Housing Benefit'.

  14. 20 February 2019

    Announced for publication on Thursday 21 February 2019, the ad hoc release 'Employment and Support Allowance underpayments: forecast numbers affected, forecast expenditure and progress on checking'. This provides an update to the previous release of this information.

  15. 11 February 2019

    Announced for publication on Thursday 14 February 2019, the ad hoc release 'Estimated cost of uprating State Pension in frozen rate countries'.

  16. 17 December 2018

    Announced for publication on Thursday 20 December 2018 of the ad hoc release 'Personal Independence Payment (PIP) administrative exercise'.
