1. Rule requirement

1.1 Architectural compliance

ICT shall conform to all technical architectures developed by the relevant authorities using the system of systems approach (SOSA).

Projects shall obtain compliance approval from each of the relevant Domain Authorities in accordance with JSP 604 part 1 section 2 – enterprise architecture.

ISS DESIGN Arch is the C4 domain architect and as such will review from an end-to-end perspective.

2. Rule rationale

Defence has a vision of what the future ICT capabilities should look like and needs to make cognisant decisions to converge towards the future state.

An architectural approach to engineering ICT solutions is fundamental to deliver this future state. The Defence Information Reference Model (DIRM) provides the MOD with an Enterprise Architectural approach to ICT solutions.

3. Who to contact

For all queries, email ISSDes-APM@mod.gov.uk

4. Rule requirements: process

4.1 Architectural compliance

Initial gate

Projects shall engage with the ISS DES Arch and relevant domain Architecture authority to determine compliance requirements. Projects shall document within the draft SRD the relevant domain architecture requirements.

Main gate

Projects shall engage with the ISS DES Arch and relevant domain Architecture authority to determine compliance requirements.

Projects shall document within the SRD the relevant domain architecture requirements.

PDR

Projects shall obtain acceptance of their ICT preliminary design from the relevant domain architecture authority.

CDR

Projects shall obtain acceptance of their ICT design from the relevant domain architecture authority.
