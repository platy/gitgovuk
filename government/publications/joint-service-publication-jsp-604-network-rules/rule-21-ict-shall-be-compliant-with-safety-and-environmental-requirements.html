1. Rule requirement

  1. Safety and Environmental 1.1 ICT shall comply with the respective Business areas Safety Management System.

IMPORTANT: The DAIS cannot make any assessment of Projects compliance to Safety Policy. It is the project managers responsibly to ensure compliance has been met.

2. Rule requirement: process

  1. Safety and environmental

Initial gate

Projects shall state that safety is being considered throughout the development of the capability.

Main gate

Projects shall state that safety is being considered throughout the development of the capability.

PDR

Projects shall state that safety is being considered throughout the development of the capability.

CDR

Projects shall state that safety is being considered throughout the development of the capability.

TRRA

Projects shall provide a statement that they comply with all relevant Safety Policy and processes with respect to the relevant business area.
