Details

The FSCS Annual Report and Accounts 2021/22 provides details on the overall performance of FSCS over the past financial year and much more. Each year, FSCS also publish Class Statements. This is a separate report which summarises the source of funds from levy payers by class. It allows levy payers to follow levies set at the beginning through to the end of the financial year. 

Both documents can be found on the FSCS website.
