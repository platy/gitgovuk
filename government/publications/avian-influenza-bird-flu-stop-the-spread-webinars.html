Details

Due to the current avian influenza (bird flu) outbreak, maintaining effective biosecurity has never been more critical.

These webinars will help you find out what you can do to keep your birds free of the disease.

For further information on the latest situation and biosecurity guidance, see the avian influenza guidance.
