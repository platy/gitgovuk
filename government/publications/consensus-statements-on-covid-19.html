Details

These consensus statements on coronavirus (COVID-19) are shared with acknowledgment to SPI-M-O, who have developed and shared modelling methodologies and contribute model outputs to these combined estimates.

Consensus statements are an assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, the UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) updates its advice accordingly.

These papers contain estimates of the reproduction number (R) and growth rate. See the latest R value and growth rates, and background information. The devolved administrations publish estimates for their own nations.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on contributors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
