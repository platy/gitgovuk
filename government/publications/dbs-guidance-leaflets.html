Details

These leaflets provide information relating to eligibility and other Disclosure and Barring Service products and services.

Please note, we are currently in the process of redesigning these leaflets to ensure they meet current accessibility regulations. If you need an accessible version in the interim, please email us at communications@dbs.gov.uk

If you have any feedback relating to these leaflets, or would like a certain topic to be covered in future publications, please email us at DBSleaflets@dbs.gov.uk

Any other DBS queries should be sent to customerservices@dbs.gov.uk
