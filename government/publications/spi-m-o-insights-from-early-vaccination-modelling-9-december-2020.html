Details

Insights from early vaccination modelling prepared by SPI-M-O on the importance of a high vaccination uptake. It was considered at SAGE 72 on 10 December 2020.

The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper discusses the ways in which vaccinations can be used to protect the most vulnerable groups before non-pharamecurtical interventions (NPIs) can be reduced. This paper also contains projections for the impact on the population with varying measures of NPIs.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
