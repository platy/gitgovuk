The incredible hard work and resilience shown by our staff keeping prisons running across the country is of huge credit to us as an organisation.

The job that they do to protect the public and support offenders to live crime free lives is unique and comes with huge challenges – challenges that have been exacerbated since the beginning of the COVID-19 pandemic.

The Prison Service is a great place to work, and it provides the opportunity for both a long and fulfilling career doing a job that really matters. Our employment offer to staff goes beyond salaries, covering a range of aspects from career progression to schemes that save you money on your weekly shopping.

This document brings together all parts of our employment offer, and outlines some of the brilliant work that is underway across HMPPS & MoJ to enhance our offer further.

I hope it acts as a useful resource that genuinely supports Prison Service employees both inside and outside of the workplace, and that it recognises the outstanding work that our staff deliver, behind closed doors, on a day-to-day basis.
