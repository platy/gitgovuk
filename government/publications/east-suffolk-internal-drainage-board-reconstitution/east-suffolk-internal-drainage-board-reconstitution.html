The Environment Agency gives notice that it has submitted to the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs, for confirmation a scheme under Section 3 of the Land Drainage Act 1991 making provision for the following matters:

  • the reconstitution of the East Suffolk Internal Drainage Board to reduce the number of elected members of the Board from 11 to 8
  • to change the name of the Board to the East Suffolk Water Level Management Board
  • matters supplemental to or consequential on that reconstitution

A copy of the scheme is available for inspection upon request by email, post or by telephone.

In view of restrictions due to COVID-19, inspections at office buildings are not currently available but we can provide hard copies by post on request.
