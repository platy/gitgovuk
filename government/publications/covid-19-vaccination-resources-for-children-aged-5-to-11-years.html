A guide for parents of children aged 5 to 11 years of age at high risk

This leaflet should be given parents and carers of eligible children prior to the vaccination appointment. Paper copies of this leaflet are available to order.

What to expect after your child’s COVID-19 vaccination

This leaflet is for healthcare practitioners to give to parents and carers of eligible children after their vaccination. It provides information on what to expect after vaccination. Paper copies of this leaflet are available to order.

Resources for children and young people aged 12 to 17 are available to download and order.
