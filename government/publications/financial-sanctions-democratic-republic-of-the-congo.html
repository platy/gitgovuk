Details

The Democratic Republic of the Congo (Sanctions) (EU Exit) Regulations 2019 ensure that sanctions measures relating to the Democratic Republic of the Congo (â€˜the DRCâ€™) are implemented effectively after the UK leaves the EU.

Context

This sanctions regime gives effect to the UKâ€™s obligations under United Nations Security Council Resolution 1807 (2008) and aims to promote:

  • the resolution of the armed conflict in the DRC

  • respect for human rights

  • compliance with international humanitarian law

  • respect for democracy, the rule of law and good governance in the DRC

UK regulations

  • The Democratic Republic of the Congo (Sanctions) (EU Exit) Regulations 2019

  • The Sanctions (EU Exit) (Miscellaneous Amendments) Regulations 2020

  • The Sanctions (EU Exit) (Miscellaneous Amendments) (No.3) Regulations 2020

  • 5.07.2012 The Democratic Republic of the Congo (Asset-Freezing) Regulations 2012 (SI 2012/1511) (applicable prior to 31.12.2020)
