Details

This release contains data about the Environmental Monitoring for Health Protection (EMHP) wastewater programme.

The data covers the period 1 June 2021 to 7 February 2022.

This includes data on the concentration of SARS-CoV-2 RNA in wastewater sampled from sewage treatment works and the number of Omicron variant detections in wastewater sampled from sewage treatment works and sewer network sites, as part of the EMHP National Monitoring programme.
