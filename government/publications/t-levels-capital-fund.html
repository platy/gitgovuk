Details

The T Levels capital fund is available to support eligible further education providers delivering T Levels from September 2023 onwards.

The fund is formed of 2 parts:

  • the building and facilities improvement grant which providers will need to bid for
  • the specialist equipment allocation which will be awarded to providers using a formula allocation (provided they have not previously received funding for the particular route/s)

The T Levels capital fund guidance provides an overview of the fund and how to apply for the building and facilities improvement grant to support T Level delivery for the academic year 2023 to 2024.

Providers delivering T Levels in earlier waves will also be able to bid for funding to support the delivery of the new T Level routes, or existing routes for which they have not previously received capital funding.
