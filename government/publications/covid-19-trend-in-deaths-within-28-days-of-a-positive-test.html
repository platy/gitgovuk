The analysis provides further detail to data available from the GOV.UK COVID-19 in the UK dashboard. It uses data for the London region from 15 March 2020 to 12 January 2021, with 7-day rolling age-standardised rates per 100,000 population.

Confidence intervals (95%) are supplied to show statistically significant differences.
