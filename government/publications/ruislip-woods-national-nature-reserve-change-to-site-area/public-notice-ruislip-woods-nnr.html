Wildlife and Countryside Act 1981

Declaration of National Nature Reserve

Greater London Authority Area

Notice is hereby given that:

In pursuance of section 35(1) of the Wildlife and Countryside Act 1981 and all other powers enabling them in that behalf, Natural England has, by the Ruislip Woods National Nature Reserve No.02 Declaration 2022 made on the 21st day of March 2022, declared the land referred to therein a National Nature Reserve.

The land having a total area of 11.98 hectares or thereabouts, situated in Hillingdon London Borough and the Greater London Authority Area and shown single hatched and shaded yellow on the maps attached to the said Declaration, is held by London Borough of Hillingdon, a body approved by Natural England, and is being managed as a nature reserve.

Natural England is satisfied that the said land is of national importance.

The declaration and associated maps may be viewed on Natural England’s website at: https://www.gov.uk/government/collections/national-nature-reserves-formal-notice-of-new-or-extended-sites

Alistair Helliwell, Area Senior Adviser, National Nature Reserves,
on behalf of Natural England.
