Details

An assessment of the effect that changes made to higher education may have on groups with particular protected characteristics. Part 1 covers the impacts of changes to the HE funding and finance system. Part 2 covers impacts potentially arising from proposed higher education reforms which include student number controls, minimum eligibility requirements and foundation years.
