Details

This Local Supported Employment (LSE) guidance sets out the:

  • background
  • payment model
  • grant application process and requirements
  • management information (MI) reporting requirements

LSE is aimed at helping adults with learning disabilities, autism or both to move into competitive employment providing the support they need to maintain that employment.
