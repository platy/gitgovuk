Four reports are being published by UKHSA on influenza surveillance and vaccine uptake:

  • Surveillance of influenza and other respiratory viruses in the UK: Winter 2021 to 2022
  • Seasonal influenza vaccine uptake in GP patients in England: winter season 2021 to 2022
  • Seasonal influenza vaccine uptake in frontline healthcare workers in England: winter season 2021 to 2022
  • Seasonal influenza vaccine uptake in school age children: winter season 2021 to 2022

The following post holders will be given pre-release access up to 24 hours prior to release. Statistical staff and those involved in the production and quality assurance of the document are excluded.
