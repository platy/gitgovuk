Four reports are being published by Public Health England (PHE) on influenza surveillance and vaccine uptake:

  • Surveillance of influenza and other respiratory viruses in the United Kingdom: Winter 2019 to 2020
  • Seasonal influenza vaccine uptake in GP patients in England: winter season 2019 to 2020
  • Seasonal influenza vaccine uptake in healthcare workers in England: winter season 2019 to 2020
  • Seasonal influenza vaccine uptake in children of primary school age: winter season 2019 to 2020

The following post holders will be given pre-release access up to 24 hours prior to release. Statistical staff and those involved in the production and quality assurance of the document are excluded.
