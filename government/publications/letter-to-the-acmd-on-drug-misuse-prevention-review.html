The letter from the Minister for Crime and Policing Probation, the Rt Hon Kit Malthouse MP, asks the ACMD to undertake a Prevention Review, which will look at preventing initial drug use in vulnerable groups and reducing escalation on to dependence on drugs.

The Home Office 10-year drug strategy was published on 6 December 2021.
