Introduction

We are offering schools and colleges (settings) a grant to pay for senior mental health lead training.

If you have already confirmed your eligibility and registered for a grant, you need to tell us which Department for Education (DfE) quality assured course you have booked to receive payment.

Your grant will be paid following a satisfactory review of the information provided in the ‘senior mental health lead training grant: confirmation of booking quality assured training’ form, which this guidance helps you to complete.

Accessing the form

You must complete this confirmation of booking quality assured training form so we can authorise payment of your grant.

You can only access this form if you have already completed the grant application form.

When to complete your submission

You must complete this form within 3 weeks of the date you received your notification email from the department.

After this date, your grant place will no longer be reserved and you will need to submit a new grant application form, subject to the availability of places.

Before you start

Make sure you have:

  • read the accompanying published senior mental health lead training grant funding guidance and reflected on the learning outcomes
  • read this guidance document
  • a DfE Sign-in account
  • evidence of the DfE approved course you have booked ready to upload, and information about your booking (see ‘confirm training booked’ section below) - this could be a scanned copy, a photograph of your confirmation email, or an invoice from your training provider

Log in to your DfE Sign-in account:

Please try your DfE Sign-in credentials as soon as possible, to avoid any issues with submitting your form.

  1. Access the senior mental health lead training confirmation of booking quality assured training form.
  2. Log in to DfE Sign-in using your username and password.
  3. If you are part of an FE college and have more than one eligible campus, please choose the individual campus you want to complete the form for.
  4. The next page will display the details we hold about the organisation you selected when you logged in. This will show the name, address, and Unique Reference Number (URN) or UK Provider Reference Number (UKPRN) of the organisation, as well as the name and email address of the person who logged in via DfE Sign-in.
  5. If these details are correct, select ‘continue’ to move to the next page
  6. If you believe they are incorrect or you are experiencing issues accessing the form, please complete the ESFA enquiry form and we will investigate.
  7. Complete the form as instructed in the following sections.

For further education (FE) providers with individual campus IDs, you will need to select the campus ID which you are registering interest for.

Your account must be connected to the organisation you are completing the form for.

You do not need to add any services in your DfE Sign-in account. It is used for authentication only, and the form will not appear in your list of services.

Once you have started the form you must complete in a single session. If you are inactive for more than 15 minutes, you will need to start again.

 If you need further assistance 

If you are having problems using the DfE Sign-in service, use our DfE Sign-in help service. You will be able to request further assistance if required. When raising a support request, please select ‘other.’

If you have problems accessing the form, please contact us.

 Completing the form

Selecting ‘continue’ on each page will take you through the pages outlined below.

 Introduction page

This is a read-only page which explains the purpose of the form.

 Your details page

This page will display the details we hold about the organisation you selected when you logged into DfE Sign-in.

Your current approach to mental health and wellbeing page

You will be asked several questions relating to your setting’s senior mental health lead, including other role(s) they may have. You must select as many roles that apply from the checklist provided.

Confirm which quality assured course you’ve booked page

In this section you are asked to provide information about the DfE quality assured course you have booked, including:

  • how you found this course
  • the course code (which will automatically populate the course title and training provider)
  • start and end dates
  • cost

Upload evidence of your booking for quality assured training page

Next, you will need to upload evidence of your booking of a DfE quality assured training course.

This could be a scanned copy, photograph, or attachment of:

  • an invoice from your training provider(s)
  • a confirmation email from your training provider(s)

You must upload at least one file (file 1).

You can submit a maximum of 5 files, up to a total of 10MB each. To do this, select ‘choose file’ in each case and navigate to the document on your own computer to upload it. You will see confirmation on screen that the file has been successfully added.

If you attempt to upload a file type which is not supported, you will receive an error message showing the types which can be uploaded.

Your grant funding page

This page tells you the amount that will be paid to you by Education Skills and Funding Agency (ESFA), subject to verification of the information and evidence provided in this form.

Summary page

You will then be asked to check your answers before submitting your claim.

If you would like to make any changes, use the ‘change’ link to amend individual data entries.

You will also be presented with several declarations.

  You will not be able to submit your form without agreeing to these declarations.

After completing the checklist of declarations, you should select the ‘accept and send’ button. You will then see the confirmation page.

 Confirmation

The final page confirms your submission relating to the senior mental health lead training grant and will provide a unique reference number.

You will also receive an email which includes your claim reference number.

Enquiries

If you have any questions about this guidance or completing the form, please contact us via the ESFA enquiry form.

When completing the enquiry form, select ‘other’ as your query type.

What happens next 

Your confirmation email contains the details of your submission and instructions for what happens next.

If you do not receive a confirmation email please contact us via the ESFA enquiry form.
