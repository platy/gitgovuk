Details

Use the ‘Vulnerable children and young people survey: waves 27 to 31’ document for the most up-to-date figures. Figures may differ to previously published documents due to late submissions and corrections to the data returned. Older documents have been included as they contain analysis of the open text questions which is not repeated in ‘Vulnerable children and young people survey: waves 27 to 31’.

These documents will help local authorities, government departments, children’s services (ADCS), local government association (LGA) and charities work with relevant agencies to plan their services.

The analysis covers contact with children supported by:

  • the local authority
  • children’s social care
  • children’s social care workforce
  • cost pressure and system pressures

These documents are planned to be published on a monthly basis until further notice.
