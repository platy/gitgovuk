Details

These guides are for education professionals including practitioners and system leaders with an interest in the these themes. They are the views of senior stakeholders, project leads, practitioners and professional beneficiaries. The themes covered include:

  • careers education
  • teacher recruitment, retention and workforce development
  • health and education
  • early years
  • place-based working

Delivery plans for the 12 opportunity areas.
