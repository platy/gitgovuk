Details

If you are a commercial specialist and you want to enter the Government Commercial Function, you will undergo 1 day of classroom based tests and training.

This is at the Commercial Assessment and Development Centre (ADC). Its purpose is to recruit commercially astute professionals into the Government Commercial Function.

This brochure shows you what to expect on the day. It also covers why the Centre is important for government.
