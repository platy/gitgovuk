Details

Letters from the Culture Secretary to the BBC and S4C, requesting specific financial information, as required under the BBC’s Royal Charter, to begin the process to agree the appropriate level of the licence fee from 2022 and to ensure a settlement which offers the best value for money for licence fee payers.
