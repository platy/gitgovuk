1. River Arun: annual governance and accountability return for the year ended 31 March 2021

Sections 20(2) and 25 of the Local Audit and Accountability Act 2014

Accounts and Audit Regulations 2015 (SI 2015/234)

Section Notes
1. The audit of accounts for River Arun internal drainage board for the year ended 31 March 2021 has been completed and the accounts have been published. This notice and sections 1, 2 and 3 of the annual governance and accountability return (AGAR) must be published by 30 September. This must include publication on the smaller authority’s website. The smaller authority must decide how long to publish the notice for the AGAR and external auditor report must be publicly available for 5 years.
