    The service availability message has been updated for Postal Operators moving goods between Great Britain and the EU.

    HMRC's technical team are working to resolve the issues with the goods vehicle movement service as quickly as possible. If you do not have a goods movement reference, you can now proceed with alternative documentation, for example a transit accompanying document (TAD), movement reference number, entry reference number or declaration unique consignment reference (DUCR).
