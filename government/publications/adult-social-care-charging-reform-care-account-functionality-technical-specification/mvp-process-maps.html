The process maps below accompany the MVP software requirements specification document. They show the anticipated processes for an individual’s user journey, including:  

  • set-up of their care account
  • provision of ongoing care
  • care account review, needs review and financial review
  • annual changes to rates
  • short-term or one-off variations
  • changes of circumstance

Like the user stories (available in the accompanying spreadsheet User stories and data points), the process maps include non-mandatory elements which are not part of the requirements for the MVP.  These are shown in green text on a white background.
