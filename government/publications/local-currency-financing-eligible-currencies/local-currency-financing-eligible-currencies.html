2. Currencies considered case-by-case

The following currencies are considered for local currency financing on a case-by-case basis:

  • Armenia, Armenian dram
  • Bahrain, Bahraini dinar
  • Benin, CFA franc
  • Burkina Faso, CFA franc
  • Cameroon, CFA franc
  • Colombia, Colombian peso
  • Cote d’Ivoire, CFA franc
  • Croatia, Croatian kuna
  • Egypt, Egyptian pound
  • Georgia, Georgian Lari
  • Fiji, Fijian dollar
  • Jordan, Jordanian dinar
  • Nigeria, Nigerian naira
  • Pakistan, Pakistani rupee
  • Paraguay, Paraguayan guarani
  • Senegal, CFA franc
  • Tanzania, Tanzanian Shilling
  • Togo, CFA franc
  • Trinidad and Tobago, Trinidad and Tobago dollar
  • Turkey, Turkish lira
  • Uganda, Ugandan shilling

We are willing to consider other currencies subject to satisfying eligibility standards.
