Information and resources on this page are for FMS software providers only.

Academy trust finance automation: getting started

This guide:

  • provides FMS suppliers with quick links to register their software, access the developer hub and test environments
  • provides details on the process for updating suppliers with the most up to date academies chart of accounts (CoA)
  • provides links to CoA versions specific for supplier use which will be uploaded onto this page in March and July/August for the relevant accounts return and draft trust financial statements collection period
  • for the most recent chart of accounts available to trusts, users should visit [Academies chart of accounts and automating the accounts return] (https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/academies-chart-of-accounts) webpage

Academies chart of accounts 2021-22 - for 2021-22 AR Automation - v4.5.0 March 22

This spreadsheet includes all updates from the CoA 2020-21, and is available for the final testing by FMS suppliers of the 2021-22 automated accounts return.

Academies chart of accounts 2021-22 - for 2021-22 AR Automation - v4.2.0 March 22

The previous release of the CoA 21-22, which included all known updates from the CoA 2020-21 was made available for the initial testing by FMS suppliers of the 2021-22 automated accounts return. This is superseded by version 4.5.0, but remains available for your information.

Trusts who wish to find out about the CoA should visit the Academies chart of accounts and automating the accounts return webpage.
