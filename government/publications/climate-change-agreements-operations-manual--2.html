Details

This manual explains how to set up and manage an agreement, and how to report energy data against targets.

The associated spreadsheets provide example calculations and technical detail on case specific issues. They support the manual and help you calculate targets as requested within the CCA manual.

If you have a CCA and receive more than €500,000 a year in state aid, find out what information you need to report to HMRC and why.
