Background

1. The purpose of this memo is to inform you of the amendments that have been made to the Job Entry: Targeted Support (JETS) in Scotland Programme Provider Guidance chapter 3

2. In addition, this chapter has been revised to read ‘JETS in Scotland’.

Summary and action

3. Please ensure, with immediate effect, that the revised information in JETS in Scotland Programme Provider Guidance should be read in full and used by you and all of your supply chain partners.
