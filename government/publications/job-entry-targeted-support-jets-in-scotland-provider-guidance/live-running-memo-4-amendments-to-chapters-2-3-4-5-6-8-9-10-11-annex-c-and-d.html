Background

1. This memo is to inform you of the amendments that have been made to the Job Entry: Targeted Support (JETS) in Scotland Programme Provider Guidance chapters 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10, 11, annex C and D.

Summary and action

2. Please ensure that the revised information in JETS in Scotland Programme Provider Guidance is read in full and used by you and all of your supply chain partners with immediate effect.

Chapter Paragraph Title Summary of Change Date of Guidance Change Version Number
2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, 10 & 11 Throughout N/A Change in Guidance to state that Annexes are on Jagger. 09/07/2021 N/A
2 2.02 Participant Identification, Eligibility and Suitability Clarity regarding that Work Coaches have the final decision regarding eligibility of Participants. 09/07/2021 3

4 Actions bullet point 4 Action planning Additional wording regarding obtaining Participant signatures on Action Plans. 09/07/2021 3
5 5.25 Data Sharing between DWP and Providers Removal of this para as it is a duplicate of para 5.23. 09/07/2021 3
