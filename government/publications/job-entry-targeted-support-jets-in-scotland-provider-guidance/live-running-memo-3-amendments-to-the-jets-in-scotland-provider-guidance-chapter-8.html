Background

1. This memo is to inform you of the amendments that have been made to the Job Entry: Targeted Support (JETS) in Scotland Programme Provider Guidance chapter 8.

Summary and action

2. Please ensure that the revised information in JETS in Scotland Programme Provider Guidance is read in full and used by you and all of your supply chain partners with immediate effect.

Chapter Paragraph Title Summary of Change Date of Guidance Change Version Number
8 8.16 (New para) Payment Model and Outcomes An additional paragraph has been included around earnings from furloughed employment (for employment which started whilst on provision). 04/05/2021 3
8 8.23 (New para) Payment Model and Outcomes An additional paragraph has been included around the Self-Employment Income Support Scheme’ grant. 04/05/2021 3
