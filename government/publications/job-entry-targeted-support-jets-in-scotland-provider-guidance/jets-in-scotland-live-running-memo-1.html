Background

1. The purpose of this memo is to inform you of the amendments that have been made to the Job Entry: Targeted Support (JETS) in Scotland Programme Provider Guidance chapters 1, 2, 8 and 11.

2. In addition, all chapters have been revised to read ‘JETS in Scotland’.

For information and action

3. Please ensure, with immediate effect, that the revised information within the JETS in Scotland Programme Provider Guidance is read in full and implemented by you and all of your supply chain partners.
