Details

The MLA guidelines are currently being translated into a number of languages, including Polish and Turkish.

You can also find forms for confiscation and freezing measures provided in the EU-UK Trade and Cooperation Agreement (TCA).

We are aware these publications may have accessibility issues. We are reviewing them so that we can fix these.
