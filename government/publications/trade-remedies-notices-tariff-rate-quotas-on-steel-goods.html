Details

Tariff-rate quotas on steel goods were imposed by the EU on behalf of the UK and the other member states. The Secretary of State for International Trade transitioned some of the tariff-rate quotas to continue applying in the UK after the transition period.

Following a transition review, the tariff rate quotas were extended.
