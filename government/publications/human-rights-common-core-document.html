Details

The Common Core Document is part of all periodic reports from the United Kingdom to the United Nations, and is a snapshot of the constitutional structure of (and key statistics on) the United Kingdom, the British Overseas Territories and the Crown Dependencies.

Details on the Common Core Document can be found on the United Nations website.
