Details

The Secretary of State has written to bereaved families and survivors, and then to residents who live close to the tower. He said that out of respect for those who have not yet felt able to have their say, more time is needed for further conversations before a decision on the future of Grenfell Tower is taken. The views, questions and concerns already shared will still be taken into account. Those living, working or studying near the tower can continue to do so, as the tower is being kept stable and secure.

Details of what you can expect from these conversations will be publicised shortly.
