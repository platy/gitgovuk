Details

These privacy notices explain how MCA collects, uses and stores your personal information during certain activities, or when you use its services.

The MCA personal information charter explains more about your rights over your personal data.

Prior to 5 December 2021, the content of these notices was included within the MCA personal information charter where they are now listed instead.
