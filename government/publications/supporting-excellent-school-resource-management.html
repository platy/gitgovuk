Details

This document is for:

  • staff and leaders in schools and academy trusts, including:
    • executive leaders
    • school leaders
    • school business professionals
    • teaching and support staff
  • responsible boards, including:
    • governing bodies for local authority maintained schools
    • boards of charity trustees for academy trusts

It applies to all state funded schools in England, including:

  • local authority maintained schools
  • academies
  • free schools
  • voluntary aided and voluntary controlled schools
  • foundation schools
  • schools with a religious character
  • SEND and alternative provision settings
  • maintained nursery schools and early years settings within a school

‘School resource management: building a stronger system’ describes how we will work in partnership with the sector to help create a strong, robust and sustainable future schools’ system, by equipping every school and academy trust with the tools they need to get best value from all their resources.
