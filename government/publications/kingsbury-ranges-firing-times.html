Kingsbury Range Complex firing times 18 April to 6 May 2022

Details

The range danger area consists mainly of Kingsbury Woods and access to these is restricted to times when the range is not in use.

Do not enter the danger area when the range is in use, this is a danger to life. If ‘red flags’ or lights are hoisted, regardless of the timings, do not enter the training area. Learn more about accessing MOD training areas safely.

The full perimeter of the range danger area is signed to warn of danger.
