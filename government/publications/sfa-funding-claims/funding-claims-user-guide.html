Introduction

The aim of this document is to:

  • provide an overview of the funding claims process
  • provide detailed key user functionality
  • demonstrate key areas of the system
  • identify where and who to go for further help and support

How do I make a funding claim?

To get started:

  1. Go to Submit learner data
  2. Sign in to the service using your usual IDAMS log in details
  3. Choose ‘Funding Claims: Submit Funding Claims’ from the options on screen as highlighted in Figure 1.
  4. You will be taken to a summary page. If a funding claim is due, you will see a green button labelled ‘Review and Edit’ as highlighted in Figure 2. Click that button to get started.

Filling in the form

If you have contracts for both adult funding and 16 to 19 funding, then these parts of the form are separate, across 2 web pages. Both 16 to 18 and grant-funded adult funded providers have to submit a Year-end and Final Funding claim.

You can save the form to complete later at any time.

For Year-end funding claim:

We need you to supply up-to-date figures for delivery.

We will pre-populate the ‘delivery to date’ figures based on your Individualised Learner Record (ILR) and Earnings Adjustment Statement (EAS) data submissions. As a provider, you need to fill in the ‘forecasted delivery’ for each field for the rest of the year.

For the deliverable lines that are non-formula funded, e.g. community learning – programme funding, you will need to enter your actual delivery and forecast figures from your own records as these are not pre-populated from your ILR/EAS data submissions.

Contract for services funded providers are no longer required to submit a claim for learner support delivery as this can now be claimed through the EAS.

National skills fund has been included in the claim for grant-funded providers.

For examples of the claim forms with the columns we pre-populate and you need to enter, see figure 3.

For the Final funding claim you only need to agree the figures as we will use your Individualised Learner Record (ILR) and, if applicable your Earnings Adjustment Statement (EAS) data to pre-populate your actual delivery figures.

For the deliverable lines that are non-formula funded, for example community learning, you will need to enter your actual delivery from your own records as these are not pre-populated from your ILR/EAS data submissions.

For those providers that are paid on profile for your Advanced Learner Loans Bursary (ALLB) allocation, you will be required to complete a funding claim.

Figure 3: a funding claims form example for adult education budget (AEB).

Figure 3a: funding claims form – ALLB example.

You can save the form using the buttons at the bottom of the form.

If you have a 16 to 19 contract then this form will need to be completed. If you do not have a Adult Funding contract then when you open the submission form the 16 to 19 funding claim form will be displayed.

Figure 3b: 16-19 funding claim example

Errors

You won’t be able to submit a form until it’s clear of any errors. Errors will appear in red on the form where the problem is, as shown in figure 4. The wording in red will explain what the error is. As soon as you correct the errors, you will be able to continue.

Figure 4: an example of errors that stops you from submitting your form.

How to submit your claim

When you are satisfied with the figures on the form, select ‘Save and Continue’.

We will check your form, and if anything does not seem right we will display a warning message. When you see this message, you can choose to go back and edit your form or continue. An example of a warning message is in figure 5. A validation message will be displayed if we were expecting a figure but you did not supply a figure.

Figure 5: a validation message example

If you continue with your form, you will see a declaration which you need to read and agree to. Tick the check box to agree and then select ‘Confirm and Submit’ as highlighted in figure 6.

Figure 6: a declaration example

You will receive a confirmation message and an explanation of what happens next. See figure 7.

Once you have submitted your funding claims on Submit Learner Data, you will be able to view it on Manage Your Education and Skills Funding Service within 15 minutes.

Figure 7: the confirmation example

Submission reports

We will store all your submissions going forward. You can access them any time from the ‘Funding claim submissions’ page.

Select ‘View submission’ next to the submission report you want to view. You will then see a page that shows you the data you have previously submitted, such as in figure 8.

Figure 8: how to view your submissions on the ‘Your submissions’ page
