Firing times

There is no firing scheduled between 1 to 31 July 2022. The range is temporarily closed.

Usual firing timings

Monday to Saturday: 8:30am to 4:30pm
Sunday: 11am to 3:30pm
Public holidays: no firing

Your safety

For your own safety do not touch any military debris, it may be dangerous.

Although the dates and timings published are correct at the time of publication, they may change.

If red flags and/or lights are hoisted, regardless of the timings, do not enter the training area. This is a danger to life.

Learn more about accessing MOD training areas safely.
