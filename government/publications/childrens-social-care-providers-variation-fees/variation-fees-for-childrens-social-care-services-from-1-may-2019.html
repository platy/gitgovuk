Variation fees may apply if a provider wishes to makes changes after Ofsted has granted registration. A minor variation is a change that does not require an Ofsted inspection visit and a major variation would require a visit.

Apply to make changes to your conditions of registration or read guidance about changes.

Variation fees for children’s social care services

Establishment or agency Major fee Minor fee
Children’s home (1 child = 1 place) £910 £176
Multi-build children’s home (1 child = 1 place) £910 £176
Residential family centre with 3 or fewer places (1 family = 1 place) £881 £146
Residential family centre with 4 or more places (1 family = 1 place) £910 £146
Independent fostering agency £910 £176
Voluntary adoption agency with 7 or fewer full-time equivalent staff £734 £122
Voluntary adoption agency with 8 or more full-time equivalent staff £910 £122
Adoption support agency with 2 or fewer full-time equivalent staff £734 £122
Adoption support agency with 3 or more full-time equivalent staff £910 £122

Fees and legislation

These variation fees are set out in legislation:

  • Her Majesty’s Chief Inspector of Education, Children’s Services and Skills (Fees and Frequency of Inspections) (Children’s Homes etc.) Regulations 2018.

  • Her Majesty’s Chief Inspector of Education, Children’s Services and Skills (Fees and Frequency of Inspections) (Children’s Homes etc.) (Amendment) Regulations 2022
