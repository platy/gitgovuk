Hull and Immingham ports procedure change

From 31 July 2022, the procedures will change for Associated British Ports (ABP) at:

  • Hull
  • Immingham

The remote procedure currently used at these locations will remain in place.

Staff of the port or carrier will need to be authorised by the holder of the transit procedure to present the documents to UK Border Force for the:

  • office of departure process
  • opening of the Transit Accompanying Document (TAD)

The transit owner may be asked by the ABP or one of their carriers to provide paperwork which shows that the relevant staff are allowed to act on their behalf to start or end a transit movement.

If the paperwork is not provided the:

  • transit documents cannot be submitted to UK Border Force
  • TAD will not be created
