Changes to the XML channel to access the New Computerised Transit Service (NCTS)

Newsletter 7 – July 2021 gave some information regarding changes to the current XML channel and the introduction of a new XML channel.

The new channel will provide a more stable service and handle the expected increase in volumes as we move forward. We still plan to close the legacy XML channel on 30 November 2021 and would encourage you to check with your software provider, how they intend to manage the change to the new channel, if you have not already done so.

We are also reviewing the email channel usage and will share more information regarding the decommissioning of email once we are able.
