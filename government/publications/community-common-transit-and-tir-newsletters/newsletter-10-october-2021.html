1. Thamesport is changing its office number for transit

With effect from 18 October 2021, Thamesport will no longer be a sub office of Tilbury. From this date it will begin using its own reference number, GB000150 in respect of departures to, arrivals from and transits through Thamesport. For transit movements involving Thamesport you should cease using the existing GB000063 office code and start using the new GB000150 office code.

Movements that have been released and that are still in transit which contain the old Tilbury main office number (GB000093) will be unaffected and can be processed by Thamesport as normal.

The UK Customs Office List (COL) will be updated accordingly and will include a link to its listing on the Europa Customs Office List (COL).
