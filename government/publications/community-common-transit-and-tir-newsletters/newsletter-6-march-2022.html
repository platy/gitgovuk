Launch of new services for guarantee checking and New Computerised Transit System (NCTS) availability for traders

Guarantee Checking Service

New GOV.UK digital service is now available. It will give traders the ability to check their available guarantee balance.

To access the service, you will need to arrange for Government Gateway access, a relevant economic operator registration identification (EORI) number, your guarantee reference number and Access Code, to check your guarantee balance on GOV.UK.

You can find out more in HMRC services: sign in or register — GOV.UK (www.gov.uk).

New Computerised Transit System (NCTS) Service Availability page

The New Computerised Transit System (NCTS) to move goods, is also available, NCTS Service Availability page on GOV.UK gives both internal and external parties a real-time view of both NCTS XI and NCTS GB service.

The new automated NCTS Service Availability pages replace the existing static page on GOV.UK and will show business continuity messages if the NCTS service goes offline due to an unplanned outage. It will help reduce the need for traders to make inbound contact to NCTS helpdesk.
