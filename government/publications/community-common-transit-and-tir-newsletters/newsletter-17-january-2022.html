New Great Britain main offices for transit

We are introducing 6 new Great Britain (England, Scotland and Wales) main offices for transit usage in the New Computerised Transit Service (NCTS). The list of UK community and common transit UK offices has been updated accordingly and shows the transit roles offered by each office and the effective date from which they can be used within your NCTS declarations, as well as contact details and opening hours.

Find the updated UK offices list.

GB005170 — Killingholme

Killingholme is now a main office and can be used with immediate effect. It is no longer a sub office of Immingham. It offers office of departure, transit and destination roles.

Check the full details of the Killingholme office.

If transit movements start, end or transit through Killingholme, you should use GB005170 as the office number. You should no longer use the Immingham office number for such movements.

Scotland Frontier offices

With effect from 1 February 2022, we are reorganising Scottish ports. The existing main office, Scotland Frontier (GB000067), will be split into 5 new main offices, each with their own associated sub offices as given in the following table.

Common transit office number Location Office type: main (1), sub (2)
GB000053 Cairnryan 1
GB000053 Loch Ryan 2
GB000054 Glasgow Airport 1
GB000054 Prestwick Airport 2
GB000054 Troon 2
GB000054 Ayr 2
GB000054 Greenock 2
GB000054 Glasgow KGV Docks 2
GB000059 Grangemouth 1
GB000055 Aberdeen (including surrounding highland and island ports) 1
GB000055 Aberdeen Airport 2
GB000055 Inverness Airport 2
GB000055 Aberdeen Harbour 2
GB000058 Edinburgh Airport 1

Check the full details of these offices.

The main offices included in the table cannot be used within your NCTS declarations until 1 February 2022 onwards.

If transit movements start, end or transit through any of the Scottish ports listed in the table on or after 1 February 2022, you should use the appropriate office number. You should no longer use the Scotland Frontier office number (GB000067) for such movements. This office will remain live for a transitional period until 1 March 2022, but only to allow for any open movements to complete.

GB000150 — Thamesport main office

This is a reminder that Thamesport is now a main office and ceased to be a sub office of Tilbury on 17 October 2021.

If transit movements start, end or transit through Thamesport, you should be using its office number, GB000150, in your transit declarations. You should not be using the Tilbury office number.
