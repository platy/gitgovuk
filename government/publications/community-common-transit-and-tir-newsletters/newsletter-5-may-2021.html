Updates to the New Computerised Transit System (NCTS) online web portal

We updated you in our December Newsletter that we are making changes to the New Computerised Transit System free-to-use web portal. The phase 2 release for departure services will go live on 8 June.

You should complete Great Britain departure declarations and messages through the new service from 8 June.

You can still access messages that relate to departure declarations submitted before 8 June 2021 through the legacy web portal.

You will still receive the same support from us when using the new service.

If you have any questions please email:enquiries.importexporttransit@hmrc.gov.uk
