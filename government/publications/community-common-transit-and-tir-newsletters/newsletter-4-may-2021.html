Removal of legacy access to the arrivals portal – New Computerised Transit System (NCTS)

In Newsletter 5 - December 2020, we told you about the phased roll out of the updated service for the free-to-use NCTS web portal.

In Phase 1, we said submission of all Great Britain arrivals and unloading notifications would be made through the new service, which went live on 31 December 2020.

Now the new service is available, the legacy system will be decommissioned.

From 16 May 2021, we will remove access to the legacy arrival’s portal. All messages lodged after 16 May 2021 should be made through the new service. Any bookmarks or saved links you may have will no longer work.

We are doing this as the new service is the route for all arrivals and unloading notifications and we cannot continue to support the legacy service.

You will still be able to view messages for arrivals notifications that were already made using the legacy portal. This will be for up to 14 days from submission.

Make sure you use the new arrivals service as soon as possible.

You will still get the same support for issues you may have.

If you have questions send an email to: enquiries.importexporttransit@hmrc.gov.uk.
