Removal of Scotland Frontier main office (GB000067) from New Computerised Transit System (NCTS)

In Transit Newsletter 2 January 2022 we told you that we were reorganising Scottish ports and that as a result, Scotland Frontier main office (GB000067) would be removed after a transitional period.

GB000067 will become invalid from midnight 1 April 2022 at which point it can no longer be used within transit declarations. For transit movements to, from and through Scottish ports you should use one of the new main offices as listed in Newsletter 2 and the updated Community and Common Transit UK offices list.
