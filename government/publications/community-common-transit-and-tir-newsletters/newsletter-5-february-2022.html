Closure of the XML channel to access the New Computerised Transit Service (NCTS)

On 28 February 2022 we will begin the closure of the New Computerised Transit Service (NCTS) legacy XML channel.

This is the case for all Great Britain (England, Scotland and Wales) and Northern Ireland movements.

We have contacted all software providers with the details of how we are manging the closure of the legacy XML channel.

If you do not move to the new XML API channel you will not be able to make declarations using the legacy XML

The alternatives to the new XML API channel are the:

  • free-to-use HMRC online portal for Transit
  • existing email channel until 31 May 2022 as this will close after this date
  • standard Import and Export procedures to move your goods

You should contact your software provider to find out what this means for you.
