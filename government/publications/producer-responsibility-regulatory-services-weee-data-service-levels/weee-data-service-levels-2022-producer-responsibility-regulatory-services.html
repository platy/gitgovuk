This is a list of:

  • data input requirements
  • Environment Agency service levels (known as ‘outputs’)

It applies to:

  • electrical and electronic equipment (EEE) producers
  • producer compliance schemes (PCSs)
  • approved authorised treatment facilities (AATFs)
  • approved exporters (AEs)

1. Waste electrical and electronic equipment (WEEE) data service level: 2022

1.1 PCS market and waste tonnage quarter returns: 2022

Deadlines for submission are:

  • quarter 1: 30 April 2022
  • quarter 2: 31 July 2022
  • quarter 3: 31 October 2022
  • quarter 4: 31 January 2023

Analysis and validation is based on:

  • late or non-submission
  • repeat offenders
  • repetitive and late re-submissions
  • EEE put on market by PCS by category
  • WEEE collected from designated collection facility (DCF) by category
  • WEEE delivered to AATF or AE by PCS by category and operator
  • WEEE returned by distributors to PCS by category
  • WEEE taken back by PCS under Regulation 50 by PCS by category

Output: scheme submitted data

Public reporting method: summary of EEE placed on the market and WEEE collected.

Publication dates:

  • quarter 1: 1 June 2022
  • quarter 2: 1 September 2022
  • quarter 3: 1 December 2022
  • quarter 4 (interim): 1 March 2023
  • quarter 4 (final): 31 March 2023

Output: PCS 2022 obligation

Reports are available online within 1 week of the publication date.

Agreement to publish final obligation by 18 January 2023.

The final 2020 data re-submission cut-off date is 15 March 2023.

Publication dates:

  • issue 1: week starting 28 March 2022
  • issue 2: week starting 27 June 2022
  • issue 3: week starting 26 September 2022

1.2 AATF quarter returns: 2022

Deadlines for submission are:

  • quarter 1: 30 April 2022
  • quarter 2: 31 July 2022
  • quarter 3: 31 October 2022
  • quarter 4: 31 January 2023

Analysis and validation is based on:

  • late or non-submission
  • repeat offenders
  • repetitive and late re-submissions
  • WEEE received by AATF by category by PCS
  • WEEE received by AATF by category retained by DCFs (Regulation 53)
  • non-obligated WEEE received by AATF by category
  • WEEE delivered to ATFs by category and operator and PCS
  • WEEE reuse by category by PCS

Output: WEEE reported as received at AATFs

Non-obligated WEEE received by AATFs.

Public reporting method: summary of WEEE received.

Publication dates:

  • quarter 1: 1 June 2022
  • quarter 2: 1 September 2022
  • quarter 3: 1 December 2022
  • quarter 4 (interim): 1 March 2023
  • quarter 4 (final): 31 March 2023

1.3 AE quarter returns: 2022

Deadlines for submission are:

  • quarter 1: 30 April 2022
  • quarter 2: 31 July 2022
  • quarter 3: 31 October 2022
  • quarter 4: 31 January 2023

Analysis and validation is based on:

  • WEEE exported by AE by category by PCS
  • non-obligated WEEE exported by AE by category
  • WEEE mass balance gap analysis

Output: WEEE reported as exported by AEs

Public reporting method: summary of WEEE exported.

Publication dates:

  • quarter 1: 1 June 2022
  • quarter 2: 1 September 2022
  • quarter 3: 1 December 2022
  • quarter 4 (interim): 1 March 2023
  • quarter 4 (final): 31 March 2023

1.4 PCS declaration of compliance: 2022

Deadlines for submission: 31 March 2023.

Analysis and validation is based on:

  • WEEE treatment by PCS by category in line with obligation
  • compliance with targets

1.5 Producer compliance scheme (PCS) member registration: 2022

Deadlines for submission: 30 November 2021.

Analysis and validation is based on:

  • public register drop-off
  • volumes of EEE placed on the market, EEE category type and distribution of producers
  • monitoring through the freerider programme
  • PCS growth

Output: registration of EEE producers

Producer numbers (to individual PCS).

Valid producer updates received during and by the last day of the month will be processed and producer numbers allocated within 10 working days of receipt.

Small producers are not included in the public register until 10 February 2021.

Drop-off details are sent to the PCS on 2 March 2022.

Public reporting method: public register of producers.

Publication date:

  • 15 January 2022
  • 10th of every month thereafter

1.6 EEE small producer registration: 2022

Deadline for submission: 31 January 2022.

Analysis and validation is based on:

  • public register drop-off
  • size and type

Output: public register of producers

Public reporting method: public register of producers.

Publication date:

  • 10 February 2022
  • 10th of every month thereafter

2. WEEE data service level: compliance period 2023

2.1 PCS approval applications: 2023

The application period is open from 1 July 2022 to 31 August 2022.

Analysis and validation is based on conditions of approval.

Output: approval or refusal (to individual PCS)

Public reporting method: public register of PCSs.

Publication date:

  • 30 September 2022
  • manual update within 10 working days of approval or removal

2.2 AATF and AE approval applications

Deadline for submission: 30 September 2022.

Analysis is done within 12 weeks.

Output: approval or refusal

Public reporting method: public register of AATFs and AEs.

Publication dates:

  • 1 January 2023
  • 10th of every month thereafter

2.3 PCS member registration: 2023

Deadlines for submission: 30 November 2022.

Analysis and validation is based on:

  • public register drop-off
  • volumes of EEE placed on the market, EEE category type and distribution of producers

Output: public register of producers

Producer numbers (to individual PCS).

Valid producer updates received during and by the last day of the month will be processed and producer numbers allocated within ten working days of receipt.

Small producers are not included in the public register until 10 February 2021.

Drop-off details are sent to PCS on 2 March 2023.

Public reporting method: public register of producers.

Publication date:

  • 15 January 2023
  • 10th of every month thereafter

2.4 EEE small producer registration: 2023

Deadline for submission: 31 January 2023.

Analysis and validation is based on:

Output: public register of producers

Public reporting method: public register of producers.

Publication date:
