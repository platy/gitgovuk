Details

Paper prepared by Imperial College London on the B.1.1.529 Omicron variant of concern and hospitalisation risk. It was considered at SAGE 101 on 23 December 2021. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper should be read alongside the accompanying paper from SAGE 101:

  • University of Edinburgh, PHS and EAVE II: SARS-CoV-2 Vaccine Protection in Scotland: ​Omicron, 22 December 2021

See more information on what epidemiological modelling is and how it is used by government. As well as more information on SPI-M modelling and medium-term projections.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
