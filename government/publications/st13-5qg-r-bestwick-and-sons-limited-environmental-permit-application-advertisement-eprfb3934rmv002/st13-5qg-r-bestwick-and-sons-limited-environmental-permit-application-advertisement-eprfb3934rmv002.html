Details of the application

The Environment Agency has received an application to vary (change) an environmental permit under the Environmental Permitting (England and Wales) Regulations 2016 from R Bestwick and Sons Limited.

Application number

EPR/FB3934RM/V002

Regulated facility type

Metal recycling site: the application is to update the existing mixed metals permit (including End of Life Vehicle dismantling and Waste Electronic and Electrical Equipment storage) to modern conditions, which will include up to date EWC codes, with an annual throughput of 25,000 tonnes. The application also seeks to add a Household, Commercial and Industrial waste transfer activity including physical treatment, with an annual throughput of 25,000 tonnes

Regulated facility address

The Scrap Yard
Barnfield Industrial Estate
Leek
Staffordshire
ST13 5QG
