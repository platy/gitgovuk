Appointment of new contractor for the Grenfell Tower site

Details

The existing contract for the principal contractor of the Grenfell Tower site expires in July and we have appointed a new contractor to take over and continue essential safety and maintenance works. This update includes information about who the contractor is, what they will do and how they will work with the local community.
