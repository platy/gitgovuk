Details

The Social Housing Decarbonisation Fund (SHDF) will upgrade a significant amount of the social housing stock currently below Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) C up to that standard. It will support the installation of energy performance measures in social homes in England, and help:

  • deliver warm, energy-efficient homes
  • reduce carbon emissions
  • tackle fuel poverty
  • support green jobs
  • develop the retrofit sector
  • improve the comfort, health and well-being of social housing tenants

Eligibility

Local authorities, combined authorities and registered providers of social housing (including housing associations and ALMOs that are registered providers) can apply to Wave 2 of the SHDF for funding.

How to apply

The competition window for SHDF Wave 2 funding will open in late August / early September 2022, but you can begin preparing applications now.

Opportunities for input from registered providers of social housing

We are looking for volunteers to help test the SHDF Wave 2 application form ahead of competition launch.

We are holding an online testing session on Monday 13 June 2022, at 1.30pm. Please register your interest in attending at SHDF.enquiries@beis.gov.uk and we will contact you with further information.

The session is open to registered providers of social housing, including housing associations and ALMOs that are registered providers, and other local authorities and combined authorities.

Contact us

If you have any enquiries about SHDF Wave 2, please email SHDF.enquiries@beis.gov.uk.

Background

The fund follows on from:

  • SHDF Wave 1, which will see energy performance improvements to around 20,000 social housing properties, reducing bills and carbon emissions
  • the SHDF Demonstrator, where around 2,000 homes were improved to EPC Band C and around 1,200 local jobs were supported
