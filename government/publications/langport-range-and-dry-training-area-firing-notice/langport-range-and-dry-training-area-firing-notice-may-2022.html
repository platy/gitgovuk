Live firing (rifle shooting)

Live firing is permitted all year round during daylight hours, usually between 10am to 3pm, on the following dates:

  • 3, 4, 5, 7, 8, 10, 11, 12, 16, 17, 18, 19, 21, 25, 26, 28, 29, 30 and 31st May 2022.

A live firing extension has been granted for Thursday 5 May from 9am to 4pm

Night live firing is not permitted on this range.

Dry training

Dry training at Beech Wood involves blank ammunition and pyrotechnics, vehicles and helicopters during the following days:

  • No firing

Dry military training is permitted at all times of day and night, all year round. The use of blank ammunition and pyrotechnics is only permitted during the following periods:

Weekdays and Saturdays: 7.30am to 11pm
Sundays: 9am to 6pm

Your safety

Please ensure you adhere to all MOD warning signs and red flags, which indicate that live firing is taking place within the Range Danger Area.

If the red flags and/or lights are hoisted, regardless of the timings or published dates, do not enter the Range Danger Area as there is a danger to life.
