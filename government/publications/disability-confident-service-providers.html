Disability Confident service providers

This document gives the contact details of service providers who may be able to help businesses become Disability Confident employers.

Details

The document also indicates the services each provider offers, such as:

  • disability awareness training
  • inclusive and accessible recruitment processes
  • reasonable adjustments
  • accessible formats
  • support with undertaking a Disability Confident self-assessment
  • validation of Disability Confident self-assessments

The list is not exhaustive and does not signify an agreement with, or endorsement of, the organisations included. It is provided solely to help employers on the Disability Confident journey.

To be included on the list of service providers you must be a Disability Confident employer and have completed your self-assessment. To apply, email dwp.disabilityconfident@dwp.gov.uk.
