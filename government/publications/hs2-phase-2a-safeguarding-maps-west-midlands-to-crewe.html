Details

Safeguarding is an established part of the planning process, designed to ensure that land which has been identified for major infrastructure projects is protected from conflicting developments.

The maps included on this page are subject to the safeguarding directions issued on 23 June 2020.

Please be aware that some of these maps are very large and may take time to download depending on your internet connection and speed, and the hardware or device you are using.
