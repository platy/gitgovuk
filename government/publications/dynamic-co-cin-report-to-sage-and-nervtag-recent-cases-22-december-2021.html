Details

Update paper prepared by COVID-19 Clinical Information Network (CO-CIN) for SAGE and NERVTAG. This paper was available for participants to read at SAGE 101 on 23 December 2021, but not considered or discussed in the meeting. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This report is based on the recent wave of infections, identified as those admitted on or after 1 December 2021.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
