Course overview

This course is aimed at unit executives and Flight Commanders who will be supervising aircrew in flying roles. Applications from senior engineering and air traffic control officers are also appropriate. Please note this course contains discussion and images of recent aircraft accidents and incidents.

On successful completion of the course the following JPA competence will be recorded for Service personnel:

  • Flight Safety I Supervisors’ Course I Joint
  • Flight Safety I Human Factors Continuation Training
  • Flight Safety I Authorizers Course I Joint (if already completed FLAC previously.)

Personnel attending courses delivered remotely will not be entitled to Human Factors competencies.

Contractors and overseas applicants are to apply through International Defence training, all enquiries to 22Gp-IDT-Multi@mod.gov.uk.

Duration

3.5 days

Location

Centre of Air Safety Training, Defence Academy, Shrivenham.

Course validity

This course is valid for 5 years from the date of completion and is recorded for military attendees as a joint personnel administration (JPA) competency. Regulations on air safety training can be found in RA 1440: air safety training.
