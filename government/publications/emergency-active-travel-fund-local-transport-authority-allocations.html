Details

Final funding allocations for the local transport authorities of the active travel fund.

The grant funding supports local transport authorities with producing cycling and walking facilities. The funding is in 3 tranches:

  • tranche 1 supports the installation of temporary projects for the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic. This funding was announced by the Secretary of State for Transport on 23 May 2020 as part of the work to combat the COVID-19 pandemic
  • tranche 2 and funding for 2021 to 2022 (announced on 14 May 2022) supports the creation of longer-term projects ​
