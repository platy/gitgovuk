Details

Guidance for billing authorities

The guidance document provides details of the changes to the NNDR payment process for billing authorities.

NNDR payment process, for 2022 to 2023 for billing authorities and schools

This document confirms which NNDR payment process, for 2022 to 2023, applies to billing authorities and schools.

Claim template

Billing authorities who are implementing the new payment process must complete the template and return to ESFA via the NNDR service which will go live on 1 April 2022.

Form guidance

Guidance for billing authorities on how to claim via the NNDR service.

Schools by billing authority

This document details the schools that fall under the remit of each billing authority.
