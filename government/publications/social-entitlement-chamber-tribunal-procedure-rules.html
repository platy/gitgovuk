The Tribunal Procedure (First-Tier Tribunal) (Social Entitlement Chamber) Rules 2008 (as amended)

Ref: SI 2008 No 2685 (L 13) PDF, 412 KB, 24 pages

Details

Includes rules on how to appeal, how any hearing will be conducted, and how the tribunal reaches decisions.

The rules are used in the following tribunal cases:

  • Social Security and Child Support
  • Asylum Support
  • Criminal Injuries Compensation

These rules, in effect from October 2014, include changes made to the original rules since 2008.
