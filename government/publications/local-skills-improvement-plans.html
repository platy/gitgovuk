Local skills improvement plans

Statutory guidance for the development and review of local skills improvement plans (LSIPs).

From:
Department for Education
Published
1 August 2022

Applies to England

Documents

Local skills improvement plans

PDF, 299 KB, 37 pages

Details

This statutory guidance is for:

  • employer representative bodies (ERBs)
  • further education colleges
  • sixth form colleges
  • designated institutions
  • independent training providers
  • higher education institutions
  • mayoral combined authorities and the Greater London Authority
  • local authorities

It sets out:

  • the process for developing and reviewing an LSIP, which is led by the designated ERB and in which relevant providers must co-operate
  • the duties placed upon relevant providers once there is an approved LSIP in place

We will update this guidance in September 2022.

If you have any comments on this initial guidance, email LSIP.programme@education.gov.uk by Friday 2 September.
