Details

The government recognises that many stakeholders are facing significant disruption due to COVID-19. Therefore, the deadlines for responses to ten tax policy documents have been extended for three months, as reflected in this spreadsheet. More detail on our plans for previously announced tax policy documents can be found here.

This spreadsheet lists tax policy consultations and any resulting legislation.
