This is a list of test centres that carries out Individual Vehicle Approval (IVA).

You need to say which of these centres you want to use when you apply for an IVA test.

London and the South East

There are 4 test centres in London and the South East:

  • Gillingham
  • Southampton (Bitterne Road West)
  • Southampton (Empress Road)
  • Yeading

Gillingham

Ambley Road
Gillingham
Kent
ME8 0SJ

View on a map

IVA vehicle categories

It can test these types of vehicles:

  • passenger cars
  • vans and light goods vehicles (weighing up to 3,500kg)
  • lorries or large goods vehicles (weighing over 3,500kg)
  • buses and coaches
  • trailers

Southampton (Bitterne Road West)

Unit R Centurion Industrial Estate
Bitterne Road West
Southampton
SO18 1UB

View on a map

IVA vehicle categories

It can test these types of vehicles:

  • passenger cars - normal IVA only
  • vans and light goods vehicles (weighing up to 3,500kg) - normal IVA only

This centre cannot do basic IVA tests.

Weight restrictions at this centre

This centre cannot do IVA tests for vehicles weighing over 3,500kg.
