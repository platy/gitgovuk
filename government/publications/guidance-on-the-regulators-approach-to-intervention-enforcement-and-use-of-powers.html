Details

These documents sets out the Regulator of Social Housing’s general approach to intervention and enforcement. It presents the high-level objectives and principles to dealing with poor performance.

The guidance notes on this page set out further details on the use of each power.

To read about what providers can expect from the regulator and how we ensure that standards are being met, see our Regulating the Standards page.

Following a consultation in June-August 2019, this guidance on intervention, enforcement and use of powers was updated in December 2019. It reflects legislative changes, including the introduction of the Housing and Administration regime, the Co-operative and Community Benefit Societies Act 2014, and the move to a standalone status for the regulator.
