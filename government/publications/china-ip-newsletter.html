China IP Newsletter (English language)

Our China IP Newsletter is published in English and is intended to inform UK stakeholders of recent developments in the Chinese IP environment. The content is collected from publically available sources, where information is often available in Chinese-language only. To be added to/removed from the distribution list or to receive past editions please contact Leo Zhuang.

UK IP Newsletter (Chinese language)

We publish a UK IP Newsletter in Chinese to inform China-based stakeholders of recent developments in the UK/EU IP environment. The content is collected from publically available sources, where information is often available in English only. To be added to/removed from the distribution list or to receive past editions please contact Emily Tan.
