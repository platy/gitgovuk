1. Before you start

Your firm or chambers must register with Common Platform before you can use it.

Once registered, you will receive an email from noreply@cjscp.org.uk. Please check junk and spam filters for this notification.

Follow the link in the email to complete your one-time account activation and security set up.

You must activate your account before attending court for your first Common Platform hearing.

Once your organisation has registered and you have activated your account, you can start accessing cases in Common Platform.

2. Check in to the hearing

When you check in on the day you attend court, Common Platform will let others that you are attending and ready.

It also records your name against the court record for payment purposes, both for magistrates’ and Crown court.

You will still need to communicate with the court ushers regarding your case.

1. Before attending the hearing, log in to Common Platform.

2. From the home screen select ‘Check-in to hearings’.

3. Search for the courthouse and view the hearings listed for the day. Enter the court name and select the green ‘Show hearings’ button.

4. Expand any courtrooms by selecting the ‘-/+’ sign (if there is more than one Common Platform hearing). Check the box against the hearing that you are checking in for. Then select the green ‘Check-in’ button.

5. A confirmation of your successful check-in will appear at the top of the screen.
