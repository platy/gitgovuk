Details

Paper prepared by the UCL Collaborative Centre for Inclusion Health on COVID-19 in inclusion health populations. This paper was available for participants to read at SAGE 40 on 4 June 2020, but not considered or discussed in the meeting. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
