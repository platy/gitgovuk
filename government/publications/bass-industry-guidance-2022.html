Details

The Sea Fisheries (Amendment) Regulations 2022 retained and amended the Bass Measures 2020 as per Article 10 of Council Regulation (EU).

This means that revised measures on vessels catching European Seabass are to be introduced on 21 May 2022.

The bass control measures described implement measures designed to manage fishing effort to help protect and improve bass stocks.

Bass limits for 2022

The Sea Fisheries (Amendment) Regulations 2022: Increased trawls and seines, fixed gillnet and hook and line bass limits for 2022 which was one of the outcomes from the annual UK/EU fishing opportunities consultations in December.

Bass authorisations letters issued remain valid.

You should refer to the Bass Fishing Guidance 2022 for further detail.
