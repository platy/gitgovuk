Details

The National Child Measurement Programme (NCMP) is part of the government’s commitment to dealing with the public health challenge of excess weight.

The NCMP operational guidance, deliverable elements, information for schools and the pre-measurement letters for head teachers, primary care practitioners and parents have been revised and updated for 2022.

Useful resources for local authorities can be downloaded from the OHID campaign resource centre.
