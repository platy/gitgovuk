Document summary

A double-page spread with branded T Level information that you can include in your institution’s prospectus design.

Template is available in the following orientations:

  • A4
  • A5
  • 210mm square

If you need the T Level brand fonts, email support.deliveringtlevels@education.gov.uk.

Note: .idml and .indd files can only be opened in Adobe InDesign. If you want to see the design before sending it to a designer, you can download a preview of the prospectus template (PDF).
