Details

On 6 May 2021, local polls will take place, including local elections in England and Police and Crime Commissioner elections in England and Wales.

This document describes how they will proceed in the COVID-19 context, from announcement to results, and then covers the four major areas that we are addressing:

  • public health and social distancing
  • nominations and campaigning
  • voting
  • the delivery of elections
