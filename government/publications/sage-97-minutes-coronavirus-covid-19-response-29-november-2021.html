Details

This is a record of the discussion of SAGE 97 on 29 November 2021. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.

Redactions within this document have been made to remove any names of junior officials (under SCS) or names of anyone for national security reasons. SAGE 97 includes redactions of 19 junior officials.
