Details

This letter from the Department for Transport and industry informs recipients of the current HGV driver shortage and the existence of job opportunities.

The letter encourages those who have left the profession to return and seek employment as HGV drivers. It also explains that the government and logistics sectors are working on a range of solutions to ease the current HGV driver shortage.
