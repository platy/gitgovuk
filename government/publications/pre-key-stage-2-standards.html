Details

Teachers should use pre-key stage standards for those pupils working below the standard of key stage 2 but are engaged in subject specific study. The use of this guidance will further support teacher assessment judgments.

Where pupils are not engaged in subject specific study the engagement model should be used.

This guidance is available for use from academic year 2020-21 onwards.

Pre-key stage standards are for pupils who are working below the overall standard of national curriculum assessments, but who are engaged in subject-specific study.

Pre-key stage 2 standards for the 2020/21 academic year onwards

Teachers must use these standards to make statutory teacher assessment judgements at the end of key stage 2 for pupils who are working below the national curriculum tests and teacher assessment frameworks, and engaged in subject-specific study.
