The guidance for the coronavirus (COVID-19) 16 to 19 tuition fund 2022 to 2023 is now available.

Read the form guidance to find out how to opt in or out of receiving 16 to 19 tuition funding for 2022 to 2023 by completing the 16 to 19 tuition fund form.

The deadline to submit the form is 11:59pm on Friday, 27 May 2022.
