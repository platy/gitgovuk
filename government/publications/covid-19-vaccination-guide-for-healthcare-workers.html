Details

All frontline health and social care workers are being offered the coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccine which will help protect you against the COVID-19 disease.

This leaflet is for healthcare workers and includes information on the COVID-19 vaccine.

Copies of the English leaflet are available to order using product code COV2020316V2.

Translated copies are available to order and more languages will be available soon.
