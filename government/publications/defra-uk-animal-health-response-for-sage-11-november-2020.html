Details

Paper prepared by Defra on the UK animal health response. It was considered at SAGE 67 on 12 November 2020. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

An update of this paper has since been made available by UKHSA.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
