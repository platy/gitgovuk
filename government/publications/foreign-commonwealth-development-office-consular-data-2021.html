Details

The Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office publishes consular data on assistance provided to British nationals around the world. This data begins in September 2020, and is published monthly.

For the data covering January to August 2020, see the Foreign & Commonwealth Office (FCO) consular data for 2020.
