Details

To join the Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA) earned recognition scheme for vehicle operators, your systems and processes must be audited by a DVSA-authorised audit provider.

You’ll also be audited every 2 years after you’ve joined the scheme. You can choose to have your audit in person or by video call.

You might need to pay for these audits, depending on the provider you choose. Providers set their own fees.
