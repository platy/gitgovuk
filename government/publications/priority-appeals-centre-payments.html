Details

This guidance is for:

  • school and college senior leaders
  • all exam centres offering GCSEs, A levels, AS levels or vocational and technical qualification (VTQ) exams

It provides information about the claims process for priority appeal payments.

This is additional funding to schools, colleges and other exam centres, for teachers to process priority appeals for teacher assessed grades awarded in summer 2021.
