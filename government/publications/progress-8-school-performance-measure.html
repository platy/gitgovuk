Details

These documents explain how secondary accountability measures are calculated. They also give more information about announced policies that apply to accountability measures for the 2021 to 2022 academic year.

This information is for:

  • school and college leaders
  • governing bodies

It applies to:

  • local-authority-maintained schools
  • academies
  • free schools
  • further education colleges with 14 to 16 provision

See school and college performance tables.

See the KS4 qualifications, discount codes and point scores for the school performance table.
