A testing service for homecare workers

HTML

Details

Regular COVID-19 testing is available to all homecare workers in Care Quality Commission (CQC)-registered domiciliary care organisations.

Homecare agencies are responsible for ordering and distributing tests to all homecare workers for them to conduct at home on a weekly basis.

Regular testing for coronavirus is also now available to personal assistants supporting adults over 18 in England.

Guidance for homecare workers

Posters are also available for homecare workers in England on how to collect, complete and register test kits.
