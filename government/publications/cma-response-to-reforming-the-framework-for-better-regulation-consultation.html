Details

The CMA has responded to the Reforming the framework for better regulation consultation, led by The Department for Business Energy and Industrial Strategy (BEIS).

The CMA’s response highlights the importance of understanding markets and potential competition impacts throughout the policy cycle, drawing upon the CMA’s markets and advocacy work. The response also provides practical recommendations on a number of the proposals to improve the way regulation is assessed and scrutinised, in line with the principles set out by the consultation.

For queries relating to the CMA’s response, please contact the CMA advocacy team by email at advocacy@cma.gov.uk.
