Details

This impact assessment outlines the expected costs and benefits of the reforms set out in the Higher Education (Freedom of Speech) Bill. It has been updated to reflect amendments made to the Bill at Commons Committee stage to ensure that the strengthened freedom of speech duties apply directly to the constituent institutions of higher education providers. The previous impact assessment is available.

The Bill provides the legislative basis for the reforms set out in the Higher education: free speech and academic freedom white paper published in February 2021. More information is also available in the letter from the Secretary of State for Education.

The memorandum addresses issues arising under the European Convention on Human Rights (ECHR) in relation to the Bill.
