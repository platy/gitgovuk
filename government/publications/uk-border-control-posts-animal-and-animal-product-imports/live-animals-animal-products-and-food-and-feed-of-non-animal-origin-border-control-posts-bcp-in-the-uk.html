3. Approved categories key

3.1 Abbreviations for animals referred to in Annex 2 of Regulation (EU) 2019/1014

LA - Live animals

U - Ungulates other than registered equidae

E - Registered equidae

O - Other animals other than Ungulates (This abbreviation includes zoo Ungulates)

Specifications

(*) Suspension of BCP and CP as referred to in Annex 2 of Regulation (EU) 2019/1014

(1) See additional specifications

3.2 Abbreviations for products of animal origin, referred to in Annex 2 of Regulation (EU) 2019/1014

POA - Products of animal origin, composite products, germinal products, animal by-products, hay and straw

HC - Products for human consumption

NHC - Products not for human consumption

NT - No temperature requirement

T - Frozen/chilled products

T(FR) - Frozenproducts

T(CH) - Chilled products

Specifications

(*) Suspension of BCP and CP as referred to in Annex 2 of Regulation (EU) 2019/1014

(1) See additional specifications

(2) Packed products only

(3) Fishery products only

(4) Liquid in Bulk only

3.3 Abbreviations for goods of non-animal origin referred to in Annex 2 of Regulation (EU) 2019/1014

PNAO - Products of non-animal origin

HC (food) - Food of non-animal origin covered by conditions or measures referred to in Annex 2 of Regulation (EU) 2019/1014

NHC (feed) - Feed of non-animal origin covered by conditions or measures referred to in Annex 2 of Regulation (EU) 2019/1014

NHC (other) - Products of non-animal origin, which are neither food nor feed

NT - No temperature requirement

T - Frozen/chilled products

T(FR) - Frozen products

T(CH) - Chilled products

Specifications

(*) Suspension of BCP and CP as referred to in Annex 2 of Regulation (EU) 2019/1014

(1) See additional specifications

(2) Packed products only

(4) Liquid in Bulk only
