Details

The Rare and Imported Pathogens Laboratory (RIPL) provides medical and laboratory specialist services to the NHS and other healthcare providers, testing for a wide range of unusual and hazardous viral and bacterial pathogens.

The user manual contains information for clinicians and referring laboratories about the range of tests offered by RIPL, up-to-date pricing and specimen requirements.
