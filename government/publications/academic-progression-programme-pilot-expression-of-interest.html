Details

In September 2022, the Department for Education (DfE) will launch a pilot with 15 providers. The DfE is now accepting expressions of interest (EOI).

The attached documents set out:

  • an overview of the pilot programme, including the expectation of providers, funding, and target cohort
  • information on the eligibility criteria
  • how to apply, using the expression of interest form

The deadline for submitting an expression of interest is 11:59pm on 22 June 2022. Submissions after this time will not be considered.
