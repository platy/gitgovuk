Role Description

Packaging Design Engineer, responsible for supporting the ongoing operation of the LLW Repository fleet of radioactive waste transport packages, associated infrastructure and processes; for the specification, implementation and delivery of packaging design related work on a portfolio of new packaging projects and tasks, with support for general transport technical and licencing operations.

Duties

General

  • Delivering assigned work packages that meet company, legal, regulatory and business requirements.
  • Producing specifications for package design and associated engineering work packages.
  • Producing and reviewing package design drawings, supporting analysis and documentation.
  • Preparing and assessing payload restraint/waste loading proposals.
  • Producing package design safety reports and associated documentation in support of licensing activities.
  • Conducting engineering reviews to ensure ongoing regulatory compliance of the LLWR fleet of licensed transport packages.
  • Serving as point of liaison between external design organisations and suppliers for project and task deliverables.
  • Reviewing and sentencing technical queries, production permits and operational concessions.
  • Identifying appropriate design and engineering standards and practices to be used for projects.

Resource forecasting, demand, assignment and deployment

  • Supporting the Package Design Authority in developing effective design strategies and plans
  • Coordinating the deployment of design resources in line with agreed resource strategies

Cost & schedule

  • Establishing and maintaining control of the mechanical engineering baseline
  • Monitoring, reviewing and reporting on packaging design engineering progress

Supply Chain Acquisition

  • Serve as Intelligent Customer for the specification and acquisition of bought in design engineering and manufacturing services

Supply Chain delivery

  • Inputting to and reviewing work packages, tender selection, and tender submissions for procurement of packaging design, manufacturing and engineering services.
  • Specifying, overseeing and reviewing package design engineering work performed by supply chain contractors.

Essential Skills/Qualifications

  • Honour’s degree in Mechanical Engineering or equivalent.
  • Chartered (IMechE) or working towards Chartered status
  • Experience of design of packaging for the transport of radioactive materials
  • Working knowledge of the IAEA regulations for safe transport of radioactive materials
  • Ability to deliver high quality design and engineering documentation
  • High digital competence, with experience in common software
  • Good verbal and written communication skills

Desirable Skills/Qualifications

  • Experience of working in the nuclear industry
  • Working knowledge and experience of legislation, inclusive of Machinery Directive, PUWER, LOLER, etc.
  • Working knowledge of lifting equipment and associated design codes.
  • Experience working in the hazardous waste transportation, storage and disposal industry.
  • Proficiency in use of MS office suite, MathCAD, and Inventor software applications
  • Ability to effectively manage supply chain contractors

As of the 31 January 2022, Nuclear Waste Services has been set up to combine all of the NDA Waste activities, giving greater flexibility and driving improvements and synergies across the NDA Group. This brings together LLW Repository Limited, Radioactive Waste Management Limited and the NDA Integrated Waste Management Programme (each with their own IT teams, infrastructure, and systems).
