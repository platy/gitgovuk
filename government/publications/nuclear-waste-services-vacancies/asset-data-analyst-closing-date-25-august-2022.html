Background Information

As of the 31 January 2022, Nuclear Waste Services has been set up to combine all of the NDA Waste activities, giving greater flexibility and driving improvements and synergies across the NDA Group. This brings together LLW Repository Limited, Radioactive Waste Management Limited and the NDA Integrated Waste Management Programme (each with their own IT teams, infrastructure, and systems).

Role Description

Provide Asset Management and Asset Information support to the Lead System and Asset Manager. Act as the Subject Matter Expert for developing the LLWR Asset Information system using MS365 applications and other associated applications.

Duties

  • Create, develop and maintain an Asset Information System and supporting databases that combines System Engineer data into an efficient capability that will be easy to use for System Engineers for data analysis and from this system, develop and maintain KPIs that allow plant owners to make decisions based on data.

  • Support the System Engineers in producing electronic Asset Management Plans.

  • Co-ordinate and maintain with the System Engineers and Plant Owners the current LLWR Asset Risk Register.

  • Lead the development and maintenance of asset management statistics and risk appetite metrics on behalf of the Lead System and Asset Manager.

  • Represent the Lead System and Asset Manager at NDA Asset Management and Asset Information workshops, supporting any work required for and from the workshops.

  • Provide day-to-day management of the LLWR Asset Management and System Engineering processes, supporting the Lead System and asset Manager in their role as Process Owner.

  • Act as Subject Matter Expert on the use of MS365/SharePoint/MS Teams to the wider engineering and LLWR Site Teams

  • Provide support to the BIM-Co-ordinator in developing and maintaining the Common Data Environment and align with the Asset Information System.

  • Provide day-to-day ad-hoc support as needed to the Lead System and Asset Manager

Essential Skills/Qualifications

  • Relevant engineering, asset management, IT, business, or administration qualification.

  • Demonstrable experience and fluency in data engineering and modelling

  • Understanding of the MS365/MS Teams suite, with demonstrable experience in using this software.

  • Proven ability to manage and prioritise own workload against multiple stakeholders and competing priorities.

  • Can demonstrate having the ability to engage and impact on others and work effectively as part of a wider team.

  • Excellent verbal and written communication and presentation skills.

Desirable Skills/Qualifications

  • Knowledge of Asset Management Principles, PAS55 and ISO55000.

  • Experience in CMMS systems at superuser level

  • Experience of managing/leading change in an organisation.
