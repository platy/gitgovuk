Background Information

As of the 31 January 2022, Nuclear Waste Services has been set up to combine all of the NDA Waste activities, giving greater flexibility and driving improvements and synergies across the NDA Group. This brings together LLW Repository Limited, Radioactive Waste Management Limited and the NDA Integrated Waste Management Programme (each with their own IT teams, infrastructure, and systems).

Role Description

The Senior Planner will be embedded within the RDP Programme team and be responsible for driving planning excellence within the Programme to help achieve delivery. This is a busy and exciting time for the Programme as they prepare for a major transition point (Gate Review) to move into the major construction phase of the works. As the Senior Planner you will have full responsibility for the development and management of the project and programme schedules and ensuring they are appropriate for current and future stages of the project lifecycle. You will be a key part of the programme team in preparing and presenting information for Business Case submissions.

The role requires strong planning and scheduling skills, coupled with excellent behavioural skills to build strong relationships with the Programme team and to maintain the integrity of the programme. As the Senior Planner you will also be required to mentor and upskill less experienced team members.

There will be a strong internal support network to help deliver the role within a satisfying and rewarding team environment that maintains a positive culture and provides a rich environment for you to further develop and demonstrate your strengths. This is a fantastic opportunity to be part of a well-established and high performing team.

Duties

  • Provision of an effective planning and reporting service including build and maintenance of integrated resource loaded schedules, application of rules of credit where appropriate, and robust challenge to contractor progress submissions

  • Providing advice, specifying requirements and challenging inputs when building schedules on construction focused work

  • Development of schedules at the most effective level for differing requirements; including detailed working schedules and the ability to advise and implement roll up of these to a higher level where appropriate

  • Working within an NEC environment with the ability to challenge contractor submissions and maintain compliance within NEC arrangements with regards to scheduling

  • Working within a regulated environment and having experience of the requirements of working within a ‘gated’ project structure; and providing support to gate and sanction submissions

  • Ensure baseline adherence, demonstrate excellence in maintaining an accurate baseline position and be proficient in the development of baseline change proposals

  • Independently develop, manage and maintain significant programmes of work in accordance with LLWR established project controls framework through detailed volume production and the trending and change control process

  • Provide Earned Value Analysis, identify critical areas, trends and recommend recovery options, and contribute to clear and concise reporting

  • Provision of accurate forecasts for schedule performance within the financial year and project lifetime

  • Maintaining planning databases for areas of work, and ensuring validation and integrity of data across systems

  • Mentoring and training less experienced team members in all aspects of planning and scheduling

  • Provision of support in the application of risk processes and to the Project Teams, Risk Coordinator and Risk Manager in risk identification, reviews and workshops

  • Use technical expertise and knowledge to undertake SME reviews and provide advice and guidance to the Sanction Board, Programme Boards and Trend and Change Control Panel on gate reviews, sanction submissions and baseline management.

  • Produce resource forecasts and communicate these effectively with the programme management teams

  • Support the implementation of best practice throughout LLWR’s programme and project controls activities and share LFE across LLWR.

  • To be aware of and abide by the company’s Standards and Expectations

  • Awareness of the environmental issues associated with the role

  • To act in a secure manner at all times, recognise potential consequences of personal behaviour and say if others fall short with respect to security

  • To consider ED&I in all conversations and interactions, ensuring you treat all individuals equally, respectfully and in line with LLWR’s ED&I principles.

Essential Skills/Qualifications

  • Demonstrable experience of building, maintaining, and progressing schedules within a highly regulated industry

  • Demonstrable experience working in an NEC environment with the ability to challenge contractor submissions and maintain compliance within NEC arrangements with regards to planning and scheduling

  • Good relationship building skills with the ability to manage relationships with key clients or stakeholders within designated programme of work and across the organisation

  • Consistent performance under pressure

  • Self-motivated and able to work on own initiative with minimal supervision

  • Experience in Change control development

  • Flexible approach to work, willing to play a full role in ensuring the success of the team

  • Excellent communication skills (written and verbal)

  • Excellent planning and organisational skills

  • Ability to prioritise own workload to meet deadlines

  • Experience of using Primavera P6

  • Excellent IT skills (MS Office / M365)

  • Relevant experience in project controls (core discipline planning)

  • Minimum qualification in English and Maths (Grade C/5)

Desirable Skills/Qualifications

  • Experience of developing schedule and project controls information for programme sanctioning activities and gate submissions

  • Working knowledge of large Civil projects that includes earthworks

  • A passion for sharing knowledge and upskilling less experienced team members with regards to Project controls and Planning and Scheduling

  • Spreadsheet manipulation skills and Excel work

  • Knowledge of commercial management

  • Relevant qualification in project controls or project management or related discipline

  • Experience with Tilos would be advantageous
