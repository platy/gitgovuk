Details

In April 2021, Ofsted and the Care Quality Commission began revisiting areas where we had significant concerns about SEND provision when we last inspected them. We began full SEND inspections to areas that have not yet been inspected under the new framework in June 2021.

Areas we will revisit

  • Liverpool City Council, 9 to 11 May 2022, (take part in the Liverpool City Council survey to give your views from midday 29 April to midday 6 May 2022)

Information for families

Ofsted and the Care Quality Commission (CQC) inspect local areas together to see how effectively they fulfil their responsibilities for children and young people with special educational needs and/or disabilities.

Ofsted and CQC carry out inspections in line with the Local area special educational needs and disabilities inspection framework and Local area special educational needs and disabilities inspection handbook for inspectors.

Read the outcome letters for inspections of local area SEND.
