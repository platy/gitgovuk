Income Tax and National Insurance contributions exemption for employer-reimbursed coronavirus antigen tests

Details

The government is committed to supporting businesses and individuals through the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic, and this measure aims to make sure the use of relevant antigen testing procedures by employers are not subject to an Income Tax and National Insurance contributions charge.
