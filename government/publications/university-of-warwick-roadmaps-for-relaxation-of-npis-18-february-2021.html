Details

Modelling from the University of Warwick on easing of restrictions. It was considered at SAGE 81 on 18 February 2021.

The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper should be read alongside the SPI-M-O Summary statement on modelling on easing restrictions, and corresponding contribution from Imperial, also released under SAGE 81.

Earlier SPI-M-O statements on modelling on easing restrictions were discussed at SAGE 80:

  • SPI-M-O: Summary of modelling on scenario for easing restrictions, 6 February 2021

These also link to earlier modelling papers from Warwick and Imperial.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
