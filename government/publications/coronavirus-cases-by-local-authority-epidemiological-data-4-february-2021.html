Details

Data for each local authority is listed by:

  • number of people tested

  • case rate per 100,000 population

  • local COVID alert level

  • weekly trend

These reports summarise epidemiological data at lower-tier local authority (LTLA) level for England as at 4 February 2021 at 10am.
