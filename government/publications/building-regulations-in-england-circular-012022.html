Details

The circular and circular letter announce the withdrawal of and replacement of Approved Documents F2, L1, L2, O and S (2021 editions). It sets out in tabular format where errors and changes are required and, where appropriate, text has been amended.

The circular and letter also retain the original transitional provisions.
