Details

The Cyber Security Strategy explains how the government will ensure that all public sector organisations will be resilient to cyber threats.

The strategy’s vision is to ensure that core government functions are resilient to cyber attack, strengthening the UK as a sovereign nation and cementing its authority as a democratic and responsible cyber power.
