Details

Paper prepared by the University of Edinburgh, Public Health Scotland and EAVE II on the B.1.1.529 Omicron variant of concern and vaccine protection. It was considered at SAGE 101 on 23 December 2021. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper should be read alongside the accompanying paper from SAGE 101:

  • SPI-M-O: Consensus Statement on COVID-19, 22 December 2021
  • Imperial College London: Report 50: Hospitalisation risk for Omicron cases in England, 22 December 2021
  • University of Edinburgh, PHS and EAVE II: SARS-CoV-2 Vaccine Protection in Scotland: ​Omicron, 22 December 2021
  • LSHTM: Omicron scenarios:​ 28 December restrictions​, 22 December 2021
  • University of Warwick: Omicron Modelling, 22 December 2021
