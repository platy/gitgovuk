Details

These guidelines will help you understand the Skills for Life brand. By using the logo, you will help to strengthen the identity and awareness of adult skills offers, and highlight the connection between learning new skills and gaining a better job.

Please review the Skills for Life guidelines before using the logo. The Skills for Life logo should not be the leading logo on your marketing materials – it should accompany your main logo.

DfE materials will use the Skills for Life logo alongside the HMG logo. Whilst we are asking providers to use the Skills for Life logo, we cannot permit you to use the HMG logo. This does not apply if you are sharing assets provided by us.

Please note that the stock imagery used in the brand guidelines has been licensed by HM Government. If you wish to use the same photography, you will be required to purchase your own license.
