Details

This joint report follows the sixth meeting of the Specialised Committee on Citizens’ Rights on 28 April 2021. The joint report provides information on the progress of implementing residence rights under the Citizens’ Rights part of the Withdrawal Agreement. It covers residence rights for EU citizens in the UK and UK nationals in the EU, including their family members.

The report includes a summary of residence systems in the UK and the EU. It provides a summary of each national system, with relevant links, details of communications and support, information on deadlines and the numbers of applications for a new residence status or registrations for a new residence document, by type and outcome.

See also the first joint report and second joint report.
