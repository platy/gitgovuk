Details

The Defence Medical Academy (DMA) Prospectus provides details of the current military medical education and training courses available to DMS personnel. The Academy offers a wide range of technical and professional development opportunities, covering fields as diverse as Pre-Hospital Emergency Care, Primary Healthcare, Occupational Healthcare and Public Health. The medical training and education provided by the DMA extends across a wide range of disciplines from Level 3, non-vocational healthcare support workers (e.g. Military Medics) to consultant Doctors, Dentists and other health professionals undertaking Level 8 (Doctorate) qualifications. The Defence Postgraduate Medical Deanery facilitates, coordinates, and assures higher professional training of Defence Medical Services Foundation, Primary and Secondary Healthcare, Dental, Specialty Nurse and Higher Specialist Scientist trainees.

The Prospectus also provides an overview of the Academy, our organisation, our people, our locations including information about our main teaching site at Defence Medical Services Whittington, Lichfield.
