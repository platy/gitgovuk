Youll need to get an official certificate for any HRFNAO you are exporting to the EU or moving to Northern Ireland.

Find out which certificates you need for HRFNAO - Exporting or moving high risk food and feed of non-animal origin to the EU or Northern Ireland from 1 January 2021

Download and fill in the certificates you need in these languages:

  • English
  • the language of the country where the HRFNAO will enter the EU
  • the language of the destination country in the EU
