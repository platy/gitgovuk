Specialised Committee on Citizens’ Rights – Agenda, 24 January 2022, 9:30 to 11:30 [2 hours] (GMT) [King Charles Street, London] / Conference Call

9:30 to 9:40 [10 mins] Item 1 – Opening remarks and adoption of agenda. Led by co-chairs, Giles Portman and Marie Simonsen
9:40 to 10:25 [45 mins] Item 2 – Presentation by British in Europe and the3million. Presentation by civil society organisations on issues encountered by UK nationals in the EU and EU citizens in the UK
10:25 to 10:50 [25 mins] Item 3 – Monitoring and complaints handling. Presentation by the Independent Monitoring Authority (IMA) and the Commission of their respective monitoring and complaints handling activities
10:50 to 11:15 [25 mins] Item 4 – Implementation and application of the Withdrawal Agreement. Discussion of relevant ongoing issues relating to the implementation and application of Part Two (Citizens’ Rights)
11:15 to 11:25 [10 mins] Item 5 – 6th Joint Report on Article 18 Presentation by the Secretariat of the sixth Joint Report on Article 18
11:25 to 11:30 [5 mins] Item 6 – AOB. Organisation of the next meeting and adoption of a joint statement
