Details

These maps show the Freeport tax sites within Liverpool City Region Freeport which are designated and recognised in law as geographical areas where businesses can benefit from tax reliefs to bring investment, trade and jobs to regenerate regions across the country that need it most.

The tax sites for Liverpool City Region Freeport have been designated with effect from 22 March 2022.

Check the full list of Freeport maps and the Freeport tax site locations within them.
