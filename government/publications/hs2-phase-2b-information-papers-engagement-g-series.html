Details

Series G of our information papers describe High Speed Two (HS2) Ltd’s consultation and engagement aims on Phase 2b (Crewe – Manchester), which include:

  • working with stakeholders and communities to improve understanding
  • providing general information on subjects and defined proposals
  • understanding general concerns and specific issues that interested parties may have
  • providing effective means for interested parties to submit views on proposals
  • contributing to a hybrid Bill that has been well-thought out
