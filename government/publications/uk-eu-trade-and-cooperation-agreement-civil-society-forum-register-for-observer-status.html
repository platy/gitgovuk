Details

The UK-EU Trade and Cooperation Agreement Civil Society Forum (CSF) meets for the first time on 4 October 2022 in Brussels. The agenda will include trade in goods, sustainability, energy cooperation and level playing field matters.

The UK representation to the CSF is limited to 60 active participants. We want to have a balanced sectoral and geographical representation of civil society organisations to conduct an effective dialogue of implementation issues relating to part 2 of the TCA.

Organisations can submit an Expression of Interest to participate in the CSF by 11:59pm on 12 September 2022.

The CSF also allows people representing additional organisations, that are not active participants, to observe meetings virtually (by video link).

To register for CSF observer status on 4 October 2022, fill in the CSF registration form and email it to dagsecretariat@fcdo.gov.uk by 11:59pm on 27 September 2022. We cannot accept forms after this time.
