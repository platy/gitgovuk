COVID-19 update

This is an unprecedented time for all of us and the FAM team is closely following government guidance and adhering to its advice. This means the FAM Cell teams are working from home unless stated otherwise, where conditions allow.

If you are already on the pilot, the FAM Cell staff are continuing to support you, and payments will continue as usual during this time.

If you have yet to submit your Accommodation Preference Form, please speak to your local FAM Cell team, you can reach them by using the following email addresses:

  • HMNB Clyde: people-famcell-cly@mod.gov.uk
  • Aldershot Garrison: people-famcell-ald@mod.gov.uk
  • RAF Wittering: people-famcell-wit@mod.gov.uk.
