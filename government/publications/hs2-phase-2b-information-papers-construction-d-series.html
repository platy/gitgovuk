Details

The documents contained in Series D of our information papers describe:

  • the design development process prior to construction
  • interactions with local planning authorities and consultation with affected communities
  • controls on construction activities outlined in the Code of Construction Practice, including working hours
  • the construction timetable for Phase 2b (Crewe – Manchester); an overview of the main stages and a timeline
  • the use of borrow pits for construction
  • how access to property (due to temporary interference with the highway) will be maintained during construction works
  • mitigation for risks of disruption to public utilities during construction
