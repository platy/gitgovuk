    Updated chapters 1, 3, 10, 17, 20, 21 and 24. Chapters 2, 4, 5, 7, 8, 9, 11 and 13 have been split into 2 chapters, one for UC claimants and one for JSA (IB) claimants.     Revised versions of chapters 1, 2, 3, 4, 7, 8, 9, 10, 11, 14, 17, 18, and 21. The revised chapters are now labelled version 2. Also published a brand new chapter 24 (Marketing, publicity and good news stories), and its associated 'Restart Scheme ‘Good News Story’ template'. The template has been published in both Word and ODT formats.

  3. 9 August 2021

    Added a new annex for chapter 12, Sharing information with stakeholders in the course of local-level engagement.
