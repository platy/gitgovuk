Recipients may freely reproduce this circular.

1. This circular is to confirm that total funding of £11,417,054 will be paid to local authorities (LAs) participating in the Housing Benefit Award Accuracy (HBAA) Initiative for the financial year ending (FYE) March 2023 from 4 April 2022.

2. The funding is intended to support LAs to proactively identify unreported changes and make sure the right amount of HB is paid to the right person, at the right time by undertaking the following activities:

  • HB Full Case Reviews
  • Housing Benefit Matching Service referrals, including:
    • Self-Employed Case Reviews
  • the correct recording of case information on the automated LA IT systems and clerical management information (MI) returns (for FYE March 2022) to the Department for Work and Pension. (Note: Clerical MI returns will be accepted for year two of the Initiative up to 1 July 2022.)

3. LAs are expected to profile their caseload consistently month on month, over the 12-month duration of the Initiative to complete 100% of their funded activities.

4. Circular HB A5/2022 provides details of the HBAA Initiative for the financial year ending (FYE) March 2023.

5. If LAs have any queries regarding the HBAA Initiative they can contact lawelfare.frauderrordebt@dwp.gov.uk

Funding and payment details

6. The individual funding payments and volumes for LAs are shown in Annex A.

7. Payments will be made to participating LAs during the week commencing 25 April 2022.
