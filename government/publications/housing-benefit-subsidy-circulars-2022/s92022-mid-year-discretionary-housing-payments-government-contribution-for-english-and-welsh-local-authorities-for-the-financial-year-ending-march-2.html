Introduction

1. This circular provides details of the mid-year government contribution and overall expenditure limit for Discretionary Housing Payments (DHPs) for the financial year ending (FYE) March 2023 for local authorities (LAs) in England and Wales.

Background

2. DHPs have been available to LAs since 2001 to help claimants who may need additional support with housing costs.

3. The total DHP funding for England and Wales for FYE March 2023 is £100 million. We made the first allocations totalling £98 million in April 2022. This is detailed in circular HB S1/2022.

4. We are now allocating the remaining £2 million of DHP funding in line with the approach taken for allocating the initial £98 million with a flat rate reduction compared to funding for the previous year.

5. Annex A provides the FYE March 2023 mid-year DHP government contribution LA allocations.

Payment of government contribution DHP funding

6. In accordance with the department’s standard DHP reconciliation and payment process, the second and final instalment of DHP funding will be paid in late November 2022.
