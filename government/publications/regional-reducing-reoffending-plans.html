Details

Regional Reducing Reoffending Plans demonstrate the most important and public-facing activities that HMPPS is prioritising to reduce reoffending. The plans set out how Regional Probation Directors and Prison Group Directors will work together and with partner organisations and local services over the next three years to cut crime and make communities safer by reducing reoffending.
