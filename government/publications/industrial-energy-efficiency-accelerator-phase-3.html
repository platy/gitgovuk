Details

Phase 3 and 4 of the Industrial Energy Efficiency Accelerator (IEEA) programme provide around £8 million in funding for the development and demonstration of technologies that could:

  • reduce energy consumption
  • maximise resource efficiency
  • cut carbon emissions

Phase 3 is closed to applications.

The Carbon Trust delivers the IEEA on behalf of BEIS. Further information, including details of previous projects, is available from the Carbon Trust site.

Phase 4 open to applications

Phase 4 of the IEEA is open to applications until 20 September 2022, 3pm. To register your interest:

  • read the scope of the IEEA document for further information about the programme
  • register your interest on the Carbon Trust website

Background

The programme is funded through the £1 billion Net Zero Innovation Portfolio (NZIP), which runs until 2025.
