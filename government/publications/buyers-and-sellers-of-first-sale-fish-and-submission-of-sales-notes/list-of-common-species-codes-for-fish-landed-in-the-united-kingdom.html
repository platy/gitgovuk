This list is the most common species caught and the associated Food and Agriculture Organisation (FAO) of the United Nations short code. The list is not a comprehensive list of all species. A full list of codes is available on the FAO website. Registered Buyers and Sellers (RBS) can also access a list on the Electronic Recording System (ERS) when entering sales information.
