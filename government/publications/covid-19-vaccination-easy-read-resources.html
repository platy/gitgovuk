Details

These resources are aimed at people who have, or care for someone with a learning disability.

All women of childbearing age must read the COVID-19 vaccination for women who might get pregnant, who are pregnant or are breastfeeding their baby guidance before going for vaccination.

An easy-read consent form for adults is available to download.

Paper copies of these leaflet are available to order for free or download via the links below.
