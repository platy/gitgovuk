Details

These resources are aimed at people who have, or care for someone with a learning disability.

All women of childbearing age must read the COVID-19 vaccination for women who might get pregnant, who are pregnant or are breastfeeding their baby guidance before going for vaccination.

An easy-read consent form for adults is available to download.

These leaflet are available to download and paper copies are available to order using the following product codes:

Other versions of these leaflets are available in braille, BSL, translated into many languages. Please visit Health Publications to order or download paper copies.

NHS England Learning Disability and Autism Programme have produced an information about vaccines video for people with a learning disability and their carers. A set of short social media clips of the video are available to download from the Health Publications website.
