Details

The investment prospectus provides guidance on how local authorities in England can access funding to improve numeracy skills for adults (aged 19 and above).

The technical guidance document provides detailed guidance for local authorities in England on Multiply, including:

  • the allocation methodology
  • assurance and grant management mechanisms
  • the reporting framework

The Multiply investment template document and spreadsheet are for the Greater London Authority, mayoral combined authorities and upper tier or unitary authorities outside these areas in England. Complete and return these by 30 June 2022.

Indicative allocations for local areas gives details of the funding allocations for local authorities in England for the financial years:

  • 2022 to 2023
  • 2023 to 2024
  • 2024 to 2025
