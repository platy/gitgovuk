Details

The Regulator of Social Housing has written to local authorities in England about the government’s Social Housing White Paper, published in November 2020. The Paper clearly outlines that social tenants should be able to expect good quality homes and services, whoever their landlord is.

RSH released a statement at the launch of the White Paper.
