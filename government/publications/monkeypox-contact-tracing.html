Details

This guidance provides principles for risk assessment and follow-up of contacts of confirmed monkeypox cases. It is intended to support risk assessment and categorisation of contacts to ensure they are offered appropriate isolation advice and vaccination.

Additional monkeypox resources are also available from the UK Health Security Agency, including information on case definitions and vaccination.

Information sheets for contacts
