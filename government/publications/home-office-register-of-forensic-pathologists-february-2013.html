Details

The Home Office register of forensic pathologists is a list of forensic pathologists who meet the criteria for registration as detailed in the process and criteria for recommendation for admission to the Home Secretary’s register of forensic pathologists.

This document lists those on the register by group practice and details the police forces within those group practice areas.
