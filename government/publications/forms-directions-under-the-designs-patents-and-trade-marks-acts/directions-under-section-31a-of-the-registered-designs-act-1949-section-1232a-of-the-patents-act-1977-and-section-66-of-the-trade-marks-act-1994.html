Changes to patents, trade marks, and designs forms made to allow for more efficient processing

1. The comptroller has made these Directions under section 31A of the Registered Designs Act 1949, section 123(2A) of the Patents Act 1977 and section 66 of the Trade Marks Act 1994.

2. These Directions set out changes to the below designs dorms, patents forms and trade marks forms whose use is required by rules.

3. These Directions come into force on 29th June 2022.

4. The following forms are set out in the Schedule to these Directions and are the forms which are required by the Registered Designs Rules 2006 (SI 2006/1975) as amended, the Patents Rules 2007 (SI 2007/3291) as amended and the Trade Marks Rules 2007 (SI 2008/1797) as amended:

5. Patents forms 1, 2, 3, 4, 7, 9A, 10, 12, 14, 15, 16, 17, 21, 23, 34, 49, 52, NP1, SP1, SP2, SP3, SP4, AF1, trade marks forms TM3, TM3A, TM5, TM7, TM7F, TM7G, TM9, TM9R, TM11, TM12, TM13, TM16, TM16P, TM24, TM24C, TM26(I), TM26(N), TM26(O), TM31C, TM31M, TM31R, TM35, TM36, TM50, TM51, TM55P, designs forms DF2A, DF2B, DF2C, DF9A, DF12A, DF16A, DF19A, DF19C, DF21, DF23, DF29 and design right forms DRF1, DRF2, DRF3, DRF4 as so set out, replace the corresponding forms in the Schedule to the Directions made on 18 August 2021 (which came into force on 21 August 2021). The Directions made on 18 August 2021 are, to that extent, revoked.

6. Patents form 51 as so set out, replaces the corresponding form in the Schedule to the Directions made on 31 March 2021 (which came into force on 1 April 2021). The Directions made on 31 March 2021 are, to that extent, revoked.

7. Patents forms 8, 20, 28, 30, 54, SP5, SP6, trade marks forms TM4, TM8, TM8N, TM9C, TM9E, TM9T, TM17, TM21B, TM26R, TM27, TM28, TM29, TM33, TM33P, TM53, TM55, Trade marks opt out, International trade marks opt out, and designs forms DF5, DF55, Designs opt out, International designs opt out as so set out, replace the corresponding forms in the Schedule to the Directions made on 22 December 2020 (which came into force on 1 January 2021). The Directions made on 22 December 2020 are, to that extent, revoked.

8. Designs form DF1A as so set out, replaces the corresponding form in the Schedule to the Directions made on 6 November 2020 (which came into force on 9 November 2020). The Directions made on 6 November 2020 are, to that extent, revoked.

9. Trade marks form TM12R as so set out, replaces the corresponding form in the Schedule to the Directions made on 13 February 2019 (which came into force on 14 January 2019). The Directions made on 13 February 2019 are, to that extent, revoked.

10. Designs forms DF5 and DF19B as so set out, replace the corresponding forms in the Schedule to the Directions made on 23 May 2018 (which came into force on 13 June 2018). The Directions made on 23 May 2018 are, to that extent, revoked.

11. The remaining trademark and designs forms as so set out, replace the corresponding forms.
