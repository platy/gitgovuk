Details

The purpose of this circular is to draw attention to:

  • the amendments and explain the changes they make to the Building Regulations 2010 and the Building (Approved Inspectors etc.) Regulations 2010; and
  • the publication of a new Approved Document S.

The circular also sets out transitional provisions.
