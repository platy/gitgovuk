Details

The Department for Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy (BEIS) is grant funding an organisation(s) to develop and deliver a voluntary business mentoring scheme for the Help to Grow: Management programme.

This national scheme will enable Help to Grow: Management participants to access the experienced business mentors they require to support their training programme and equip them with the skills to improve their productivity and grow their business.

We invited relevant organisations to submit an application to deliver this programme.

How to apply

This closed to applications on 18 March 2022.

Briefing event for potential applicants

We hosted an Applicant Briefing Event on 3 March 2022 to provide an overview of the competition and an opportunity to ask questions. We have published the set of questions raised and our answers.
