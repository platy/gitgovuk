National lockdown rules apply in England because of coronavirus (COVID-19). Find out what the rules are in Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland.

The government has published the COVID-19 Response - Spring 2021 setting out the roadmap out of the current lockdown for England. This explains how restrictions will be eased over time.

The most important thing you can do to fight coronavirus is to follow social distancing guidance. This will help control the virus and save lives.
