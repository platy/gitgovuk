Details

The list gives details of of honorary awards to foreign nationals approved by Her Late Majesty The Queen in 2022. These awards recognise foreign and Commonwealth nationals who have made an outstanding contribution to the UK.

It includes awards to foreign nationals announced as part of The Queen’s Birthday Honours 2022.
