Plan for Jobs: Skills Revolution campaign toolkit

Example social media posts to share on your channels.

General

T Levels

Designed by employers for employers, #TLevels are helping businesses like yours to supercharge their talent pool. Join the #SkillsRevolution. Visit: https://find-employer-schemes.education.gov.uk?utm_source=StakeholderToolkit&utm_medium=ToolkitSocial&utm_campaign=SkillsRevStakeholderToolkit #SkillsForLife

Apprenticeships

SMEs can get 95% of their training costs for #Apprenticeships paid for by the government. Join the #SkillsRevolution. Visit: https://find-employer-schemes.education.gov.uk?utm_source=StakeholderToolkit&utm_medium=ToolkitSocial&utm_campaign=SkillsRevStakeholderToolkit #SkillsForLife
