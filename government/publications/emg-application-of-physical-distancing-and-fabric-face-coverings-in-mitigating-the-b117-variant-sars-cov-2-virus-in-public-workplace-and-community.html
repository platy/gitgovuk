Details

Paper presented by the EMG on physical distancing and face coverings in mitigating the spread of the new SARS-CoV-2 variant. It was considered at SAGE 76 on 14 January 2021.

This paper mainly focuses on physical distancing and fabric face coverings. The paper does not cover healthcare settings. See the latest government advice on face coverings in public settings.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
