Details

Consensus statement from SPI-M-O on COVID-19. It was considered at SAGE 101 on 23 December 2021. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper should be read alongside the accompanying modelling papers from SAGE 101:

  • Imperial College London: Report 50: Hospitalisation risk for Omicron cases in England, 22 December 2021
  • University of Edinburgh, PHS and EAVE II: SARS-CoV-2 Vaccine Protection in Scotland: ​Omicron, 22 December 2021
  • University of Warwick: Omicron Modelling, 22 December 2021
  • LSHTM: Omicron scenarios:​ 28 December restrictions​, 22 December 2021

See more information on:

  • epidemiological modelling frequency asked questions
  • more information on SPI-M modelling and medium-term projections.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
