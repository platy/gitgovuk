Title of case (including tribunal or court reference) Tribunal or court Decision (including date of decision) Current status
Ampleaward Ltd A3/(2020)/1416 Court of Appeal 8 October 2021 The UT allowed Ampleaward’s appeal. The CoA dismissed HMRC’s appeal.
Balhousie Holdings Ltd (2021) UKSC 11 Supreme Court 31 March 2021 The SC allowed Balhousie’s appeal. See Revenue & Customs Brief 13 (2021)
Beigebell Ltd (2020) UKUT 176 (TCC) UT/2019/0109 (V) Upper Tribunal 4 June 2020 The UT allowed HMRC’s appeal. First-tier Tribunal (FTT) decision set aside and appeal remitted to a differently constituted FTT for rehearing.
Bluejay Mining Plc (2020) UKFTT 473 (TC) TC/2017/00987 TC/2018/06090 First-tier Tribunal 18 November 2020 Appeal allowed. HMRC withdrew its appeal to the UT so the FTT decision is final.

DCM (Optical Holdings) Ltd (2020) CSIH 60 Court of Session 8 September 2020 The Inner House of the Court of Session allowed HMRC’s appeal (evidence of facts) and refused DCM’s appeal. The Inner House has granted DCM permission to appeal to the Supreme Court. Supreme Court hearing 8 February 2022.
Gray & Farrar International Ltd (2021) UKUT 0293 (TCC) UT/2020/0024 Upper Tribunal 26 November 2021 The Upper Tribunal allowed Gray & Farrar’s appeal. HMRC refused permission to appeal by the Upper Tribunal. HMRC now applying direct to the Court of Appeal for permission.
Hotel La Tour Ltd (2020) UKFTT 0451 (TC) TC/2018/06939 (V) First-tier Tribunal 3 December 2021 Appeal allowed. HMRC seeking permission to appeal to the Upper Tribunal.
Mid Ulster District Council (2020) UKFTT 434 (TC) TC/2011/687 and TC/2012/9523 First-tier Tribunal 17 October 2020 Appeal allowed. HMRC granted permission to appeal the distortion of competition issue. Upper Tribunal hearing listed for May 2022.
Netbusters (UK) Ltd (2020) UKFTT 438 (TC) TC/2018/05959 TC/2019/00214 First-tier Tribunal 2 November 2020 Appeal allowed. HMRC granted permission to appeal to the Upper Tribunal. Upper Tribunal hearing listed for May 2022.
News Corp UK & Ireland Ltd A3/2020/0449 Court of Appeal 28 January 2021 The CoA allowed HMRC’s appeal. News Corp seeking permission to appeal to the Supreme Court.
NHS Lothian Health Board (2020) CSIH 14 Court of Session 9 April 2020 The IH allowed NHS Lothian’s appeal and remitted the case to a differently constituted FTT. HMRC granted permission to appeal to the Supreme Court.
Revive Corporation Ltd (2020) UKUT 320 (TCC) UT/2019/0154 (V) Upper Tribunal 17 November 2020 The UT set aside the FTT’s decision (dismissing the appeal) and remitted the case on a limited basis to a differently constituted FTT for rehearing.
The Prudential Assurance Company Ltd (2021) UKFTT 50 (TC) TC/2018/07480 First-tier Tribunal 26 February 2021 Appeal allowed. HMRC seeking permission to appeal to the UT.
The Rank Group Plc & 2016 G1 Ltd (2021) UKFTT 241 (TC) TC/2013/04417 TC/2010/08268 First-tier Tribunal 30 June 2021 Appeals allowed. HMRC have not sought permission to appeal so the decision is final. See Revenue & Customs Brief 12 (2021).
