Details

This is the third data report on the national cervical screening programme standards.

This report focuses on performance in England from April 2020 to March 2021 but also includes trend data from previous years where this is available.

It provides data on:

  • coverage
  • timeliness of receiving results
  • laboratory standards
  • colposcopy standards
