Details

If you are making a declaration using the Customs Declaration Service (CDS) follow the guidance in the UK Trade Tariff: volume 3 for CDS.

HMRC is closing the CHIEF system on 31 March 2023. The Customs Declaration Service is replacing it as the UK’s single customs platform.

Use this list to decide which airport International Air Transport Association (IATA) code and percentage you should use on CHIEF.

The list also has the location and name of the airport, as well as the country and the associated zone.
