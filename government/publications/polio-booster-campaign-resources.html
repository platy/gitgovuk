Details

All children aged 1 to 9 years in London are being offered a polio vaccination. For some children this may be an extra dose of polio vaccine, on top of their routine vaccinations. In other children it may just bring them up to date.

This leaflet includes information on polio, vaccination and the booster campaign.

Paper copies of the English version of this leaflet are available free to order or download.

This leaflet is being translated into the languages and alternative formats below and will be available soon to download. Please place orders for any paper copies you require and these will be dispatched as soon as they are available.
