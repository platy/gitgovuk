1. What is the premium customer service?

This service for sponsors is designed to:

  • offer your institution an enhanced level of support in sponsoring all international students
  • provide you with tailored advice and support with your sponsor licence and your students’ immigration needs

2. Included services

2.1 Named account manager and regional account manager

You will be allocated a account manager and will be given a telephone number and e-mail address to contact them. Your account manager will be your first point of contact for any enquiries relating to your student sponsor licence or migrant related problems. You will be able to contact your account manager between 9am and 5pm on weekdays. The service standard is 5 working days.

You will also be allocated a regional account manager from within our premium customer service team, who will be available to support your account manager with more complex queries and strategic issues.

You will be given the opportunity to regularly talk with us at annual premium customer service events and through meetings with your account manager and senior members of the team.

2.2 Student immigration history details

With the consent of the student, we will provide you with information about the student’s immigration history, which will assist you in deciding if they can apply for permission on the Student or Child Student routes and whether you should issue a confirmation of acceptance for studies (CAS).

These requests will be carried out within 3 working days.

2.3 Priority treatment on changes to your student sponsor license

You will receive priority treatment on requests to make changes to your student sponsor licence. Requests will be carried out within 10 working days. We do not process basic compliance assessment (BCA) applications which can take up to 18 weeks to be completed.

Complex requests, such as mergers and takeovers, may take longer than 10 working days to complete. If this happens, your account manager will be in contact with you throughout this period.

2.4 Management information reports on CAS allocation

You will receive a monthly report on request detailing the CAS certificates which you have used in the previous 3 months. These reports will provide you with an indication of where your CAS outcomes place you in relation to the BCA criteria.

3. What we cannot provide

We cannot:

  • consider an application outside of the immigration rules
  • consider migrant applications quickly, although we can provide updates on the progress of an application, with the permission of the applicant

4. Eligibility to apply

Any licensed sponsor with student sponsor status can apply to join the service provided they don’t have outstanding compliance actions. We may withdraw the premium customer service if we take compliance action against you through our normal procedures.

5. The cost of the service

The premium customer service is available for £8,000 for a 12 month period. This includes the fee for your next BCA.

6. How to apply

We are unable to accept new subscription applications to the service at present. If you wish to be entered on to the waiting list then please contact premiumeducationteam@homeoffice.gov.uk including:

  • your institution’s name
  • sponsor licence details
  • your contact details

You can apply for the premium customer service using your sponsorship management system (SMS) account and selecting ‘Apply for or renew Student Sponsor Premium Customer Service’. You do not need to send us any supporting documents.

7. How long it will take

We aim to consider your application within 10 working days.
