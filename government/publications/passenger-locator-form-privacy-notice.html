Details

Privacy notice and current data protection impact assessment for the passenger locator form and for pre-departure testing. The notice explains:

  • why we collect your data
  • how we protect your personal information
  • who may have access to your data
  • how long we keep will your data
  • the legal basis for collecting and processing your data
  • contacting you using your information
  • how to make a complaint
