Details

Defra lists recognised breed societies for bovine, ovine, porcine and caprine species.

It also lists recognised breeding operations for hybrid breeding pigs.

The list of recognised equine breed organisations is also available.

Zootechnical rules and standards support trade in:

  • purebred breeding cattle
  • sheep and goats
  • pigs and hybrid pigs
  • equines
  • genetic material of all these animals

Read the guide to zootechnical certificates and how to get one.

Read about the work of the Farm Animal Genetic Resources Committee.

Contact: fangr@defra.gov.uk

Member State colleagues

Send your queries, including notifications about the extension of the geographical territory of breeding programmes into the UK to fangr@defra.gov.uk.
