ANNEX F (AMENDMENT) REGULATION 24

PAY

The following determination “Annex F (Amendment) – Pay” is made by the Secretary of State under regulation 24 of the Police Regulations 2003 (S.I. 2003/527), following consultation in accordance with regulation 46 of those Regulations. This determination was made on 18 March 2022 and amends the determination “Annex F - Pay” (“Annex F”). The amendment comes into force on 1 April 2022.

1. Annex F is amended as follows.

2. Omit Part 1C.

3. In Part 2—

(1) in paragraph (1)—

(a) in note (d), omit the words from “, with the exception” to the end of the note;

(b) omit note (e);

(2) in paragraph (2), omit note (c).
