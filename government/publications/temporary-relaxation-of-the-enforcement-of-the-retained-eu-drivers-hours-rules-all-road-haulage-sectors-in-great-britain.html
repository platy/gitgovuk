Details

The relaxation of the rules that applied until 8 May 2022, subject to review, is terminating early at 11.59pm on 22 April 2022.

If you used the relaxation, you need to email a completed follow-up notification of relaxation form to FOLRnotification@dft.gov.uk by 29 April 2022 at the latest.

The Department for Transport (DfT) announced a temporary relaxation of drivers’ hours rules in England, Scotland and Wales from 9 April 2022 until 8 May 2022.

This was in response to urgent situations arising from the cancellation of P&O Ferries services and the congestion of international freight traffic.

In light of the improvement of the situation, DfT is terminating the relaxation early at 11.59pm on 22 April 2022.

The situation continues to be under review.
