The approval standards are the criteria UKHSA uses to assess applications to access ‘Protected’ data. They set out the minimum requirements for a data application and enable the UKHSA to make decisions that are consistent and objective.

Each standard has accompanying guidelines. The guidelines describe the general rules and principles around each standard and provide guidance that will help you properly address the requirements for a successful application. Where possible, we have also included examples in the guidelines.
