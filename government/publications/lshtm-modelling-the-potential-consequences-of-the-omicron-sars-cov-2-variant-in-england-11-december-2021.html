Details

Paper prepared by the London School of Hygiene and Tropical Medicine (LSHTM) on early modelling scenarios for potential B.1.1.529 (Omicron) epidemic trajectories. It was considered at SAGE 99 on 16 December 2021. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper is available as a pre-print on the LSHTM website.

This paper should be read alongside the accompanying modelling papers from SAGE 99:

  • SPI-M-O: Consensus Statement on COVID-19, 15 December 2021
  • University of Warwick: Early Omicron Results, 13 December 2021

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
