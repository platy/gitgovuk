This memo is for Work and Health Programme (WHP) providers.

Date: 9 May 2019

Action: For information

Timing: Immediate

Background

1. The purpose of this memo is to inform you of recent amendments made to chapters 3, 7 and 14 of the Work and Health Programme provider guidance chapters.

Summary and action

2. Please ensure, with immediate effect, that the revised information in the following Work and Health Programme provider guidance chapters is used by you and all of your supply chain partners.
