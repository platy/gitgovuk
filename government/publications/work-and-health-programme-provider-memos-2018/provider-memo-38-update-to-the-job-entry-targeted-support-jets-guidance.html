This memo is for Work and Health Programme (WHP) providers.

Background

1. The purpose of this memo is to inform you of amendments that have been made to Work and Health Programme (COVID-19) including JETS Provider Guidance chapter 2.

This is a summary of the change made to Chapter 2 with updated version number.

Summary and action

2. Please ensure, with immediate effect, that the revised information in the following Work and Health Programme (COVID-19) including JETS Provider Guidance chapter 2 is read in full and used by you and all of your supply chain partners.

Chapter Paragraph Title Summary of Change Date of Guidance Version Number
2 7 Participant identification, eligibility and referral Updated eligibility criteria 7/10/2020 3

Further information
