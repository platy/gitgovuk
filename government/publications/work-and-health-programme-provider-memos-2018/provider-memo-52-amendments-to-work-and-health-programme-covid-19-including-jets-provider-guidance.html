This memo is for Work and Health Programme (WHP) providers.

Issued by: Work and Health Programme Operational Policy Team

Background

1. This memo informs you of amendments that have been made to the following Work and Health Programme (COVID 19) including JETS Provider Guidance Chapters.

  • Work and Health Programme COVID-19 provider guidance Chapter 1 Introduction and overview

  • Work and Health Programme COVID-19 Chapter 3b: (for WHP JETS) Acknowledging referrals, initial participant engagement and registering a start

  • Work and Health Programme COVID-19-provider-guidance Chapter 11 programme completers and early exits

  • Work and Health Programme COVID-19 Provider Guidance Chapter 12 Participants Complaints

  • Work and Health Programme COVID-19 Provider Guidance Chapter 14 Validation

  • Work and Health Programme COVID-19 provider Guidance Chapter 15 Data sharing between DWP and providers

Summary and action

2. The information in Work and Health Programme (COVID-19) including JETS Provider Guidance should be read in full and used by you and all of your supply chain partners. Particular attention should be given to Chapters 1, 3b,11,12,14 and 15.
