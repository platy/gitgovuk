This memo is for Work and Health Programme (WHP) providers.

Issued by: Work and Health Programme Operational Policy Team

1. Background

1. The purpose of this memo is to inform you of recent amendments made to annex 2 of the Work and Health Programme provider guidance chapters.

2. Summary and action

2. Please ensure, with immediate effect, that the revised information in the following Work and Health Programme provider guidance annex is read in full and used by you and all of your supply chain partners.
