This memo is for Work and Health Programme (WHP) providers.

Issued by: Work and Health Programme Operational Policy Team

1. Background

1. The purpose of this memo is to inform you of amendments that have been made to the Work and Health Programme Provider Guidance. Attached is a summary of all changes made to Annex 2 with the updated version number.

2. Summary and action

2. Please ensure, with immediate effect, that the revised information in the following Work and Health Programme Provider Guidance chapters is read in full and used by you and all of your supply chain partners.

Chapter Paragraph Title Summary of change Date of guidance change Version number
Annex 2 Chapter 5a Forms and Letters Updated logos on JSA and UC MAN letters attachments dated 01/20 for immediate use 10/01/2020 7
Annex 2 Chapter 5b Forms and Letters Updated logos on JSA and UC MEN letters attachments dated 01/20 for immediate use 10/01/2020 7

3. Further information

3. If you have any queries about this memo, please contact: whp.enquiries@dwp.gov.uk
