This memo is for Work and Health Programme (WHP) providers.

Issued by: Work and Health Programme Operational Policy Team

Background

You will be aware that adaptations to the WHP design are being considered to take into account a number of temporary measures necessitated by the Coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic.

You have received letters of change or contract variations and will have noted that a self–referral process has been developed. This memo notifies you that a draft version of the Coronavirus (COVID-19) self-referral process will shortly be available through the Jaggaer system. The guidance is not yet available for formal use as this is subject to the CV04 variation.

Action

Please review the self-referral guidance to familiar yourself with the process. Go–live of the process will be agreed as part of the CV04 variation. This is a draft version for information only – we will issue the finalised version prior to go live and alert you accordingly.

Further information and contact details

If you have any queries about this live running memo please consult your performance manager in the first instance.

If your enquiry is of a commercial nature, or if you have any queries or concerns regarding contract variations or letters of change, please direct them to the Commercial Directorate email inbox: employmentcategory.covid19@dwp.gsi.gov.uk.

If your enquiry is specifically in connection with these temporary alterations to the WHP provider guidance, please contact the WHP policy team: whp.enquiries@dwp.gov.uk.
