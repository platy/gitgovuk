Background

This memo is to inform you that the definition of the Early Access Disadvantaged Group (EAG) ‘Young people in gangs’ has changed to ‘Young people identified as being involved or at risk of being involved in gangs or serious violence’.

Summary

This change will impact:

Work and Health Programme including JETS Provider Guidance

Please note: The National WHPSO1 Signposting Organisation forms and packs are impacted but will be updated in the coming weeks to reflect this change.

Actions

You and your supply chain partner[s] must now use the version of Provider Guidance published on Gov.Uk. Work and Health Programme including JETS provider guidance - GOV.UK (www.gov.uk)
