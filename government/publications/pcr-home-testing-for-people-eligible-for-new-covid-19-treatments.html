Details

The NHS is offering new antibody and antiviral treatments to people with coronavirus (COVID-19) who are at highest risk of becoming seriously ill.

Find out more about who can have a COVID-19 treatment on NHS.UK

Only use this guide if both the following apply:

  • the NHS has written to you because youâ€™re eligible for these treatments
  • you have received a polymerase chain reaction (PCR) test kit to keep at home

Call 119 if you:

  • have not received a PCR test kit by 10 January 2022
  • develop symptoms before your PCR test arrives
  • have lost your PCR test or it has any damage or missing parts

A PCR test is a test for COVID-19 that is sent to a lab.

This guide tells you how to:

  • do your PCR home test if you have symptoms of COVID-19
  • register your test kit so you can get your result
  • return your completed test kit
