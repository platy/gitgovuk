Details

This is an indication of the process only. All timings are approximate and will vary according to each individual mark.

The trade mark timeline provides an overview of the process of applying to register a trade mark. The timeline gives you an indication of your responsibilities and what happens next, once you’ve submitted your application.

Each stage of the process is hyperlinked, allowing you to easily access relevant information.
