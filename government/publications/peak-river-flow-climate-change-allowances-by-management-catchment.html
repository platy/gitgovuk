Details

This table provides the peak river flow allowances for each management catchment. You need this information if you are a:

  • local planning authority preparing strategic flood risk assessments
  • developer or agent preparing flood risk assessments for planning applications, and development consent orders for nationally significant infrastructure projects
  • a risk management authority developing a flood and coastal risk project, scheme or strategy

To use the correct climate change allowances for peak river flow you need to identify the management catchment for your assessment. If you do not know the name of your management catchment, use the guidance and link to the peak river flow map in:

  • Flood risk assessments: climate change allowances
  • Flood and coastal risk projects, schemes and strategies: climate change allowances
