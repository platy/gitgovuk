The Independent Medical Expert Group (IMEG) provides independent evidence-based advice to Minister for Defence People and Veterans (DPV) on the medical and scientific aspects of the Armed Forces Compensation Scheme (AFCS) and related matters so that AFCS policy and individual decision making reflects contemporary medical understanding on causation and progress of disorders.

All appointments are not remunerated posts however reasonable travel and subsistence expenses will be paid.

All appointments and re-appointments have been made in accordance with the Governance Code on Public Appointments.

2022

January

Following approval from the Minister of Defence People and Veterans, Dr Louisa Murdin has been appointed as a Member of the Independent Medical Expert Group (IMEG). The period of appointment is for three years, with effect from 10 December 2021 and will expire on 30 September 2024.

2021

February

The Minister for Defence People and Veterans Johnny Mercer MP has agreed a six-month extension to the existing appointment of Professor Peter White, as member of the Independent Medical Expert Group (IMEG), from 1 September 2021 to 28 February 2022.

2020

28580 Two Medical members for the Independent Medical Expert Group (IMEG)

The Minister for Defence People and Veterans has appointed Prof Paul Cullinan as one of the medical members of the Independent Medical Expert Group for a period of three years. Prof Cullinan is replacing Dr John Scadding who is now the Chair of IMEG. The appointment will commence on 1 September 2020 and expire on 31 August 2023. The Minister for Defence People and Veterans has also appointed Dr Ali Hashtroudi as one of the medical members of the Independent Medical Expert Group for a period of three years. He will be replacing Dr Snashall who is leaving on 30 September 2020. The appointment will commence on 1 October 2020 and expire on 30 September 2023.

28664 Ex-Service Charities organisation member of the Independent Medical Expert Group (IMEG)

The Minister for Defence People and Veterans has appointed Mr Nicholas Bunting as Ex-Service Charities organisation member of the Independent Medical Expert Group (IMEG) for a period of three years. Mr Bunting will be replacing Mr Barry Le Grys. The appointment will commence on 1 September 2020 and will expire on 31 August 2023.
