Details

This guidance has been produced by the Consultative Committee of Accountancy Bodies and is based on law and regulations as of 13 June 2021.

The guidance helps accountancy related businesses meet their obligations for money laundering supervision, including customer due diligence, record keeping and reporting suspicious activity.

More information about recognising and reducing risk of money laundering if you’re an accountancy service provider.
