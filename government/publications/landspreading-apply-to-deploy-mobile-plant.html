Details

Use Landspreading: form LPD1 application for deployment to apply to deploy mobile plant for landspreading waste. We will accept applications using LPD1 version 3 until 19 August 2022 while you update your systems and processes. After that date, please use LPD1 version 4.

Landspreading: form LPD1 guidance explains what information we require to assess your application.

Use these documents to make sure you give us the right information at the right level of detail:

  • Landspreading: produce a benefit statement
  • Landspreading: benefit statement template
  • Landspreading: provide a waste and soil analysis

See also these supporting documents:

  • Landspreading: benefits and risks of the waste types you can use
  • Landspreading: how to manage soil health

Once we have assessed and agreed your application for deployment, you must operate in line with:

  • your environmental permit
  • the agreed deployment form and supporting documents
