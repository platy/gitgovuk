Details

The Future Nuclear Enabling Fund (FNEF) provides £120 million in total to help mature potential nuclear projects ahead of any government process to select the next nuclear projects.

We expect the fund to support a limited number of nuclear projects through grant funding to be awarded following a competitive process.

We are asking the nuclear industry and developers of potential new nuclear projects in the UK:

  • to register their interest in bidding for funding
  • to provide further information on projects to inform bids

We are also inviting nuclear stakeholders who are not planning on bidding for the FNEF to provide information from their experience that will help to mature fund design ahead of opening the bid window in summer 2022.

How to register your interest

If you wish to register your interest to be considered for support through the FNEF, please send the information outlined in Table 6 of the attached document:

  • provide all information as a word or PDF attachment with a maximum of one side per section in Table 6
  • present any financial information in £ sterling

Send your response to us at FNEF@beis.gov.uk, with the subject heading ‘Request for Information Response’ and the name of your project, by Friday 27 May.

Please include contact details for follow up discussions. We are happy to respond to queries at FNEF@beis.gov.uk.

Please coordinate any responses for consortia, with only one response per consortium or prospective consortium.
