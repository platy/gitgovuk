Details

Paper prepared by the DELVE Initiative on nosocomial infections. This paper was available for participants to read at SAGE 45 on 2 July 2020, but not considered or discussed in the meeting. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper is a draft version of the findings, and superseded by version 2 which is available on github.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
