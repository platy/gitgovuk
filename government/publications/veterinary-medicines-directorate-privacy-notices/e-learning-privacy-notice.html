This privacy notice sets out what you can expect from Defra when we process your personal information. This is complemented by Defraâs Personal Information Charter, which broadly sets out our standards for requesting or storing personal information.

1. What data do we collect

The information that we collect from you includes:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Country
  • Job Title

2. Why are we processing your personal data

VMD will collect your name and email to enrol you onto the Learning Management System to allow access to the e-learning material. Country and job title will also be collected to ensure that the training material accessed is relevant to your role.

3. What is the legal basis for processing your personal data

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Article 6 legal basis for processing your data is that you have consented to it.

4. Who will we share your personal data with

We will not be sharing this personal data with any other organisations.

5. Will your data be processed in a country outside of the United Kingdom or European Economic Area (EEA)

Your information will not be shared, transferred or processed outside of the EU.

6. Will your data be processed using automated decision making or profiling

Your information will not be processed using automated decision making or profiling.

7. How long will we keep your data for

Your data will be kept for 5 years.

8. What are the consequences if I do not supply the requested data

If you do not supply the requested personal data, it is more than likely that we will not be able to provide the service you wish to use or are applying for.

9. What are my rights to my personal data

Your rights and our commitments to your personal data are described in the Personal Information Charter as well as on the ICO website.

Where you have consented to the processing of your personal data, you have the right to:

  • To access and request a copy of the information we hold about you
  • To correct inaccurate or complete incomplete personal data we hold about you
  • Be forgotten, and request for us to erase your personal data

10. How to get in contact

For day to day use, please look to contact the team you are already communicating with. They are best placed to manage general enquiries or to update the accuracy of your data or provide you with information.
