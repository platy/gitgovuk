Details

This document explains the provisions of the UK-New Zealand Free Trade Agreement in relation to inclusive trade including:

  • trade and gender equality
  • trade and development
  • labour protections
  • Māori trade and economic cooperation

The agreement is not yet in force. Both the UK and New Zealand are required to complete their respective domestic procedures for the agreement to come into effect. Once approved by both parliaments, businesses will be able to trade under its terms.

An accessible version of this document is being created and will be available soon.
