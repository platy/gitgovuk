The Enabling Technologies Programme is a UK Space Agency funding programme to research and develop new and emerging space technologies, bringing step- change increases in Technology Readiness Levels (TRLs) and building strong foundations to underpin the UK’s global leadership in space technology.

The ETP is open to UK industry, academia and other not-for-profit organisations to build UK capability by supporting the space industry.
