Details

Mupe Bay warning: stay clear of the north half of the beach at Mupe Bay. Due to the possibility of rock fall, for safety you are strongly advised to observe warning signs in the area.

Do not enter the danger area when the range is in use, this is a danger to life. Learn more about accessing MOD training areas safely.

The monthly firing times also includes information on road closures in the surrounding areas.

Grange Hill road closure

From February 2020 military training will take place in the Creech Car Park area 4 times over the year. This means that Grange Hill, the road between Creech and Steeple, will be closed to the public when ranges are active. Exact dates will be published in the monthly firing times programme.
