Details

Publication of the revised code of practice for commercial relationships between landlords and tenants, replacing the previous code. This code of practice aligns with the Commercial Rent (Coronavirus) Bill, providing further guidance on how parties should negotiate, and can be used by any business to help them resolve rent disputes even if they fall outside of scope of the new legislation.
