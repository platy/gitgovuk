To: Access to Work Mental Health Support Service Providers

Subject: Amendments to Access to Work Mental Health Support Service Provider Guidance

Action: For information

Timing: Immediate

Background

1. This memo informs you of amendments that have been made to the Access to Work Mental Health Support Service Provider Guidance.

2. Please see table below for further detail.

Summary and action

3. As these revisions constitute a policy change, please ensure you and your supply chain partners are familiar with the revisions, particularly the changes in Chapter 1 of the AtW MHSS Provider Guidance.

Further Information

All enquiries on this memo should be raised with your Performance Manager in the first instance, they will endeavour to provide you with an answer as soon as possible.

Queries relating to the Provider Guidance should be directed to the Access to Work Provider Guidance Policy inbox: atwproviderguidance.policy@dwp.gov.uk

If your enquiry is of a Commercial nature, please direct it to the Commercial Directorate inbox: commercialdirectorate.employmentcategory@dwp.gov.uk
