Background

1. This memo informs you of amendments that have been made to the Access to Work Holistic Assessments Provider Guidance regarding the procedure for actioning PRaP 14 forms.

2. Paragraphs 5.31.and 5.32 have been revised in line with the new process and para 5.33 is new.

Summary and action

3. As this revision constitutes a policy change, please ensure you and your supply chain partners are familiar with the changes in paragraphs 5.31. 5.32 and 5.33 of the Holistic Assessment (HA) Provider Guidance.

4. All PRaP 14 forms should now be sent to the Access to Work Provider Guidance Policy inbox: atwproviderguidance.policy@dwp.gov.uk.

5. The Access to Work Policy team will consider your request and you will be notified of the outcome.

6. We may need to gather further information to enable us to process your request.

7. If the PRaP 14 request is agreed, the POST team will then take action to remove the incorrect date(s) enabling you to input the correct ones, if required.

Further Information

All enquiries on this memo should be raised with your Performance Manager in the first instance, they will endeavor to provide you with an answer as soon as possible.

Queries relating to the Provider Guidance should be directed to the Access to Work Provider Guidance Policy inbox: atwproviderguidance.policy@dwp.gov.uk

If your enquiry is of a Commercial nature, please direct it to the Commercial Directorate inbox: EMPLOYMENTCATEGORY.COVID19@DWP.GSI.GOV.UK
