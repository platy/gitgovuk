Providers and courses

Course information comes from providers. Always check the details with them directly.

The tables use the terms ‘course delivery’ and ‘course length’ which are explained in this section.

Course delivery

Courses are delivered:

  • face to face
  • online
  • blended (a mix of face to face and online)

Course length

Courses are:

  • short, 8 to 12 weeks
  • medium, 16 to 20 weeks
  • long, 24 to 28 weeks
