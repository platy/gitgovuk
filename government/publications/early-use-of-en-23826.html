Details

This approval recognises the early use of EN 23826:2021.

EN 23826:2021 will be adopted for reference in a future edition of the Agreement Concerning the International Carriage of Dangerous Goods by Road (ADR).

It remains valid until 31 December 2022 unless otherwise amended or revoked.
