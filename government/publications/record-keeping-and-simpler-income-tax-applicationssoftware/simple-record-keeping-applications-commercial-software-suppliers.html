This is a list of software suppliers that have produced simple record keeping applications to help small businesses with record keeping. Some of the applications meet cash basis and simplified expenses specifications, and have been produced after consultation with HMRC. Many of the applications are free.

HMRC cannot recommend or endorse any one product or service over another and will not be responsible for any loss, damage, cost or expense in connection with using this software.

HMRC has not carried out any testing of the listed applications and do not carry out any form of security testing of developer products or services. Customers should ask their suppliers for information about the security aspects of the products and services they provide.
