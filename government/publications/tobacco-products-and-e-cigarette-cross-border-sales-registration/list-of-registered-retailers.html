These tables contain information about the retailers which have registered with the UK government for cross-border distance sales of e-cigarettes and tobacco products.

This is in line with the requirement to publish a list of retailers, including the retailer’s name, trading name, website address, and registration type.

This information is provided by retailers and it’s their responsibility to make sure their information is up to date. Public Health England does not accept responsibility for any broken links caused by retailers closing or moving websites.
