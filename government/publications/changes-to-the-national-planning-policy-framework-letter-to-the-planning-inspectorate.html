The Rt Hon Christopher Pincher MP has written to Sarah Richards, Chief Executive of the Planning Inspectorate to confirm the publication of updated planning practice guidance relating to recent changes to the National Planning Policy Framework. This letter has been published as it raises matters that are likely to be relevant to local authorities.
