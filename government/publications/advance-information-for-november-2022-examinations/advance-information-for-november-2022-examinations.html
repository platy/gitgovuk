Acknowledge by 3 May 2022

Dear Responsible Officer,

I am writing to confirm the date on which you should publish your advance information for the November 2022 exams for GCSE English language and GCSE mathematics.

Requirement to provide advance information

As you are aware, DfE decided, as a matter of policy, that advance information about the focus of the content of exams should be provided for summer 2022 exams for all subjects at GCSE, AS and A level except GCSE English literature, history, ancient history and geography and GCSE, AS and A level art and design. This policy also applies to GCSE English language and GCSE mathematics exams to be taken in November 2022.

Date for publication of advance information

In accordance with GCSE 4.4(a)(i), you must publish information in relation to the content that will be assessed in the November 2022 exams for GCSE English language and GCSE mathematics on 27 June 2022.

Yours sincerely,

Julie Swan
Deputy Chief Regulator
