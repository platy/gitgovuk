Driving test centre waiting rooms: rules during coronavirus (COVID-19)

HTML

Details

Not all driving test centre waiting rooms are open because of COVID-19. The closed waiting rooms are on the list. If a test centre is not on the list, it means that the waiting room is open as normal.

There are temporary rules for using waiting rooms which are open. Check the rules before you go with someone to their driving test.
