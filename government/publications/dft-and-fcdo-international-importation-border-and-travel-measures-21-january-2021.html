Details

Paper prepared by the Department for Transport (DfT) and Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office (FCDO) on international importation and border measures. It was considered at SAGE 77 on 21 January 2021.

The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly. These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
