Ipsos MORI, in association with Simetrica and George Barrett, was commissioned to undertake an evaluation of the SuperConnected Cities Programme (SCCP) in April 2018. This report brings together the findings of the evaluation in a final synthesis report to complement the individual case studies presented as annexes to this report.
