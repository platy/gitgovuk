Details

This leaflet is available for healthcare professionals to download and print for patients who are included in post-discharge surveillance submitted to the UK Health Security Agency.

Details on how patients’ data is collected and used were published by Public Health England via the ‘Surgical site infection surveillance privacy notice’.

Translated versions of ‘Monitoring surgical wounds for infection: information for patients’ were published by Public Health England in Arabic, Bengali, Chinese, Gujarati, Polish, Punjabi and Urdu.
