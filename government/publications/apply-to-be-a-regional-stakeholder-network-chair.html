Details

Please note the deadline for applications for Regional Stakeholder Network (RSN) has now been extended to Friday 2 September 2022 at 5pm.

The Disability Unit is seeking 9 new chairs for our Regional Stakeholder Networks (RSN).

See the Documents section for:

  • an overview
  • an information pack
  • an application form

If you would like to apply, submit your application by Friday 26 August 2022 at 5pm. You can download the form as a Word document, OpenDocument, Easyread or British Sign Language (BSL).
