Apply to be a Regional Stakeholder Network chair

We are inviting people to apply to become a chair of one of our 9 Regional Stakeholder Networks, helping to ensure disabled people's voices are heard throughout England.

From:
Disability Unit and Chloe Smith MP

20 May 2022

The Disability Unit is seeking 9 new chairs for our Regional Stakeholder Networks (RSN).

  • an overview
  • an information pack
  • an application form

If you would like to apply, submit your application by Friday 12 August 2022 at 5pm. You can download the form as a Word document, OpenDocument, Easyread or BSL.
