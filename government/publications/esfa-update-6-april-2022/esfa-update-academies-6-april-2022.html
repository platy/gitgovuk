1. Action: academy NNDR claim form for 2022 to 2023 is now open  

If your academy is continuing to pay the national non-domestic rates (NNDR) bill directly to the billing authority, the claim form is now open for you to re-claim this from ESFA. 

The deadline to submit your claim is 11:59pm on 22 May 2022. 

You can check the NNDR payment process your billing authority is implementing.    

If you have a query about NNDR visit the ESFA help centre.  

2. Information: Academies Accounts Direction 2021 to 2022 

We have published the Academies Accounts Direction 2021 to 2022 - the information for trustees, accounting officers, external auditors and reporting accountants use when preparing and auditing the trust’s annual report and financial statements. Compliance with the Accounts Direction, including the separate model accounts document, is a condition of an academy trust’s funding agreement.    

Details of the main changes to the Academies Accounts Direction are on pages 5 and 6 of the document.  

We have also published a model set of accounts, along with a framework and guide for external auditors and reporting accountants of academy trusts.   

Please take a few minutes to complete the short feedback survey, which will help us to ensure that the Academies Accounts Direction and the model accounts document continue to meet your needs.

6. Information: financial returns go live and submission dates 

Please make a note of the following schedule for financial returns online forms. 

The budget forecast return (BFR) for 2022 will go live on 21 June 2022. The deadline for submission is 26 July 2022, as previously announced. 

The land and buildings collection tool (LBCT) for 2022 will go live on 4 October 2022. The deadline for submission is 8 November 2022. 

The academies accounts return (AR) for 2021 to 2022 will go live on 18 October 2022. The deadline for submission is 19 January 2023.
