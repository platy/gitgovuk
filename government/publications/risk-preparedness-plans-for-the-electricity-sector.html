Details

The risk preparedness plans for Great Britain and Northern Ireland include:

  • defined roles and responsibilities of the BEIS Secretary of State, and tasks delegated to other bodies
  • a description of measures designed to prevent or prepare for the risks identified and how these risks are managed
  • a description of procedures to be followed in an electricity crisis, including corresponding schemes on information flows
  • a summary of market-based measures for coping with electricity crises
  • a summary of non-market-based measures to be implemented in electricity crises, specifying the triggers, conditions and procedures for their implementation
  • details of the mechanisms used to inform the public about electricity crises
