Details

Modelling from the Imperial College London COVID-19 Response Team on easing of restrictions. It was considered at SAGE 80 on 11 February 2021.

The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper should be read alongside the following papers also released under SAGE 80:

  • SPI-M-O: Summary of modelling on scenario for easing restrictions, 6 February 2021
  • University of Warwick: Roadmaps for relaxation of NPIs, 6 February 2021

Updated SPI-M-O statements on modelling on easing restrictions were also discussed at SAGE 81. An earlier statement was discussed at SAGE 79. These also link to corresponding modelling papers from Warwick and Imperial from SAGE 81 and 79 respectively.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
