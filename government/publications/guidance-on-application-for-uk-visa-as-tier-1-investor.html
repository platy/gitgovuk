Details

Use this guidance if you are applying for a visa or want to extend your stay in the UK under Tier 1 (Investor). This document is useful if you want to understand the details of the policy for this tier.

The Tier 1 (Investor) Migrant route is closed to new initial applications for entry clearance or leave to remain. If you make such an application after this date and time it will be void and not considered. This includes no longer being able to switch into this route from other routes.
