1. About the programme

National leaders of further education (NLFEs) provide mentoring and improvement support to colleges as part of the active support outlined in the College oversight: support and intervention guidance.

NLFEs:

  • are serving college leaders who have a strong track record of delivering improvement – both at their own colleges and in working with others
  • determine the best way to support the needs of the college they are working with
  • may use experienced members of their own college staff to help

We are recruiting for finance specialists to become part of the NLFE programme.

To apply use the National Leaders of further education: guidance for potential applicants.

You’ll be invited to attend an induction meeting in London on 11 October 2022 if your application is successful.
