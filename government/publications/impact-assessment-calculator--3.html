Impact assessment calculator: user guide

PDF, 272 KB, 4 pages

Details

Provides help for policy officials to calculate the figures needed for their impact assessment. The calculator automatically generates the main figures needed for the impact assessment summary pages, using the profile of costs and benefits for each option.
