Details

The Department for Digital, Culture, Media and Sport commissioned an independent provider to undertake an evaluation of the Youth COVID-19 Support Fund which was delivered from January to March 2021. The evaluation uses mixed methods to explore the following research questions:

  • Who applied for the fund?
  • Who was the fund provided to and what was its reach?
  • How far and in what ways did the fund meet its objectives?
  • What worked well / less well with the fund?
  • What were the perceived impacts for organisations and young people (if any)?
