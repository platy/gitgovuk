Details

The school-led tuition deadline has been extended to 30 September 2022.

This guidance is for:

  • schools
  • academies
  • independent special schools
  • local authorities
  • virtual school heads

It covers how to complete the year-end statement for the school-led tutoring (SLT) grant.

Use the form on the Education and Skills Funding Agency school-led tutoring guidance page to complete the year-end statement. This page also has the calculator and tracker tool and the school-led tutoring allocations.

Schools that chose not to use the grant must also complete the year-end statement.

Find out more about the school-led tutoring grant.
