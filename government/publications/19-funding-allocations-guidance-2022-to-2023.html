Details

The 19+ allocations technical guidance sets out how we have calculated funding allocations for the 2022 to 2023 funding year for:

  • ESFA funded adult education budget (AEB) including 19 to 24 traineeships
  • advanced learner loans facilities and bursaries
  • 16 to 18 traineeships and bursaries (for providers without 16 to 19 funding)

The allocation calculators can be used to understand your allocation step by step. They are an excel tool for you to enter your data and use in conjunction with the technical guidance.

Further information on 19+ funding allocations is available.
