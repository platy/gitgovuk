OISC Online competence assessments: dates

Details

The Office of the Immigration Services Commissioner (OISC) has a duty to assess the competence of immigration advisers.

The OISC introduced online competence assessments on 14 September 2020.

Details of the assessment dates and the level of competences which will be assessed can be viewed via the link above.
