Details

There are 2 NHS COVID Pass letters:

  • for travel abroad – to show others that you’ve had a full course of the COVID-19 vaccine (including a booster vaccination if you’ve had one) when travelling to some countries or territories abroad
  • for domestic use – to demonstrate your COVID-19 status as a condition of entry to certain venues or events in England

You will be sent the letters you are eligible for. You may be sent a travel letter, a domestic letter or both.

The letters are valid for 30 days from the date you order them. You should get them within 7 working days from the date of request.

If you do not have access to a smartphone, computer or tablet, you can call 119 from within the UK. Select the ‘NHS COVID Pass service’ and ask for an NHS COVID Pass letter to be posted to you.

You should keep the original English language version of your letter safe. You will be asked to show it if you travel abroad or attend a venue or event in England where you must prove your COVID-19 status to enter.
