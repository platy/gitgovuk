Details

Do not enter the danger area when the range is in use, this is a danger to life. Learn more about accessing MOD training areas safely.

Usual firing times for Straight Point Ranges Complex (SPRC)

Summer

Weekdays: 9am to 4pm
Evenings: from 30 mins before dusk until 11.45pm
Weekends: 9am to 12.30pm

Winter

Weekdays and weekends: 9am to 4pm
Evenings: from 30 mins before dusk until 9:30pm
