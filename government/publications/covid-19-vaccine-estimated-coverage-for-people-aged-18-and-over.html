Details

This spreadsheet contains the following estimates for the UK population aged 18 and over:

  • total number and percentage of UK population to date with first dose
  • total number and percentage of UK population to date with second dose
  • total number and percentage of UK population to date with third dose or booster dose

These statistics will be updated daily (excluding weekends or bank holidays).

This daily publication will be discontinued from 31 January 2022.

Vaccinations data is available on Vaccinations in the UK. A UK-wide estimate can be calculated by adding the relevant ages and vaccination counts from individual devolved administrationsâ€™ dashboards.

For more information on how to calculate the UK adults vaccinated estimate, review the cover sheet of the most recent publication.

Should you still require this data, contact Statistics@dhsc.gov.uk
