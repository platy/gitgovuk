Details

This spreadsheet contains the following estimates for the UK population aged 18 and over:

  • total number and percentage of UK population to date with first dose
  • total number and percentage of UK population to date with second dose
  • total number and percentage of UK population to date with third dose or booster dose

These statistics will be updated daily (excluding weekends or bank holidays).
