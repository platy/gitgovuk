Weâ€™re inviting employer representative bodies (ERBs) to apply to lead the development of the local skills improvement plan (LSIP) for Cheshire and Warrington.

To express your interest:

  1. Read the guidance on LSIPs and the role of employer representative bodies.

  2. Complete the expression of interest (EoI) form.

  3. Email the completed form to LSIP.Programme@education.gov.uk.

The deadline to apply is is 23:59 on Friday 23 September 2022.

For updates and frequently asked questions, register for the Jaggaer government portal. You can ask questions up until 23:59 on 16 September 2022.

DfE will be holding an event for interested ERBs at 11am on Monday 12 September. The event will be held at the Department for Education, Piccadilly Gate, Store Street, Manchester M1 2WD.

To attend please register by emailing LSIP.Programme@education.gov.uk by midday on Friday 9 September.

You can also:

  • find out the designated employer representative bodies leading on the development of LSIPs in other areas of the country
  • read the statutory guidance which sets out the processes for the development and review on of a local skills improvement plan (LSIP) by a designated ERB.
