Basic Payment Scheme

  • 1 January 2022: BPS 2022 scheme year begins
  • 15 March 2022: BPS applications open
  • 16 May 2022 (midnight): deadline for BPS 2022 applications without penalty (including Young and New Farmer applications). Deadline to transfer entitlements and to have land at customer’s disposal
  • 10 June 2022 (midnight): deadline for late BPS 2022 applications (with penalty) and for making certain changes without penalty to a BPS application already submitted by midnight on 16 May
  • 31 December 2022: BPS 2022 scheme year ends
  • 1 December 2022 to 30 June 2023: RPA makes BPS 2022 payments

Lump Sum Exit Scheme

  • 12 April 2022: scheme opens for applications
  • 30 September 2022: deadline for applications
  • From 1 November 2022: RPA makes Lump Sum Exit Scheme payments

Countryside Stewardship and Environmental Stewardship claims

  • 15 March 2022: CS and ES revenue claims open
  • 16 May 2022 (midnight): deadline for CS and ES revenue claims without penalty. Deadline to transfer entitlements and to have land at customer’s disposal
  • 31 May 2022 (midnight): deadline for making certain changes to a CS revenue claim that was submitted before midnight on 16 May without receiving a reduction
  • 10 June 2022 (midnight): deadline for late CS and ES revenue claims (with penalty)
  • 1 December 2022 to 30 June 2023: RPA makes CS and ES 2022 payments
  • ES Higher Level Stewardship (HLS) and CS capital claims can be submitted all year

Countryside Stewardship applications

Higher Tier

  • 8 February 2022: applications open
  • 31 March 2022: deadline to request a paper or online application pack
  • 29 April 2022: deadline for applications
  • September 2022 to December 2022: agreement offers are sent out

Mid Tier and Wildlife Offers

  • 8 February 2022: applications open
  • 27 May 2022: deadline to request a paper application pack. No need to request a pack for online applications
  • 29 July 2022: deadline for applications
  • September 2022 to December 2022: agreement offers are sent out

Open all year for applications

  • Protection and Infrastructure (from 8 February 2022)
  • Capital Grants (from 8 February 2022)
  • Woodland Tree Health
  • Woodland Management Plan
