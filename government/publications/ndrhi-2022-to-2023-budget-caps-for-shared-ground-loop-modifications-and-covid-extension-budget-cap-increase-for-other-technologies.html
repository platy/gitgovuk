Details

The non-domestic Renewable Heat Incentive (NDRHI) 2022 to 2023 sectoral budget caps have been announced for shared ground loop modified capacity:

  • £4 million for ‘domestic’ applications
  • £1 million for ‘other’ applications

There is also an increase of £178,000 in the budget cap for ‘other technologies’ COVID-19 extension applications to help progress queued applications.
