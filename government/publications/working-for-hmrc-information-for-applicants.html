Details

If you are thinking about applying for a role at HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) please read Working for HMRC on GOV.UK.

The information on this page can help you with your application.

You can read:

  • Disability Matters: your quick guide to support during our selection process. This provides you with information on the help and assistance we can provide during your application, should you need it
  • Terms and conditions in HMRC
  • General information regarding pay on transfer from other government departments to HMRC
