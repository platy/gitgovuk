Details

We publish our provisional estimates for error and fraud for each of the main COVID-19 support schemes - the Coronavirus Job Retention Scheme, the Self-Employment Income Support Scheme and Eat Out to Help Out - in our Annual Report and Accounts 2020 to 2021.

The methodology and approach paper provides further breakdowns of those provisional estimates and sets out in detail the data sources and methodology for deriving those estimates.

We also set out our overall approach to error and fraud across the schemes.
