Details

Guidance on charges for:

  • permit application
  • varying a permit
  • transferring a permit
  • surrendering a permit
  • annual subsistence charges

This document gives information on how to pay.

You should also read the document Environmental permits and abstraction licences: tables of charges which explains the legal basis for the Environment Agency’s charges. It also gives the:

  • application charge tables for environmental permits
  • subsistence charge tables for environmental permits
  • miscellaneous environmental permitting charge tables

Contact the Environment Agency if you are not sure which charge applies to your operations.
