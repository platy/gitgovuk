The Public Attitudes Tracker (PAT) survey covers public attitudes, awareness and behaviours relating to Department for Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy (BEIS) policies such as energy, climate change and workersâ€™ rights. Kantar, an independent research organisation, has been commissioned to conduct this survey.

The survey began in March 2012 and runs 4 times a year. A new methodology was adopted from Autumn 2021 onwards. The methodology is described in detail in this Technical Report, together with key survey metrics.

If you have questions about the methodology, please contact us at BEISPAT@beis.gov.uk.
