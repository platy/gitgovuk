Details

Paper from SPI-M-O summarising modelling on easing restrictions for England. It was considered at SAGE 80 on 11 February 2021.

The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper should be read alongside the other roadmap papers released under SAGE 80:

  • University of Warwick: Roadmaps for relaxation of NPIs, 6 February 2021
  • Imperial College London: Unlocking roadmap scenarios for England, 5 February 2021

Updated SPI-M-O statements on modelling on easing restrictions were also discussed at SAGE 81. An earlier statement was discussed at SAGE 79.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
