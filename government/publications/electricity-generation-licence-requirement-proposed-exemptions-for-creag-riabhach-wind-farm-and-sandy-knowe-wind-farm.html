Details

A proposal to make exemptions from the requirement to hold a generation licence for:

  • Creag Riabhach Wind Farm Ltd in respect of the Creag Riabhach Wind Farm, a 92.4MW plant located in Scotland in the Higland Council area, at approximately 85km north of Inverness
  • Sandy Knowe Wind Farm Ltd in respect of the Sandy Knowe Wind Farm, a 86.4 MW plant located in Scotland at approximately 1.5km south west of Kirkonnel and Kelloholm, Dumfries & Galloway Council

Please send responses by 15 June 2022 to:

Chris Chown, email: chris.chown@beis.gov.uk.
