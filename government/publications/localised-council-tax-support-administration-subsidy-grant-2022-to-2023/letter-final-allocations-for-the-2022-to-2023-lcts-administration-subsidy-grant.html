Letter from:

Chris Megainey
Local Taxation Division
Department for Levelling Up, Housing and Communities

to:

Chief Finance Directors of local authorities

18 May 2022

Dear Chief Finance Director,

On 29 March, the Department wrote to you setting out the provisional allocations of the Local Council Tax Support Administration Subsidy Grant 2022-23, seeking any comments. I am pleased to now confirm the final grant allocations.

Please find enclosed a copy of the “Local Council Tax Support Administration Subsidy Grant 2022-23 (No 31/6145)”. This specifies the amount of grant due to your council. This grant should be paid on or around 18 May 2022.

If you require any further information regarding the payment, please do not hesitate to contact the Local Taxation team at: council.tax@levellingup.gov.uk.

Chris Megainey
Local Taxation Division
