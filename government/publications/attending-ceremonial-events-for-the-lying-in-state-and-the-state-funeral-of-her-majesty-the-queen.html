Details

This guidance sets out information on ceremonial events in London and Windsor that members of the public can attend.

Further information will be added to this guidance in the coming days.

Wednesday 14 September:

  • Procession to the Lying-in-State of Her Majesty The Queen (Buckingham Palace to the Palace of Westminster). Read guidance about how to watch the Lying-in-State procession

Wednesday 14 September to Monday 19 September:

  • Her Majesty The Queen’s Lying-in-State at the Palace of Westminster (Houses of Parliament). Read guidance about how to attend the Lying-in-State

Monday 19 September:

  • State Funeral procession in London (information to be added shortly)
  • State Funeral procession in Windsor (information to be added shortly)
