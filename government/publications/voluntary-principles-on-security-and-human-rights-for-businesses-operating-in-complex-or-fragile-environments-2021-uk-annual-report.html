Details

This report details the work the United Kingdom undertook in 2021 to implement the Voluntary Principles on Security and Human Rights (Voluntary Principles). The UK produces an annual report on its work as part of its membership of the Voluntary Principles Initiative.

The UK government is committed to supporting the growth, sustainability and impact of the Voluntary Principles Initiative. The Voluntary Principles a key tool in helping implement the UN Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights (UNGP) alongside the UK National Action Plan.
