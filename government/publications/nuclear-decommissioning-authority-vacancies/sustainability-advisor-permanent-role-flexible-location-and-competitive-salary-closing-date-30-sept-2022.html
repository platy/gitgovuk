Role title: Sustainability Advisor

Salary range: Competitive

Function: Operations and Performance Improvement

Role level range: 4-5

Role type: Permanent

Base location: Flexible

Reports to: Sustainability Manager

Closing date: 30 Sept 2022

The role:

Together with the Sustainability Director and Sustainability Manager, you will form a formidable team based on trust and respect who will be the engine of change towards sustainability within the NDA group, representing NDA on sustainability externally too. You will be ensuring that we deliver our mission with care for our people, communities and the environment as committed in the Energy Act and the NDA Strategy 4. You will be supporting and coordinating the delivery of the sustainability strategy for the NDA group.

Key accountabilities:

Co-ordination and leadership (40%):

  • Providing timely support to the NDA Director of Sustainability and NDA Head of Sustainability, in particular on the NDA Sustainability Strategy delivery.
  • Leading Next Generation engagement with Sustainability.
  • Ensuring the smooth organisation of internal and external meetings and events.
  • Producing detailed reporting for measuring the performance of sustainability in the business.
  • Be the focal point and the lead on Sustainability among the wide NDA group Next Generation community.
  • Undertaking general administration tasks within the Sustainability team.

Internal and external engagement; Communications (40%):

  • Presenting the company’s sustainability vision and strategy to internal audiences at events, workshops, and other internal meetings
  • Use internal communication tools to engage with colleagues from across the business (e.g. The NDA intranet and Hub)
  • Support internal awareness raising of the sustainability agenda
  • Working with the NDA Communications teams to developing materials, events, and other communication tools to drive campaigns
  • Developing, running, and analysing the success of internal campaigns that support the growth of the sustainability agenda
  • Developing metrics for measuring the impact of campaigns and producing strategies to improve these
  • Being an ambassador for the NDA and the wider industry, at external events; including conferences, university lectures, and other industry meetings
  • Engaging with external organisations to strengthen the organisation’s position
  • Own and lead a vibrant and enriching debate of Sustainability within the NDA group.

Research and Development (20%):

  • Support production of reports looking at national and local trends and industry specific innovations and developments
  • Working with colleagues across the business to develop insights and recommendations for the business
  • Leads the programme work with the Next Generation representatives from the NDA group, being responsible for the budget allocation and expenditure
  • Supports delivery of the Sustainability Beacon Projects (FY 22/23 and 23/24)
  • Supports effective delivery of the technology programmes including reviewing and input towards investments (e.g. PhDs)
  • Supports budgets for NDA CC sustainability and wider group requirements
  • Works with Sustainability Coordinators and Next Generation representatives across the group.
  • Works with cross departmental representatives with NDA CC to embed sustainability within their functions (e.g. Business cases, HR etc.)
  • Supports engagement with regulators with bi-monthly meetings and support delivery of shared programmes of work for shared benefit.
  • Supports engagement externally (e.g. YGN, NIA, BEIS).

Knowledge, skills and experience required:

  • Shares and demonstrates the NDA values of, respect, openness, collaboration, performance and creativity.
  • Degree or equivalent, with experience in managing and coordinating work across numerous functions/ disciplines.
  • Project management experience.
  • Very good communication, both written and verbal.
  • Strong attention to detail, without losing sight of the big picture.
  • Passion for sustainability and the UNs’ sustainable development goals (SDGs).
  • Able to implement impact strategies.
  • Excellent stakeholder engagement skills.
  • Ability to efficiently research and produce reports and presentations.
