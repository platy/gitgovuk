Role title: Insurance Advisor

Salary range: Competitive

Function: Finance

Role level range: 3-4

Role type: Permanent

Base location: Flexible

Reports to: Head of Group Insurance & Claims

Closing date: 20 September 2022

The role:

You will report to the Head of Group Insurance providing professional and advisory support. You will deliver insurance services; including claims handling, query resolution, analytical reporting and provision of management information. You will collaborate with a large number of functions across the NDA Group and with external service providers.

Responsibilities:

  • Providing professional advice and analytical diagnostic guidance on Insurance-related matters to NDA Group ensuring stakeholders satisfactory settlements
  • Assisting with ad hoc major projects and researching a wide variety of information requests
  • Liaising with stakeholders within NDA businesses and external insurance brokers to obtain and analyse exposure metrics and values for the Annual Renewals
  • Maintaining and continuously reviewing departmental processes, procedures and documentation across NDA Group incorporating innovation
  • Managing Construction declarations to insurers across NDA Group for projects up to £500m
  • Arranging Annual Insurance Workshop (venue / attendees / presenters/ presentation review / hotels) for approx. 70 internal and external delegates.

Knowledge, skills and experience required:

  • Shares and demonstrates the NDA values of, respect, openness, collaboration, performance and creativity
  • Have a high level of initiative to take ownership of queries, ensuring quality of responses
  • Be able to manage a large number and variety of tasks to deadlines
  • Demonstrate influencing, leadership and stakeholder management skills to communicate and resolve complicated and technical insurance issues
  • Able to work unsupervised with a high level of initiative (full training will be given)
  • Display solid communications skills with a customer centric approach and be Excel and Powerpoint proficient
