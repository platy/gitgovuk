Role title: ICT Assurance Analyst

Salary range: Competitive

Total cash: Competitive

Function: Security & Corporate Services (ICT)

Role level range: 6-7

Role type: Permanent

Base location: Flexible

Reports to: ICT Group Assurance Prog Manager

Closing Date: 2 September 2022

The Role:

The NDA is seeking an Assurance Manager to join the team who are innovative, curious and prosper against the challenge of working closely with multiple businesses in the group to conduct analysis, process support and engagement across the NDA group for ICT programmes and projects. This is a key role within the NDA Corporate Centre Business Facing Team (BFT) that will facilitate the planning and operational activity through the NDA governance and assurance processes supporting the NDA group business in the delivery of value ICT transformation management.

Accountabilities:

The role requires strong experience in the following:

  • Ensure strategic alignment and performance of ICT programmes and projects in relation to annual and operational plans for all OpCOs.
  • Working with policy and governance teams to update and assess legislation & policies applied to NDA project operations throughout the NDA.
  • Work with NDA Group ICT teams, (Solution Architects, Service Team, Delivery and other functions including Cyber, to review and assure alignment on Group projects.
  • Working with Information Governance and Cyber security teams on IT lifecycle processes, legislation and associative risks which are key in ensuring governance, control and assurance throughout delivery.
  • Work with suppliers, building relationships to provide projects & Services to the NDA.
  • Ensure that projects do not duplicate functionality or diverge from each other affecting delivery of business and ICT objectives.
  • Facilitate key processes supporting ICT programme activity, performance reviews and NDA business.
  • Identifying and reporting risks and changes around key ICT programmes and projects using appropriate NDA control processes.
  • Attending planning and quality assurance meetings across the NDA Group and OpCOs to understand customer needs, validate product against requirements and ensure that the appropriate levels of governance are in place to successfully deliver across the business.
  • Create a suite of reports assess and validate programme and project output risk and benefit in relation to project objectives.
  • Exceptional communication skills with diverse and adverse audiences - Strong critical thinking and analytical skills.

Knowledge, Skills and Experience required:

  • Demonstrable experience of delivering IT programmes, projects using a structured methodology.
  • Experience in defining IT vision, strategy and developing roadmaps.
  • Excellent influencing skills, together with the ability to engage and motivate others with the ability to communicate at all levels.
  • Excellent relationship building, senior stakeholder management and the ability to work collaboratively to deliver the required outcomes of the role.
  • High resilience and comfortable to deal with ambiguity and change.
  • The ability to think analytically, solve problems to complex business processes and document results.
  • The ability to clearly articulate and present the status of programmes and projects in their strategic context to stakeholders.
  • Experience in a business analysis role who has managed requirements elicitation and developed/re-engineered processes.
  • Ability to live the NDA values of respect, openness, collaboration, performance and creativity.
  • Candidates must either hold or be capable of achieving SC security clearance.
  • Shares and demonstrates the NDA values of, respect, openness, collaboration, performance and creativity
