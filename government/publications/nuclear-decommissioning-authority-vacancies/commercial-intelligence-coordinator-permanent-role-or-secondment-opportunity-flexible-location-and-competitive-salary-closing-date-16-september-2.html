The role:

The NDA Commercial Standards Team is delivering an NDA group-wide project to replace and enhance our commercial IT systems capability and associated ways of working. This includes the commercial IT systems we use to manage our procurement pipelines, for sourcing, for managing our contracts with our supply chain (and customers), for tracking commercial benefits and savings, for identifying and managing supply chain risk, and for strategic supplier relationship management.

Procurement of the new systems suite is divided into five “Lots” which will share data via APIs:

  • Lot A: Source-to-Contract System
  • Lot B: Supplier and Market Intelligence
  • Lot C: Contract Management System for End-to-End Project Management Contracts (typically “Works” contracts)
  • Lot D: Supply Chain Risk Management System
  • Lot E: Reporting, Analytics and Dashboarding System.

The systems suite will fulfil the requirements of the procurement lifecycle, ensuring commercial activity is planned, controlled, and complies with procurement legislation (currently the Public Contracts Regulations 2015). The systems will also ensure risks are identified early e.g. contract expiry and supplier financial distress; enable senior level oversight and confidence in processes and reporting; and fulfil the requirements of the NDA’s Commercial Capability Blueprint and performance against the Government’s Commercial Operating Standards (“GCOS” / CCIAF).

Key accountabilities:

The post holder will coordinate processes for assuring the integrity of data held in the Project Victory systems. They will also guide the development of ways-of-working that continuously and materially improve the quality and accuracy of commercial data in NDA group.

This will involve:

  • Following assurance processes to monitor data integrity in the systems at frequent intervals, and coordinating the process of resolving identified issues
  • Acting as a single point-of-contact for commercial data assurance, for NDA group and central government stakeholders, escalating to team members as appropriate Devising and maintaining a log of data integrity issues
  • Inputting to the design, and then using, a series of outputs (metrics, dashboards, reports) on data integrity in the Project Victory Commercial Reporting system
  • Developing strong working relationships with data managers across NDA group
  • Assisting the Capacity, Capability and SME Agenda Lead with administering the collection and reporting of small business spend data
  • Undertaking ad-hoc analysis of commercial data as driven by business need
  • Coordinating team administrative activities as required by team members (e.g. minute taking, maintaining document repositories on shared intranet spaces)
  • Assisting team members with structured and ad-hoc analysis and reporting activities in the other Project Victory systems, including peer-review quality assurance, and stepping up to provide cover in the event of absence

Knowledge, skills and experience required:

  • Shares and demonstrates the NDA values of, respect, openness, collaboration, performance, and creativity.
  • Working knowledge of Microsoft Excel, familiar with other Office suite programs like Outlook, Word and PowerPoint.
  • Clear and concise communication style, adapted to meet audience needs
  • Working understanding of UK government procurement policies, legislation and processes.
  • Familiar with fundamentals of project management.
  • Collaborative working and development of positive relationships with fellow practitioners.
