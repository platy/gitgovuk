Role title: Contract Manager

Role Level Range: NDA level 6-7

Base Location: The location for this role is flexible

Role Type: Fixed Term Contract role for up to 24 months.

Closing Date:

The Role:

Responsible for managing specified Commercial aspects of existing Spent Fuel Management contracts and leading specified aspects of contract negotiations with minimal supervision to include supporting the management of contracts and commercial relationships associated around a large contract and supporting the AGR Defueling Programme.

Key Accountabilities:

  • To manage the flask maintenance contract for NDA and be one of the key interfaces with EDF and Sellafield Ltd (SL) with respect to the AGR Spent Fuel Management suite of contracts
  • To be the commercial lead on the development of flask maintenance/repair scheduled in line with the contract obligations including any discussions with SL/EDF on flask maintenance rebasing, updates to the package and operations manual and any relevant amendments to the AGR Spent Fuel Management contracts
  • Provide support to SL in discussions/interactions with EDF relating to flask maintenance and defueling matters
  • Support to the AGR Operating Programme (AGROP) and associated working groups on flask related commercial and operational matters
  • Responsible for the preparation of contractual responses, including obtaining necessary NDA Group approvals
  • Manage EDF Audits of the NDA Group under the contracts from and NDA point of view
  • To identify risks and opportunities associated with the AGR Spent Fuel Management contracts and develop appropriate risk management strategies
  • Commercial Lead for NDA at the flask engineering meeting with EDF and SL
  • Commercial Lead for NDA at the flask quality meeting with EDF and SL
  • Member of and prepare/present papers to the SL interface meetings
  • Responsible for QA requirements of the EDF contracts
  • Attend the EDF commercial update meetings
  • To work flexibly as a member of the UK Contracts Team and provide support in other areas as required
  • Maintain and identify improvements in the interfaces and interactions with key stakeholders

Knowledge, Skills and Experience required:

  • Shares and demonstrates the NDA values of, respect, openness, collaboration, performance and creativity
  • Experience of managing complex contracts
  • Experience of negotiating contracts and contract amendments preferred
  • Ability to demonstrate clear and concise presentation and communication skills
  • Capability to produce high quality succinct documentation, reports and dashboards
  • High level of drive and personal integrity
  • Ability to build productive external relationships and network effectively with key personnel across the business
  • Ability to represent NDA in an external environment
  • Ability to quickly grasp and simplify technical issues
  • Understanding of the Nuclear Industry and key business issues affecting NDA, EDF, NTS and Sellafield Ltd would be beneficial
  • Good awareness of key stakeholders and their key drivers, with an awareness of wider stakeholder not affiliated to this role would be beneficial
