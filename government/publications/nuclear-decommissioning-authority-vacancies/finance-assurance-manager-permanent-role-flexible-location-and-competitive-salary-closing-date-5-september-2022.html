Role title: Finance Assurance Manager

Salary range: competitive

Function: Risk and Assurance

Role level range: 6-7

Role type: Ppermanent

Base location: flexible

Reports to: Head of Programme (NLA)

Closing date: 5 September 2022

The role:

To key role supports NDA is its client agent role (on behalf of BEIS) of a multi £Bn 3rd party nuclear decommissioning programme

This role leads and coordinates NDA’s contractual financial management assurance obligations of a 3rd party’s activities to ensure NDA’s contract delivery commitments are met to time and quality and that the NDA’s reputation with regard to its external stakeholders is protected and enhanced.

This role leads and coordinates financial assurance activities to ensure that contract delivery commitments are met to time and quality.

To provide financial assurance in reviewing / challenging the basis of 3rd party’s decommissioning assumptions costs and plans.

Key accountabilities:

To provide first point of contact for financial assurance and finance related contractual assurance matters of a multi £Bn 3rd party nuclear decommissioning programme

Provide assurance and operational reports on the basis of a 3rd party’s planned expenditure and performance in line with specified contractual obligations.

To undertake analysis and audit of 3rd party’s nuclear decommissioning cost plans and expenditure

To manage the day to day relationship with the 3rd party’s financial management teams.

To lead cross-functional assurance reviews in a matrix environment of decommissioning costs, plans, cost allocation methodology and financial arrangements.

Knowledge, skills and experience required:

  • The ability to analyse, interpret and appropriately utilise a range of financial information.
  • Ability to liaise closely and inform NDA’s Executive, senior officers in BEIS (Director level) and other key director level stakeholders.
  • Highly developed interpersonal skills including the confidence and ability to relate to and influence peers and senior stakeholders, to lead cross-functional teams and build and maintain strong credible relationships with colleagues.
  • The role requires financial and management accounting experience and contract management skills.
  • Familarity with cost modelling and Macpherson business critical modeling compliance
  • Educated to degree standard or equivalent
  • Relevant professional qualifications such as MCIPS and/or Qualified CCAB member would be an advantage
  • Practical commercial management experience in a contracting business, manufacturing or commercial operation (or equivalent)
  • Diplomatic, persuasive and unbiased communication skills, negotiation skills and good team working style in cross functional teams.
  • Shares and demonstrates the NDA values of, respect, openness, collaboration, performance and creativity
