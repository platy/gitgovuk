Role title: Risk Manager

Salary range: Competitive

Total cash: Competitive

Function: Risk & Assurance

Role level range: 6-7

Role type: Permanent

Base location: Flexible

Reports to: Group Risk Manager

Closing date: 26 August 2022

The role:

The purpose of Risk and Assurance within the group is to provide independent assessment of the confidence and predictability of mission success. Risk and assurance are also fundamental to informing and underpinning decision making within group governance.

This role is to manage and lead risk management across various corporate departments, businesses, projects and/or programmes through the provision of technical and non-technical input and coaching. Assessing, analysing, reviewing, evaluating and implementing risk policies, processes and procedures.

Accountabilities:

The role requires strong experience in the following:

Deliver the risk management policies and procedures, contributing proactively to organisational risk management strategies and overseeing implementation;

Evaluate the extent to which individual risk strategies are aligned to the overall risk strategy, feeding back on the performance of risk management processes to ensure compliance with agreed processes and procedures and inform future development;

Act as an interface between NDA Corporate Centre and Programme Risk Capability, ensuring lines of communication remain open, to ensure collaborative working;

Advise the business on the benefits and appropriateness of different approaches to managing risk, leading major initiatives;

Provision of timely and accurate reporting of risk information to support decision making across the group.

Knowledge, skills and experience required:

  • Shares and demonstrates the NDA values of, respect, openness, collaboration, performance and creativity
  • An enquiring mind and an appetite to make change happen;
  • Strong analytical skills;
  • Ability to flex their working style in working with individuals at all grades;
  • Strong communication, influencing and persuasion skills at all levels of management;
  • Experience of delivering high quality written reports covering complex issues and data visualisation;
  • An understanding of the latest best practice approaches to Risk Management;
  • Experience of working with senior stakeholders;
  • Knowledge management;
  • Effective project management skills;
  • Professional qualification with proven, direct experience in project risk management across a regulated organisation or industry;
  • Appropriate professional body where appropriate (Membership of IRM, APM or similar), relevant accreditation/experience in project management.

Other knowledge, skills and experience which are advantageous:

  • Risk modelling and analysis for projects / programmes to produce and advise on levels of contingency, having the appropriate level of contingency in place to protect undue risk.
