Details

ACCIA application portal is open

NHS consultant doctors, dentists and academic GPs can now apply for a new national Clinical Impact Award (NCIA) on the ACCIA application portal.

The application window will be open until 5pm on Wednesday 22 June.

Who this guidance is for

The applicants’ guide is for NHS consultant doctors, dentists and academic GPs applying for a new national Clinical Impact Award in 2022.

The employers’ guide is to help employers who are supporting an application for an award.

The nominators’ guide is for national nominating organisations, recognised by ACCIA, who are providing a supporting citation for an application.

The assessors’ guide is for anyone assessing applications for a national Clinical Impact Award.
