Airmyn foreshore stabilisation

Announcement of intention not to prepare an environmental statement: Airmyn foreshore stabilisation

Details

The Environment Agency gives notice that it proposes to carry out improvement work to the flood defences on the River Aire in Airmyn between Airmyn Clock Tower (NGR SE 72496 25530) and the access gate opposite 97 High Street (NGR SE 72326 25002), a distance of 0.5 kilometres.

The proposed improvement work will involve stabilisation of 5 areas of riverbank in front of the existing flood defences in Airmyn. This involves driving piles at the toe of the riverbank and placement of rock armour on the riverbank.

This is in accordance with Regulation 5 of the Environmental Impact Assessment Land Drainage Improvement Works (Amended) Regulations 1999 SI 1999/1783.
