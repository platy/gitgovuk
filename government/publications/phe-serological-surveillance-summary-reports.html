Details

Supporting papers written by PHE on serological surveillance. The following papers were available for participants to read, but not considered or discussed in the following meetings:

  • PHE: Serological surveillance - summary report 4, 12 May 2020 was available at SAGE 36 on 14 May 2020.

  • PHE: Serological surveillance - summary report 5, 19 May 2020’ was available at SAGE 38 on 21 May 2020.

  • PHE: Serological surveillance - summary report 6, 27 May 2020 was available at SAGE 39 on 28 May 2020.

  • PHE: Serological surveillance - summary report 7, 3 June 2020 was available at SAGE 40 on 4 June 2020.

  • PHE: Serological surveillance - summary report 8, 10 June 2020 was available at SAGE 41 on 11 June 2020.

  • PHE: Serological surveillance - summary report 9, 17 June 2020 was available at SAGE 42 on 18 June 2020.

  • PHE: Serological surveillance - summary report 12, 9 July 2020 was available at SAGE 46 on 9 July 2020.

  • PHE: Serological surveillance - summary report 13, 15 July 2020 was available at SAGE 47 on 16 July 2020.

  • PHE: Serological surveillance - summary report 18, 24 September 2020 was available at SAGE 59 on 24 September 2020.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
