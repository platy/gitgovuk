The Lord Chancellor, having consulted the Lord Chief Justice, directs that Medway County and Family Court will, for a period of 18 months starting on 14 June 2021, sit at Holiday Inn Rochester - Chatham, Maidstone Rd, Rochester ME5 9SF.
