Details

This register holds information about your holding, tags and replacement tags, and all movements of sheep and goats. We have produced a printable version of the register to assist keepers to record the required information.

A separate holding register is required for each different holding. It must:

  • record movements of sheep born after 31 December 2009 individually, unless they are identified with a slaughter tag
  • record details of any replacement identifiers (section 2), including any cross-references where appropriate

Hard copies are available from the Defra Helpline on 03459 33 55 77, quoting reference PB13281. Due to the impact of coronavirus (COVID-19) and homeworking, there may be a short delay in these being issued.

Electronic version

The Livestock Information Service (LIS) online movement reporting service for sheep and goats has a user friendly and feature rich holding register function.
