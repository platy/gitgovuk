Details

When you arrive in England, you must travel directly to the place where you’re staying and self-isolate for 10 days. The 10-day period is counted from the day after you leave the departure country, territory or island.

This guidance sets out:

  • who must self-isolate
  • how you should travel to the place where you are self-isolating
  • how to self-isolate
  • support to help you self-isolate
  • what to do if you get coronavirus symptoms
