Details

The Trade and Cooperation Agreement (TCA) Partnership Council oversees UK and EU implementation, application and interpretation of the Trade and Cooperation Agreement.

This document contains the second decision of the TCA Partnership Council. The Council have agreed a decision on the extension of the interim period arrangements for UK processing of EU Passenger Name Record (PNR) data.
