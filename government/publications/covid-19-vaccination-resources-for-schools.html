Details

These guidance documents have been produced to assist school staff, headteachers and parents with any questions about the coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccination programme.

Information leaflets on the vaccination for children and young people are available to download or order for free:

  • COVID-19 vaccination: a guide for eligible children and young people aged 12 to 17
  • information for children and young people on what to expect after COVID- 19 vaccination

COVID-19 vaccination programme for children and young people: guidance for parents

Copies of the version 3 leaflet in English are available to order. Translated versions will be available shortly to order.

Copies of version 1 of this leaflet are available to order or download in the languages below.

Albanian, Arabic, Bengali, Brazilian Portuguese, Bulgarian, Chinese, Estonian, Farsi, French, Greek, Gujarati, Hindi, Latvian, Lithuanian, Panjabi, Pashto, Polish, Romanian, Romany, Russian, Somali, Spanish, Tagalog, Turkish, Twi, Ukrainian, Urdu and Yiddish.

COVID-19 vaccination programme for children and young people: guidance for schools

Copies of version 3 are available to order for free from Health Publications.

The COVID-19 vaccination invitation letter template can be downloaded and adapted to suit the needs of local healthcare teams. It should be sent to parents of eligible children, along with an accompanying information leaflet and consent form.
