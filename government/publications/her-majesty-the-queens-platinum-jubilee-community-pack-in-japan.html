Details

Learn about what UK Government is doing to mark The Queen’s Platinum Jubilee in the UK and Japan. The community pack aims to inspire communities in Japan to join The Queen’s Platinum Jubilee celebrations.

Get involved in the celebrations in Japan by hosting your own Platinum Jubilee event or activity. This community pack provides key links to ideas and information so you can be a part of this historic celebration.

The pack includes:

  • an information pack: includes details of the UK and Japan celebrations and how you can get involved
  • an educational materials pack: presents fun and educational ideas for celebrating the Jubilee in schools and classrooms

Follow us on Facebook and Twitter to stay up-to-date on the latest activities related to The Queen’s Platinum Jubilee. Share your celebrations with us by using our Platinum Jubilee hashtags.
