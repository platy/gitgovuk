Her Majesty The Queen’s Platinum Jubilee Community Pack in Japan

This information pack provides details of the Platinum Jubilee events taking place in the UK and Japan. It aims to inspire communities in Japan to join in with these celebrations and provides ideas of how the public can get involved. The pack of educational materials presents fun and educational ideas for celebrating the Jubilee in schools and classrooms.
