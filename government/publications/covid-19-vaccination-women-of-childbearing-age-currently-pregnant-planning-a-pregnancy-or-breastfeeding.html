Details

Leaflet for all women of childbearing age, those currently pregnant or breastfeeding on coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccination.

All women of childbearing age must read this leaflet before you go for vaccination.

Copies of the English leaflets are available to order using product code COV2020374V2 or COV2020374V2LP for the large print version.

Translated copies are available to order and more languages will be available soon.
