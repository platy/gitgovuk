Details

This protocol is for the administration of Comirnaty® 10 microgram/dose concentrate for dispersion for injection COVID-19 mRNA vaccine to children aged 5 to 11 years by appropriately trained persons.

National protocol for Comirnaty® 10 microgram/dose COVID-19 mRNA vaccine v01.00 is valid from 22 January 2022 to 31 March 2022.
