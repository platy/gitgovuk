Details

The coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccination consent form and letter templates are available in different software versions and can be downloaded. The letter templates can be adapted to suit the needs of local healthcare teams.

Copies of the adult consent form (PDF version) are available to order using product code COV2020376V2.

A British Sign Language (BSL) video explaining the COVID-19 vaccination consent form is available to view and download.
