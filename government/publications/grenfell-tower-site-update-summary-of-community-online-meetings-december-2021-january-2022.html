Details

At our community online meetings in December 2021 and January 2022, we updated on the future of Grenfell Tower, and tower site maintenance and safety works. We gave an update on further soil sampling at two locations, the relocation of the local electricity substation in Grenfell Tower, and local opportunities available from the Grenfell Tower site principal contractor, DUK. We also gave a reminder about the health and wellbeing services available to the community.
