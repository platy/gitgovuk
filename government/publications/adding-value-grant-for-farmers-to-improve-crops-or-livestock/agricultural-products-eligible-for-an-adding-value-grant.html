This is a list of eligible agricultural products under the Adding Value grant. The products are taken from the list for agricultural products referenced in Annex 1 of the World Trade Organisation (WTO) Agreement on Agriculture.

Some chapters, or sub-headings of the annex, list ineligible products, for example, fish or fish products. Therefore, only the products listed in this table are eligible for an Adding Value grant.
