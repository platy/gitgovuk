Details

We have recently consulted on changes to our charging scheme. If government approves the changes they will take effect in England from 1 April 2022.

Applications received before the 31 March 2022

The current charges will apply to any applications we receive before 31 March 2022.

Applications received on or after 1 April 2022

The new charges will apply to any applications we receive on or after 1 April 2022.

Applications we reject

We may reject your application if you do not provide enough information. If you reapply with the missing information, but we do not receive this by 31 March 2022, the new charges will apply.

Find full details on the Water resources charge proposals from April 2022.

Regional charges (standard unit charges and environmental improvement unit charges) for water abstraction licences from April 2020.

We have made 3 changes to this year’s scheme, the:

  • source factor for sources supported by the Kielder Reservoir transfer scheme
  • standard unit charge for the Dee regional charging area
  • standard unit charge for the Wye regional charging area
