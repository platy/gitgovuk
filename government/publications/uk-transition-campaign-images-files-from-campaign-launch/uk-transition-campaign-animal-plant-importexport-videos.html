Videos about the new rules for moving plants, animals, and products of animal origin, now that the Brexit transition period has ended.

1. Exporting animals or products of animal origin from GB to the EU

This video looks at:

  • the new rules for moving animals or products of animal origin from GB to the EU
  • how to register for an Export Health Certificate (EHC)

Please note; the guidance in this video does not apply to Northern Ireland. If sharing on your channels, please include the following disclaimer in the accompanying post copy:

  • Exporting these products from Northern Ireland to the EU will not change.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gXpmY5ejEzM

2. Moving plants between GB and the EU

This video looks at:

  • the new rules for moving plants into, or out of, Europe
  • how to register for the relevant plant I.T. systems for exporting and importing

Please note; the guidance in this video does not apply to Northern Ireland. If sharing on your channels, please include the following disclaimer in the accompanying post copy:

  • The way you move these products between Northern Ireland and the EU will not change.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AuhoEwNfu08
