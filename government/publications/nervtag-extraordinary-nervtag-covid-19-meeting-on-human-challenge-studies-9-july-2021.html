Details

This is a note from the record of the discussion at the NERVTAG extraordinary meeting on human challenge studies on 9 July 2021. It was considered at SAGE 94 on 22 July 2021. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

This paper is a summary note supplement to the NERVTAG Extraordinary meeting minutes. These documents discuss Human Challenge Studies, available as a pre-print from ResearchSquare

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
