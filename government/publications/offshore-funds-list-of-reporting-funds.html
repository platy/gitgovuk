Details

This is a list of offshore funds that have successfully applied to HMRC for reporting fund status.

The list is updated on a monthly basis and includes details of the:

  • reporting fund reference
  • previous distributing fund reference
  • name
  • sub fund reference
  • International Securities Identification number
  • Stock Exchange Daily Official List number
  • Committee on Uniform Securities Identification Procedures number
