Details

The Coronavirus Community Support Fund formed part of the overall £750 million government funding package of support for the voluntary, community and social enterprise sector during the pandemic.

The ‘Coronavirus Community Support Fund (CCSF) Grantholder Learning Hub’ was open to all recipients of the CCSF and National Lottery emergency funding. Some 3,200 organisations came together online to share lessons learnt during the pandemic.

The Learning Hub reports contain their reflections and noted areas of improvement for the sector as a whole.

All of the reports, including the impact report and value for money report, can be found on The Coronavirus Community Support Fund Evaluation and Grantholder Learning Hub.
