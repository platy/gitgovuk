Details

We have published an initial list of level 2 ICT user and related qualifications and level 2 ICT functional skills qualifications that will have funding approval removed for new starts from 1 August 2022.

We have also published guidance for awarding organisations to provide background and information to support organisations with the notification to appeal process. All notifications to retain a qualification for funding approval must be submitted no later than 11:59am on Friday 8 April 2022.
