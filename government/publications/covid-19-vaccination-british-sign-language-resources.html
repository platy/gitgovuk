Details

British Sign Language (BSL) videos on COVID-19 vaccination.

These videos are available to download from Health Publications.

The COVID-19 vaccination leaflets, consent form and the COVID-19 vaccination and blood clotting leaflet on which these videos are based on are available to download. Paper copies are also available to order.

A BSL video of the COVID-19 vaccination observation period leaflet is available to download.

A BSL video of the COVID-19 vaccination: a guide to a second dose for young people aged 16 to 17 is available to download.
