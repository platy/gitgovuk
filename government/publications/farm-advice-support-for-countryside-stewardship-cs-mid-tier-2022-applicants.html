Details

Support and advice is available by booking onto an on-line event (webinar) for your area from the contact details attached. Applicants are also encouraged to book a slot for a clinic session to help consider which options are suitable for their holding. These clinic sessions last up to 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Booking is essential as places will be limited.
