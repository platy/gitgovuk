Details

Members of the Poultry Health Scheme are approved poultry establishments.

A poultry premises must have membership of the PHS to export to the EU, Northern Ireland and some non-EU countries, or sell to other PHS members:

  • breeding poultry
  • day old chicks
  • hatching eggs

An official vet will refer to this list when completing the export health certificate.

Read the guidance on how to register for the PHS.
