Details

The T Level Transition Programme provides a high-quality route onto T Levels for students who would benefit from the additional study time and preparation that it will give them before they start their T Level.

In December 2021, we published guidance for providers delivering the T Level Transition Programme from academic year 2022 to 2023 onwards:

  • Framework for Delivery setting out information and expectations for how the programme should be delivered from academic year 2022 to 2023 onwards
  • national technical outcomes for the first 3 waves of T Level routes
  • a resource that providers can use to help design and deliver effective route-based projects on the programme
  • a work experience template that providers, employers and students can use to design and deliver work experience placements on the programme
  • a guide for prospective students, setting out what they can expect from the programme

In September 2022, we published:

  • national technical outcomes for the fourth wave of T Level routes (available to deliver from 2023)

Future updates to the Framework for Delivery and accompanying guidance and resources will be made as required.

See T Levels: next steps for providers for further information on delivering T Levels and the T Level Transition Programme.
