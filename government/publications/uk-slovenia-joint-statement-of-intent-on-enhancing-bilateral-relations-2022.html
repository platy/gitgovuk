Details

This joint statement enhance the existing relationship between the UK and Slovenia across 3 important pillars:

  • foreign and security policy
  • economic cooperation
  • science, education and culture

It also commits both countries to support the rights of citizens protected under the Brexit Withdrawal Agreement.
