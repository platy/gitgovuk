Details

This guidance sets out the terms and conditions applied to the targeted support payments for state-funded schools and 16 to 19 organisations with 1 to 600 pupils that employ teachers and leaders who take national professional qualifications (NPQs) from October 2022.

The payments of £200 per teacher or leader will be made to schools directly, or local authorities for local authority-maintained schools.

The payment will be made in summer 2023 or summer 2024 depending on which academic year the participant starts the course.

This guidance is for:

  • headteachers
  • schools staff
  • local authorities

It applies to:

  • academies and free schools
  • city technology college
  • community schools
  • foundation schools
  • further education colleges
  • pupil referral units
  • secure units
  • service children’s education
  • sixth form colleges
  • special schools and special post-16 institutions
  • studio schools
  • university technical college
  • voluntary aided and voluntary controlled schools

You can read further information on targeted support funding, including a list of 16 to 19 organisations eligible for targeted funding in the academic year 2022 to 2023.

You can read more information about National professional qualifications (NPQs) reforms.

For any further information, contact continuing-professional-development@digital.education.gov.uk.
