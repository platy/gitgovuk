Details

The Secretary of State for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs, George Eustice MP encourages food, drink and environmental services industries to test their staff for coronavirus by registering for free testing kits provided by the government.

These tests are for staff who cannot currently work from home. Only businesses registered in England can apply for free testing.
