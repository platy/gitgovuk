This guidance provides advice for drivers, operators and owners of taxis or private hire vehicles (PHVs) who choose to fit a screen or barrier to their vehicle(s) to reduce the spread of coronavirus (COVID-19).

Advice on how to carry out a risk assessment and implement various control measures is available at Coronavirus (COVID-19): taxis and PHVs.
