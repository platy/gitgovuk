Details

UK-Guernsey memorandum of understanding on arbitration under Article 25 of the Agreement

The memorandum was signed on 16 June 2022 and has not yet entered into force.

2021 UK-Guernsey exchange of letters — in force

The provisions of Article 27, concerning assistance in the collection of taxes entered into force on 1 January 2022 following an Exchange of Letters between the UK and Guernsey under Article 28(2)(b) of the Agreement.

It is effective in the UK and Guernsey from 1 January 2022.

2018 Guernsey-UK Double Taxation Agreement — in force

The Double Taxation Agreement and Protocol entered into force on 7 January 2019. It was signed in London on 2 July 2018.

It is effective in the UK from:

  • 6 April 2019 for Income Tax, for any year of assessment beginning on or after this date
  • 1 April 2019 for Corporation Tax

It is effective in:

  • the UK and Guernsey for taxes withheld at source, for amounts paid or credited on or after 1 March 2019
  • Guernsey for Income Tax, for any year of assessment beginning on or after 1 January 2020

2009 Tax Information Exchange Agreement as amended by the 2013 Protocol — in force

The Tax Information Exchange Agreement (TIEA) and Protocol amending the 1952 Double Taxation Arrangement entered into force on 27 November 2009.

Guernsey-UK Double Taxation Arrangement as amended by the 2009 arrangement — in force

The Guernsey-UK Double Taxation Arrangement as amended by the 2009 arrangement entered into force on 29 July 2014.
