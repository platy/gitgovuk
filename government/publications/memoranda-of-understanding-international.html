Details

We work closely with the Department of International Trade, the government department that secures UK and global prosperity by promoting and financing international trade and investment.

We also liaise with the Science and Innovation Network, set up by the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office, to encourage exchange between science communities around the world and foster closer links between science and business.
