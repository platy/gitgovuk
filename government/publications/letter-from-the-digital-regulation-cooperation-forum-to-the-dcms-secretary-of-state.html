The attached letter shows correspondence between Gill Whitehead, DRCF CEO, on behalf of the member regulators of the DRCF (the Competition and Markets Authority, the Financial Conduct Authority, the Information Commissioner’s Office and the Office of Communications) and the Rt Hon Nadine Dorries.

It sets out the response of the DRCF to a letter from the Rt Hon Nadine Dorries in which she set out the government’s priorities for the digital regulatory landscape and cross-cutting policy areas relevant to the DRCF’s work.
