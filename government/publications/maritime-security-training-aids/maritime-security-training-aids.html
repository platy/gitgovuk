We have a series of training films and other guidance material to assist in the security training of personnel working at UK ports This applies to:

  • passenger ships and cargo ships engaged on international voyages
  • port facilities that service international passenger and cargo ships
  • certain other domestic ships and associated port facilities on the basis of risk assessments, as extended by UK regulations

Requesting training materials

Training materials are only available for:

  • Department for Transport (DfT) accredited Port Facility Security Officer training providers and with agreement of DfT, those giving training to port security staff
  • Maritime and Coastguard Agency (MCA) approved training providers for Standards of Training, Certification and Watch-keeping (STCW) security training and with agreement of the MCA vessel owners and operators giving onboard security training to vessel crews

Training materials are available by contacting MaritimeSecurityTraining@dft.gov.uk.
