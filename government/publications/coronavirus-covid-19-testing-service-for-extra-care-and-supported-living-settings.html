Details

The UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) offers regular testing to extra care and supported living (ECSL) settings in England. We are offering routine testing to reduce infection risk in ECSL settings and to identify any positive cases in these settings as early as possible.

Eligible ECSL settings must meet at least 1 of 2 criteria:

  • the setting is a closed community with substantial facilities shared between multiple people
  • it is a setting where the majority of residents (more than 50%) receive the kind of personal care that is CQC-regulated (rather than help with cooking, cleaning and shopping)

ECSL settings managers can order tests online using their Unique Organisation Number (UON). To get a UON, managers can register their eligible centre on the self-referral portal. See the guidance document on this page for further information.
