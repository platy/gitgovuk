The Qualification

Overall Objective for the Qualifications

This handbook relates to the following qualification: Level 2 Award In Introduction to Leading and Supervising

This qualification provides the standards that must be achieved by individuals that are working within the Armed Forces.

Pre-entry Requirements

Learners who are taking this qualification will need to working within a leading and supervisory role.

Unit Content and Rules of Combination

This qualification is made up of a total of 1 mandatory unit. To be awarded this qualification the candidate must achieve a total of 4 credits as shown in the table below.

Mandatory Unit

URN Unit Title Level Credit Value GLH TQT
T/507/3093 Principles of Leading and Supervising 2 4 32 40

Age Restriction

This qualification is available to learners aged 18 years and over.

Opportunities for Progression

This qualification creates a number of opportunities for progression through career development and promotion.

Exemption

No exemptions have been identified.

Learning outcomes and Assessment criteria

Learning outcomes Assessment criteria
The learner will: The learner can:
1. Understand the differences between leading and supervising 1.1 Explain the roles and responsibilities of leading a team 1.2 Describe the skills associated with leadership 1.3 Explain the difference between Commanding, leading and managing 1.4 Describe the differences between leading and supervising
2. Understand key issues of leadership 2.1 Describe issues that affect leadership 2.2 Explain ways of managing conflict 2.3 Describe the links between leadership skills and motivation
3. Understand the implications of delegating work to others 3.1 Describe the purpose of delegation 3.2 Describe ways of co-ordinating delegated work 3.3 Explain possible leadership barriers to delegation
4. Understand the purpose of setting work objectives with team members 4.1 Explain your own and employees contributions to the organisation’s objectives 4.2 Describe the value of setting agreed work objectives with team members 4.3 Describe ways of providing support to meet work objectives with team members
5. Understand the organisations welfare support functions 5.1 Describe where to find the organisations community and welfare support policies and documentation 5.2 Describe the appropriate support and guidance for different welfare issues 5.3 Explain the organisations drug and alcohol misuse policy
6. Understand the organisations performance management processes 6.1 Describe the organisation’s annual reporting system. 6.2 Explain the impact of annual reports on employees career 6.3 Explain why we have a Defence Writing policy 6.4 Describe the organisations disciplinary process
7. Understand the need for good presenting skills 7.1 Explain the effective requirements of a presentation 7.2 Carry out a short presentation

Additional information about the unit
Unit purpose and aim(s) This unit is about understanding leading and supervising within the organisation. Understanding performance and welfare issues and being able to use presentation skills effectively.
Assessment requirements specified by a sector or regulatory body (if appropriate) Assessed must be conducted in the workplace, in a training environment. Simulation is not permitted for this unit.
