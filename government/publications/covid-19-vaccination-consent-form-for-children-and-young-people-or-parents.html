Details

The coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccination consent form for children and young people or their parents and carers are available in different software versions and can be ordered or downloaded.

A template letter is available for the schools COVID-19 vaccination programme. Information leaflets aimed at children and young people are available to download or order.

The easy-read consent form is aimed at children and young people who have a learning disability is available for download only. Further easy-read resources for children and young people are available to download.
