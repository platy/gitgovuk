Final Report

HM Treasury is committed to regularly reviewing its relationship with Pool Re to ensure that it remains in the best interests of Pool Re and its members, the government, the taxpayer, and the wider economy. This report concludes the review and details the outcomes, setting the strategic direction for Pool Re for the coming five years.

Call for Evidence Response

The Call for Evidence has concluded, and a summary of the responses has been published. The Call for Evidence covered how the guarantee HM Treasury extends to Pool Re impacts the market for terrorism (re)insurance and how any amendments to the terms of the guarantee and the rules that govern HM Treasury’s relationship with Pool Re would impact the availability, affordability and take-up of terrorism insurance.
