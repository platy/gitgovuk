Information Rights appeals: register of tribunal cases

A list of Information Rights appeals currently being dealt with by the General Regulatory Chamber, including Freedom of Information cases.

Documents

First-tier Tribunal (General Regulatory Chamber) Information Rights appeals register (as at 6 June 2022)

HTML

Details

The list includes the following details for each case:

  • appeal number
  • the person or organisation who made the appeal
  • Information Commissioner’s Office reference number
  • the public authority involved in the case
  • any joined parties
  • details of any upcoming hearings
  • the outcome of the appeal, where relevant

Cases will be removed from this register 14 days after the decision is published.
