Overview

Sign up for the school business professional (SBP) newsletter for occasional updates about SBP initiatives than can help you in your role.

You can use the School Business Professional Networks Directory to:

  • find groups in your area
  • get peer-to-peer support for school business tasks such as buying for schools
  • contact leads in your area (please use contact details for network enquiries only)

Email schools.commercial@education.gov.uk if you cannot find a network in your area or wish to add your own network to the directory.

Guidance on creating a network for school business professionals is also available.

Please note that being included in the directory does not constitute a recommendation by the Department for Education (DfE) for the network, forum or any commercial suppliers. DfE accepts no responsibility for the accuracy or content provided by individual networks or forums listed on this directory or for the promotion of any commercial supplier goods or services.
