Correspondence overview: Letter to Make UK and BCC

A letter to Make UK and the British Chambers of Commerce in relation to disruption to container shipping.

Published

Documents

Letter to Make UK and BCC

PDF, 121 KB, 4 pages

Details

Make UK and the British Chambers of Commerce (BCC) wrote to the CMA to share concerns about the impact to British businesses of increased costs and supply disruption in relation to container shipping. This letter explains the actions that the CMA has taken to understand these issues and includes details of how affected can share relevant intelligence with the CMA.
