Details

The Home Secretary wrote to Cressida Dick, Commissioner of the Metropolitan Police Service (MPS), on 15 June 2021 and received a reply dated 29 July 2021.

Cressida Dick wrote to the Home Secretary and the Mayor of London on 27 January 2022 to provide them with an interim update ahead of a more significant report expected in March 2022.
