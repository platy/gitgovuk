Details

Transparency information about outside appointments or employment taken up by former members of the Foreign and Commonwealth Office (FCO) at SMS1 and SMS2 level and equivalents (including special advisers of equivalent standing), and which fall within the scope of the business appointment rules.

During the period July to September 2018 the FCO did not issue any decisions on applications submitted under the Business Appointment Rules.
