Following the selection of Hynet and East Coast Cluster as Track 1 CCUS clusters in November 2021, we have selected the power CCUS, industrial carbon capture (ICC), waste and CCUS-enabled hydrogen projects to proceed to the due diligence stage of the Phase-2 Cluster Sequencing process.

The 20 projects represent a range of innovative CCUS technologies that have the potential to:

  • accelerate our decarbonisation ambitions
  • realise economic benefits in the North West, North Wales, Teesside and Humber regions
  • kick start the hydrogen economy
  • and put us on a path to decarbonising our power system by 2035, while maintaining security of supply

This shortlist does not imply availability of funding for any or all of the shortlisted projects, but is purely the outcome of assessment against the Phase 2 criteria.

We will share further information on Track 2 in due course.
