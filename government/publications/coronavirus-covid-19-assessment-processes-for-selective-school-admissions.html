COVID-19: assessment processes for selective school admissions

Details

This guidance is for:

  • grammar schools
  • partially selective ‘bilateral’ schools
  • schools which band applicants by ability to achieve a comprehensive intake
  • schools which select up to 10% of their cohort by aptitude in a prescribed subject

  8. 6 July 2021

    We have published new guidance which comes into force from Step 4 of the Roadmap. Until Step 4 the current guidance (dated May 2021) remains in place. The new guidance advises that the protective measures detailed will only be required where the test venue is in an area of particular concern offered an enhanced response package to help limit COVID-19 transmission, where local authorities, directors of public health or PHE health protection teams recommend the measures are adopted as part of their outbreak management responsibilities or where a school admission authority chooses to adopt those protective measures following a risk assessment.
