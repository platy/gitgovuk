Details

This interim guidance will continue to be updated and used as the basis for changes made to relevant excise public notices.

You can find out:

  • about changes to rebated diesel and biofuels
  • how to prepare for the changes
  • if you can use rebated fuel from 1 April 2022
  • how HMRC will carry out compliance checks

If you’re no longer able to use rebated fuel, and you’re unable to use a cleaner alternative, you will need to use diesel or biofuels, which has had full excise duty paid (including white diesel).

This new approach will help to make sure fairness between the different users of diesel fuels. The tax system also encourages the development and adoption of greener alternative technologies. It should also encourage fuel users to improve the energy efficiency of their vehicles and machines, or to use less fuel.

You can sign up to receive email alerts which will notify you when the guidance on rebated fuels is updated.
