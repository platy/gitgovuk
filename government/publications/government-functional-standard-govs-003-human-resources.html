Details

The purpose of the HR Government Functional Standard is to set expectations for the leadership and management of human resources across-government, ensuring people are recruited, developed and deployed to meet the government’s needs.

For more guidance on the Government HR Functional Standard, please contact Civil Service HR: hrfunction@cabinetoffice.gov.uk
