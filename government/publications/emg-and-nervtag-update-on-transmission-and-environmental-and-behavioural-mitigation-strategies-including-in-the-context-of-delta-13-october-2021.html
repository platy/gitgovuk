Details

Paper by EMG and NERVTAG on transmission with context of the Delta variant. It was considered at SAGE 96 on 14 October 2021. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.

An updated version of this paper was uploaded 26 November 2021 to clarify some caveats around some of the evidence. The key messages and conclusions of the paper do not change. Any changes are signposted in the footnotes.
