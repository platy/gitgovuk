Details

This grant provides additional funding that publicly funded schools in England use to support disadvantaged pupils. Schools and local authorities must follow the terms and conditions set out in the pupil premium conditions of grant.

Published allocations

We’ve published spreadsheets showing allocations at school and also national, local authority and parliamentary constituency level for the 2021 to 2022 financial year.

Further information

Read more about how much pupil premium funding schools and non-mainstream schools receive, how they should spend it, and how we hold them to account.
