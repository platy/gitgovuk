Details

Public Health England (PHE) has undertaken rapid assessments of commercially provided diagnostic tests for SARS-CoV-2 nucleic acid detection with further evaluations ongoing.

Included here are:

  • a summary report for the laboratory assessments of COVID molecular diagnostic tests undertaken by PHE and completed in the period February and April 2020
  • a series of individual assay assessments
