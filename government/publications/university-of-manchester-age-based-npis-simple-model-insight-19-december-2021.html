Details

Paper prepared by the University of Manchester on non-pharmaceutical interventions (NPIs). This paper was available for participants to read at SAGE 101 on 23 December 2021, but not considered or discussed in the meeting. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

An earlier version of this paper was published as an annex within SCWG Chairs: Summary of role of shielding, 20 December 2021, available at SAGE 100.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
