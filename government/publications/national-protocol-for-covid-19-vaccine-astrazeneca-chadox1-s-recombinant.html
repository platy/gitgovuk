This protocol is for the administration of coronavirus (COVID-19) vaccine AstraZeneca (ChAdOx1-S [recombinant]) by appropriately trained persons.

National protocol for AstraZeneca (ChAdOx1-S [recombinant]) v05.00 is valid from 29 October 2021 to 31 March 2022.
