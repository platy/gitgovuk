Details

Following our consultation on e-scooter rental trials, this guidance document informs local authorities and e-scooter operators about trial requirements, covering:

  • background
  • timescales
  • regulatory changes to allow trials
  • trial requirements
  • monitoring, evaluation and DfT data requirements
