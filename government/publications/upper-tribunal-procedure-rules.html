Upper Tribunal Rules: Consolidated version

PDF, 530 KB, 46 pages

Details

Includes rules on how to appeal, how hearings are conducted, and how the tribunal reaches decisions.

The rules are used in the following chambers:

  • Administrative Appeals
  • Immigration and Asylum
  • Tax and Chancery

This version of the rules incorporates changes to the law since 2008.
