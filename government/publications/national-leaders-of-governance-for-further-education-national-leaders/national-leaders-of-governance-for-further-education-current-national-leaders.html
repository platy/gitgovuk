Overview

National Leaders of Governance (NLGs) are experienced college governors, governance professionals and clerks with a strong record of supporting college improvement. They provide mentoring and improvement support to governance boards.

NLGs can determine the best way to support the needs of the college(s) they are working with.

The NLG programme operates alongside the National Leaders of Further Education programme as part of the active support for colleges outlined in the College oversight: support and intervention document.

Information/queries

Colleges seeking support from a National Leader of Governance should contact the Office of the Further Education Commissioner at FEC.OPERATIONS@education.gov.uk.

The application process is currently closed to become a National Leader of Governance. If you would like to get in touch regarding future applications, please email FE.Commissioner@education.gov.uk.

If you are interested in becoming a National Leader of Governance, further information and an application form is available in the National Leaders of Governance for further education: guidance.
