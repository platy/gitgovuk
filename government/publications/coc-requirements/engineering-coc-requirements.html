Important information

This page outlines the requirements for an engineering CoC application, after a successful oral exam. These requirements apply to all UK engineering CoC applicants.

Unless specifically requested on the application form, STCW certificates, academic examination passes and other certificates will not have been recorded with your NOE application. Please ensure you provide every document listed below for your CoC application, even if you have previously sent these in.

Failure to provide all of the required documentation may result in your CoC application being delayed or even rejected.

The following certificates require updating every 5 years:

  • Personal Survival Techniques (STCW Code A-VI/1-1)
  • Fire Prevention & Fire Fighting (STCW Code A-VI/1-2)
  • Proficiency In Survival Craft & Rescue Boat (STCW Code A-VI/2-1)
  • Advanced Fire Fighting (STCW Code A-VI/3)
  • Tanker Training Certificates (if no sea service in last 5 years)

Medical Fitness Certificates are valid for no more than 2 years.

SQA Engineering Knowledge examination certificates are valid for 3 years only. However, if you completed the SQA Management Level examinations for your Second Engineer CoC, you will not need to take these again for your Chief Engineer CoC.

Yacht and Small Vessels SQA examination certificates are valid for 3 years only.

If you wish to carry a tanker endorsement over to a higher capacity CoC, we require evidence of 3 months service on the relevant tanker, or a valid MCA approved tanker training certificate.

At least 6 months of the qualifying seagoing service must have been performed within the 5 years immediately preceding the issue of your Certificate of Competency.

Full information can be found in MSN 1857, MSN 1859, MSN 1860 and MIN 524.

Officer of The Watch Unlimited

Foundation Degree/Scottish Professional Diploma Route

The following documents are required for the issue of your CoC:

  • ENG1 or acceptable equivalent
  • Personal Survival Techniques (STCW Code A-VI/1-1)
  • Fire Prevention & Fire Fighting (STCW Code A-VI/1-2)
  • Proficiency In Survival Craft & Rescue Boat (STCW Code A-VI/2-1)*
  • Advanced Fire Fighting (STCW Code A-VI/3)*
  • Medical First Aid (STCW Code A-VI/4-1)*
  • High Voltage (Operational Level)**
  • 3 Months Approved Workshop Skills
  • HND or Degree certificate, or college letter confirming completion

If your Foundation Degree/Scottish Professional Diploma is over 3 years old, you will need to sit the IAMI Engineering Knowledge General and IAMI Engineering Knowledge Motor and/or Steam

*These certificates must be issued under the authority of the MCA, any EU Member State, New Zealand, Canada or South Africa. These certificates can be updated by any flag on our FSE list.

**These certificates must be MCA Approved.

If you do not provide an optional High Voltage certificate, your CoC will carry a limitation.

Full information can be found in section 3.2 of MSN 1857.

  • HND or HNC certificate, or college letter confirming completion
  • IAMI Engineering Knowledge General

HNC candidates are exempt from Control Engineering if they can show completion of HND level Control unit as part of HNC (the unit title is “Fundamentals of Controls and Transducers”) or they commenced the SQA HNC after 01/07/2013. If you are an HNC candidate and this does not apply to you, you will need to sit the IAMI Engineering Knowledge Control Engineering examination.

*These certificates must be issued under the authority of the MCA, any EU Member State, New Zealand, Canada or South Africa. These certificates can be updated by any flag on our FSE list.

  • ENG1 or acceptable equivalent

  • Medical First Aid (STCW Code A-VI/4-1)* - If this is your first UK CoC
  • HELM (Management Level)**
  • High Voltage (Management Level)**

The following academic examination pass certificates are also required:

  • SQA Management Level Engineering Knowledge General
  • SQA Management Level Engineering Knowledge Motor and/or Steam
  • SQA Management Level Mathematics
  • SQA Management Level Engineering Drawing
  • SQA Management Level Applied Heat
  • SQA Management Level Applied Mechanics
  • SQA Management Level Electro Technology
  • SQA Management Level Naval Architecture

If you believe you are exempt from any academic examinations, please contact your college for a letter of exemption or contact the IAMI Exemption Service. There are no exemptions from the Engineering Knowledge exams.

*These certificates must be issued under the authority of the MCA, any EU Member State, New Zealand, Canada or South Africa. These certificates can be updated by any flag on our FSE list.

**These certificates must be MCA Approved.

If you do not provide an optional High Voltage certificate, your CoC will carry a limitation. If you hold an Operational Level High Voltage but not a Management Level High Voltage certificate, your Second Engineer capacity will carry a limitation.

Full information can be found in section 3.5 of MSN 1857.
