1. Name of project

INEOS Project One

2. Description of project

The project involves construction and operation of an ethane cracker plant to convert ethane to ethylene, with propylene as one of the by-products.

3. Location of project

Industrial zone of the Port of Antwerp between Scheldelaan and Kanaaldok in Belgium.
