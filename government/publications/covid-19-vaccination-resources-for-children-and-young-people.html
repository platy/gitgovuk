Details

Resources on COVID-19 vaccination for eligible children and young people.

Leaflets

COVID-19 vaccination: a guide for eligible children and young people.

These leaflets should be given to eligible children and young people or their parents and carers prior to the vaccination appointment.

Copies of these leaflets are available to order using the following product codes:

  • COVID-19 vaccination: a guide for eligible children and young people aged 12 to 17 years - product code COV2021CYPEN

  • COVID-19 vaccination: a guide for eligible children and young people aged 16 to 17 years - product code COV2021YPM

Information for children and young people on what to expect after their COVID-19 vaccination

This leaflet is for healthcare practitioners to give to children, young people or their parents and carers after their vaccination. It provides information on what to expect after vaccination.

Copies of this leaflet are available to order using product code COV2021YP.

COVID-19 vaccination for at-risk children and young people aged 12 to 15 years (simple text)

This leaflet should be given to eligible at-risk children and young people or their parents and carers prior to the vaccination appointment.

Copies of this leaflet are available to order using product code COV2021WYP.

Consent form

Consent form for eligible children and young people or their parents and carers.

The consent form is available for download only.

Easy-read resources for children are available to download.
