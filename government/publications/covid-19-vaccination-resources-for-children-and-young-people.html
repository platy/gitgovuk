Details

Resources on COVID-19 vaccination for eligible children and young people.

Leaflets

COVID-19 vaccination: a guide for eligible children and young people aged 12 to 17

This leaflet should be given to eligible children and young people or their parents and carers prior to the vaccination appointment.

Paper copies of this leaflet are available to order for free or download in the following languages:

English, Albanian, Arabic, Bengali, Brazilian Portuguese, Bulgarian, Chinese, Estonian, Farsi, Greek, Gujarati, Hindi, Latvian, Lithuanian, Panjabi, Pashto, Polish, Romanian, Romany, Russian, Somali, Spanish, Tagalog, Turkish, Twi, Ukrainian, Urdu and Yiddish.

A Braille version of this leaflet is available to order.

An audio version of this leaflet is available to download.

A British Sign Language (BSL) video of this leaflet is available to view or download.

COVID-19 vaccination: a guide for eligible children and young people aged 16 to 17

This leaflet should be given to eligible children and young people or their parents and carers prior to the vaccination appointment.

Copies of version 2 will be available shortly to order.

Copies of version 1 in English are available to order using product code COV2021YPM.

COVID-19 vaccination: a guide to a second dose for young people aged 16 to 17

This leaflet is currently available to download only. Paper copies will be available shortly to order.

Information for children and young people on what to expect after their COVID-19 vaccination

This leaflet is for healthcare practitioners to give to children, young people or their parents and carers after their vaccination. It provides information on what to expect after vaccination.

Copies of version 2 will be available shortly to order.

Copies of version 1 in English are available to order using product code COV2021YP.

COVID-19 vaccination for at-risk children and young people aged 12 to 15 years (simple text)

This leaflet should be given to eligible at-risk children and young people or their parents and carers prior to the vaccination appointment.

Copies of this leaflet are available to order using product code COV2021WYP.

Consent form

A consent form for eligible children and young people or their parents and carers is available to download.

Easy-read resources for children are available to download.
