Details

Ukraine is the fastest-growing refugee crisis since the Second World War. Over 4 million refugees have arrived in European countries since the conflict started.

In response to the continuing humanitarian crisis in Ukraine, the UK government is providing humanitarian assistance to Ukraine and neighbouring countries.
