Details

Read the guidance to understand how to submit a claim for eligible costs related to qualifications funding for the period October 2021 to March 2022.

Access the coronavirus (COVID-19) qualifications fund for schools and colleges online form to submit your claim.

The deadline to submit your claim is in the table below.

Claim type Deadline
Private candidates 10 January 2022
Priority appeals 10 January 2022
Autumn 2021 Exam costs undertaken between 1 October 2021 and 25 February 2022 4 March 2022
Autumn 2021 Exam costs undertaken between 28 February 2022 and 31 March 2022 6 April 2022
