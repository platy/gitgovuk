Details

The structure and the format of the UK Sanctions List is changing in February 2022. This guidance is to help you understand and prepare for the change. There will be no material updates to listings when this occurs. Updates to the list will continue to be communicated separately.

This guidance is for businesses and industry that regularly use the UK Sanctions List and the Office of Financial Sanctions Implementation (OFSI) consolidated list, and need to understand the change to prepare their own systems.

Samples of the new structure in the 4 new formats can be found on the UK Sanctions List page.

OFSI has published a draft format guide for the new version of the consolidated list.
