This page provides the agenda for the ninth meeting of the Specialised Committee on Citizens’ Rights, which will take place on Monday 24 January. It outlines the areas that the Committee will discuss, and includes items for presentations from British in Europe and the3million, as well as from the Independent Monitoring Authority.

You can read more about the Committee, including joint statements and reports from previous meetings.
