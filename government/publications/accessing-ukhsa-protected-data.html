Details

The UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) generates, curates and integrates high quality data on infectious diseases and other external public health threats.

This is done to prepare for, prevent and respond to threats to health, as well as to deliver world-class research with partners in our research networks.

The documents provided here can be used to access UKHSA protected data.
