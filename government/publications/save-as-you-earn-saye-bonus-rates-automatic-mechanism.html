Details

HMRC are undertaking a review to consider options to simplify the SAYE Bonus Rate Mechanism. The bonus and interest rates are being held at 0% with effect from 30 June 2022 while this review is being undertaken.

Check Employment Related Securities Bulletin 43 (June 2022) for more information.
