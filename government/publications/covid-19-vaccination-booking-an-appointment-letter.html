Details

People who are eligible to book a COVID-19 vaccination via the NHS national booking service are being sent letters inviting them to book their appointments. This page provides the booking information provided in those letters in a number of alternative formats.

For information on who is currently eligible to book via the national booking service, please visit NHS.UK

Alternative formats

A braille version of the English letter is available to order.

An audio, large print and British Sign Language video will be available soon.

Translated versions of this letter are available in the following languages

Albanian, Arabic, Bengali, Chinese, Farsi, Gujarati, Hindi, Kurdish, Nepali, Panjabi, Polish, Romanian, Somali, Spanish, Tagalog and Urdu.
