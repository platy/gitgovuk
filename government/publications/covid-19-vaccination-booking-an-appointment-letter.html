Details

People who are eligible to book a COVID-19 vaccination via the NHS national booking service are being sent letters inviting them to book their appointments. This page provides the booking information provided in those letters in a number of alternative formats. A COVID-19 vaccination information leaflet for eligible adults is available.

For information on who is currently eligible to book via the national booking service, please visit NHS.UK

Alternative formats

A braille version of the English letter is available to order.

A British Sign Language video of the consent form is available to view or download.

An audio and large print version will be available soon.
