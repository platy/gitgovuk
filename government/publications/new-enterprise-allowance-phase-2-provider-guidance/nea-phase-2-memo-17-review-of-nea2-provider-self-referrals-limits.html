This memo is for New Enterprise Allowance (NEA) Providers.

Background

Providers will be aware that the NEA 2 programme design was adapted to take into account a number of temporary measures necessitated by the ongoing coronavirus (COVID-19) event, including the introduction of Provider Self-Referrals.

Jobcentre Plus offices have now reopened and are referring participants to NEA provision at or above the expected levels. In view of this development we have amended the self-referral limits for each provider to zero from 1 November 2020.

Further information and contact details

If you have any queries about the NEA programme or this Live Running Memo, please consult your Performance Manager in the first instance.

If your enquiry is of a Commercial nature, or if you have any queries or concerns regarding Contract Variations or Letters of Change, please direct them to the Commercial Directorate email inbox: employmentcategory.covid19@dwp.gov.uk
