Details

The Chancellor announced a series of measures to support businesses at Spring Statement 2021, building on measures announced at at Autumn Budget and Spending Review 2021.

This Spring Statement set out plans to create a new culture of enterprise, which will support sustainable growth by focusing reforms of the tax and regulatory system on three priorities: Capital, People and Ideas.
