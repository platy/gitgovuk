Details

This check list of early years qualifications achieved in the United Kingdom details all full and relevant qualifications accepted by the Department for Education (DfE) to work in an early years setting as well as common qualifications that are not accepted.

This accompanying guidance includes further information on qualifications achieved in England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ieland:

  • started before 1 September 2014
  • started after 1 September 2014
  • at level 2 and 3
  • in special educational needs and disability (SEND)

For early years qualifications achieved outside the United Kingdom you should check the Early years qualifications achieved outside the United Kingdom guidance.

If the answer to your question or query cannot be found in this guidance, please contact Ecctis. Further information on the guidance that Ecctis is able to provide can be found on the Ecctis website.

Please note that if you have contacted the early years workforce team at the DfE prior to 5 September 2022, you do not need to contact Ecctis.
