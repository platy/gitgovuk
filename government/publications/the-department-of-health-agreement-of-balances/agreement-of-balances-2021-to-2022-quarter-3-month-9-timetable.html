The NHS receivables and payables, and income and expenditure reconciliation

Organisations should note the following definitions:

  • receivable organisation: the organisation sending the invoice, carrying the trade receivable or receiving the income (that is, the supplier or provider)
  • payable organisation: the organisation receiving the invoice, carrying the trade payable or recording expenditure (that is, the purchaser or commissioner)
Date (close of play unless otherwise stated) Receivables and payables or income and expenditure reconciliation, or both Detail: receivables and payables (RP), income and expenditure (IE) reconciliation
Wednesday 23 March 2022 RP Final date for sending March 2022 dated invoices (email where possible).

These invoices relate to activity and services up to and including February and should include estimates for March 2022 activity and services where possible.

Please note statements are not required to be sent until Tuesday 29 March 2022.
Thursday 24 March 2022 RP Final date for despatch of payments to NHS Bodies for 2021-22. This means that there should be no payments made after 24th March 2022 without prior agreement.
Tuesday 29 March 2022 RP Final date for receivable organisations to e-mail payable organisations a statement detailing outstanding invoices dated and invoiced by 23rd March 2022, using the 2021 to 2022 month 12 AoB contact list.

Payments received up to and including 24th March 2022 must also be included.

Please note: only one statement must be sent to each payable organisation (see note at bottom of timetable).

A statement must be sent to each payable organisation for all statement balances over £2,500. Agreement is required for all statement balances over £300,000.
Wednesday 30 March 2022 to Friday 1st April 2022 RP If a statement detailing invoices outstanding has not been received by the deadline stated, the payable organisation is to inform the receivable organisation. In such cases, the receivable organisation must email such a statement immediately.
Thursday 7 April 2022 RP Final date for agreement of outstanding receivables/payables dated up to 23rd March 2022 and above £300,000.
Thursday 7 April 2022 RP Final date for receivable’s organisations to email a statement of accruals to the payable organisations, listing all 2021 to 2022 activities and services not invoiced by 23rd March 2022.
Thursday 7 April 2022 IE Final date for receivable organisations to e-mail payable organisations an income statement to 23rd March 2022, using the 2021 to 2022 month 12 AoB contact list.

Please note: the statement will include income that has been invoiced and income received without an invoice e.g. grants, R&D payments etc. Only one statement must be sent to each payable organisation (see note at bottom of timetable).

I&E statements must be sent to each payable organisation where total statement income is over £2 million. Agreement should be reached for all statements above £3 million.
Friday 8 April 2022 IE If an income statement has not been received by the deadline stated, the payable organisation is to inform the receivable organisation. In such cases, the receivable organisation must email an income statement immediately.
Tuesday 19 April 2022 IE Final date for agreement of income and expenditure statements above £3 million in respect of invoices dated up to 23rd March 2022.
Tuesday 19 April 2022 RP Final date to complete discussions regarding accruals for inclusion in 2021 to 2022 and agree wherever possible.
Tuesday 26 April 2022 (9am) Both NHSE group bodies must submit their data collection forms to NHSE, incorporating income/expenditure and payables/receivable to england.consolidationandreporting@nhs.net
Tuesday 26 April 2022 (midday) Both FTs and trusts must submit their data collection forms with income/expenditure and payables/receivables to NHSI via the NHSI portal.
Wednesday 27 April 2022 (9am) Both Data collection forms are to be submitted to DHSC (receivables/payables and income/expenditure)

ALBs to: ALBFinance@dhsc.gov.uk
Tuesday 3 May 2022 Both National bodies to distribute DHSC mismatch reports to their respective group organisations.

DHSC to distribute ALB variances directly.

Variances must be cleared down to zero and organisations must ensure they do not use the adjustment or disputed lines inappropriately in resolving variances.
Tuesday 10 May 2022 (9am) Both NHS England group bodies must resubmit all Payables/Receivables, Income/Expenditure forms to NHSE to england.consolidationandreporting@nhs.net
Tuesday 10 May 2022 (9am) Both Resubmission of all ALB Payables/Receivables and Income/Expenditure forms to DHSC.

ALBs to: ALBFinance@dhsc.gov.uk
Tuesday 10 May 2022 (midday) Both FTs and trusts resubmit all receivables/payables, income/expenditure via the NHSI portal.
Monday 16 May 2022 Both National bodies to distribute DHSC mismatch reports to their respective group organisations.

DHSC to distribute ALB variances directly.

Remaining mismatches should be cleared down to zero through continued discussions between local bodies.
Wednesday 22 June 2022 (9am) Both NHS England group bodies must submit their audited submission, all receivables/payables, income/expenditure forms to NHSE to england.consolidationandreporting@nhs.net
Wednesday 22 June 2022 (midday) Both FTs and trusts must submit their audited submission, all receivables/payables, income/expenditure forms to NHSI via the NHSI portal.
Wednesday 22 June 2022 (midday) Both Audited submission of all ALB receivables/payables and income/expenditure forms to DHSC.

ALBs to: ALBFinance@dhsc.gov.uk

Note: NHS England’s 7 Regions, NHS England Central Specialised Commissioning Hub (13Q) and NHS England Central Core are all separate bodies for the purposes of AoB, see Appendix 3 of AoB Guidance.

Bills should not be settled between NHS organisations during the last 5 working days of the financial year without prior agreement of the receiving body. This is, no payments made after 24th March 2022.
