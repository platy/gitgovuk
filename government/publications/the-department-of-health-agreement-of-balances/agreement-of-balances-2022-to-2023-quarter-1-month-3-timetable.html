The NHS receivables and payables, and income and expenditure reconciliation

Organisations should note the following definitions:

  • receivable organisation: the organisation sending the invoice, carrying the trade receivable or receiving the income (that is, the supplier or provider)
  • payable organisation: the organisation receiving the invoice, carrying the trade payable or recording expenditure (that is, the purchaser or commissioner)

The agreement of balances (AoB) exercise at month 3 is a limited exercise. Only CCGs, London Shared Services (NEL CSU), NHS TDA and Monitor will be formally agreeing balances with organisations outside of the NHS England Group (for example, NHS trusts, NHS foundation trusts and other ALBs). All intra-NHS England Group balances will be monitored through the internal mismatch process. For more detail, see the Agreement of balances 2022 to 2023: quarter 1 (month 3) guidance.
