Lulworth Range walks are open to the public every weekend with the exception of the following:

  • 19 to 20 February 2022
  • 12 to 13 March 2022
  • 25 to 26 June 2022
  • 24 to 25 September 2022
  • 8 to 9 October 2022
  • 5 to 6 November 2022

Lulworth Range walks are also open every day over the following periods (all dates are inclusive):

  • Christmas: 18 December 2021 to 3 January 2022
  • Easter: 9 to 24 April 2022
  • Spring stand-down: 28 May to 5 June 2022
  • Summer stand-down: 29 July to 31 Aug 2022
  • Christmas: 17 Dec 2022 to 2 Jan 2023

Mupe Bay warning: stay clear of the north half of the beach at Mupe Bay. Due to the possibility of rock fall, for safety you are strongly advised to observe warning signs in the area.

On conclusion of weekday firing, the gates to the range walks are opened as near as possible to 4.30pm on the Friday afternoon. However, this is dependent on completion of range clearance and weather. The range walks will remain open until 8am on the Monday morning.

Tyneham village gate permits vehicle access. It is opened at 9am when no military activity is taking place and is closed at dusk each evening. The exhibitions in Tyneham School and Tyneham Church are open from 10am until 4pm on non-firing days.

For general information please phone 01929 404714.
