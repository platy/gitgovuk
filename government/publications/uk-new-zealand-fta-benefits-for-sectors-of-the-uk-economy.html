Details

This document explains the specific benefits for consumers and business of the UK-New Zealand Free Trade Agreement.

It contains an overview of the following sectors:

  • agriculture, food and drink
  • automotive
  • creative
  • consumer goods and fashion
  • financial services
  • manufacturing, chemicals and life sciences
  • digital and data
  • telecommunications
  • transport services
  • professional and business services
  • small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs)

The agreement is not yet in force. Both the UK and New Zealand are required to complete their respective domestic procedures for the agreement to come into effect. Once approved by both parliaments, businesses will be able to trade under its terms.
