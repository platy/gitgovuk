A list of eligible jobs for Global Business Mobility visas:

  • Senior or Specialist Worker
  • Graduate Trainee
  • UK Expansion Worker
  • Secondment Worker
  • Service Supplier

The table is sorted in order of occupation code, with the smallest numbers at the top. You may be able to use your web browser to search for it on the page.

If you cannot find your job on the list, try looking for similar job titles. Use full words, such as ‘laboratory’ rather than ‘lab’.

In certain cases, there is more flexibility for the eligibility requirements:

  • if you’re applying for the Senior or Specialist Worker visa and are applying to extend your stay in the UK in the same job with the same employer, you may be able to qualify in the listed creative occupations
  • if you’re applying for the Service Supplier visa, you can apply in jobs not on the list if you have relevant qualifications or experience

Creative occupations

These occupation codes have been removed from the list of eligible occupations.

However, if you’re already sponsored in one of these occupation codes you can continue working in your job until your visa expires. You can also extend your visa to continue working in the exact same job.

If you do not want to continue working in one of the occupations above, you can switch your job if it is on the list of eligible occupations. If your job changes you must apply to update your visa.
