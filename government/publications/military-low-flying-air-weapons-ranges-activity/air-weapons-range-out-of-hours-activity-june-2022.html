DIO Holbeach Air Weapons Range

Day Time More information
Tue 7 June 5pm to 11.30pm (Rotary traffic)
Thurs 9 June 5pm to 11.30pm (Rotary traffic)

MAMC/AirSec – this additional hours activity at DIO Holbeach AWR caters for USAFE CV22 Osprey Live Firing.

If red warning flags or red lights are displayed, regardless of timings, please remain clear of the range area.

Whenever warning signals are displayed, do not cross the range boundary.

Do not touch any military debris it may be dangerous: note the location and inform a member of range staff or police.

Dates and times published are correct at the time of publication, however they may change without notice.
