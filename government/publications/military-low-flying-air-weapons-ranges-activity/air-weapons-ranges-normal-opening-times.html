Safety guide

If red warning flags or red lights are displayed, regardless of timings, please remain clear of the range area.

Whenever warning signals are displayed, do not cross the range boundary.

Do not touch any military debris it may be dangerous. Please note the location and inform a member of range staff or police.

All ranges with scheduled hours may close earlier due to weather or low user requirement, particularly evening activity.

Dates and times published are correct at the time of publish, however they may change without notice.
