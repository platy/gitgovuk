Throat and nose test (Innova 25) for care home staff: COVID-19 rapid test kit instructions

Step-by-step guide for COVID-19 self-testing for adult health and social care staff. Rapid tests are only for people who do not have symptoms of COVID-19.

Your step-by-step guide for COVID-19 self-testing for adult social care staff

Ref: PHE publications gateway number: GW-10134 PDF, 839KB, 24 pages

Details

Social care managers are responsible for making sure that all their staff are following the guidance in this step-by-step guide for self-testing.

This guide provides information on tracking:

  • staff training
  • kit distribution
  • staff results

This guide also tells you how to:

  • prepare the test
  • take a swab sample
  • process the sample
  • read and report the result

Find help to do the test and report your result

To watch a video demonstration and for help with reporting results, see how to do a COVID-19 rapid test at home.
