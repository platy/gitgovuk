Details

The manual includes criteria for prioritising sites and information on climbing substrates. It considers eel passage solutions at weirs, tidal flap gates and sluices.

Find out when you may need to fit an eel pass or screen to a water structure.

Contact the Environment Agency if you need a copy of updated technical guidance on:

  • design and implementation of passage solutions for eel and elver
  • screening at intakes and outfalls: measures to protect eel

You can also ask to speak to your local Environment Agency Fisheries Officer if you need advice on the Eels (England and Wales) Regulations 2009 and providing safe passage for eels.
