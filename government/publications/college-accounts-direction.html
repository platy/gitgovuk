Details

College accounts direction for the year 2021 to 2022

The college accounts direction for the year 2021 to 2022 sets out ESFA’s financial reporting requirements for further education and sixth form colleges.

The accounts direction outlines the requirements for each college to:

  • prepare audited accounts to 31 July 2022
  • submit accounts and associated documents to ESFA by 31 December 2022
  • publish accounts on the college’s website as soon as possible after signing but no later than 31 January 2023

Colleges must refer to the college accounts direction 2021 to 2022 when preparing their accounts.

If colleges have concerns about meeting the 31 December submission deadline they should contact their ESFA territorial contact as soon as possible and prior to the submission deadline.

Colleges, rather than their auditors, will need to submit all required documents by following the submission process below.

Finance record for the year 2021 to 2022

ESFA intends to issue an updated finance record for colleges to submit with their audited annual report and accounts for the college financial year 2021 to 2022. This will be published later this year.

Submitting your documents

The above documents should be submitted to the ESFA via the College financial data submissions portal.

Once a college has logged in they will be able to upload their documents by selecting the tile for ‘Annual financial statements return’. The four mandatory elements (audited financial statements, auditor’s management letter, finance record and the audit committee annual report) must initially be uploaded at the same time by colleges but can subsequently be resubmitted separately, if required. There are also options to upload subsidiary financial statements and additional supporting documentation if applicable. Colleges will receive email confirmation that their upload has been successful.

Further details are available within the submission portal. If the person responsible for submitting these documents does not have access to the IDAMS system they can request an account using the ‘I don’t have an account’ link on the login page.

Please complete ESFA’s online enquiry form if you have any questions about your submission.
