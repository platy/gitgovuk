Transitional arrangements for Forms - 2018 Standard Civil Contracts

New versions of paper forms relevant to the 2018 Civil Contract were published on GOV.UK on 31 August 2018. Providers are advised that the LAA will continue to accept previous versions of forms until 19 October 2018, at which point the transitional period will end and only the latest version of each forms will be accepted. The forms affected are listed below:

  • CWC (MH) - Mental Health Application for Prior Authority to incur enhanced rates for Counsel
  • CW1&2 - Legal Help and Controlled Legal Representation - Mental Health
  • CW3A (IMM) - Application for extension of Upper Costs Limit (v9)
  • CW3B (IMM) - Application for extension of Upper Costs Limit
  • Civ App 1 - Application for Civil Legal Aid Legal Representation Non-Family Proceedings (V18)
  • Civ App 6 - Legal Aid emergency application
  • Civ App 8A - Application for prior authority/change to cost limitation (where applicable) (v4)
  • Civ Claim 5 - Claim for Family Graduated Fee (Counsel)
  • CW3C immigration.

The 2018 Civil Contract

The 2018 Civil Contract consists of the:

  • Standard terms
  • Specification (General Provisions & Category Specific Rules)
  • Schedule
  • Contract for Signature
  • Category Definitions 2018

Standard terms

The standard terms govern the commercial relationship between the Legal Aid Agency (LAA) and providers.

  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Standard Terms

Specification

The specification sets out the rules governing the provision of civil contract work.

The specification is divided into provisions of General Provisions (sections 1 to 6) and Category Specific Rules relating to the different categories of civil law.

The procedures for controlled and licensed work and rates for licensed work can be found in legal aid legislation and are referred to in this Specification (General Provisions sections 1-6).

  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Specification (General Provisions 1-6)
  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Category Specific Rules Family (Section 7)
  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Category Specific Rules Immigration and Asylum & IRCs (Section 8)
  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Category Specific Rules Mental Health (Section 9)
  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Category Specific Rules Housing and Debt (Section 10)
  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Category Specific Rules Community Care (Section 11)
  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Category Specific Rules Welfare Benefits (Section 12)
  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Category Specific Rules Claims Against Public Authorities (Section 13)
  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Category Specific Rules Public Law (Section 14)
  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Category Specific Rules Clinical Negligence (Section 15)
  • 2018 Standard Civil Contract Category Specific Rules Family Mediation
