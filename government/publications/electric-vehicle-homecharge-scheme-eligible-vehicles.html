Details

All vehicles that have been approved as ultra-low emission vehicles by the Office for Zero Emission Vehicles (OZEV) are eligible for the Electric Vehicle Homecharge Scheme.

This includes vehicles that may no longer be eligible for a plug-in vehicle grant, because:

  • there is no longer a grant offered for the vehicle type
  • they exceed the category price cap
  • they are no longer on sale in the UK

See the list of the vehicles that are eligible for the plug-in vehicle grant.
