If you need to put your cat or dog into quarantine when it enters England, Scotland or Wales, you must use one of these:

  • authorised quarantine premises
  • authorised quarantine transporters (known as ‘carriers’) to transport it to the quarantine premises

Read our guidance on putting your pet in rabies quarantine.

See a map of quarantine premises and carriers.
