Flu vaccines

Flu vaccines available for the 2022 to 2023 season:

Vaccine Manufacturer Licensed from age
Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine, egg-grown (QIVe) [footnote 1] Sanofi Pasteur licensed from 6 months of age
Fluenz Tetra, live attenuated influenza vaccine (LAIV) [footnote 1] AstraZeneca licensed from 2 years to less than 18 years of age
Influvac sub-unit Tetra▾, Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine, egg-grown (QIVe) [footnote 2] Viatris (formerly Mylan) licensed from 6 months of age
Cell-based Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine (QIVc) ▾[footnote 1] [footnote 2] Seqirus licensed from 2 years of age
Supemtek▾, recombinant Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine (QIVr) [footnote 2] Sanofi Pasteur licensed from 18 years of age
Adjuvanted Quadrivalent Influenza Vaccine▾ (aQIV) [footnote 2] Seqirus licensed from 65 years of age

Eligible groups

  • aged 2 and 3 years on 31 August 2022
  • school aged children (all primary school aged children (reception year to year 6) and eligible secondary school aged children)
  • those aged 6 months to under 50 years in clinical risk groups
  • pregnant women
  • 50 to 64 year olds
  • all those aged 65 years and over
  • those in long-stay residential care homes
  • carers / in receipt of carer’s allowance / or main carer of an older or disabled person
  • close contacts of immunocompromised individuals
  • frontline health and social care staff

See the Green Book Influenza chapter 19 for full details on eligible groups.

Age group and vaccine type

6 months to under 2 years in a clinical risk group

Give QIVe [footnote 3].

2 years to less than 18 years in eligible year groups or in a clinical risk group

If no contraindications to LAIV - give quadrivalent LAIV nasal spray suspension

If LAIV contraindicated or otherwise unsuitable [footnote 4] give QIVc.

18 years to less than 65 years in a clinical risk group; pregnant women

Give QIVc or QIVr (18 years of age and above only).

If QIVc or QIVr are not available give QIVe.

50 to 64 years not in a clinical risk group [footnote 5]

Give QIVe.

If QIVe is not available give QIVc or QIVr (if this does not divert stock from clinical risk groups or those aged 65 years and over)

65 years and over

Give aQIV [footnote 6] or QIVr

If aQIV or QIVr are not available give QIVc.
