Details

This guidance is for businesses supplying consumer and non-consumer products on the UK market and summarises the requirements to notify relevant authorities if products pose a risk to the health and safety of consumers or in the workplace and/or are found to be noncompliant with the relevant legislation.

This guidance applies to businesses supplying goods on the Great Britain and Northern Ireland markets and sits alongside PAS 7100:2018 on Supporting better product recalls.

If you require any support in completing a notification, please contact the relevant authority in the first instance. Alternatively, the UK Product Safety Contact Point can be contacted via ukproductsafetycp@beis.gov.uk to assist.
