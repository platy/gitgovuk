Resources for communicating about the new rules relating to Northern Ireland and signposting to available support, now the Brexit transition period has ended.

1. Movement Assistance Scheme

This video looks at:

  • the new rules for traders moving agri-food goods from Great Britain to Northern Ireland
  • support available through the Movement Assistance Scheme.
  • how the Movement Assistance Scheme works and what assistance it can provide

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jEUPOVqWdMQ&feature=youtu.be

2. Moving agrifood goods between Great Britain and Northern Ireland

This video looks at:

  • the new rules for traders moving animals and goods of animal origin from Great Britain to Northern Ireland
  • the new rules for traders moving plants or plant products from Great Britain to Northern Ireland
  • certification requirements for moving different agri-food goods and the support available

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2RCJt5CD6Ns&feature=youtu.be

3. Certifying animals and animal products

This video looks at:

  • the new rules for traders moving animals or goods of animal origin from Great Britain to Northern Ireland
  • the actions that vets or environmental health officers need to take in order to certify animals and products of animal origin

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=3KPje7YEV_E&feature=youtu.be

4. Keeping your business moving

This video looks at:

  • highlighting that new rules apply for moving good into, or out of, Northern Ireland
  • encouraging businesses in Northern Ireland to use the Brexit checker tool to receive personalised actions for their business to take

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SiLP9Z9Q7ZA&feature=youtu.be

5. Using the Brexit Checker Tool

This video looks at:

  • encouraging businesses in Northern Ireland to use the Brexit checker tool to receive personalised actions for their business to take
