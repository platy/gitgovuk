House to House Collections Act 1939

The Secretary of State for Digital, Culture, Media and Sport is responsible for the National Exemption Order scheme for house-to-house collections under the House to House Collections Act 1939 (as amended).

Details of the application process are available from charity-neo@dcms.gov.uk
