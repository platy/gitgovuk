The vessel was built in 2009 and is in good condition.

Length: 26.16m
Draught: 3.14m
Beam: 7.94m
Gross Tonnage: 134 Tonne
Speed: 12.4kn
Crew: 6
Passengers: N/A
Construction: monohulled construction with screws and two fixed propellers and rudders
Type of craft: IX tug

All parties interested in purchasing the vessel will need to complete and submit the declaration for the statement of understanding to the following: DESLSOC-DESANavy@mod.gov.uk and DESLD-DESA-Commercial@mod.gov.uk by 5pm (GMT) on Wednesday 31 August 2022.

It is hoped that viewings will take place late September 2022 but please do not book any travel until you have received the Invitation to Tender (ITT).

Note:

The issue of this notice is not a commitment by the Secretary of State for Defence “The Authority” to commit to a sale as a result of this notice and this process commit to a sale as a result of this notice and this process may be discontinued at any time should a Government to Government (G2G) sale option be forthcoming.
