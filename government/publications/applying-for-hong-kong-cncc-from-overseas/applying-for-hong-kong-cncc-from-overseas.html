Letter of verification

Supporting documents

To apply for a letter of verification in order to get to a certificate of good conduct from the Hong Kong Police, you will need to provide the following documents:

  • your British passport or British National Overseas passport or a certified copy of the photo information page. Your local Post Office may be able to provide this service. Alternatively, a solicitor or notary public can make a certified copy

  • a letter from your employer/university/school or adoption agency that gives details of why you need to have the certificate of good conduct

Where to send your application

You must send the documents above to the following address:

Consular
Hong Kong letter of verification
Rm K4.12
Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office
King Charles Street
London
SW1A 2AH

How to pay for the letter of verification

The fee for the letter of verification is £50.00, payable by postal order or banker’s draft made out to the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office.

We do not accept personal cheques.

Next steps

Once we have this information, we aim to issue the letter of verification within 20 working days. You then will need to forward the letter of verification to the Hong Kong Police with your application.

Certificate of good conduct

On issuing the certificate of good conduct, the Hong Kong Police will return it to this office and we will forward the certificate to you.

This process can take up to 12 weeks. We cannot intervene at this stage because it is the responsibility of the Hong Kong authorities.

For more information about additional payment and extra documentation that may be required contact the Hong Kong Police.
