Details

The NHS is offering new antibody and antiviral treatments to people with coronavirus (COVID-19) who are at highest risk of becoming seriously ill.

This document includes information for those who have been prescribed Molnupiravir including how long to take the medicines for, what to do if you are pregnant or breastfeeding, and how to report side effects.
