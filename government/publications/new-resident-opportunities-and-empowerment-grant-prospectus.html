Details

The Resident Opportunities and Empowerment Grant is new funding up to 31 March 2025, for an organisation to provide training, capacity building and access to independent information open to all social housing residents to support their effective engagement with landlords.

The deadline for applications is 11:59 pm on 2 October 2022.
