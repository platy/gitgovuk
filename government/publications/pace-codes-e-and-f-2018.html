Details

Revised codes of practice E and F have effect from 31 July 2018.

Code E outlines the requirements for audio recording interviews with suspects. Code F should be considered if an interviewing officer makes a visual recording with sound of an interview with a suspect.

They were revised following a statutory consultation.
