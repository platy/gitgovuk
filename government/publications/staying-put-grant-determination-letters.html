Details

Staying Put arrangements enable care leavers to remain with their former foster carers after they turn 18.

This letter to local authorities, from DfE’s Director for Children’s Social Care, Practice and Workforce, sets out the grant funding for the current year. The 3 previous years’ letters are also available.
