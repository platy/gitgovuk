Firing times

  • Day firing will take place on: Wednesday 5 January 2022 and Thursday 27 January 2022

  • No night firing has been scheduled.

Your safety

The red flags by day and the red lamp by night indicate that the range is active and live firing is taking place. Do not enter the firing range.

For your own safety do not touch any military debris, it may be dangerous.

Learn more about accessing MOD training areas safely.

The only guarantee that there is no firing is the absence of the Red Flags on the range on the day. Changes are posted on the noticeboards next to the sentry posts on the coastal footpath.

There is no live firing after 3pm on Sundays.
