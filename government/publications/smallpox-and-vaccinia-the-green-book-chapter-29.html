Details

In December 1979, the Global Commission for the Certification of Smallpox Eradication declared the world free of smallpox and this declaration was ratified by the World Health Assembly in May 1980.

Since May 2022, cases of monkeypox have been reported in multiple countries that have not had endemic monkeypox virus in animal or human populations, including countries in Europe, North America and Australasia.
