Details

Use this form to apply for new BPS entitlements and to prove that you meet the ‘young’ farmer and /or ‘new’ farmer rules.

RPA has simplified the paperwork for young farmers. For more information about the young farmer payment and the evidence we need, read the Basic Payment Scheme: rules for 2022.
