Details

Paper prepared by OpenSAFELY on living with children and COVID-19 outcomes. This paper was first published by the British Medical Journal in March 2021. It was available for participants to read after SAGE 100 on 20 December 2021, but not considered or discussed in the meeting. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
