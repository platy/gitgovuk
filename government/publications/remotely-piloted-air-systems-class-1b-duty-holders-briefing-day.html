Remotely piloted air systems open category duty holders briefing day

This briefing day is a mandatory requirement for remotely piloted air systems (RPAS) duty holders that operate RPAS in the open category.

Aim

The briefing day aims to improve defence air safety by ensuring that duty holders are fully conversant with their role and responsibilities in the safe management of RPAS that operate in the open category.
