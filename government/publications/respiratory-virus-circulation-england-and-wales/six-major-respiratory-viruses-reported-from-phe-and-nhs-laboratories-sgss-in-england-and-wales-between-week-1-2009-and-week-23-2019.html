This chart (Figure 1) shows the weekly distribution (using a 3-week moving average method) of the number of samples that tested positive for 6 major respiratory viruses.

The 6 viruses are:

  • influenza A
  • influenza B
  • RSV
  • rhinovirus
  • parainfluenza
  • adenovirus

These viruses were reported from UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) and NHS laboratories’ voluntary surveillance database, the Second Generation Surveillance System (SGSS).
