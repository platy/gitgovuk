Healthcare practitioners should refer to the influenza chapter in ‘Immunisation against infectious disease’ (the ‘Green Book’) for further detail about clinical risk groups advised to receive influenza immunisation and advice on contraindications and precautions for the influenza vaccines.

Information for healthcare practitioners about the childhood influenza programme and the inactivated influenza vaccines, and links to an influenza vaccination training slide set and the influenza vaccination e-learning programme will be available on the Annual flu programme webpage and the e-learning for healthcare Flu Immunisation web page.

The UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) will develop Patient Group Directions (PGDs) that will be available prior to commencement of the programme at Immunisation PGD templates and Community Pharmacy Seasonal Influenza Vaccine Service.

Resources for the public facing marketing campaign to encourage take-up amongst eligible groups and for adaptable assets for NHS and social care organisations to use in their own staff vaccination campaigns will be available from the Campaign Resource Centre.

Template letters for practices to use will be available on the Annual flu programme webpage.
