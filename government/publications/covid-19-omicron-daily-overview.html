Details

This includes total cases (UK), England regional breakdown, S-gene target failure (SGTF) as a percentage of cases, hospitalisation and death information.

England, Scotland and Wales data reported is up to 6pm on 14 December. Northern Ireland data reported is up to 4pm on 14 December

Hospitalisation and death is data as at 7pm on 14 December for cases reported on 13 December 2021.
