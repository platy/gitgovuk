Details

This includes total cases (UK), England regional breakdown, S-gene target failure (SGTF) as a percentage of cases, hospitalisation and death information.

  • England data reported is up to 6pm on 26 December
  • Wales data reported is up to 5pm on 26 December
  • Northern Ireland data reported is up to 4pm on 26 December
  • Scotland is no longer updating Omicron figures daily – this will now be a weekly addition to UK totals

Hospitalisation data is up to 26 December and may be lagged up to 48 hours.
