Exporting goods from GB to the EU via the Short Straits

The webinar was delivered on 14 January to highlight and provide clarity on the issues arising from goods moving from GB to the EU via the short straits from 1 Jan 2021 at the end of the transition period.

The event included presentations from UK officials from the Department of the Environment, Food and Rural Affairs, (DEFRA), HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) and the Border and Protocol Delivery Group (BPDG). The event attracted over 2000 attendees and over 600 questions were raised in the chat forum which ran throughout the event.
