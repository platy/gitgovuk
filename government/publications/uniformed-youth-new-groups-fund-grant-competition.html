Details

The Department of Digital, Culture, Media and Sport is providing £1.5 million of grant funding for Uniformed Youth Organisations to establish new groups/units in deprived areas in England, currently underserved by uniformed youth organisations. An assessment panel, appointed by DCMS, will review bids from eligible organisations using the evaluation criteria set out in section 8 to determine which organisations should be awarded funding and the amount awarded.

Application details:

  • Applications open from Tuesday 2 September 2022
  • The deadline for applications is 23:59 on Sunday 2 October 2022
  • Applications received before the closing date will be assessed after the closing date
  • Applications received after the closing date will not be assessed

Guidance relating to applications for this funding and the application form is contained in the documents above.

We will receive any clarification questions up until 5pm 21 September 2022. Clarification questions will be published on gov.uk on an ongoing basis.

We are not able to respond to requests for support in completing this application as this is a competitive process.

Send your completed application to: michaela.wragg@dcms.gov.uk and tasnia.chowdhury@dcms.gov.uk by 23:59 Sunday 2 October 2022.
