Details of the application

Rhona J K Bulwer-Long & Sarah J Bulwer-Long t/a William Long Farms has applied to the Environment Agency for a licence.

The Environment Agency is giving notice of this application in accordance with Section 37 of the Water Resources Act 1991 and Regulation 6 of the Water Resources (Abstraction and Impounding) Regulations 2006.

The application is for a full licence to abstract water from a reach between National Grid References TG 12534 25448 and TG 12534 25265 from un-named drains within the Norfolk Rivers IDB on a tributary of the River Wensum near Cawston, Norfolk.

The application is to abstract water as follows:

  • 72 cubic metres an hour
  • 1,728 cubic metres a day
  • 90,000 cubic metres a year

Water is proposed to be abstracted between November and March and stored in a reservoir for the subsequent use of spray irrigation.
