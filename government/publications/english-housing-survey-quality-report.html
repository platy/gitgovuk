Details

These reports summarise key issues relating to the quality of the statistics presented in the English Housing Survey reports.

The reports begin with the purpose and background of the English Housing Survey, a brief introduction to methodology, and a summary of work undertaken to improve the quality of English Housing Survey statistics.
