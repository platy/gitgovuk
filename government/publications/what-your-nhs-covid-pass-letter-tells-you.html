What your NHS COVID Pass letter tells you (English, text only)

HTML

Details

An NHS COVID Pass letter allows you to show others that you’ve had a full course of the COVID-19 vaccine when travelling abroad to some countries or territories.

You may be asked to demonstrate your COVID-19 status as a condition of entry to a venue or event in England.

From Wednesday 15 December, the coronavirus (COVID-19) rules for visiting certain venues and events have changed. See more information on using your NHS COVID Pass for travel abroad and at venues and settings in England.

If you do not have access to a smartphone, computer or tablet, you can call 119 from within the UK. Select the ‘NHS COVID Pass service’ and ask for an NHS COVID Pass letter to be posted to you.

You should keep the original English language version of your letter. You will be asked to show it if you travel abroad.
