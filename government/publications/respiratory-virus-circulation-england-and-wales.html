Six major respiratory viruses reported from UKHSA and NHS laboratories (SGSS) in England and Wales between week 18, 2012 and week 16, 2022

HTML

Details

This document highlights the 6 major respiratory viruses reported from UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) and NHS laboratories’ voluntary surveillance database and provides a chart illustrating the number of reports by specimen year, between week 18, 2012 and week 16, 2022.

It can be used for research purposes by healthcare professionals.
