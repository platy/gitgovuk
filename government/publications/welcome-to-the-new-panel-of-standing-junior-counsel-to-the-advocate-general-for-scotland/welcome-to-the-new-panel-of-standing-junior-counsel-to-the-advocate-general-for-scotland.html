Following the recent appointment process, the Advocate General for Scotland is pleased to announce his new panel of Standing Junior Counsel which will deal with UK Government work arising in Scotland. He welcomes those re-appointed and those newly appointed. The appointments which have been approved by him are as follows:

The First Standing Junior to the new panel is Chris Pirie who was appointed to this post in January of this year. The new post of Second Standing Junior was also introduced in the recent appointment process and the Advocate General for Scotland is pleased to announce that the successful applicant for that post is Graham Maciver.

The Advocate General for Scotland would wish to congratulate all those who have been successful and welcome in his new panel of Standing Junior Counsel.
