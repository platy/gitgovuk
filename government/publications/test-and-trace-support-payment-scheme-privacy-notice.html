Details

Privacy notice enabling people who are considering applying or who have already applied for the Test and Trace Support Payment scheme to understand how and why their personal data may be used in the process.

Published 4 December 2020
Last updated 25 March 2021 + show all updates
  1. 25 March 2021

    Updated to reflect the extension of the Test and Trace Payment Support scheme to include a qualifying parent or guardian of a child who has been told to self-isolate by either NHS Test and Trace or their education or childcare setting.

  2. 11 February 2021

    Updated to reflect that local authorities may now use Test and Trace Payment Support data for wider benefit accuracy checks, where they meet specific criteria, and when making a payment under the scheme, local authorities will validate bank account details to ensure they align with the applicant, for anti-fraud purposes.

    First published.
