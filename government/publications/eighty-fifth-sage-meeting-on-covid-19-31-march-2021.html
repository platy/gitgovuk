Details

This is a record of the discussion of SAGE 85 on 31 March 2021. The paper is the assessment of the evidence at the time of writing. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.

Redactions within this document have been made to remove reference to R and growth estimates for Wales not yet published and to remove any names of junior officials (under SCS) or names of anyone for national security reasons. SAGE 85 includes redactions of 17 junior officials.
