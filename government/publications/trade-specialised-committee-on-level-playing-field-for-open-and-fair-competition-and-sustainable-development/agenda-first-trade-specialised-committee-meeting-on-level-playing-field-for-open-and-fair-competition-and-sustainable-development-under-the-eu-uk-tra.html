Agenda of the first Trade Specialised Committee on Level Playing Field for Open and Fair Competition and Sustainable Development under the EU-UK Trade and Cooperation Agreement

Via videoconference, date 12 October 2021

Introduction /opening session

Discussion on issues related to subsidy control:

  • Update on the United Kingdom’s Subsidy Control Bill
  • EU’s proposal for a Brexit Adjustment Reserve
  • Update on the United Kingdom’s renewable energy schemes
  • EU proposed Regulation on Foreign Subsidies

Discussion on issues related to labour and social standards, environment and climate and other instruments for trade and sustainable development:

  • European Social Charter; Update on UK initiative for a Single Enforcement Body
  • Update on the EU’s Occupational Health and Safety Strategy 2021
  • Update on the United Kingdom’s climate agenda; EU’s presentation on Fit for 55 package
  • Update on the Environment Bill; EU update on implementation of the 8th Environmental Action Plan
