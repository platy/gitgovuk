Details

Paper prepared by the Environmental Modelling Group (EMG), EMG Transmission Group, and SPI-B on transmission in managed quarantine facilities. This paper was available for participants to read at SAGE 95 on 9 September 2021, but not considered or discussed in the meeting.. As new evidence or data emerges, SAGE updates its advice accordingly.

These documents are released as pre-print publications that have provided the government with rapid evidence during an emergency. These documents have not been peer-reviewed and there is no restriction on authors submitting and publishing this evidence in peer-reviewed journals.
