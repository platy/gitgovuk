MOT special notice 03-22: DBS checks for new and returning MOT testers

From 1 April 2022, some MOT testers will need a basic disclosure certificate (DBS check) before carrying out a demonstration test.

From:
Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency
Published
1 March 2022

Applies to England, Scotland and Wales

Details

This special notice tells you about how new and returning MOT testers will need to provide a basic disclosure certificate (also known as a DBS check) before carrying out a demonstration test from 1 April 2022.

Special notices contain changes to the MOT scheme that are not yet in the MOT testing guide and inspection manual.
