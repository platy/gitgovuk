Details

The following list of funeral directors has been prepared by the British Embassy, Guatemala City for the convenience of British nationals who may require this service and assistance in Honduras. It is provided on the understanding that we (the British Embassy/Consulate) do not assume or undertake any legal responsibility, to you, or those affected, if you choose to take it into account when instructing a local funeral director.
