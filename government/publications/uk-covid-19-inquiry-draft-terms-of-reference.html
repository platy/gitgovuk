Details

The draft terms of reference set out the aims of the UK COVID-19 Inquiry.

This follows consultation with the Inquiry Chair, Baroness Hallett, and ministers in the devolved administrations.

A final terms of reference will be published once Baroness Hallett has consulted with the public, including with bereaved families and other affected groups.

The public consultation on the inquiry’s terms of reference is now open. You can submit your response on the UK COVID-19 Inquiry website. The consultation will close at 23:59pm on 7 April 2022.
