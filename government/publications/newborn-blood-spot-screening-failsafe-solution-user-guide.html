Details

The newborn blood spot screening failsafe solution (NBSFS) is an IT system that minimises the risk of babies missing, or having delayed, newborn blood spot (NBS) screening.

This user guide includes:

  • processes between England and Wales and Scotland
  • forms, for example to request a new user or query a record
  • operational level agreements
  • quick reference guide
  • training guides
