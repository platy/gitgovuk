Details

Public Health England and NHS Test and Trace produced the delivery plan, July to September 2021, that outlined how these organisations supported delivery of Step 4 of the roadmap.

This followed and supplemented the delivery plan, April to June 2021, which outlined work done across these organisations that supported the roadmap out of lockdown.

The previous NHS Test and Trace business plan – published in February 2021 – set out an earlier approach to disrupt and prevent COVID-19 transmission, to protect people’s health and to enable people to return to a more normal way of life.
