Details

Building on the UK Hydrogen Strategy, this action plan assesses the supply chain, skills, investment and export opportunities across the value chain necessary to achieve our 10GW ambition for hydrogen.

It also sets out our approach to monitoring and evaluation (M&E) to learn lessons and a benchmark for progress going forward.

The action plan was informed by this report produced by Wood PLC and Optimat Ltd on behalf of the Department for Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy: Supply chains to support a UK hydrogen economy.
