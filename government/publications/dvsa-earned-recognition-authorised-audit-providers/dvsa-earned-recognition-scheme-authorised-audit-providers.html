When you’ve had your vehicle operator licence for 2 years, you can apply to join the Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA) earned recognition scheme. This is the best way to prove your business meets driver and vehicle standards.

Your systems and processes must be audited by a DVSA-authorised audit provider:

  • once your application has been reviewed
  • every 2 years after you’ve joined the scheme

Some auditors provide audits for:

  • heavy goods vehicle (HGV) operators
  • public service vehicle (PSV) operators
  • both types of operator
