This notice is published under regulation 96A(1) of the Trade Remedies (Dumping and Subsidisation) (EU Exit) Regulations 2019.

It suspends the collection of the anti-dumping duty applied by Taxation Notice 2020/24 (anti-dumping duty on electric bicycles originating in the People’s Republic of China) on the goods listed under ‘Goods subject to suspension of duty’ produced by the overseas exporters in the table listed under ‘Overseas exporters: additional codes’.

Overseas exporters: additional codes

The collection of this amount is suspended for:

Company Additional code
Jinhua Otmar Technology Co Limited (“Otmar”) 8A10
Jinhua Seno Technology Co Limited (“Seno”) 8A11

Goods subject to suspension of duty

The suspension applies to the goods described, or imported under a commodity code specified, below.

Goods description

Cycles, with pedal assistance, with an auxiliary electric motor.

Commodity codes:

  • 87 11 60 10 00
  • 87 11 60 90 10

Imposition date

This public notice takes effect, and the suspension applies, from 24 June 2022.
