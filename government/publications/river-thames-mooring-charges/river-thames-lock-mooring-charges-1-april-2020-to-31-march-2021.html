Base moorings at locks

No charges increase for the year ending 31 March 2023

Lock base moorings are invoiced annually in advance, based on the appropriate tariff under one of the boat type descriptions below. To calculate the cost, the tariff is multiplied by the length of the boat (in metres) and then multiplied by the number of months depending on whether a summer (6 month) or winter (12 month) mooring. Find additional charges and discounts and tariff descriptions below.

The tariffs shown below include VAT at 20%

Additional charges and discounts

  • Sites downstream of Reading (including Blakes Lock) attract a market supplement of 25%
  • Remote sites with poor access for moorers (Days and Temple locks) have a 25% discount
  • Training clubs and youth groups are allowed a 25% discount
