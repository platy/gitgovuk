Details

The UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) has a programme of work that aims to support rapid deployment of specific COVID-19 therapeutics by undertaking genomic, virological, and epidemiologic surveillance, through both national surveillance systems and academic collaboration.

This report is produced regularly to share information from these developing systems with partner organisations.
