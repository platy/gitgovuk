Details

On 10 March 2022 we wrote to chief executives of private providers with 1,000 or more units (large providers), those with fewer than 1,000 units (small providers) and local authority social housing providers in England to draw attention to our processes and guidance for data submission during the 2022-23 period.

Further details can also be found on our Information required from registered providers.
