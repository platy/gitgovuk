1. Check your licence

It is your responsibility to check your licence documents before going to sea as licences can be changed at any time. The most up to date licences can be accessed on the understand your fishing vessel licence page.

The variations effective from 00:01 hours, Sunday 06 September 2020 are as follows:

2. Western Waters Scallop Effort days increase

Affecting Over 10m licence Schedules at section 5.2.1 for Cat A (11), Cat A Islands (12), Cat A Pelagic (17), Cat B (31) and Cat C (41)

• Increasing the Western Water Scallop Effort days for the period 00:01 1st July 2020 – 23:59 30th September 2020 from 45 to 55 days.
