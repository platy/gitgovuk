1. Check your licence

It is your responsibility to check your licence documents before going to sea as licences can be changed at any time. The most up to date licences can be accessed on the understand your fishing vessel licence page.

The variations effective from 00:00 hours, Saturday 29th August are as follows:

1.1 Dogger Bank Scallop Fishery Closure date extended

Closure date changed from 29th August 2020 to 10th October 2020

  • Affecting Under 10m Licence Conditions Cat A (91), Cat A Capped (94) at section 16.1

  • Affecting Under 10m Licence Conditions Cat A PO (91) at section 13.1

  • Affecting Over 10m Licence Conditions Cat A (11), Cat A Islands (12), Cat B (31) and Cat C (41) at section 25.1

  • Affecting Over 10m Licence Condition Cat A Pelagic (17) at section 32.1
