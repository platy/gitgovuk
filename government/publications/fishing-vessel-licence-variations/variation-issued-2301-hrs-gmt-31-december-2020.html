1. Check your licence

It is your responsibility to check your licence documents before going to sea as licences can be changed at any time. The most up to date licences can be accessed on the understand your fishing vessel licence page. $CTA

2. The variations effective from 23:01 hrs (GMT) 31 December 2020 is as follows:

3. Important change to your authority to fish from 23:01 hrs (GMT) on 31 December 2020

UK vessels will be prohibited from fishing in EU, Norwegian or Faroese waters unless the necessary international agreements are reached to allow them to do so and they are authorised to do so by the relevant competent authority. Non-UK vessels will be prohibited from fishing in UK waters in the same way unless they are granted access under such future agreements. This variation, therefore, amends UK vessel licences to remove authority to fish outside British fishery limits.

4. General conditions amendment.

  • The authority to fish in EU, Norwegian, Faroes and NEAFC waters is rescinded and all commercial fishing activity outside of the UK EEZ must stop and all fishing gear be removed by 23:00 hours (GMT) on 31 December 2020.

  • Within this licence any reference to an EU Regulation is a reference to that Regulation as it forms part of United Kingdom domestic law by virtue of Section 3 of the European Union (Withdrawal) Act 2018 in accordance with Schedule 8 (1) of that Act.
