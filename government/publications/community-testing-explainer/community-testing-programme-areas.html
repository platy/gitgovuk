Following national lockdown restrictions, we’re expanding the eligibility of community-led testing to all local authorities across England.

If you are a business or member of the general public, find out if your area offers rapid testing for people without symptoms.

The lower-tier local authorities that are currently rolling out community testing are listed below. This list will be updated on a regular basis.
