Details

This series is part of the COVID-19: mental health and wellbeing surveillance report.

The Spotlight series describes variation within the population. Public Health England are working with partners so that policy-relevant findings about variation can be published in time for relevant Spotlights.

Where evidence permits, Spotlights will be published for each of the protected characteristics (under the 2010 Equalities Act).

Spotlights are also planned for ongoing policy priorities, such as deprivation.

We would like to express our thanks to partners without whom, this work would not have been possible.
