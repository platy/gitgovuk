As a vehicle operator, you will usually use IT systems and software to help manage your drivers and vehicle fleet.

When you’ve had your vehicle operator licence for 2 years, you can apply to join the Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA) earned recognition scheme. This is the best way to prove your business meets driver and vehicle standards.

You need IT systems and software that can report on some important performance measures to join the DVSA earned recognition.

The IT systems and software on this list work with the DVSA earned recognition scheme.

It’s a good idea to buy these when you set up your vehicle operator business. This will make joining the scheme easier as soon as you’re eligible.
