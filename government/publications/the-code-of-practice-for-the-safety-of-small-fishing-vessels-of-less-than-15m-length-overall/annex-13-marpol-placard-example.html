Every Vessel shall display a placard displaying the legal requirements of dumping waste in accordance with the requirements of MARPOL.

An example placard is shown below:

Atlantic Ocean and Irish Sea

Outside 25 Nautical miles from nearest land 12 to 25 Nautical miles from nearest land 3-12 Nautical miles from nearest land 0 to 3 Nautical miles from nearest land
Illegal to dispose of Platics & Oily Wastes Plastics, Lining & Packaging Materials that float. Oily Wastes Plastics, Lining & Packaging Materials that float, all other Garbage if not ground to less than 25mm. Oily Wastes Total Restriction

North Sea and English Channel (special areas)

Nearest land to 12 Nauticlal miles Outside 12 Nautical miles from nearest land
Illegal to dispose of Total restriction Plastics & Any Garbage other than food wastes. Oily Wastes
