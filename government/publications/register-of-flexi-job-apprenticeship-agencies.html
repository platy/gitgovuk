Details

Flexi-Job Apprenticeship Agencies recruit and employ apprentices and place them with employers. To be eligible to operate a Flexi-Job Apprenticeship Agency, organisations must be registered on the Register of Flexi-Job Apprenticeship Agencies.

As part of the process to become a Flexi-Job Apprenticeship Agency, applicants were able to apply for a share of the Flexi-Job fund to support them with their start-up costs. Currently, we have 15 organisations on our Register of Flexi-Job Apprenticeship Agencies and have awarded a total of £5m in grant funding to 11 of these organisations.

Organisations listed on the register have been through an application process with the ESFA that considers due diligence, financial management and operational details.
