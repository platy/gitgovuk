Details

The NHS sends this leaflet to anyone who needs to have further tests after using the NHS bowel cancer screening test kit.

Free printed copies of this leaflet are available to the NHS Bowel Cancer Screening Programme hubs (and their mailing companies) to send with letters referring people for a specialist screening practitioner (SSP) appointment to discuss having a colonoscopy.

For more information, see the ‘order printed leaflets’ section of our guidance on how to access and order leaflets.

This leaflet is also available in A4 PDF format (in English) to print out and provide for people who cannot access digital information (see list above)

Translations are available to view or download in 10 languages. Please note these will shortly be updated.

Contact the screening helpdesk with any queries about this publication, making sure you include its full title.
