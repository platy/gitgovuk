Abdominal aortic aneurysm (AAA) screening, a free test for men aged 65 and over

HTML

Details

This information describes the NHS abdominal aortic aneurysm (AAA) screening test, possible outcomes and an overview of the condition being screened for.

Free printed copies of this leaflet are provided for local screening services to send to men with screening invitations. Screening services can view the full PDF of the printed leaflet and order copies on the APS online ordering portal.

This information is available in 10 other languages: Arabic, Bengali, Chinese, Gujarati, Hindi, Polish, Portuguese, Punjabi, Romanian and Urdu.

Resources for people with learning disabilities and people who struggle with written information is also available, including:

  • an easy guide
  • a simple animation showing screening tests offered to men throughout their lives, including AAA:
    • in English
    • in the 10 other most requested languages

Contact the screening helpdesk with any queries about this publication, making sure you include its full title.
