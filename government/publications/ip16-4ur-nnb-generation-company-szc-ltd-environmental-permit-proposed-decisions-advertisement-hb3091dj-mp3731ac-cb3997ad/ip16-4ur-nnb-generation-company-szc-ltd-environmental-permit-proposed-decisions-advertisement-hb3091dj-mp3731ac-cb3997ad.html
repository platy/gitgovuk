Details of the applications

The Environment Agency received applications in May 2020 from NNB Generation Company (SZC) Ltd for environmental permits under the Environmental Permitting (England and Wales) Regulations 2016. It is now consulting on the proposed decisions.

Applicant name

NNB Generation Company (SZC) Ltd

Radioactive Substances Activities Reference number

EPR/HB3091DJ/A001

Brief description of activity

Proposed disposals of radioactive waste to air, water and by transfer.

Combustion Activities Reference number

EPR/MP3731AC/A001

Brief description of activity

Proposed operation of diesel generators to be used to provide back-up electrical supply at the site.

Water Discharge Activities Reference number

EPR/CB3997AD/A001

Brief description of activity

Proposed discharges of cooling water and liquid process effluents during operation of the power station.

Application types

New permits

Regulated facility address

Sizewell C Site
Sizewell
Suffolk
IP16 4UR

Local authority

East Suffolk Council
