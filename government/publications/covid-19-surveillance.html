Details

This guidance of the UK’s COVID-19 surveillance and immunity studies is for healthcare professionals, academics, industry and the general public.

Programmes are managed by the UK Health Security Agency (UKHSA) in collaboration with a number of delivery partners, including the Office for National Statistics (ONS), research, academic and commercial partners. They are taking forward COVID-19 research as outlined in the government’s COVID-19 Response: Living with COVID-19 plan.
