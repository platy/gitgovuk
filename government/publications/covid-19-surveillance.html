Details

This guidance is an overview of current population surveillance studies in the UK and is for healthcare professionals, academics, industry and the general public.

These robust population surveillance programmes from the UK Health Security Agency’s (UKHSA) Joint Biosecurity Centre (JBC) are taking forward Pillar 4 of the UK’s Testing Strategy. The studies are essential to understanding SARS-CoV-2 (the virus that causes COVID-19) infection and its spread across the country.

The work is funded by the Department for Health and Social Care (DHSC), commissioned and administered by UKHSA, in collaboration with a number of delivery partners including the Office for National Statistics, research, academic and commercial partners. These studies provide insights that help assess the impact of measures taken to contain the virus, inform current and future government actions.
