Details

From September 2022, the Department for Education (DfE) is making funding available to local authorities that have participated in either the social workers in schools or designated safeguarding lead supervision trials, run by What Works for Children’s Social Care. This is to continue delivery of these intervention models during the 2022 to 2023 academic year.

The grant determination letter sets out the terms of the section 31 grant that local authorities will receive to support programme delivery.
