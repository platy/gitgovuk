Details

The Geospatial Commission partnered with Innovate UK to design the Transport Location Date Competition to look at how location data can spark innovation and support the future of mobility for the United Kingdom.

Smarter mobility solutions, underpinned by location data, will enable us to make the most of our transport networks by boosting capacity, reducing environmental impacts and decreasing travel times. The competition aimed to stimulate commercial innovation to help create geospatial solutions to our transport challenges. The competition had four themes:

  • Mobility as a service - to help better integration of transport modes
  • Active travel - creating safer ways to enable active travel
  • Supply chains - helping better distribution, storage and delivery
  • Boosting capacity - increasing efficiency of transport networks
