Details

The Disability Unit is seeking 2 senior business people to become Disability and Access Ambassadors. They will be champions for disability and access inclusion in their sector for 1 to 3 years.

Read the overview and candidate information pack to find out more. You can also find out how to apply.

The closing date for expressions of interest is 11:59pm on Sunday 10 July 2022.
