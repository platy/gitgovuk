Trust Capacity Fund (TCaF)

Apply for TCaF 2022 to 2023, a fund to help trusts develop their capacity and take on underperforming schools in education investment areas and other places of higher need.

Details

  1. Read the guidance document to find out more about TCaF and who it is for.

  2. Use the eligibility checker to find out whether you can apply – there is one for schools and one for trusts.

  3. Use the application form to apply – applications will close on 30 June 2022.

Funding for the window will be drawn from up to £86 million in trust capacity funding committed in the schools white paper available over the next 3 years. Details for allocating the remainder through a new fund will be announced in due course.

The use of any information sent to us is governed by the privacy policy.

There are also separate lists of Trust Capacity Fund award recipients from 2019 to 2022.

We have also published the methodology and datasets used to identify education investment areas and priority areas for strand A applications.
