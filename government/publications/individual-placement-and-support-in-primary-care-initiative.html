Details

The Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) and Department for Health and Social Care (DHSC) are providing Grant Funding, to take part in the Individual Placement and Support in Primary Care (IPSPC) initiative.

The initiative is aimed at adults who have a physical or mental health disability, as defined by the Equality Act 2010, to help them to move into competitive employment providing the support they need to maintain that employment.

Who can apply

Upper Tier LAs in England (including County, Metropolitan Councils, London Boroughs and Unitary Authorities) and Combined Authorities.

How to apply

Apply using the attachment at Appendix B: Grant Funding Application and return to:

Email: laped.individualplacementsupportfund@dwp.gov.uk

Closing date

By 5pm Friday, 31 August 2022
