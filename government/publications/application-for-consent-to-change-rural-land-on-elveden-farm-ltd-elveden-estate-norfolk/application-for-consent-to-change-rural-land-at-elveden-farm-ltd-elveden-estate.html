An application for consent and Environmental Statement has been received by Natural England for a project to plough 5 fields of improved grassland on Elveden Farm Ltd, Elveden Estate, to revert to arable use. The site is west of Lord’s Well Field SSSI, Breckland Farmland SSSI, Breckland SPA.

Comment on proposals

You have the opportunity to comment on the likely effects this proposal may have on the environment. You must email or post your comments to Natural England within 6 weeks of the date on this notice.

You can ask for details of the application and the associated environmental statement by email, or view the documents by visiting the Natural England office. Natural England may ask you to pay for a paper copy, if you want to take it away.

Use reference EIA 2845 in your correspondence.
