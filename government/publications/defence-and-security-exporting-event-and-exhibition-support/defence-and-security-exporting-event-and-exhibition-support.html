1. Exhibition support

Support offered by UK Defence and Security Exports (UKDSE) at exhibitions, where appropriate, includes:

  • government-to-government relationships
  • UK VIP programmes
  • delegation management (targeted audience)
  • military/security/cyber adviser(s)
  • bilateral meetings
  • government stand utilising high quality build
  • strong UK brand
  • UK speakers at selected conferences
  • marketing package, which may include:
    • brochures
    • postcards/invitations
    • presentations on capability or equipment

Specifically companies can participate on UKDSE stands through the following methods:

1.1 Exhibition booth / pod system

At certain exhibitions, UKDSE will offer companies the opportunity to exhibit as part of a UKDSE organised UK pavilion.

Main benefits of co-locating with UKDSE are:

  • exhibiting under clear UK/HMG branding
  • more cost effective in terms of stand space
  • senior delegations booked to attend the stand and spend time with companies
  • benefit from government-to-government relationships
  • UKDSE staff on stand alongside your company representatives
  • enhanced marketing before and during the event
  • access to a meeting room and on stand refreshments

The pod system typically comprises of 4 square meters of space, a modular stand system plus flooring; power socket; track lighting; 1 x lockable cupboard; 1 x stool; 1 x fascia panel (company name); 1 x graphic panel; meeting/hospitality room; refreshments; internet access; catalogue entry; 2 x exhibitor passes per company.

Cost is variable depending on the exhibition.

1.2 Export Support Team (EST) display area

Dependent upon UKDSE exhibition objectives, an EST display area may be included on the exhibition stand. Space and appropriate build with power is provided to display a range of UK capabilities and equipment as part of an agreed theme on the UKDSE exhibition stand. A suitable receptacle for marketing literature is also included.

Cost based upon the amount of equipment displayed.

1.3 Free build

UKDSE can provide free build space for companies within a UKDSE organised stand, subject to approval and availability.

Costs will be dependent on organiser space costs.

3. Export Support Team (EST)

The EST comprises of serving military personnel with a wide range of expertise.

EST is staffed by regular officers and soldiers, from the British Army. It includes members of the Royal Artillery, the Royal Armoured Corps, the Royal Engineers and the Infantry, who collectively possess a wealth of knowledge and experience both in training and on operations. The EST is a unique and intrinsic part of UKDSE.

The purpose of EST is to provide specialist military services and advice to legitimate UK defence and security companies in order to help them succeed in the export market.

The services the EST can provide include:

  • support at exhibitions including ‘soldier on stand’
  • impartial military advice
  • assisting with promotional material, by participating in photographic and video shoots
  • demonstrations and presentations (home and abroad)
  • hosting visits (foreign delegations)
  • after sales training and support

There are standard charges applicable to EST services. A detailed estimate of costs will be provided in response to requests for assistance.

The EST are co located with regular British Army units where they can arrange access to training grounds, including:

  • an all weather cross country driving area
  • ranges for gunnery and live firing purposes

These along with other areas and ranges throughout the UK can be used for product evaluations and demonstrations.

Contact trade-est-Ihubmailbox@mod.gov.uk for further information on the EST.

4. Media support

UKDSE has a dedicated senior press officer who can help UK companies attending exhibitions. This includes liaison with international, national and specialist media attending an event. Support will be provided onsite and remotely based on availability.

Contact adam.thomas@trade.gov.uk for more information on media support.

5. Defence and Security Industry Days (DSID)

UK companies can display their products onboard a Royal Navy warship on DSID. Reception and catering are provided. The service costs from £1,980 + VAT per company.

You can also request a private company visit which provides a similar event on a Royal Navy warship for up to 3 companies, without the reception or catering. The service costs from £1,980 + VAT per company.

Contact dit-ukdse.enquiry@trade.gov.uk if you are interested in attending future events.

6. Small and medium enterprises (SME) networking events

UKDSE SME Support team provides and supports a wide range of events and activities designed to help UK SMEs win overseas defence and security business.

Support includes:

  • regular networking sessions at Farnborough International Air Show and DSEI, which introduce UK SMEs to important overseas government, military and industrial procurement officials

  • supply chain events which give UK SMEs the opportunity to discuss their goods and services with leading UK and overseas prime contractors

  • regularly organising and attending UK conferences and seminars, to promote UK Defence and Security Exports support and services

If you are contacting us for the first time fill in our short contact form.

If you have already engaged with us email ukdse.smeenquiry@trade.gov.uk for further information on SME events.
