Details

At our community online meetings in September, October and November 2021, we updated on the future of Grenfell Tower, safety works at the Tower, noise monitoring and Tower banner illumination. We also gave updates on further soil sampling at two locations, the relocation of the local electricity substation in the Tower, and local opportunities available from the principal contractor, DUK. And we gave a reminder about the health and wellbeing services available to the community.
