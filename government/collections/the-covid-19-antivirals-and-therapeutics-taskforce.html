The COVID-19 Antivirals and Therapeutics Taskforce

The COVID-19 Antivirals and Therapeutics Taskforce is responsible for the end-to-end provision of treatments for coronavirus (COVID-19) in the UK.

The taskforce is responsible for:

  • identifying potential COVID-19 therapeutics
  • trialling these as part of an advanced programme of clinical trials
  • deploying effective treatments to UK patients

The taskforce works with industry experts to identify, develop and procure novel oral antivirals (those in pill or tablet form) that patients can take promptly following infection.
