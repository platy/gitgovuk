Contact us 24/7

You can call us, write to us or message us on social media.

If you need to contact us urgently or in an emergency, for example, if you have been in an accident overseas or arrested abroad, it’s best to call us.

Call the nearest British embassy, high commission or consulate , or the FCDO in UK on +44 (0)20 7008 5000, selecting the option: ‘Consular services for British nationals’. You’ll be put through to our 24/7 Consular team in our Consular Contact Centre.

You can also contact us by:

  • our web form
  • Twitter @FCDOtravelGovUK
  • Facebook FCDOTravel

We aim to reply to you in line with our service guidelines.

If you need to visit a British embassy, high commission or consulate then you should make an appointment first. For an appointment, call your nearest British embassy, high commission or consulate at any time, selecting the option ‘Consular services for British nationals’.  You’ll be put through to our 24/7 Consular Contact Centre who will make the appointment for you.  British embassies, high commissions and consulates normally follow opening hours of the country they are located in.
