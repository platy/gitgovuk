These maps show Freeport maps and Freeport tax sites which are designated and recognised in law as geographical areas where businesses can benefit from tax reliefs to bring investment, trade and jobs to regenerate regions across the country that need it most.

More maps of Freeports and Freeport tax sites will be added after they are designated.
