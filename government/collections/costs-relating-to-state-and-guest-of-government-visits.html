This collection brings together the costs relating to State and Guests of Government visits to the UK as Guests of Her Majesty The Queen (State Visits) or Guests of Government at the invitation of the Prime Minister or Foreign Secretary.

State Visit delegation lists are published on the Buckingham Palace Court Circular during the State Visit.
