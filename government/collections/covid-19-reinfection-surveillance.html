We are still at a relatively early point in the COVID-19 pandemic regarding opportunities for reinfection and limited cases have been published worldwide.

The aim of COVID-19 reinfection surveillance programme is to look at how long immunity lasts, protection against clinical disease (disease with symptoms) and protection against more severe disease. It is also important to understand whether those who become reinfected can pass the virus on to other people.
