These statistics have changed to a monthly publication schedule from 8 June 2021. We’ll publish the monthly statistics at 11am on the second Tuesday of each month.

The new publication now covers the most recent calendar month of PPE distribution.

The February 2022, March 2022 and November 2022 iterations of this publication will have a later publication date for operational reasons. Some of the data needed to feed in to this publications will not be available in time to support the usual publication date. These 3 will be published on the third Tuesday of the month instead.
