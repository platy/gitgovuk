COVID-19 antibody and antiviral treatments

Collection of documents relating to COVID-19 treatments.

From:
UK Health Security Agency
Published
28 February 2022

The NHS is offering new antibody and antiviral treatments to people with coronavirus (COVID-19) who are at highest risk of becoming seriously ill.

See treatments for coronavirus for further information including treatments available and eligibility for COVID-19 treatments.

These documents include information for patients on how long to take the medicines for, what to do if pregnant or breastfeeding and how to report side effects.

Leaflet versions of these documents are available to order and are for use by pharmacies to give to individuals who have been prescribed the treatments listed below.
