EU citizens and their families will be able to apply to get either settled or pre-settled status through the EU Settlement Scheme.

Guidance on settled and pre-settled status for EU citizens and their families.

Contact the EU Settlement Resolution Centre if you need help or information about your EU Settlement Scheme application.
