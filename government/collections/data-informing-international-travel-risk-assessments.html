Summaries of key data used by the Joint Biosecurity Centre (JBC) to inform international travel risk assessments. Red, amber and green list country decisions are taken by ministers every 3 weeks, informed by evidence including JBC’s analysis as well as other relevant information about the risk of the spread of SAR-CoV-2 and its variants.
