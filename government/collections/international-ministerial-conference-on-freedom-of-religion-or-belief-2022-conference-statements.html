International Ministerial Conference on Freedom of Religion or Belief 2022: conference statements

Countries represented at the conference were invited to co-sign statements on protecting, promoting and preventing violations of freedom of religion or belief.

From:
Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office
Published
5 July 2022

On 5 and 6 July 2022 the UK government hosts a human rights conference to urge increased global action on Freedom of Religion or Belief (FoRB) for all.

The conference brings together governments, parliamentarians, faith and belief representatives and civil society. National governments represented at the conference were invited to co-sign one or more of a set of 8 written statements. The set includes an overarching statement on the conference itself and 7 thematic statements covering freedom of religion or belief issues.

Each statement includes a list of government co-signatories. Further co-signatories will be added to each statement when governments confirm that they will sign.
