The UK-EU Trade and Cooperation Agreement (TCA, or the Agreement) provides for involving of civil society representatives from both Parties through the Civil Society Forum (CSF).

At the CSF, UK and EU participants can submit their views or recommendations on Part 2 of the Agreement, which includes trade, transport, energy, fisheries and social security coordination.

The first meeting of the CSF will be on 4 October 2022 in Brussels.

The CSF will meet at least once a year, unless otherwise agreed by the UK and EU, and it may also meet virtually.

For more information, see the operational guidelines for the conduct of the Civil Society Forum.
