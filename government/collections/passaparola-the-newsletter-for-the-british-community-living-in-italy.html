Newsletter

The British Ambassador’s update for British nationals living in Italy.

You can read the latest information about issues which affect UK nationals living in Italy in these newsletters, which are written by Her Majesty’s Ambassador Ed Llewellyn. This includes updates on topics such as residency, healthcare and travel.

Further information

You can also read more detailed guidance about a range of topics on our Living in Italy page.

For questions concerning your rights as a British citizen in Italy contact us.
