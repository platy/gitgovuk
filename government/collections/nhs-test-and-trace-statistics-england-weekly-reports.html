Supporting information

View an interactive map showing the percentage of cases and contacts reached and asked to provide details of recent close contacts by upper tier local authority, 28 May 2020 to 17 March 2021.

Due to preparation work for the next statistical release, this map will be unavailable on 1 April from 9:30am until 11:00am.

See experimental statistics from the Test and Trace Cases Insights Survey on people required to self-isolate after testing positive for COVID-19 and people required to self-isolate after being identified as having been in contact with someone who has tested positive for COVID-19.
