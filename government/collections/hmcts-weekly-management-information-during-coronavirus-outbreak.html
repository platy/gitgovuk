HMCTS weekly management information during coronavirus outbreak

The management information used by HMCTS for understanding the impact of the coronavirus (COVID-19) outbreak on workload volumes and activity at a national level on a weekly basis.

From:
HM Courts & Tribunals Service
Published
11 June 2020
Last updated
14 October 2021 — See all updates

We’re currently looking at the topics we cover and how we present the monthly management information to make sure it meets this criterion. Contact us if you have any feedback or questions on our MI publications.

We published this information during the COVID-19 outbreak to help explain how we delivered critical public services, from June 2020 to June 2021.

In July 2021, we paused publication of this information while we conducted a review. Following this review, we stopped publishing data on a weekly basis as this is included in the HMCTS monthly management information.
