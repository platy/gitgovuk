Details

This is an Experimental Official Statistics publication produced by HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) using HMRC’s Self-Employment Income Support Scheme (SEISS) data.

This publication covers the third grant for the SEISS administered by HMRC up to 31 December 2020.

The information presented includes:

  • age of claimants
  • gender of claimants
  • sector of self-employment activity
  • geography

For more information on Experimental Statistics and governance of statistics produced by public bodies please see the UK Statistics Authority website.
