Details

The COVID Winter Grant Scheme was made available from early December 2020 to 31 March 2021 to support those most in need across England with the cost of food, energy (heating, cooking, lighting), water bills (including sewerage) and other essentials. The scheme was extended to 16 April 2021.

The Department for Work and Pensions (DWP) provided funding to the 151 county councils and unitary authorities in England to deliver the scheme.
