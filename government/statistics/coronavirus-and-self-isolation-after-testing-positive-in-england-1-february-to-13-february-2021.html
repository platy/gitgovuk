Coronavirus and self-isolation after testing positive in England: 1 February to 13 February 2021

Experimental statistics on individuals required to self-isolate after testing positive for coronavirus (COVID-19), from the COVID Test and Trace Cases Insights Survey. This includes information on behaviour after testing positive for coronavirus (COVID-19) as well as the impact of self-isolation on well-being and finances.
