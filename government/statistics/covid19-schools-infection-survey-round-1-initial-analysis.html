COVID19 Schools Infection Survey, Round 1 initial analysis

Initial insights from the first round of questionnaire data collected as part of the School Infection Survey. Questionnaires are completed by parents of participating children, secondary school aged children and headteachers. Topics cover vaccine sentiment, ventilation in schools and preventative measures implemented in schools.
