Coronavirus and self-isolation after being in contact with a positive case of coronavirus in England: 1 March to 6 March 2021

Experimental statistics on individuals required to self-isolate after being identified as someone who had been in contact with an individual who has testing positive for coronavirus (COVID-19), from the Test and Trace Contacts Insights Survey. Includes information on behaviour after being contacted by the NHS Test and Trace service as well as the impact of self-isolation on well-being and finances.
