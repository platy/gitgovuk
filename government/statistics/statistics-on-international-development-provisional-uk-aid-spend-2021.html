Details

The United Kingdom provides official development assistance (ODA) to developing countries or through international organisations.

This publication presents provisional spending statistics on UK ODA for the last calendar year (2021), and UK ODA as a proportion of UK Gross National Income (the ODA: GNI ratio). It also presents ODA spending by main delivery channel (bilateral or multilateral delivery), by government departments and other official sources. It also supplies FCDO bilateral ODA by major regions.
