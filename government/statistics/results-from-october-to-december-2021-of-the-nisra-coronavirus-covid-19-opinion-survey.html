Results from October to December 2021 of the NISRA Coronavirus (COVID-19) Opinion Survey. Details

On 20 April 2020, NISRA launched a new NISRA Coronavirus (COVID-19) Opinion Survey designed to measure how the COVID-19 pandemic was affecting peoples’ lives and behaviour in Northern Ireland.

This publication summarises the key findings from October to December 2021 of the NISRA COVID-19 Opinion survey and is based on interviews carried out with members of the public and includes comparisons with previous quarters.
