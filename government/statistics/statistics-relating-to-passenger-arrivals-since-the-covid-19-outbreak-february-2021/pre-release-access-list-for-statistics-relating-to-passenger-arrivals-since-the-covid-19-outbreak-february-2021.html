Pre-release access list for:

Statistics relating to passenger arrivals since the COVID-19 outbreak, February 2021

Experimental Statistics published at 9.30am on 25 February 2021

This statistical bulletin was produced by analytical professionals and production staff in Home Office Analysis and Insight Directorate.

Pre-release access was given to the following persons 24 hours in advance of release:

Home Office

  • Home Secretary
  • Minister of State for Security and Immigration
  • Minister of State for Immigration Compliance and the Courts
  • Permanent Secretary
  • Director General of Borders, Immigration and Citizenship System Policy and International Director of Private Offices
  • Director of Compliance and Returns
  • Director General of Immigration Enforcement
  • Director of Border Force
  • Head of Migration Policy
  • Deputy Director, European Migration & Citizens’ Rights
  • Migration Policy Briefing Official
  • UK Visas and Immigration Official
  • Immigration Enforcement Official (2)
  • Special Advisers (3)
  • Press Office, Immigration Desk (4)

In addition, Private Secretaries in the Ministers’ and Special Advisers’ Private Offices will have access to pre-release figures for handling arrangements.

In addition to staff in the Home Office responsible for production of the statistics, the following staff also have access to pre-release figures for quality assurance purposes.

  • Analyst, Performance Reporting and Analysis Unit, Home Office (2)
  • Analyst, Border Force
