This statistical bulletin was produced by staff within the Forced Marriage Unit. Pre-release access was given to the following persons 24 hours in advance of release:

Home Office

  • Home Secretary
  • Minister for Safeguarding
  • Permanent Secretaries (2)
  • Special Advisers
  • Director General, Public Safety Group
  • Directors of Tackling Exploitation and Abuse (2)
  • Chief Statistician
  • Statistician, Crime and Policing Analysis
  • Press Officers, Vulnerable People Desk, Press Office (2)
  • Interpersonal abuse policy advisers (2)

In addition, Private Secretaries in the Ministers’, Special Advisers’, Permanent Secretaries’, Director General’s and Directors’ Private Offices will have access to pre-release figures for handling arrangements.
