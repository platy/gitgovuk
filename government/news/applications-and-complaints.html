How to submit applications and complaints to the CAC

The submission of applications and complaints to the CAC.

From:
Central Arbitration Committee
Published
17 March 2020
Last updated
30 December 2021 — See all updates

In light of the Government’s announcement on Monday 16 March 2020, all applications and complaints to the CAC should be submitted electronically to: enquiries@cac.gov.uk
