Upholding Standards in Public Life - Report

The Committee’s final report from its Standards Matter 2 review will be published on Monday 1 November.

From:
Committee on Standards in Public Life
Published
11 October 2021

The Committee’s final report from its Standards Matter 2 review will be published on Monday 1 November.

The report looks at the institutions, processes and structures in place to support high standards of conduct in government and makes recommendations to the Prime Minister.

The Committee launched this review in autumn 2020 and published its findings in June.

The Committee will also be publishing new public polling and focus group research conducted as part of this review.
