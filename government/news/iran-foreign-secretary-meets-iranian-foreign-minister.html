Iran: Foreign Secretary meets Iranian Foreign Minister

Foreign Secretary Liz Truss met Iranian Foreign Minister Hossein Amir-Abdollahian today at the UN General Assembly.

From:
Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office and The Rt Hon Elizabeth Truss MP
Published
22 September 2021

A Foreign, Commonwealth and Development spokesperson said:

The Foreign Secretary held her first meeting with Iranian Foreign Minister Hossein Amir-Abdollahian to discuss bilateral, nuclear and regional issues. She called for the release and return home of British dual national detainees.
