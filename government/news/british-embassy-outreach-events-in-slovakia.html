British Embassy outreach events in Slovakia

The British Embassy in Bratislava and senior Slovak officials will be holding a series of meetings to inform UK nationals living in Slovakia about their rights as the UK leaves the EU.

From:
British Embassy Bratislava
Published:
2 April 2019
Last updated:
16 March 2021, see all updates

If you are unclear about current residency requirements in Slovakia, or how the UK’s exit from the EU might affect you, come along to one of our upcoming events to ask the questions that most concern you.

We will add future events to this page in due course.

These events are free and open to all interested UK nationals. Please be aware that registration in advance is obligatory and ID will be requested on the door.

Upcoming meetings for UK nationals in 2020

The British Embassy will host a virtual outreach event for UK residents in Slovakia on Thursday 25 March 17.00-18.00 CET.

This will be an opportunity for you to hear about the main actions you need to take or consider and to ask questions about your citizens’ rights, such as on residence cards, driving licences, pensions and healthcare.

Due to the current Covid-19 epidemiological situation in Slovakia, for everyone’s safety, this event will be held via Microsoft Teams. You can join the meeting using the link below, even if you do not have Microsoft Teams on your computer.
