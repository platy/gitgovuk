Sign up to live step by step webinars which focus on moving specific goods between the Netherlands and GB

On demand events

Moving meat between the Netherlands and GB

Watch a recording of the ‘Moving meat between the Netherlands and GB’ webinar to help you understand how you can move meat, in this example frozen chicken, from the EU (the Netherlands) to Great Britain (GB) taking into account the further changes that will come into place from January 2022 onwards.

Moving fruit between the Netherlands and GB

Watch a recording of the ‘Moving fruit between the Netherlands and GB’. This webinar is to help you understand how you can move fruit, in this example peaches, from the EU (the Netherlands) to Great Britain (GB) taking into account the further changes that will come into place from January 2022 onwards.

Moving dairy between the Netherlands and GB

Watch a recording of the ‘Moving dairy between the Netherlands and GB’ webinar, recorded in English. This webinar will help you understand how you can move dairy, in this example cheese, from the EU (the Netherlands) to Great Britain (GB) taking into account the further changes that will come into place from January 2022 onwards.

Moving fish between the Netherlands and GB

Watch a recording of the ‘Moving fish between the Netherlands and GB’ webinar, recorded in English. This webinar will help you understand how you can move fish, in this example mackerel, from the EU (the Netherlands ) to Great Britain (GB) taking into account the further changes that will come into place from January 2022 onwards.
