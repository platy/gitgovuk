Extension to submission slots requirement

Requirement for submission slots for applications for Biological and Bio-Pharm new or extension variation marketing authorisation applications.

From:
Veterinary Medicines Directorate
Published
21 September 2021

The requirement for submission slots to be obtained for new and extension variation applications for biological and bio-pharm products, as described in our announcement on 7 May, has been extended until 30 June 2022.

Full details can be found in the guidance for Submission of an application for an animal medicine authorisation.

Please contact us at submissionslot@vmd.gov.uk to arrange a meeting.
