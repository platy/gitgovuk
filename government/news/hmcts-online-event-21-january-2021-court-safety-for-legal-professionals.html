HMCTS online event, 21 January 2021: Court safety for legal professionals

This event provides information on the arrangements in place at courts and tribunals to meet the Government’s COVID-secure requirements and Public Health guidelines.

Published 22 January 2021
Last updated 5 February 2021 — see all updates
From:
HM Courts & Tribunals Service

The webinar panel included:

  • Paul Harris – Director of Operations, HMCTS (Host)
  • Susanne Howes - Consultant in Health, Public Health England
  • Craig Robb - Deputy Director, Governance and Assurance, HMCTS
  • Mark Evans - Deputy Director, COVID-19 response unit, HMCTS
  • Mark Stewart - Acting Courts and Tribunals Director, HMCTS
  • Will Breame - Deputy Director, COVID-19 Response & Recovery, Central Operations and Tribunals Scotland, HMCTS
  • Louise Alexander - HR Director, HMCTS

If you attended, or have watched the recording, it would be great if you could take 2 minutes to fill out our post event survey. This really helps us to improve these events for the future.

We invited questions to be submitted in advance of the webinar. We have published answers to the questions received before and during the online event. More will be published in due course.
