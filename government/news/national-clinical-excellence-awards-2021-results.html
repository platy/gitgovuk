National Clinical Excellence Awards: 2021 results

Information about timings for the 2021 awards results.

From:
Advisory Committee on Clinical Excellence Awards
Published
23 December 2021

The results of the National Clinical Excellence Awards will be communicated to applicants in early January 2022. Nearly 2,000 applications were received for new and renewed awards in this round and we will be working over the festive period update our records.

All applicants will be informed of the outcome of their application using their registered email address, so please update your details via your online account if this has changed. Employers will also be informed of the new and renewed awards for their organisation.
