SK Rapid Wien v West Ham United travel advice

Travel advice for West Ham United fans travelling to Vienna for the SK Rapid Wien v West Ham United European League match on Thursday 25 November 2021.

From:
British Embassy Vienna

Due to UEFA sanctions, fans of West Ham United FC will not be allowed to attend the Europa League match against Rapid Vienna on 25 November 2021.

Now that attendance at the match is no longer permitted, West Ham United strongly urges supporters not to travel to Vienna. Fans will not be granted access to the Allianz Stadion.
