Change of Her Majesty’s Ambassador to Turkmenistan: Lucia Wilde

Ms Lucia Wilde has been appointed Her Majesty’s Ambassador to Turkmenistan.

From:
Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office
Published
7 October 2021

Ms Lucia Wilde has been appointed Her Majesty’s Ambassador to Turkmenistan in succession to Mr Hugh Philpott OBE who will be retiring from the Diplomatic Service. Ms Wilde will take up her appointment during November 2021.
