Government announces closure of Office of Tax Simplification

The Chancellor announced on 23 September as part of the fiscal event that the Office of Tax Simplification will be closed.

The Chancellor announced on 23 September as part of the fiscal event that the Office of Tax Simplification will be closed.  

The Chancellorâ€™s statement is part of The Growth Plan 2022.  As the Office of Tax Simplification is a statutory body, this closure will take effect when the next Finance Bill receives Royal Assent.
