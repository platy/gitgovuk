Autumn Budget 2021

A GAD technical bulletin has been published.

From:
Government Actuary's Department
Published
29 October 2021

In the latest Technical Bulletin, we look at the implications of this week’s Autumn Budget and Spending Review. The bulletin focuses on areas in which GAD advises, such as pensions and savings. The Chancellor also announced a reduction in the Universal Credit taper as part of a plan to build “a society that rewards work.”
