Change of British High Commissioner to Solomon Islands: Thomas Coward

Mr Thomas Coward has been appointed British High Commissioner to Solomon Islands and non-resident High Commissioner to the Republic of Nauru.

From:
Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office
Published
22 September 2021

Mr Thomas Coward has been appointed British High Commissioner to Solomon Islands and non-resident High Commissioner to the Republic of Nauru, in succession to Dr Brian Jones who will be transferring to another Diplomatic Service appointment. Mr Coward will take up his appointment during November 2021.
