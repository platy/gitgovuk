Kenya: call for bids for supply of drinking water

The British High Commission Nairobi is inviting bids for the supply of drinking water for its operations.

From:
British High Commission Nairobi
Published
28 May 2021

On behalf of the Secretary of State for the Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office (FCDO), the British High Commission (BHC) in Nairobi are seeking the services Supply of Drinking Water to be delivered for the High Commission’s staff. We are therefore looking for a Supplier that has the relevant professional skills, experience, technical resources and financial capability to provide comprehensive Supply of drinking Water for its operations.

Full details on the requirements, including instructions for interested bidders and registration are available via the FCDO’s e-Procurement portal, which requires registration. Competent and financially stable suppliers are invited to access the invitation to tender documents by following these steps:

  1. Open the Bravo Solutions website and sign in
  2. Navigate to Supply of Drinking Water CPG/3858/2021. ITT 4346. Project 6540

Contact the Regional Procurement Lead, Mr Tankiso Potiane at Tankiso.Potiane@fcdo.gov.uk for any queries.

Kindly note that the responses will be required to be in English.

Deadlines

  • please note that the invitation to tender documents should be completed and uploaded on the Bravo System by 17h00 CAT on 11 June 2021
  • please note there will be an E-Auction after submitting your bidding quotes

Disclaimer

The BHC reserves the right not to select any service provider and will only reply to the best-suited organisation.

The BHC will not meet any expenses incurred in preparing your Invitation to tender Documents.
