Civil news: Immigration and asylum advice in prisons

Amendments have been made to the immigration and asylum category specification to allow 30 minutes of advice to be given to immigration detainees.

From:
Legal Aid Agency
Published
1 November 2021

Following consultation with the representative bodies, changes have been made to the immigration and asylum category specification.

The changes will allow 30 minutes of initial advice to be available to immigration detainees held in prisons without reference to means or merits. This work will be paid at hourly rates and travel time can be claimed. Contract amendments will apply from 1 November 2021.

Full details of the contract amendments can be found on the Standard Civil Contract 2018 page on GOV.UK
