1 June 2021: Update to the Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT) fees

The Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT) fees changed on 1 June 2021.

From:
Intellectual Property Office
Published
10 May 2021
Last updated
1 June 2021 — See all updates

The Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT) fees changed on 1 June 2021 and is now as follows.

Transmittal Fee:

  • £75

Search Fee:

  • £1,526

International Fee:

  • for the first 30 sheets: £1,028
  • for each sheet over 30: £12

Restoration for Restoration of Priority: £150

Reductions for E – FILING

  • electronic filing (not being in character coded format): £155
  • electronic filing (being in character coded format): £232

Fees for preparation of Priority Document:

  • £20
