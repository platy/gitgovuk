Foreign Secretary Statement on Hong Kong Councillors

Foreign Secretary Liz Truss issued a statement today (21 October) on the disqualification of District Councillors in Hong Kong.

From:
Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office and The Rt Hon Elizabeth Truss MP
Published
21 October 2021

Foreign Secretary Liz Truss said:

It is deeply concerning that 55 District Councillors have been disqualified and over 250 pressured to resign for political reasons â€“ the first time that such action has been taken against democratically elected District Councillors in the Hong Kong SAR.
