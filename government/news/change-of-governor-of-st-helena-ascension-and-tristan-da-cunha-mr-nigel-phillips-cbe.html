Change of Governor of St Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha: Mr Nigel Phillips CBE

Mr Nigel Phillips CBE has been appointed Governor of St Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha in succession to Dr Philip Rushbrook.

From:
Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office
Published
4 April 2022

Mr Nigel Phillips CBE has been appointed Governor of St Helena, Ascension and Tristan da Cunha in succession to Dr Philip Rushbrook. Mr Phillips will take up his appointment during August 2022.
