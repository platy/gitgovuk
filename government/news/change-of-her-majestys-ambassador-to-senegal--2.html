Change of Her Majesty’s Ambassador to Senegal

Ms Juliette John has been appointed Her Majesty’s Ambassador to the Republic of Senegal and Non-Resident Ambassador to the Republic of Guinea-Bissau.

From:
Foreign, Commonwealth & Development Office
Published
6 April 2022

Ms Juliette John has been appointed Her Majesty’s Ambassador to the Republic of Senegal and Non-Resident Ambassador to the Republic of Guinea-Bissau in succession to Ms Victoria Billing OBE. Ms John will take up her appointment during April 2022.
