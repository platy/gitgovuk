Find out about all the latest events for all government analysts.

19 March 2021 | Webinar: Analysis in Government sharing webinar

Organised by the Best Practice and Impact Division (the delivery arm of the Analysis Function), these webinars will be a great chance for you to help create a culture of shared innovation across the Analysis Function and find out more about the most important topics for analysts across government.

The first webinar on 19 March will be on the evaluation of complex policies. Whether you work in evaluation and would like to build on your knowledge or are just interested in finding out more about this exciting area of analysis, it’ll be an exciting opportunity to learn about some of the complex and important analytical work that is underway across government.

Sign up on Eventbrite here – Analysis in Government Sharing Webinar

21 March 2021 | Census Day (England and Wales)

The census is a survey about all the households in England and Wales. The next census takes place on Sunday 21 March 2021. Read more about Census Day

24 March 2021 | Leaders in Analysis event - International Women’s Day

Our next Leaders in Analysis event on March 24 will celebrate International Women’s Day. We’ll be joined by Sophie Ingram (job share Director of Analysis and Finance Director for DNO (interim) at the Ministry of Defence) and Cat Little (Director General Public Spending at HM Treasury). Sophie and Cat will share their career stories, discuss moments that really mattered in their development and talk about the use of analysis in their current roles.

The event is a career development opportunity for analysts at all grades and working anywhere in the Analysis Function.

You’ll be able to ask questions via the chat function or Slido. Sign up now: Leaders in Analysis careers event (International Women’s Day) Tickets, Wed 24 Mar 2021 at 13:00 | Eventbrite
