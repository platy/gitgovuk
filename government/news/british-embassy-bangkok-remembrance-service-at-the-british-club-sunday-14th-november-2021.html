British Embassy Bangkok Remembrance Service at The British Club Sunday 14th November 2021

The British Embassy in Bangkok will be hosting this year's annual Remembrance Day Service at The British Club.

The British Embassy will be hosting the annual Remembrance Day service on Sunday 14 November at the British Club. Owing to the uncertainty over Covid restrictions, a final decision on format will be taken in mid to late October. If public gatherings continue to be restricted as at present, unfortunately it will not be possible to open up attendance to the public as in previous years, but we will endeavour to accommodate the maximum number of guests possible within whatever regulations apply at the time. We apologise for the uncertainty, but request your understanding given the circumstances. We will provide a further update in the weeks ahead.
