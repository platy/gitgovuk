Clipper Pennant safety bulletin published

Safety warning issued following a fatal crushing injury to a crewman on the upper vehicle deck of a roll-on roll-off ferry during cargo loading operations.

From:
Marine Accident Investigation Branch
Published
4 November 2021

Our safety bulletin based on the initial findings of the investigation into the fatal accident on board the ro-ro ferry Clipper Pennant while alongside in Liverpool, England on 20 July 2021, is now published.

Read our safety bulletin for details on the background of the accident, initial findings and safety lessons.
