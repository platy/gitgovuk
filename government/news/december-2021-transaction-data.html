December 2021 Transaction Data

This data provides information about the number and types of applications that HM Land Registry completed in December 2021.

From:
HM Land Registry
Published
24 January 2022

Please note this data shows what HM Land Registry has been able to process during the time period covered and is not necessarily a reflection of market activity.

In December:

  • HM Land Registry completed more than 1,480,810 applications to change or query the Land Register
  • the South East topped the table of regional applications with 342,488

HM Land Registry completed 1,480,815 applications in December compared with 1,840,720 in November and 1,431,694 last December 2020, of which:

  • 338,689 were applications for register updates compared with 345,740 in November
  • 691,482 were applications for an official copy of a register compared with 973,605 in November
  • 228,687 were search and hold queries (official searches) compared with 241,525 in November
  • 13,528 were postal applications from non-account holders compared with 16,310 in November
