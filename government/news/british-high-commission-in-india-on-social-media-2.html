British High Commission in India on social media

Follow the news and activities of the British High Commission in India.

British High Commission New Delhi
Published

As social media continues to evolve into one of the most important ways to communicate online, we are developing a strong presence across a range of channels, making us more accessible, more open and more transparent in the way we communicate.

You can follow us on:

  • Facebook: Follow updates on our events, news and activities from across India.

  • Twitter: Information for British Nationals in India, Department of International Trade (DIT) events, news and activities from the Foreign and Commonwealth Office (FCO), British High Commission, Deputy High Commissions and British Trade Offices in India.

  • Instagram: Follow us for latest images and videos from across India.

  • Flickr: Images from our events, High Commissioner’s and Deputy High Commissioner’s visits and ministerial visits.

  • YouTube: Watch our videos with UK ministers, policy experts, case studies, and much more.

  • LinkedIn Invite to all professionals to join our network for opportunities.

  • Follow DIT India on Twitter
