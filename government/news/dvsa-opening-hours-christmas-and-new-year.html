DVSA opening hours: Christmas and New Year

The Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency (DVSA) will be closed at times during Christmas 2021 and New Year 2022.

From:
Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency
Published
17 December 2014
Last updated
17 December 2021 — See all updates

Driving test centres, vehicle test stations and DVSA customer service centres will have different opening hours during Christmas and New Year.

DVSA’s online services and information will still be available on GOV.UK.

The bank holidays will affect the last date to change or cancel your test. Check the dates for a:

  • theory test or driving test
  • specialist vehicle approval tests
