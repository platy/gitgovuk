Call for bids to deliver climate action projects in Slovakia

British Embassy Bratislava invites Slovak non-profit organisations to submit proposals for projects to raise awareness and engagement on climate change.

From:
British Embassy Bratislava
Published
15 September 2021

Bids can be submitted for projects up to â‚¬5000 on one or both of these topics:

  • youth engagement with climate change in relation to COP26
  • promotion to a wide audience the real world impact of climate change in Slovakia

Successful bidders will be expected to provide end-to-end delivery of their proposals, including regular updates, as agreed in advance with the Embassy. We can consider co-funded activity but please make clear in any proposal if this is the case and the details of any co-funding.

All project activity must be complete by 14 March 2022.

How to apply

Please complete project proposal form (ODT, 55KB) and activity based budget (ODS, 10.3KB) documents and submit to BritishEmbassyBratislava@fcdo.gov.uk by 6 October 2021.

An Embassy programme board panel will assess the bids and the outcome will be communicated by 15 October 2021.

Assessment criteria

  • we can only grant this funding to non-profit organisations
  • feasibility of proposals setting out how this activity would be delivered
  • value for money
  • alignment with Embassy objectives
  • how impact will be sustained
  • consideration of risks to delivery
  • impact of proposal on gender equality, the climate and environment
