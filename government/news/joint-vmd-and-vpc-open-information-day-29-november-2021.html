We have published a series of pre-recorded presentations in our YouTube playlist for you to view at your leisure.

We have also opened a Slido Q&A which will be available until the 3rd December for your comments and questions. We will publish our responses on 10th December.

Go to slido and enter the event code: 603908:

  • using the Q&A tab, type the speaker’s name or presentation title and your questions / comments and either leave your name or you can remain anonymous. You can also view other entries and ‘like’ these.
  • using the Polls tab, please tell us about your overall experience of this years’ open day content. You can choose from: interesting and informative, satisfactory or could be improved. You are welcome to leave further comments in the Q&A tab.

Or email postmaster@vmd.gov.uk and quote “open day” in the subject. Please use the speaker’s name or presentation title or use “VMD” for general questions and comments.
