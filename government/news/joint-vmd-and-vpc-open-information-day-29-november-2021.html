Joint VMD and VPC open information day - 29 November 2021

Details of the joint VMD and VPC open information event.

From:
Veterinary Medicines Directorate
Published
14 October 2021

Following the success of last year’s virtual-only event and your feedback we will repeat the same format for this year’s VMD and the Veterinary Products Committee (VPC) open information event.

On 29 November, we will release a series of pre-recorded webinars for you to view at your leisure. We will also open a Slido Q&A for a few days for you to share your comments and questions. Responses to these questions will be published on 6 December.

There will be no ‘live’ event.

We will update this news item with more information including links to the webinars and full instructions on how to use Slido.

The webinars will cover a range of subjects, including:

  • Fipronil and the environment
  • The effect of ectoparasiticides on the environment
  • The effect of climate change on the use of medicines
  • Zinc oxide
  • Cannabidiols
  • Anthelmintic resistance
  • Regulatory Science Strategy
  • Enforcement
  • Inspections
  • E-learning
  • Our work in Sub-Saharan Africa
  • Veterinary Medicines Regulations: consultation plans

If you would like to be included on the mailing list for this event please contact postmaster@vmd.gov.uk.
