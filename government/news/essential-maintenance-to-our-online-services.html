There could be disruption to some Companies House online services while we complete important maintenance work.

Monday 12 September from 7am to 2pm.

We’re sorry for any inconvenience. Our Find and update company information service will be available as usual. Customers using the WebFiling service may experience some disruption. Submitted documents will be queued until the work is completed.
