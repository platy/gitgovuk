Watkins v British Medical Association: 14 October 2021

This hearing is on an issue remitted back to the Certification Officer by the Employment Appeal Tribunal UKEAT/0125/20/JOJ (V) following her decision of 14 October 2019 on Dr Watkins’ complaint relating to the union’s disciplinary procedure.

The case will be heard remotely using Zoom videoconferencing facilities. If you wish to join the hearing via Zoom, please contact the Certification Office by email info@certoffice.org or by calling 0330 109 3603.

If you have a disability and need help attending a hearing, please contact the Certification Office (info@certoffice.org / 0330 109 3602) to discuss any reasonable adjustment you need.
