Dawes v Royal College of Nursing of the United Kingdom:

Monday 3 October and Tuesday 4 October 2022

The applicant has made a number of complaints under section 108A (1) of the Trade Union and Labour Relations (Consolidation) Act 1992 that the union breached rules relating to its disciplinary process.

The case will be heard remotely using Zoom videoconferencing facilities. If you wish to join the hearing via Zoom, please contact the Certification Office by email at info@certoffice.org or by calling 0330 109 3603.

If you have a disability and need help attending a hearing, please contact the Certification Office (info@certoffice.org / 0330 109 3602) to discuss any reasonable adjustment you need.
