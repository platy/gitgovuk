February 2021 Transaction Data

This data provides information about the number and types of applications that HM Land Registry completed in February 2021.

From:
HM Land Registry
Published:
22 March 2021

Please note this data shows what HM Land Registry has been able to process during the time period covered and is not necessarily a reflection of market activity.

In February:

  • HM Land Registry completed more than 1,710,800 applications applications to change or query the Land Register
  • the South East topped the table of regional applications with 404,287

HM Land Registry completed 1,710,808 applications in February compared with 1,635,591 in January and 1,780,074 last February 2020, of which:

  • 284,618 were applications for register updates compared with 299,991 in January
  • 929,292 were applications for an official copy of a register compared with 870,631 in January
  • 226,733 were search and hold queries (official searches) compared with 204,003 in January
  • 14,368 were postal applications from non-account holders compared with 15,979 in January
