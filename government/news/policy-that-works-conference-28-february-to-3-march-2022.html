Policy that Works conference: 28 February to 3 March 2022

The Evaluation Task Force will be hosting the Policy that Works virtual conference between Monday 28th February and Thursday 3rd March 2022.

From:
Evaluation Task Force
Published
31 January 2022

The conference will be open to all civil servants and feature presentations, panel events and workshops designed to support good quality policy evaluation and evidence use across government.

It will include inputs from the Chancellor, Ministers and key international experts on ‘what works.’
