Original consultation

Summary

The SSRO’s reporting guidance helps defence contractors to prepare and submit reports required under the Defence Reform Act 2014 and the Single Source Contract Regulations 2014. Contractors must have regard to the SSRO’s guidance when preparing contract and supplier reports under the Act and the Regulations.

This consultation ran from
5pm on 13 April 2022 to 5pm on 8 June 2022

Consultation description

The SSRO is consulting on changes to parts of its current guidance. The topics we are consulting on are:

  • Reporting of sub-contract information and associated application of contract aggregation principles;
  • Reporting framework contracts;
  • General requirements for supplier reports; and
  • Company registration numbers for overseas contractors.

This consultation is open to all interested persons. The consultation runs until 8 June 2022. Should you wish to discuss aspects of this consultation then please contact: Simon.McCullough@ssro.gov.uk

Written responses should be sent by email to consultations@ssro.gov.uk

Please indicate on your consultation response if you are content with the SSRO publishing your specific comments.
