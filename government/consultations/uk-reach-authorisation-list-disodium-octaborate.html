UK REACH authorisation list: disodium octaborate

From:
Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs
Published
17 June 2022

Applies to England, Scotland and Wales

Summary

Call for evidence seeking information on the use of disodium octaborate.

This consultation is being held on another website.

This consultation closes at
11:45pm on 12 August 2022

Consultation description

We are seeking evidence on the use of disodium octaborate in Great Britain and the potential impact that adding disodium octaborate to the UK REACH authorisation list could have.

This is a joint call for evidence, issued by Defra on behalf of the UK Government, the Welsh Government, and the Scottish Government.
