Detail of outcome

The Lump Sum Exit Scheme is now open. Find out how to apply for a lump sum payment to leave or retire from farming.

Find out more about delinked payments and how they’ll replace the Basic Payment Scheme in 2024.

We received 654 responses to the consultation. We will be laying a statutory instrument to take forward some of these proposals

Detail of feedback received

We received 654 responses. The responses were from a variety of respondents including farmers, members of the public, environmental groups and farming organisations.

Original consultation

Summary

Seeking views on our proposals for a lump sum exit scheme and delinked payments.

This consultation was held on another website.

This consultation ran from
10:30am on 19 May 2021 to 11:45pm on 11 August 2021

Consultation description

We wanted to know what you think about the proposed lump sum exit scheme for farmers and our proposed approach to delinked payments.

We were seeking views on a set of questions including:

  • the eligibility criteria and aspects of how we should calculate the value of the lump sum
  • the reference period to be used to determine eligibility for, and calculate the value of, delinked payments
