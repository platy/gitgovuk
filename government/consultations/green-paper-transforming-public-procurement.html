Detail of outcome

This publication summarises the Government response to the consultation and elaborates on its plans for how public procurement will be transformed.

Detail of feedback received

The Government has now analysed the responses to the Transforming Public Procurement Green paper published in December 2020.

Original consultation

Summary

The government's goal is to speed up and simplify procurement processes.

This consultation ran from
15 December 2020 to 11:45pm on 10 March 2021

Consultation description

The proposals in this Green Paper are intended to shape the future of public procurement in this country for many years to come.

The government’s goal is to speed up and simplify our procurement processes, place value for money at their heart, and unleash opportunities for small businesses, charities and social enterprises to innovate in public service delivery.
