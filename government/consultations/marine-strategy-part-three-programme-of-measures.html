Detail of feedback received

We received 16 responses to the consultation and are considering the proposals as we update the UK Marine Strategy Part Three programme of measures. We aimed to publish this in summer 2022. We now aim to publish by the end of 2022.

Summary

Seeking views on the programme of measures proposed as part of the updated UK marine strategy part three.

This consultation was held on another website.

This consultation ran from
9:30am on 6 September 2021 to 11:45pm on 29 November 2021

Consultation description

This consultation sets out proposals for updating the UK marine strategy part three (2015). It shows the programme of measures the UK intends to use to achieve or maintain Good Environmental Status (GES) for UK seas.

We want to know what you think about this programme of measures. The measures will be used to help the UK achieve or maintain GES for UK seas.
