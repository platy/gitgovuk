Detail of outcome

The government’s response to the consultation on modifications to the Code of Practice for Schedule 7 to the Terrorism Act 2000 has now been published.

Original consultation

Summary

Consultation on whether the Schedule 7 Code of Practice is sufficiently clear to ensure the effective, fair and proportionate use of these powers.

This consultation ran from
9am on 15 March 2022 to 11:59pm on 12 April 2022

Consultation description

To: Representations are welcome from operational organisations who will use the powers, as well as interest groups and the wider public.

Please indicate in your response whether you are content for it to be published, with or without attributing it to you/your organisation.

Response paper: Following the consultation period, responses will be analysed and the draft code revised as necessary. They will then be laid before Parliament for approval.

Topic of this consultation: This consultation concerns modifications to the Schedule 7 Code of Practice as a result of the Nationality and Borders Bill and other general updates.

Scope of consultation: This consultation seeks representations on whether the Schedule 7 Code of Practice is sufficiently clear to ensure the effective, fair and proportionate use of these powers.

Geographical scope: UK wide.
