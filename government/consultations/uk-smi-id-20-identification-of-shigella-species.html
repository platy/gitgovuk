Consultation description

In September 2021, Public Health England opened a consultation in joint partnership with professional organisations.

This consultation asked for feedback in relation to the UK Standards for Microbiology Investigations (UK SMI) ID 20: identification of Shigella species.

We refined the process so that UK SMI users could comment more easily if they saw no requirement for amendments to the document out for consultation.

We requested that all feedback be provided through the survey form and not submitted as a PDF. Enquiries may be directed to standards@phe.gov.uk
