Detail of outcome

The government’s response to the Canada call for input is included in the UK approach to negotiating a free trade agreement (FTA) with Canada.

The government’s response to the Mexico call for input is included in the UK approach to negotiating a free trade agreement (FTA) with Mexico.

Detail of feedback received

We received 260 responses to this call for input. There were 149 responses for Canada and 111 responses for Mexico.

Original consultation

Summary

Seeking input on which aspects of current trading arrangements with Canada and Mexico we should look to improve or amend.

This consultation ran from
18 May 2021 to 11:45pm on 12 July 2021

Consultation description

We are preparing for trade negotiations with Canada and Mexico to upgrade our existing trade deals.

We want to know what you think about our trading arrangements with Canada and Mexico and where we could make changes or improve. We will use this information to help inform our approach for starting negotiations later this year.

We ask that all responses are submitted via the online call for input platform:

  • Canada
  • Mexico

If you have further accessibility needs please email DIT.MexicoCanada.CFI@trade.gov.uk.
