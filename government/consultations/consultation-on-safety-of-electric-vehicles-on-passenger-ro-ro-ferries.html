Original consultation

Summary

This consultation seeks the views of industry and stakeholders on proposed guidance for the safety of electric vehicles on passenger Ro-Ro ferries.

This consultation ran from
12:15am on 25 October 2021 to 11:45pm on 20 December 2021

Consultation description

This consultation seeks comments on a proposed new Marine Guidance Note which provides the UK shipping industry with best practice guidance to facilitate safe carriage and charging operations of electrified vehicles being transported onboard roll-on roll-off passenger (Ro-pax) ferries. The MCA has developed this guidance in conjunction with, and at the request of, industry.
