Detail of outcome

This is the outcome of the consultation on possible changes to rule 10 of the Upper Tribunal (Lands Chamber) Rules 2010 in relation to the costs of ‘CAAD appeals’.

This publication contains a summary of the consultation responses and includes the TPC’s reply setting out its approach following the consultation’s conclusion.

Original consultation

Summary

The TPC is interested to receive your views as to possible changes to rule 10 of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal) (Lands Chamber) Rules 2010.

This consultation ran from
midday on 14 December 2021 to 11:59pm on 8 February 2022

Consultation description

This consultation considers a potential change to rule 10(6) of the Tribunal Procedure (Upper Tribunal) (Lands Chamber) Rules 2010. The proposed rule change concerns the power of the Upper Tribunal to award costs in “CAAD appeals”. Responses to the consultation will be considered by the TPC.
